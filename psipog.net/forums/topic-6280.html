It was kinda cool. This morning I went to cash my paycheck, but because I live in MN, the window was iced over at 7:30 in the AM.

So I got to live the joys of scraping the window off. I decided to have some fun with it, considering no one in my neighborhood ever wakes up that early on a saturday, so I figured I could try a little pryo-PK.

Basically, I just did the psi ball, and did the pyro thing with it... put it on/in/around/whatever the window. I kept my hands down and away from the window(as in, I didn't raise them)

It worked- just not as well as I'd hoped. after about two minutes, it DID create a circular patch about an inch in diameter that had melted in the middle of a part I hadn't scraped off yet.

So I wrote of my pyro-PK skills as an alternative to scraping the window- judging by what I saw, I don't have enough focus or power to do a whole window.

It didn't even occur to me untill about four hours later that I should have taken a picture.

It was nice to see that it works, but my pyro skills need work.
