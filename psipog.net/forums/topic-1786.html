I think you are being too obsessed and too focussed at your environment. You do not focus on your meditation, instead you focus at the noisy things that might distract you. Just let the outside world go. And say to yourself: "Outside noises will not disturb me in my meditation. Outside noises will help me to concentrate on my meditation even more."

And if you do get distracted, do not feel frustrated, just listen to the sound that distracted you, and let it go by, and then relaxely go back to your meditation.
