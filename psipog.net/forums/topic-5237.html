Hi all,

wow - it feels great to associate and get to know other people who share the same interest in developing their minds. I'm Sam, 25 yrs old, from Finland, Europe. Been interested in metaphysics, spiritual growth, mind power, the power of imagination and different applications of those etc. for about a decade now. I've been experimenting with different methods from basic-natured Silva Method Basic Lecture series to more "modern-day" systems. Now I've been working on my own techniques, on the basis "the results are the acid test for truth. If I get results, then it is working. If not, then I need to revise & change, until it yields something concrete."

While I have experienced some fairly wonderful results, I'm still a student learning the ropes...

And believe it or not -- I do face periods of somewhat great bewilderment and uncertainty -- even at this point So it is not like I'd be at my best everyday, but I'm getting there. The thing is that "letting go of the need" to control the results makes the process ever so much faster. I have had seemingly indifferent goals "manifest" while those goals I'm most "concerned" about seem to delude me. But I'm learning

Look forward to participating in all the fun here -- and hope to make some new friends along the way.
