I'm tired and didn't have the patience to read all of the posts, so I'll tell you what I think happened. Hopefully Im not repeating someone, sorry if I am. =x

I believe you had Sleep Paralysis. I've had it many times, and usually I see an entity, but don't hear anything. Basically your subconscious displays the last mental picture it had of your room, and tricks you into thinking you're awake, but in reality, you're still sleeping. I forget if there's a chemical involved, but there's a reason for your paralysis.

Some people report having a pleasurable experience during SP, but the majority are horrifying experiences. I've only heard voices during it once, and it was a maniacal, high-pitched laughter. The others have been visions of an alien, a shadow figure, etc. One time my whole vision shook and I felt just a presence of something malicious. A girl I know has only heard voices in her SP, so it's perfectly normal.

So, in conclusion, they're just constructs of your subconscious. Many people claim it has links to alien abduction; however, regardless of what I think, it's most likely nonsense. There is no boogie man out to get you, it's all in your head. So no worries. =P

My advice? Try not to sleep on your back, it happens most in that position. Also, try to relieve any stress you've built up through the day, or it will transfer out to when you're sleeping. If you're caught in an SP experience, focus on one part of your body (the solar plexus, in your case) and try to move only that. It should wake you up pretty quick. Hope I helped!

As for the ACTUAL topic at hand...
I'm not too sure about the most recent theory of the presence of a demon. Not that I'm saying they don't exist, but encounters I've had with anything that could possibly -be- a demon haven't really worked that way. Yeah, they're creepy, but it takes quite a bit for them to work that kind of effect, if that is even a valid explanation.
I'm more for the sleep paralysis explanation. After all, the explanation with the fewest amount of assumptions is more likely to be correct.

For something such as the 'demon' explanation, there are quite a few assumptions you would have to make, nearly if not entirely on faith, for it to work out. First, you'd have to assume that demons exist. Then, you'd have to assume that they would be capable of possession and/or causing the phenomenon of evil voices and frozen body to occur. Then, on that assumption, you'd have to assume they'd -want- to screw with Xiou's mind. I mean, no offense, but wouldn't screwing with World Leaders be more fun and have a greater effect when it comes to fucking with humanity? For that matter, that would require the assumption that they want to screw with any human minds. Do you see where I'm going here? Saying it's demons, or any other type of 'psychic entity' just takes too many assumptions.

The response of sleep paralysis or any other more... normal... explanation takes a lot fewer steps. The only assumption necessary was that... Well, there aren't really any assumptions that I can see. We already know that our minds can be mistaken. If nothing else, it could've been Xiou waking up in the middle of a dream or something.
That's my opinion, though. Feel free to disagree.
