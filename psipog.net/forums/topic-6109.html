This will perhaps sound a little strange, and I don't know if you can consider it DMILS or not, but whenever I experience pain nowadays I try to take a moment away from the instinct of yelling, or suffering or whatever said reaction may be and just feeling it for simply what it is. I find that if you do that it calms your mind, you can think more clearly about what to do about the situation, and makes the pain much more tolerable.
