I've had many many astral-projections over the course of the last 5 or so years. Over that time, I've seen and experienced the wonders associated with this phenomenon. I've met "demons", spirit ?guides, and energies found in the astral layers. Here's one for you all to check out and comment. I've posted this story on another website. I decided to copy/paste it cause it would be a pain to re-type it all out again.

This is a real story, not some made up flight of fancy. This did happen, and I remember it as if it were yesterday-

-----------------------------------------------------------

The first encounter with an entity happened about a couple of years ago or so. I was sleeping when I found myself slightly woken to my father comming home from work. I usually hear his keys click against one another when he's about to open the door downstairs. Anyhoo, he does his usual thing by turning on the hallway light and then sit on the stairs to take off his shoes. When the light was turned on, I was woken even further to the point of me being able to see the light was turned on, and I turned my attention to my open bedroom door. Apparently I awoke to find myself in an astral state. Totally clear, focused, and consious.

The first thing I noticed when I looked at the door was a dotted black mass swirl and take the shape of a person riding a camel, a dromedarie to be exact. When the camel came in my room, its head was bobbing slowly up and down as it started to walk past my feild of view. The entire figure was like a silouette, but I could tell there was someone riding the camel. The camel/figure had a well defined egde, except for the head of the rider, the riders head was vibrating back and forth very fast.

Truly an amazing sight to behold.

I imagine that if others would have seen the same thing they would have freaked out bad. I on the other hand wasn't scared, it was more like sheer wonder and awe. I felt no evil, or malice from this entity, just a relaxed calm as it passed me by.

THEN the entity stopped in it's tracks. It must have noticed me noticing it. It checked me out as I checked it out. After a few seconds, it continued on it's way. and I slipped back into unconsiousness just as my dad was making his way upstairs.

After a few days of mezmerizing over my experience, I decided to do some research online. I went to a few wiccan/occult chatrooms and told my story. Someone said that I have came across what as known as "The Shax". So, I looked it up and seen the image of the demon in question. It sort of looked like what I have saw, but not really. I knew I saw a camel, and a small figure riding it. The Shax's image is a stork with a horse. So I went ahead and clicked randomly on the demon names to see the images.

And then I found him, Paimon.

THIS is who I saw that night. The silouette matches much better with this image than any other. The camel with the small rider who was wearing a large crown.

I wish I would have noticed a forum section on this site a long time ago. I would have been glad to put this for all to read then. Better late than never I suppose.

But- I feel that this was a chance encounter and consider myself very lucky to be allowed the privilege to know of entities just beyond our general perception. I would love to have an extended "sit-down" with Paimon, at his own leasure of course. I'm sure he has business to attend to like anyone else and it would be rude to yank him away from his life. Whenever I project, and have the presence of mind, I try to politely call out to him to see if he's in the mood to talk. So far, nothing yet as far as a "sit-down" but I think I saw him a couple more times and even spoke with him on matters that I forgot about.

But that is another story all togeather for another post.
