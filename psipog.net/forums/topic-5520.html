ugh. your post gave me a headache from trying to decipher it.

basically, if you cant remember your dreams, go to sleep five minutes earlier. spend those 300 seconds counting large numbers in the hundred thousands. and inbetween say i will remember my dreams. example

two-hundred-thousand-five-hundred-and-fifty-four
i will remember my dreams
two-hundred-thousand-five-hundred-and-fifty-five
i will remember my dreams
two-hundred-thousand-five-hundred-and-fifty-six
i will remember my dreams
two-hundred-thousand--five-hundredand-fifty-seven
I will remember my dreams
two-hundred-thousand-five-hundred-and-fifty-nine
I will remember my dreams

sometimes you can skip a number so it confuses the shit outta your brain to where you wont be able to count much longer at a steady pace and you'll doze away quite quickly. when you wake up you should have a clear memory of what happened. give it a shot
