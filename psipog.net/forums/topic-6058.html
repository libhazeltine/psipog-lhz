Hmm...I always thought micro-PK dealt with odds, and random number generators and such. But I agree with Bladeslinger, there is only psychokinesis, which is the manipulation of matter. The others branch off of psychokinesis. Also, please, PLEASE note that all of those branches are the manipulation of the substance, not the creation. For example, "pyrokinesis" is the manipulation of fire, not the creation of fire, or heat for that matter. The creation of heat or fire is probably caused by plain old psychokinesis, and the reason the object/substance is heating up is from the violent vibrating of the atoms that make up the object. People are also debating whether "cryokinesis" is real or not. The reason things cool down is because the atoms are being slowed down in their movements, and because heat is being taken away from the object/substance/area. Both of these are due to plain old psychokinesis, which (as you will find out the more you study/research psionics or physics) is the only "physical ability" or only ability dealing with telekinesis.
