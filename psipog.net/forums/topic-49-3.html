I've been an empath for seven years or so.

A lot of what I've learned is that although shields are necessary, when an empath shields too much, there is always the possibility of the shields becoming ingrained. Some people who are empathic that I know had natural shields to the point that they were headblind.

All I can say is that either end of the extreme with empathy can be problematic. Overloads are not fun, and if you can't shield, or have no natural shields, I strongly recommend learning to shield.
