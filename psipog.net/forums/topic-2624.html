What part of the field do you intend to study? Most of the study of psionics is properly part of Physics and Engineering: Physics, for the theoretical stuff, and Engineering, for the applied. If you're looking at how or whether a person's state of mind affects their ability, overall or in a specific circumstance, then it falls under Psychology or Biology....depending on how you are going about the study. How does psionic activity affect the body (such as the potassium issue)? Biology, Biochemistry, Physiology.... There are aspects of psionic investigation that wander into the other fields, but those are the main ones to start from.
