How are you requesting the information?

Do you want visual information? Then ask for visual information. You can ask for what the environment smells like, or ask to feel like you're there. Or ask to see a birds eye map of it. Or ask to see the predominant color.

I personally usually ask for colored geometric shapes. That way I can assemble the shapes after I'm done, and try to figure out what it is (to emphasize: I guess what the shapes make AFTER I'm done viewing, NOT WHILE viewing). I outline my method in my article, Receiving Images using your Subconscious.
