Psipog was a great place back in the day. Now it seems like the focus has shifted from psionics to mental retardation. I came back here recently looking for some real discussions of psionics, where view points come from experience. But now all posts are basically rants about the mind, ego and spirituality. Where every post is like some fierce cry for attention and acknowledgement of wisdom. These topics are paradoxes. If you are truly wise you will abstain from discussion as they only cause more confusion on the search for truth. All posts concerning these things are just one of the thousand artificial ways of looking at the truth, they are dregs on the path of wisdom. If you do not think they are true then do not accept them. They are like temporary ways of thinking which can cause more headaches and more suffering. My advice before I leave is that if you have any wisdom whatsoever then why expend on people who will not listen? If you want to have ultimate knowledge then why give your secrets away? Just listen to other people and learn, accumulate, and don't add more to the fire.

So long Psipog,
psiready
