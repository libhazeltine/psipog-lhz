I've eliminated it down a lot :

1. Kyo (samurai deeper): he's quiet, calm, cool, barely talks, fast, almost invincible (because you'd have a hard time trying to touch him), scary as hell, "a bit" of a jerk, perverted, and bloodthirsty. What more could you want from a samurai?

2. Kyoshiro (samurai deeper): funny as hell, calm, nice, secretly perverted, venerable and noble, and as skilled and fast if not more so than Kyo.

3. Rock Lee (Naruto): Fast, hard-working, bushy eyebrows, outcast. I can't think of a better anime character that suits my ideals (and some of my personality traits)

4. Naruto: A funny knuckle-head ninja who works harder than almost anyone else. How could I forget him?

5. Sasuke: calm, cool, barely talks, mean looking, highly skilled. That's probably how a few people at school percieve me (omitting the skilled part though)
