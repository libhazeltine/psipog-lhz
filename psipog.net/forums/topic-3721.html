Don't worry too much - I have only been trying this for a couple of days, and I can get the psiwheel to move, but can't 'get' anything from the whole psiball thing. I just sit there feeling silly with my hands together for 5 mins hah.

A good term I have heard in the past, which seems to work for me on the psi wheel is 'focused inattention'. i.e. be very aware of the wheel in front of you, but don't strain yourself to 'force' it to move. Be very casual and almost keep looking away from it every now and again, then let your eyes come back always keeping the wheel in your mind.

Good luck, and let us know how you get on

I personally have never heard of an instance such as that, however I do have one word of caution: if you do become adept at PK it may be a little difficult to get into those abilities because they don't bring definite, visual evidence of its success like PK does. When PK works, you see it. WHen telepathy or empathy works, how do you know it isn't just a random thought or feeling floating through your mind? If you do get PK down, and with patience and practice you will, just make sure to have plenty of patience when dealing with the less visible disciplines.

EDIT: Also, I can't stress enough the importance of daily, or nearly daily practice. I used to be able to manipulate up to 5 psiwheels at once, but i stopped practicing for several months and now can barely work 2, an thats only after a long time of warming up on 1. Taking a short break every now and then can actually help though.
