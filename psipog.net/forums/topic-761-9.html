You just program a construct to do something. If you can make a semi-decent psiball, then that is what you should start with. Make a psiball and tell it to obey you and then tell it to for example orbit your head and don't forget to give it a destruction word, when you don't want it around anymore. I usually programmed my beasts to dissipate into air when i verbally say their name 3 times.

Don't go nuts on the programming, if you give it too much or too complex commands, it won't understand them and you'll end up with a retarded pet like i once did.

I even had to tell her to use her wings to get around. Honest mistake, yes, but when i talked to it, it kept yelling nonsense like: 'red pants on the hose!'

So figure out your programming BEFORE you start making the pet, so you know exactly what you want and can make it understand you perfectly.
