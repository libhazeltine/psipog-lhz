Robert Bruces' explanation for this, in his book Astral Dynamics, discusses the astral body and its jolt back to the physical body. When you are 'dreaming' having an OBE during regular sleep which you are not concious of, and you encounter a potentially threatening experience, such as missing a step on a set of stairs, falling off a cliff, or what have you, the astral body is YANKED back to the physical body. when it reenters you jolt awake to make sure your physical body is still ok. this can also happen if you are concious and physical body starts to fall asleep to enter an OBE, the sudden rush of vertigo tricks your subconcious into thinking your falling and you have to jolt awake to get a grip on reality. im not 100% sure if i correctly restated his thoughts, they may have been intertwined with my own, but thats what i have come to believe.
