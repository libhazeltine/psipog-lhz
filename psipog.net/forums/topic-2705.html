If I have no shields up at the time I activate an "expander" first, which just pushes all stray energy out as it expands to shield size. You can make a shield this way as well, start it out small, program it to do what you wish, then expand it slowly adding more psi until it is the required size and shape. Then shell it and add more programming on what you want it to do.

Always when practicing shielding for the first times check them every hour or so. They will tend to fall apart easily until you get more experience.
