I'm totally new to all this psi stuff, but recently I found out something
very interesting.

I don't know if I should post this up in Dream section or here...

I started to have dreams about other people's memory.
For example: I was dreaming about crawling down mountain, and
it was fall. I felt desperate, and I saw leaves everywhere, orange,
brown, yellow colored leaves were everywhere. It was very vivid,
and the whole time I was dreaming, I thought of my boyfriend.
I "knew" that somehow, this dream is related to him, even if he
wasn't in it actually.

So I asked him if he could recall anything like that, and he was
very much surprised to hear my story, because it was what
happened to him when he was 7, at a camp, while hiking,
he fell off to a cliff, (slid off really) and luckily survived.
But he was so shocked and paranoid after the accident, he basically
"crawled" down the mountain. Literally like a baby.
He also said that he saw leaves everywhere, it was september.
(I didn't mention it to him. but HE TOLD ME) The mountain was
basically Covered in fallen leaves (this is exactly what I saw)

And things like this kept occuring to me with people I'm close with
I saw my girl friend's middle school day(another interesting story)
I remembered how exactly her school looked like, what color the walls
were, what kind of windows they had, how her friend looked like,
how their school uniform looked like, and what they were talking about.
It was so detailed and specific, that my friend ended up really
freaking out about.

I don't know if this is related to any of the "telepathy" thing
Maybe it was just a coincidence.. but to every detail, it was amazing
how I could see it so accurately.
