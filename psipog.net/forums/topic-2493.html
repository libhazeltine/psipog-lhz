You aren't physically relaxed enough.

You need to be very physically relaxed to OBE. Essentially, your body needs to be asleep. Next time you attempt an OBE, work your way from your toes to your head and feel each individiual muscle and joint relax; feel the tension leave each one. Take as long as you need.

Ideally, you'll get the arms-rising sensation without the arms rising.
