Want to make things easier? Make a HUGE psiwheel. Get a sheet of loose leaf notebook paper, and square it up, the holes in it are fine, just leave those. It'll end up being around 6" by 6", make your psiwheel and find a way to balance it. Maybe on a pen/pencil shoved in a shoe crammed tight with a few socks. You might even have to put that whole set up on a couple books if the wheel is still hanging off and dragging. Forget about the toothpick, just focus on psiwheels. For some reason larger psiwheels are easier to move, even if they move more slowly. Good luck!
