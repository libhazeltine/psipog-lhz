I just thought of a cool exercise and tried it out. Works pretty well and it's easy to do. Sort of similar to the psiwheel (because it involves paper) but different.

Al you need is a 1, 5, 10, 20 etc dollar/pound/any other nationality note. Fold it slightly in half and balance it on a table so that neither or one side of the note touches the table. Then simply try and wobble the side of the note that is in the air. I tried making it touch the table by pushing down on it. I havn't managed to hold it down yet, keeps sprining back up.
