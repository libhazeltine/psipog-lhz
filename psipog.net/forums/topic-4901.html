You should probably be more able by now . But if you have had no "Goal" it would have just been a little game to make the sensations and what not.

Try mimicking physical objects as your template for something more substantial. For example:

If you want to form it into a ball, think of the shape of a tennis ball or softball (I like the smaller ones), and then think of a physical sensation you have experienced to give it that sensation. For example, if you run your hand under some warm water, memorize the feeling and impose it on the Psiball.

Same can be said for any feeling. It'll just take a little bit of practice, and creativity.
