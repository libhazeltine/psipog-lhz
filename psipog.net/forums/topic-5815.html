From time to time (once a month is a reasonable estimate), I will have a dream in which I am exactly where I physically am, aware of everything that should be surrounding me -- bed, blanket, my room is as it should be. I can't SEE that, but I just know it. I have had a bit of an internal conflict concerning whether I am dreaming or awake when this happens.

I will believe I am awake and will try to open my eyes. I get that feeling that you get when your eyes are adjusted to darkness and you brightly light up the room in which you are, and my eyelids won't open the slightest bit.

I try to sleep in the closest thing to complete darkness that I can -- there are dim lights from the air conditioner, alarm clock, computer LEDs, and some light leaking through the bottom of the door from the kitchen, but major things like the computer monitor, television, light bulbs are off. I pretty much only see darkness unless I go out of my way to find a weak light source, such as those that I mentioned I leave on.

Though I'd have no trouble with my eyes refusing to open if I were to wake up in the middle of the night and still be sleepy, one thing that these dreams have in common is that I shield my eyes from any "light" with my hand and then attempt to open them again. I succeed, but see nothing, and when I remove my hand, the "overwhelming brightness" (though all I see is darkness) will force my eyes shut.
