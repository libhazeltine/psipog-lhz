The scientific evidence for telekinesis is actually quite obvious, and you don't really need a website to prove it. Only look at what you see around you and what is in history. (I may get a little into religion here, but I promise to keep it brief and objective, so please don't lock the topic)

From what I have seen, there is evidence for a LOT of stuff that we previously thought impossible or nonexistant. Take dragons, for example. There are several mentions of dragons in ancient texts from around the world, including the Bible (the Leviathan is, in fact, a dragon, and not a crocodile as some translations would have it. Crocodiles do NOT breathe fire). There is also equal if not more evidence of a worldwide flood; every ancient culture has a flood story. The same is true with psionics: Magic in England, Chi in China, and Chakra in India. These places were huge distances apart, and yet there were many practitioners everywhere. Where there's smoke, you can usually count on a fire of one kind or another. What I'm trying to figure out is where this fire is, and how big it is, figuratively speaking; how much you can do with psionics before it goes into a realm I'd rather not mention (and cannot mention) on this forum. How far we can go with just human power alone, basically.

As for evidence today... yeah, it's possible that this entire site is a hoax (I don't think it is, but anything's possible... there's another evidence in itself). If that were true, a lot of people went to a lot of trouble for such an elaborate hoax, and that's just one site out of thousands. My basic "proof" for this is "Why not?" I have seen a lot of things that say it could be possible, and no evidence to the contrary. (Not saying that just because you can't disprove it it doesn't exist; psi has some hard evidence, even if it's hard to come by)

Well, there's all the evidence I can come up with off-hand. The old addage, "If so many people agree, it's most likely true" applies here. I know for a fact that there is something in psi. I'd just like to find out how much.

PS: Sorry if I pissed off a moderator with my religious stuff. I'm keeping it objective here, and I continue to insist that, although a large amount of its evidence comes from religious texts, psionics itself is a completely religion-neutral ability. Hope that cools your jets a little
