I suppose it could go either way on this one Serge.

If you are thinking a lot about a givin subject, chances are it will end up in your dreams. This has often happened to me with various things not limited to Psi. Ie. Video Games, Martial Arts.

However, I wouldn't discount the possibility that it could be a bi-product on practicing empathy. Think about who you are practicing with, if anyone, or if anyone near you is going through an emotional time.

Either way, it's not too significant that you're having those dreams about that stuff, it's all part of life .
