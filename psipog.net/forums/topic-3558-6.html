Regardless, you missed my point. It's quite obvious that your measures toward addressing a psychic attack complaint (or however you'd like to reference it) is to use psionics. You would, in my opinion, need a great degree of skill to know whether or not someone is being attacked, and you'd need even more skill to stop it. A lot of newbie psions have the tendency to wrongfully attribute the most normal physiological and emotional feelings to something psychic. Tell me if any of these sound familiar:

1) I'm in a bad mood, it must be my empathy and I'm surrounded by lots of angry people.

2) I have a headache and feel tired. I think I'm being drained by a psionic vampire.

3) I felt like coming to the chatroom. Someone must have pinged me.

These claims are hard to verify and are 9 times out of 10 results of a certain paranoia. I'm sure everyone goes through this stage at one point or another. It's not their fault, it's part of the process of assimilating this new paradigm of information (psychic functioning) and learning to differentiate between a normal effect and a psionic effect.

The fact of the matter remains whether or not you can truly tell when someone is being attacked or has been attacked, whether or not you can CORRECTLY identify the attacker (should there actually be one), and whether or not you can effectively resolve the situation.

I think you and those who've volunteered are incapable of all three. I think you would do more harm than good. I think it's a stupid idea.
