darkvoid wrote:
btw I have another question, would it increase my telepathic skill if I gave time on increasing my psi energy, I mean like taking an hour or two to focus and increase the energy on my forehead. Would it help to increase telepathy?


I don't recommend focusing lots of energy on your forehead - we have no evidence to demonstrate what part of the brain, or even the body, is responsible for telepathy - we could do it through our skin. Focusing energy in any area of your brain is somewhat risky as it carries the chance of disrupting neuroelectrical activity.

As far as psi workings influencing it, I'm unsure there, too...there's little evidence that telepathy works along the same pathways and channels as normal psi workings - shields do seem to influence telepathy, but so do power plants. Also, typical psionic shields don't seem capable of blocking telepathic intrusion or reception, instead it requires some specialized programming.
