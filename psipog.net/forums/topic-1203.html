ok, so in experimenting with various psi techniques i found an interesting pattern. I noticed that if i concentrate hard enough i seem to have changed the reality of that space, where what i visualized is then possible. For example, i used to practice psychokinesis in a certain deserted bathroom at my college. I used to concentrate on moving the water in the toilets. This was reccomended to me by a person very strong in psychokinesis. Anyways, one day i actually did it, and the water looked like it had been punched. It scared me to death, but my point is, everytime before that the water did nothing, but after that first time, i could go in that bathroom and vaguely think about it and the water would move.

Another example is when i was reading about physical mediums (people that can manifests spirits) and while reading about a certain medium, the things i was reading became real in front of me (metaphorically speaking.. it was as real as you can get by way of imagination) and then i heard knocking on my walls, and a sound like someone snapping their fingers. Long story short, after that incident, now those very same knocks and taps happen at all times of the day. Even other people besides me have heard it and were baffled.

My theory is that something is imprinted on the reality of the location and then exists there like a ghost. In fact i think this is exactly what ghosts are in the first place.

Now, does anyone think that this can be done purposefully? I think it would be an excellent technique if it could be mastered.
