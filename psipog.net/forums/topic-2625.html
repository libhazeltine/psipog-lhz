This is a list of good online free games that you can play with other people with. Here they are:

Runescape: Medieval times, do anything you want. Fun for some time, can get bad at times.

Tactics Arena Online: A good Warhammer 40K like game with nice graphics and awesome strategy gameplay.

Unification Wars: Like being a space empire? Ever wished to be a empire in space? This is the game for you. Plenty of strategy and action, but a 1 hour learning curve. Not for the weak of heart.

Some Downloadables:

Starsiege Tribes: Free 5 minute download, enter the games name into the search bar. Don't forget the patch.

Knights Online: Haven't played this one, figure it out on your own.
