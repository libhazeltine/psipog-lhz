Hello,

I read about the details that you have mentioned -

One must never analyse anything that appears in meditation, that will again activate your mind and NS(central nervous system) which will not let your mind transcend into its pure realm.

The opening of eyes is usually stiffness in somebody parts only realised after meditating for a while, next time you get lights or waves just smile a bit and let them pass by .. keep the body loose but still.

you will see the stages in which the body relaxes, first you sit still but loose ,( the mind follows the body and the body follows the mind),try to keep the body movement minimum, and slowly you will see you eyes will relax move a bit and then become calm.

Impurities and stiffness will go away gradually.
