Let me crack the physics down for you on some things:

The mass of a pencil- 0.005 kg
The mass of a semi average human - 7.405 kg

Now to levitate a pencil you'd need greater than the force keeping it down by gravity, which would be F = mass * gravity. 0.005 * 9.8 = 0.049 N

Now to levitate a human:
F = mass * gravity
7.405 * 9.8 = 72.57 N

72.57 / 0.049 = 1481.0204

So you would be required to provide a force nearly 1500 times what you would need to levitate a pencil, and a pencil would be considered somewhat advanced tk.
