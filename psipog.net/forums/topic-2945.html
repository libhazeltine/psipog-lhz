ok recently ive been delving into the creation of psipets. that and leaching but i will leave that at that. but anyhow i want to share to the ppl about psipets.

so far i can create 2 animals, a dragon and a swarm of mosquitos.

now onto creating them

ok first create a psiball and put ALOT of energy into it. you will want huge amounts if you want it to last. my dragon has lasted for about 4-6 hours.

ok once you have a psiball compress it and shell it while programming into it to be very hard and to flare into a color when it gains enough energy if you want. also include the feel of it such as scaly for a lizard fluffy for a cat or chinchilla.

now program how it will move. birds fly, rats scurry, snakes slither, dogs walk on all fours...etc, etc.

got that down? good now onto the actual shaping of it. first start by putting more energy and then making a tubular structure. then pull from it the legs and tail and (maybe ) its head

once you get the shape and details down is when you program it what to do. for instance my dragon is able to gather energy from others and create firey psiballs and fire them at what i want. as well as it flares and grows as its energy does. it also floats around randomly. for dogs have it lie down and alert you of others.

gl!

Float around and gather energy from others.
flare into a orangish reddish color when its gained a suffecient amount of energy

and my mosquitos

create a prickly itching feeling

return the energy to me

it doesnt last long but hey its a good way to get energy.
