This is a long story but just read it becuase it is pretty interesting.

My best friend, Zach told me this very weird story. It was when he was in 3rd grade. He was very very sick with a 104 degree fever.

Anyway, he told me that he was falling asleep and he pulled the covers over his head, and he said about 5 seconds later he saw his self under the covers gasping for breath. And he wen into his living room and he saw his dad reading a book. (you will see why this is interesting in a second) and he saw the page number (Lets say it was like 408), then he heard his mom yell and he was back in his body.

His mom was feeling his head and his dad came in there shortly after. And his dad asked what happened. And His mom said "Zach was Sufficating" and his dad said ok well i will stay in here and watch him. And when his dad opened his book he was on the same page that he was on when zach saw him reading in the living room. Which means it couldnt have been a dream.

Ok so what I wanted to ask was, is it possible to have OBE's in near death expierences. Becuase I trust zach and everything but he could of just been trying to impress me.

So thats it. If you didnt like the story than sorry I wasted your time.
