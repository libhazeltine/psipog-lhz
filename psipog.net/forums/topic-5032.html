For me, it's not absolute 100% concentration, I may think something else [example what was that noise heard from the street] and still keep my psiwheel spinning, BUT, it's a lot harder. I personally don't even meditate before practice, but I do it all the time when I'm practicing, hard to describe how I do it, but try it out yourself and I think you'll get the idea at that second. If you want to keep your concentration on 1 thing, then do like this : Everytime you lose your touch of thoughts, then force it back. Example : if you think of psiwheel, and your mind wanders to nearby glass of water, then bring it back to wheel, and start to figure out new stuff like what it seems like, how light/heavy it is etc. Find your best way, don't read topics like "I must do this thing like this, and there's no other way to succeed on it". Almost on every case, own way is the best way, feel what suits for you, and ask yourself "Am I good when doing like this?". Learn to know your innerself [sub-c]

Hope it helps
