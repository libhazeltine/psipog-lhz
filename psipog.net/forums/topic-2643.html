A shield would be a force of surrounding energy? (Y) or N

A shield is a Psi construct that usually ressembles a sphere around ones body. However, it does not have to be in that form. It is designed to keep unwanted psi from interacting with your mind.

You form this shield through pure concentration? Y or (N)

I do not like your wording. You form a shield like you form a Psiball, it does take concetration, but it also takes the process of moving Psi . There is a fine line between concentrating, and concentrating on making a shield. Such as concentrating on drawing a picture, you still have to draw, not just concentrate.

To form this shield, you must concentrate on nothing at all? Y or (N)

No. You concentrate on making the Shield. It's structure and it's Program <-important. The program dictates what the shield allows through and does not allow through.


To form a psiball, you concentrate on your own energy flow? (Y) or N

When you form a psiball you concentrate on your energy flowing into the shape of a ball, generally between your hands. With a shield, you would concentrate on your energy flowing into a sphere around your body, or molding around your body.

A psiball serves no purpose? Y or (N)

Psiballs can serve a purpose, like any construct (programed strucutre of psi). It can be used to create stimuli, transmit information, or just for "Fun". Purposless exercises do not aid you in growing your skills, thus, if it helps it isn't purposless.
