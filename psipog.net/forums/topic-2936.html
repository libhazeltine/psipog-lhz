I think we need a disambiguation sticky on "lucid dreaming", but I don't feel up to the task. I think a lot of people are slightly confused as to what is a lucid dream, and what isn't.

avillax, were you actually aware that you were dreaming while in the dream? Were you able to do whatever you wanted to do in the dream? I'm sorry for putting you to the question, but your "one of them felt lucid" comment makes me think that you are misinterpreting the term "lucid dream". In my experience, if a dream is lucid it is quite clearly lucid - none of this "feels" or "maybe" or "might have been" stuff that I've been seeing a lot of in the forum lately.

That's not to say that your dream is not useful or interesting. It may be, though the dreams that I've had about psionics have not been very helpful. But I want to ensure that we're all on the same page before we start using the term "lucid dream".
