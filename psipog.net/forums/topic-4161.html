Thunderkinesis?

Personally, I like to call the whole phenomenon psychotelekinesis instead of psychokinesis or telekinesis. Mostly because it's much more descriptive than either of the two term; "psycho" meaning mind, "tele" meaning far, "kinesis" meaning movement. I don't think that "telekinesis" on it's own is descriptive enough. The literal meaning of the word could also mean stuff like magnetic effects, since they move stuff without touching them. So, I added the "psycho" part to make it clear that this is far-movement caused by the mind.

The other reason is that it's longer than telekinesis and psychokinesis.
