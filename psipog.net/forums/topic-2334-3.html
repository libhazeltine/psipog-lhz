How many of you have actually managed to have so much control over the PK target that you it will flinch when you flinch? I'd venture to say none.

Also, he is able to move the ball laterally, in any of the three dimensions. To be able to control an object like that with conventional physics would require a complex system. For instance, just to move a ball from laterally (without gaining or losing height, and without it swinging) when the control pivot for the string is more than 1 foot above would be impossible. This system would surely be easily visible to any onlooker. He has no issues with obstacles being above or below the target, including his hands, a jar lid etc.

Also, all of his movements in the video are clearly seen. If he were controlling the ball in some other manner, he would have to make other motions that would be VERY obviously controlling the ball from elsewhere. The only other possibility is that he has an accomplice... and this is highly unlikely also, for reasons previously mentioned (i.e. practically impossible to control a light ball like that with string without it being VERY obvious) and also the preparation for the "stunt" would be again, next to impossible, as there are always onlookers.
