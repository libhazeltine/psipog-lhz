Ingo Swann, one of the original STARGATE viewers, once wrote a paper on the phenomenon of Telepathic Overlay. Essentially, he supported the use of a double-blind in order to prevent the telepathic transfer of information from interfering with remote viewing - telepathic results could be misconstrued as RV when they were, in fact, telepathic in origin.
