I'm not sure what you mean by "see things in dreams", but about your question on improving the quality of you dreams I can answer. I suggest keeping a dream journal and every time you wake up write down as much as you can remember about your dreams that you had that night. This well tell your subconscious that your dreams are important to remember and you will gradually start remembering more dreams as well as remember more things in your dreams.

EDIT: If you are saying that you can see things before they happen in your dreams, those are know as pre-cognitions.
