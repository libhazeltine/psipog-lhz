Maturity is knowing when something is beyond you. I commend you for your maturity.

You might still try OBE though. Personally, I've never heard any voices, evil or otherwise, during my OBE exit attempts. I generally get what I call the "astral breeze" - not to be confused with Robert Bruce's "astral wind" which is a completely different animal altogether - a noise that sounds like wind whistling through a forest in winter. I've also occasionally heard other sounds, but nothing voicelike at all - I've heard a sound like a Swiss alpenhorn, and another which I don't even remember. If I open my eyes or wake myself up out of the trance state, the noises immediately stop.

It's important to realize that any noises are completely inside your head, and that you can stop them at any time by simply opening your eyes or thinking yourself more awake. You cannot possibly be harmed by these noises, because they are not real.

Hope that helps. Good luck!
