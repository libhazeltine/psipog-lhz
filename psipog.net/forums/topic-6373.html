ah yes my old enemy... sleep paralysis, god ive had this problem for YEARS, and it really doesnt help that i have asthma and possibly sleep apnea(need tested to be sure) anyway yes this has happened to me ALOT not recently but in past years but ever since i moved from my old house i noticed i havent had one yet, but before i moved i atleast got this once a month, but usually 3 times. this really scared the hell outta me cuz normally i would just see some really strange image of my face with like i see my face blue and my eyelids completely open and really wide eyes with a scared look on it, one time i even saw other things. now anyway as you noticed oncc a month does seem like a lot for this to happen so i eventually had to learn how to stop it as there was the whole i could cause it immediatly thing.

and this is how. now im kinda special because at any point in time of my dream i can wake up instantly, really strange huh? but yea if i can focus enough i can wake up, trust me its really hard, especially in the middle of a quiet dark room, but it has been done alot, but this sometimes messes up and causes sleep paralysis because all i do is focus enough to open my eyelids. so i would be yet again trapped in something that looks like a horror film on acid...so yet again with the focusing at nearly unfocusable moments, i just force my body to move and my mind to awake all in one big thought. its really as simple as that, but you have to gain focus, which is really hard when your pretty much asleep, and watching some wierd ass clip play in your head, really really hard. so anyway yes its possible to revive from dreams and sleep paralysis, no its not easy doing it my way.

does anyone else have any other experiences similar or techniques with dealing with it?
