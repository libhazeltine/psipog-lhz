I've been practising PK for a few days now, and became more skeptical as the days went on, as although I could get the psiwheel to spin, I convinced myself it was due to the heat from my hands etc. I tried it under a glass, but got not so much as a twitch.

So I devised a new 'halfway' method - I placed a big roll of parcel tape around the psi wheel - this way it was protected from the sides (i.e. where the heat from my hand would potentially be coming from...). I held my breath for the length of the clip, in case any breathing effected it. Here is the result. I'm perhaps a little less skeptical now! (although I will only be 100% convinced when I manage to do it under complete cover)
