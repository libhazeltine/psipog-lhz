If you blocked out light, depending on how you do it, you'd either be a blob of pictch-black or you'd be a walking mirror. Neither are that stealthy

Fatal flaws in both of those options are that if you block out light, it can't get into your eyes so you can't see. If you bend the light, same case. If you wanted to be truly invisible, you'd need to be comfortable with being blind for the same length of time. The only other option is to make yourself partially invisible so you could see. Like you bend 75% of the light around you, the other 25% you let do whatever so you can see. But then you'd be 25% visible, and you could only see 25% as it were.
