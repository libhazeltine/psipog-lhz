This question may be seem obvious to everyone, but I believe that this is something we take for granted. I think that the root beliefs of people as to what psi actually is varied significantly. This variety of opinion is part of the reason that some people get labelled as nons (not because they are any less capable) because they are being instructed by others who have separate beliefs about what psi is but didn't take the time to find out what their students beliefs and impressions were. With various understandings of psi, the end result is a serious problem that could be likened to teachers who use base 10 math educating students who only understand math as base 8. Confusion, frustration and a stunting of the collective learning process result from this misunderstanding.

So, to settle this problem, I'm wondering what people have found out through their experiences and what conclusions they've drawn concerning the nature of psi. If you're inexperienced and you BELIEVE one option is true with no real evidence to support your beliefs, I'd prefer you didn't vote since this poll is not about beliefs but objective conclusions of those who are imparting their knowledge and experience to others.

I'd also like those who vote to elaborate on their conclusions and how they came to them. Hopefully we can gain a better understanding of psi as a result of the evidence and conclusions of our peers and while I have no problem with questioning others' conclusions (I may do some myself ), I do not want to see any challenging or attacking. Be aware that people who take the time to write down their conclusions are presenting the very foundations of their metaphysical beliefs and attacking something so deeply rooted in and valued by a person can only result in disaster so be considerate.
