Anyone else watch the TUF 4 finals last night? There was some good stuff...

especially the Scott Smith/Pete Sell fight.

Sell got him with a nice body shot to the floating rib(which I assume is the one at the bottom of the ribcage), probably got it cracked at minimum, started to fall back, and as Sell came in for the kill... Smith threw a very nice straight a KTFO'd Sell... then the both proceded to fall over- Sell from getting KO'd, Smith from what could be a very not good injury. Smith was down for awhile.

Then there was the Thales Leites/Martin Kampmann fight... WOW... just... WOW on this one.

Thales was 9-0 in MMA untill last night, when he became a 9-1 record. Anyway, the fight was amazing- at the end of round 3, I think EVERYONE was wondering how he was staying up- his legs were taking a beating from leg kicks, he was all wobbly and woozy, yet he still took a few pretty good shots and stayed up. Well, he lost the decision pretty badly there, but the guy has heart.

Great fights overall, but the Serra/Lytle fight... well, the judges had to be on crack to give Serra a 30-27 fight... at BEST, he made a 29-28 win, though, after watching the fight three times, Lytle MOST DEFINATELY had control in most of the fight, not Serra. Serra seemed to do very little as opposed to Lytle, though Serra attempted TD's more than Lytle, Lytle did more damage and actually did more- I think Lytle got screwed out of the $200K($100K fight contract/$100K Xyience contract)
