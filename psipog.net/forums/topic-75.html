But whats so kool about construct flaring, if the newbies can't make a psi ball!

Don't worry kids, I have my own method for making constructs (a psiball for those who don't know what a construct is ) First I get in a room that I have all to myself where their won't be any distractions. I get in a good positiong and sit down. I start to draw in energy before i make that psiball. I fill my entire body with energy. I visualize the sun sending energy to all parts of my body. After i do this for about a couple of minutes, i place my hands in front of me and cup them. I start to draw all the energy in my body(and I mean all of it) into my hands and visualizing the psiball into the shape of a ball( of course!). Incase any of you are wondering, after i empty all the energy i have in my body, thier is no more. So what I do next is picture the sun sending energy into my arms only, then into my hands to then go into the ball. I always keep the ball no larger and no smaller than a melon or mango. Anyway, I also try to use my sences. For example, I try to feel my psi ball and try to sence a change in temperature. A strong visualization is one of the best tools I have. After a while you should have made a psiball and congradulate yourself! your getting closer to learning construct flaring!

Be determined guys! Don't give up too easily. try try try!

I found that when i'm trying to make psiballs I first, as you said, sit in a comfortable position. Then, i visualize that the air around me is filled with static, and is the color of whatever i want my psiball to be. Then, as i breathe in i visualize the blue energy filled with static going into me and then into my arms. Next, i cup my hand and visualize the blue, static energy into a ball the size of a basketball. I start to see layer and layers of in my ball so that when i put energy into these layers, they get REALLY tight. Then, when I can not let anymore energy in, i make the ball smaller and then i have a psiball, very strong one.
