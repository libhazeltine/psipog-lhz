I realize that most of the time on the forums, there's been a lot of questions about this whole 'talking to your subconscious' article. From what I've noticed, a lot of people think that it's mostly just talking to yourself. Basically, this thread exists as a place where people can put down thier experiences, when they talk to thier subconscious, to perhaps clear up some of the confusion. Please move this if it belongs in general, wasn't too sure where to put it

I'll start off:

I notice that when talking to your subconscious, you usually get the answers even before you can finish the question - there's no 'thinking' before you get an answer. If I were to talk to myself:

'How are you?'
'Fine.'
'What do you think about so and so?'
'Blablabla...'

Try it, especially with difficult questions. From my experiences, there's usually a pause before you answer, because you yourself are thinking of an answer. When I ask my subconscious questions on the other hand, the answer is usually
a) NOT in words.
b) Near instant.
