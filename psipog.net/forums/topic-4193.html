This isn't very pressing, and may or may not be needed, I just thought I'd throw it out there.

I have no problem with most of the videos on Psipog, as they are done by experienced Psions. For future videos, however, a certain grading system may help the reliability of a video. Such as, if they are spinning a psi wheel, you can give it a 5 star rating depending on how far away the hands are, how steady they remain, if their mouth is covered and/or breathing away from the psiwheel, if most of their body is in view, etc.

Or maybe you should be skilled enough to move a pencil or something more advanced before you can submit a video. These are just ideas, and some of them may be unreasonable, haha. =P But I do believe some sort of rating system should be involved, at the least.
