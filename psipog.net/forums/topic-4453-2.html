My Method:
Its very short and simple, but requires time, patience and a clear and focussed mind.
Step 1. Lie down, get yourself relaxed, take deep breathes and clear your mind.
Step 2. Hold the neck of the spoon between index finger and thumb whilst still relaxing.
Step 3. Whereas you used energy to move the psiwheel, you must put lots and lots of energy into the spoon.
Step 4. When you feel the energy 'wanting' to burst out of the spoon, take it in both hands and bend it, do not apply much physical force at all, because the chances are it wont bend, you will loose concentration, and have to start over, just give it a little 'try' every now and again when you feel the energy pulsing through the spoon, if you keep giving it tries and it doesnt want to bend or even go soft, put more and more energy into it until you feel that it will bend.

Im currently working on just bending the spoon with my mind now, no hands involved whatsoever, just put the spoon on the desk and bend it, im making slow progress.
I hope this method will make many more spoon benders out of the people on psipog.
After doing this spoon excercise, i have found that my initial Tk, and remote viewing abilities to be greatly increased.
If the spoon doesnt bend no matter how hard you try, all it takes is practice, practice for a week or 2, until you feel that the spoon really wants to bend.
