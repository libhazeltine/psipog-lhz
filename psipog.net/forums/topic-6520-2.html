At last, I finally have some time. Here goes nothing:


1. Like everyone else- fix the environment:
ensure the eternal protection and regulation of the environment.

2. People would not need jobs and would not need money because I would give everyone machines (which would be small handheld devices) that allow them to make anything they want appear; but of course there would be safety precautions against misuse that I’m not going to bother go into.

3. Also, I would have to somehow control population growth, maybe just with influence; it would be one of the greatest challenges to do humanely!

4. Hunting would banned except for in virtual reality games, which would be one the most fun things about my world.

5. Violence in the real world simply would not happen.

6. Prisons would not exist; criminals would be healed psychologically.

7. Same goes with mental asylums.

8. Hospitals wouldn’t be necessary, everyone would be able to heal instantly with a command word or something.

9. Everyone would be granted the ability to use psionics with ease; again, abuse wouldn’t be possible.

10. I guess, since I could do anything, I would grant everyone the power to become enlightened, attain kundalini or raise his/her consciousness to whatever level desired in an instant.

11. I would allow people to have whatever abilities they want; abuse would be impossible.

12. Technology would only be limited by the mind but would not interfere with the environment.

13. Everyone would be able to find his/her true love or at least a very close match.

14. I would probably create several more earths, with similar orbits but not too close to close to each other to give everyone more space for their mansions/homes/palaces (if they want or need any).

15. Schools would not exist, but everyone would be free to acquire as much knowledge as they want; people would just be introduced to what is generally out there especially things like enlightenment so that everyone has at least some awareness of what they can learn/experience.

16. No governments would exist because they would not be necessary what so ever.

17. Things like racism and other forms of discrimination like social status probably would not be able to survive for long in such a society.

18. Wars would not exist.

19. Hunger would not exist.

20. As for religion, I can’t really go into that, but I can tell you that there wouldn’t be any problems with it like there are in today’s world.

In general, people would have immense freedom and opportunity. If you ended up miserable in this fantasy utopia it would surely be by your own choice.
