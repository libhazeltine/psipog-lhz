OK, those of you who know me know that I adore OBE’s and can induce them with some ease, well I could any way. About a month ago, I stopped being able to go OOB. No matter what technique I try (and I have many) whenever I would usually get to the vibration stage of inducing an OBE I instead seem to fall into a blank and empty frame of mind. Whilst I’m in this frame of mind I don’t think or comprehend anything. When I (for lack of a better term) wake up from this mind set (which I have been trapped in for hours at a time), I almost always do so into sleep paralysis and often have the feeling that I’m being attacked (which I hear is a common phenomenon when waking up while still in sleep paralysis). My lucid dreams also seem to end this way (waking up into sleep paralysis), my ‘normal’ dreams seem to have stopped happening and I have woken up into sleep paralysis from normal (now dreamless) sleep a couple of times (prior to these strange happenings I can only ever remember waking up into sleep paralysis twice in my entire life).

I have no real idea why this has started to happen. The only real change I have recently made to my psionic schedule is that I have stopped practicing energy manipulation, which I have been phasing out for a while any way and I doubt would have this affect.

So does anyone have any idea why this might be happening and how I may go about getting back to my usual dream occurrences?
