I use a technique called emotional anchoring. Basically what it involves is subconsciously linking a specific emotion, in this case the emotions caused by the music, to a physical action.

For example, every time you feel the desired emotion you could clap your hands. Eventually your subconscious will begin to associate clapping your hands with the emotion, and you would find that clapping your hands when your not feeling the emotion results in you suddenly feeling it
