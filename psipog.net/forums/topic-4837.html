I believe it may be a good idea to have a forum into which admins/mods can move dead threads which they believe were especially tantalizing, informative, and thought-provoking: essentially, the "best" threads. This would give posters something to read on subjects that are informative, as well as demonstrating to newbies who read them what does and doesn't become immortalized in the annals of history.

The motivation behind the recommendation is my thinking that the ethics post in the Telepathy subforum is most excellent, and when it is finished it should not merely fade of the edge of the page. Instead, it should be recorded as having been a very good thread, and kept as a reference on the topics involved. There are other threads that are similarly good and should be set apart as distinct and better than the typically droll.
