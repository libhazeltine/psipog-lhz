well I just had my first real lucid dream! I am SO HYPER AND EXCITED. I used the FILD technique, if you don't know what it is, I'll post it.
I did the technique for about 5 minutes, I think, and then I opened my eyes and looked at my clock.
It read:

1st:
2nd:
3rd:

Instead of 4:35 horizantally. I was like, wtf??!!! , but I pinched my nose and I could breathe, so I didn't care what my clock looked like, I was lucid!! Then an invisible robot started lifting my foot up into the air like he was going to rip me in half. So of course I willed a gun to appear in my hand and blew him away. Then I got out of bed and tried teleporting somewhere where it was light, I thought of the beach for some reason and so I tried teleporting there. It didn't work. Then it faded into a visualization and I drifted to normal sleep.

The bad news? I didn't have very much control. It took me a couple tries to summon the gun and I couldn't teleport anywhere. I couldn't make people appear in my room or anything. And it was really short, but you kind of have to expect that with your first lucid. I forgot to spin around does anyone have any tips on increasing control or teleporting?
