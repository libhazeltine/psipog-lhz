reikichannel wrote:
My reiki master (teacher/instructor/"spiritual guidance councellor") thinks I am an empath. Is this psychic? or is it just emotional?


You've not provided enough information to comment on whether the empathy referenced is psionic in nature. However, both types are valuable.

Quote:
I believe that she is a telepathic catalyst. (if thats the right term...)
Whenever I meet with her, I gain a deeper awareness of myself, and I seem to become better at precognition, only for a short time.


I do not recognise the term 'telepathic catalyst'. Could you define the term? The website you linked to was laid out in such a way that I found it a maze. Perhaps you can link to the specific page, if it's referenced there?

As to the gaining a deeper awareness of yourself after speaking to her... that occurs after speaking to people trained in or naturally good at psychology. It is possible that increased precog is related to an increased comfort level with your understanding of who you are. In other words, simply being honest with yourself might have the same result. Naturally, surrounding yourself with people that encourage you with self-honesty would be helpful.

Quote:
We had a talk with my mother, and she told me that this is a gift, and I have it for a reason, and I need to be balanced, grounded and protected.


Advice that is good for any gift, actually. When people 'make the shot of a lifetime', they are very likely internally balanced and grounded. However, especially with telepathy and empathy, grounding and shielding are very important. Without those protections, the skills become unreliable.

Quote:
I can feel the emotional situation of someone I know when I am in thier presence. I can feel the physical situation of my family members while Im near them, and sometimes I can just know if something is wrong when we are far apart.


Understanding emotional bits when in someone's presence is very difficult to demonstrate as psionic empathy. The distance bit is more easily explained as psionic empathy. Roy was just commenting in the chat about the way that body language, vocal tones, etc, are used to communicate. We pick up on those automatically, often without thinking about them.

The distance bit removes that possible explanation for why you know how someone feels. The likelihood is that you will be more sensitive to family members or close friends first, because bits of 'feelers' are connected to those people naturally. Gradually, you'll be able to pick up on people you don't know as well.

Quote:
Any advice as to where I can go with this ability/what I can do with it?


Psychometry can use empathic input. Bevy Jaegers discusses in some of her books how she used psychometry to solve crimes, find people, make money in the stock market, and generally discover interesting information from various objects people would give her. Pretty nifty things, imo.

Good luck,

Jael
