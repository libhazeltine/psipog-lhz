The Dream Phenomen forum has been, to my knowledge atleast, very light on the topic of "Dream Walking". For those who havent read the article it's the skill of projecting your conscious into anothers dream.

I dont know how many people have practiced this, since I dont even do it that often or at all lately given the fact its more then a bit intrusive and infringement of privacy on the dreamers part, unless permission is given.




I've done this a few times in practice long ago, but not really much anymore. It was a notable one due to the fact the things I saw and experiences, sparing most of the details I was Dream Walking into the dream of my girl friend. I did as the article suggested and was very passive and friendly in approaching, now I was getting the normal vision I got when I try remote viewing, but the difference was that in the dream I was greeted and kissed, now what made this notable was the fact that I felt the kiss on my lips, not in the full sense but I did feel some pressure and reception from them like they were being touched like in a kiss.

I dont know if this was just my imagination or what, but this is my most notable experience in dream walking. The point of this thread, however, is for others to explain their experiences.
