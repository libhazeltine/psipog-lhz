I understand now. Well, it is not a problem, just your shadow side . We all have a shadow-side. It lies in the mind and we cannot see it unless we either attempt to observe it unattached to our ego, or get it reflected by someone else who notices it or reacts to it. The latter was the case in your situation as I understand it. The reflection given by that specific person you speak of, was a very direct, yet very good reflection of your shadow-side. (That means: Acepting it but intending to change it to turning inward and finding the energy there, instead of finding them in others.)
