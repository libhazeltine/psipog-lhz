Hello, poggers. Got an interesting deal that could use some decent responses.

I have a friend, natural empath, who's lately been having some problems with hypersensitivity. Which wouldn't be so odd, but lately it's been close to overload proportions and it's showing up with some physical oddities as well.

The hypersensitivity hasn't all been empathic, some of it almost seems telepathic. He's had occasions in crowded resturaunts where he can identify exactly what every person in the place is talking about or thinking. I did a scan on him last night, and there's some very unusual feedback coming from his sub-c. The scan, which ususally comes out very clear for me, was jumbled and somewhat chaotic. It was almost as if part of his mind had taken up the tricolor and was running around yelling "Viva la revolution!". I took a break and on a second scan where I was trying to strengthen some of my impressions, I got a very distinct impression of a building on fire that was about to cave in, only with blue flames instead of normal reddish-orange. We both have feelings about the meaning, but I'd rather not give them to keep you folks with an unbiased opinion. Either way, shook me up pretty bad, and was one of the strongest, most unusual readings I've ever gotten. Any ideas?

The other part of this is that the hypersensitivity started at the same point some physical abnormalitites started going on with this friend. He's having problems with sensitivity to light- in that when he goes out into bright sunlight, he can't really see anything but the light. He's taken to wearing sunglasses constantly outside. He has been to see a couple of different eye doctors, but none of them have come up with anything concrete yet, and he's being referred to a different specialist each time. He's also constantly thirsty- and I've honestly seen him down a helluva lot of water and still be complaining of being thirsty. Again, it's entirely possible the two are unrelated, or that it's a primarily physical issue, but I'm trying to look more into the psionic side to see if there's anything I can do to help. I'm going to start him off with some grounding and centering before getting him to shielding, but I'm wondering if anyone else has any better/more fitting ideas.

Sound off! Thanks, Poggers.
