I was sitting in my bed thinking one day... thinking about lucid dreaming, how addictive it is, how vivid the dreams can become, how powerful you are in them once you fully grasp the proper technique, how it makes you feel in the real world when you wake up- i started thinking "how could one put this to use in the real world?" People say things like, ok it's a good ego boost b/c it seems real but it can't do much for you other than possibly encouraging you to be a bit more assertive. Well it's a fact of the human brain that the more you put yourself in a particular state, the easier it is for your mind to return to that state. That's why fun people are usually fun, sad people are usually sad and dumb people.. well, you get the idea. Try this on for size-

If you can dream to perform and be successful in TK over and over again, you are using your subconscious, although you are asleep it's your subconscious that continues to return to this state of possesing the ability to perform Tk based on success with Tk in your dream. So being awake or asleep has no bearing.

-So, by using Lucid dreaming it's my hypothisis that you can overcome the fact that you can't initially DO something and gain positive re-inforcement. It's kind of like a game genie for you subconscious... I'm going to try this, but please don't wait for me to give results, it could take a bit. If it sounds interesting then do it! Make your dreams come true... literally.
