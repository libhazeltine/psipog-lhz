I have decided that I wanted to create a ranking system that Psions may compare themselves against. Feel free to disagree with it but do not post critisisms on this thread.

Level 0: Normal person. Someone who doesn't believe in Psionics or has never heard of it. Has never used any Psionic abilities.

Level 0.5: A believer. This person has seen or strongly believes in Psionics. They are looking into becoming a Psion.

Level 1: The first level of Psionics. This person has actually performed a simple telekinetic feat such as moving a Psi Wheel or a simple, light object. Can make some simple Constructs. Cannot use any specialised abilities such as Cryokinesis or Telepathy.

Level 2: A somewhat accomplished Psion. They have all the abilities of Lv. 1. Has a mild understanding of at least one of the specialised abilities: Pyrokinesis, Cryokinesis, Telepathy, Empathy, Healing, or Remote Viewing. Can create Constructs and Shields with only mild difficulty. Can move slightly heavier objects. Capable of teaching a Lv. 0.5 until Lv. 1.

Level 3: A noteable Psion. Has all the abilities of Lv. 1 & 2. Proficient in one of the specialised abilities and is beginning to dabble in one or two of the others. Many Psions will never reach this level. Only the commited may achieve this level. All Lv. 1 & 2 abilities are simplistic to these Psions. The most suitable teacher for beginners.

Level 4: A powerful Psion. Has all the abilities of Lv. 1, 2, & 3. Can use a very high percentage of the specialised abilities. Can move heavy objects, maybe even around their own weight! It is impossible to teach this level of Psionics, it must be learned through oneself. Can teach anyone willing to learn.

Level 5: A legendary Psion. There is no one more powerful than these Psions. Who knows what they are capable of? This may take a lifetime of training and even then unobtainable.
