Point made. Now to answer your question, Damien. One way you could do it (as suggested by Peebrain in his Telepathy seminar) is to ask yourself "What is <insert name here> trying to send me?" and wait for some kind of answer. The hard part is keeping your mind empty of rubbish, but that'll come with practise. Another way you can clear your mind is to count 8 seconds breathing in, hold for one second, then breath out until you reach 15 seconds, hold your breath for one second and repeat. That should slow your mind down enough. The best thing to do is to read the Telepathy practise log and try out some of the ideas there and see which of those (if any) work best for you. You can always try something different.
