Soul Transfer Article

This technique is used in PK. I find it works best with toothpicks, but thats just me. Make sure to read all the steps, and if it doesnt work or your experiencing problems, let me know. Also... i'd really like a confirmation from one of the psions here in case this technique could be harmful to the body. I dont think it is; i havent experienced any pain/problems while using it. But i want the confirmation to make sure.

OK.
First thing you got to do is focus on the object (in this case the toothpick).
Focus till you can feel the toothpick, and when you kinda "zone out".
I find that this step is complete when i see the toothpick and everything else is a jumbled ball of colour.

Next, visualize a part of your soul ripping from your body and becoming absorbed into the toothpick. At this stage, the toothpick will shake. Its ok if it doesnt, your still doing it right. Make sure your "soul" is planted securely inside the toothpick, and if you want you can look around from the toothpick's point of view, or feel yourself rolling. You can also add special effects, like sparks or fire but i find that a waste of energy; again, thats just me. Do what works for you.

Now, feel it move.
Your "soul" is in the toothpick, and you should be able to feel it move and have a distinctly powerful connection with it. If this doesnt work, you can use visualization. I wont go in-depth because i dont have much time to write this article, but i'll explain briefly.
Feel the toothpick in your hands. Guide it with your mind, just "push" it mentally. Give it a shot of psi, and send it on its way. Focus is eessential at this part. Move it like a muscle in your body. I find this feels like attraction, but instead the object moves the opposite way.

If it wont move at all, then just focus on it. This, i find, always makes an object move. If you focus hard enough, you should be able to at least shake it, and thats the first step.

Feedback/Replies appreciated


~ Wolverine

Nice article Wolverine

However I would like to adress some tips. You say:
Quote:
Next, visualize a part of your soul ripping from your body

When teaching people a new method, sentences like these are kind of aggressive. People don't want to rip their souls out of their bodies.

See what I mean? The sentence suggests something that I associate with an aggresive action against my being.

Quote:
Make sure your "soul" is planted securely inside the toothpick

I would not want to prison my soul in some toothpick. See?

So, it may be personal, but as a reader of this new method, as a student of yours so to say, I would not like these aggresive and somewhat dangerous suggestions. I know what you mean by the exercise though, and it can indeed work quite good, however, whatever it is you enter in that object, i can assure you, it will never be your soul. Therefore I would change the word soul into something else, and I would not say: "Ripping this or that out of your body."

You could for example say:
"For this exercise you move your sense of body into the object of choice, and become one with it."

Thats what 'bothered'me in your article, for the rest, it is fine .

You don't need to change anything, that's just what I would have done if I wrote it.

Putting "put some of your energy/presence in the toothpick" sounds like all the other techniques and someone who was still on the psiwheel and not very advanced might not know what you meant.... So, I like the soul thing because people know what you mean and have a good visualization for that. Also, most people think "ripping the soul out" rather than "letting the soul flow out" simply because that's what they've heard before. So it might be better to say what you did for visualization purposes and because people are familiar with that concept. I think you get the same result, but it makes it easier for people less experienced with things that involve your spirit/energy, such as RPing.
