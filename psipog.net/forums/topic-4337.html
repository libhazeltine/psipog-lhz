Burn the book, it is trying to increase your 6th sense by increasing your awareness of your existing bodily senses? wtf? does that make any sort of sense to you?

I personally find that in order to increase my ESP I have to actually stop relying on my existing body senses in order to make use of a new sense.

For example when walking the streets, I see a corner, rather than turn and look, I stretch out mentally and tactically feel the area, if I feel a presense, I scan for information, is it male or female? young or old? black or white? tall or short? and then I look to see if I sensed correctly.

You want to increase awareness of your psychic senses? Focus on that entirely, there is no relation between body senses and psychic senses. They work through different mediums.
