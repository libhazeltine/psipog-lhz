I sometimes awake consciousness in a dream and i can't get out of them easily. I have two ways: The fast one and the slow one
The fast one is the most easy way but the most impressive and it can hurt your emotions (nothing important but it can make you really stressed). To escape from a dream by this way, simply die in your dream, the impression of diying in a dream will awake you up, you will be sweating and really stressed but you will be back into reality.
The slow one is to concentrate and try to remake the dream with your mind. For example if you see a dream monster in front of you if you convince yourself that what you see its a squirrel when you open your eyes (in the dream) it will be a squirrel. Also if you're in middle of the rainforest and you want to be in your house, close your eyes and convince yourself that you're in your house. Then you will have to convince that you're going to wake up or that you want to escape from the dream to wake up or to have an OBE.
Be warned, if you don't have control of your mind and if you think things like "could it be a monster" it will be a monster for sure. So don't let your mind to trap yourself.
"Lucid dreaming rules"
