Let me ask you a few things. First, why did you decide on trying PK? If you say, "Because it looks cool" or "Because the ladies dig it", those aren't very strong reasons. Anything can look cool, and the ladies vary in what they dig. Ask yourself why you chose to practice PK; to discover and learn more about yourself? Maybe to learn more about what you are capable of? In order to have motivation, it needs to be important and have meaning to you. Your motivation has dropped because you've hit a plateau, so to speak. But refocus on your goals, and realize that this is just a part of PK, like anything in life. Find a solid reason to be the foundation as to why you do PK. And when you still feel down and you lose hope, try this; what or who makes you the most happy in this world? A sport? A special someone? What or who can instantly brighten your mood from apathetic to emphatic and ready for challenges?

Also, come to the understanding that this happens, and that it's not all going to be as ideal as we all hope it to be. You lose hope because you can't move anything else? Well, you'll probably scream and want to kill me for saying this, but it's the truth that prevails time and time again; Just keep going, just keep practicing. You can't expect to get better by not pushing yourself. The thing to realize is that it's possible, but it's if you allow it to be so. I'll leave you with some quotes that help me when I start to lose motivation;

"Excellence is not a skill; it's an attitude."
- Ralph Marston

Success means having the courage, the determination, and the will to become the person you believe you were meant to be."
-George Sheehan

"If the going seems easy, check if you're going backwards!"
- Author Unknown

"The most important question to ask on the job is not "what am I getting?" The most important question to ask is "What am I becoming'?"
-Jim Rohn

"Perhaps I am stronger than I think."
-Thomas Merton

So, there you have it. It's up to you as to what you will do. You can wallow in the hopless feelings, or you can gather yourself and meet the challenge waiting for you, because you may be able to delay a confronation, but you can never avoid it.
