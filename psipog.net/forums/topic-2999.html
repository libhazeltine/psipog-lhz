Well, as was mentioned, your hands aren't required for PK. Once your mind connects and discovers that it can move the objects, you shouldn't need to keep your hands there anymore, unless you've trained yourself to use your hands.

When starting out many people find that using their hands is necessary. I think this is just because your mind has difficulty affecting energy over distance due to a lack of experience. Since your body is full of energy which is far easier for you to move within yourself, placing a hand near the wheel creates a 'path of least resistance', which we all know nature loves. Thus the energy flows through your body, out of your hand, and past/through/around the wheel.

Personally I find I need to use my hands as my mind is reliant on distance. Once I get a few larger items rolling I'll likely work on not using my hands, but for now they're pretty key to making progress forward.
