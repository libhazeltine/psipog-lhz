Thats the only way I can describe it. OBE. At first I delved into my "psi experience" with a psi wheel, and am still pretty horrible at that kind of stuff, but tonight, minutes ago, I attempted OBE.

Using this article - art-outline-to-shielding.html153

Wow, amazing, and weird, are the only words I can describe it with...and I didn't even acomplish OBE, I was close though...I know that for sure.

I followed the instructions pretty much, just layed down on my bed, flat, with my head barely propped up by a pillow. Got relaxed, untensed all of my musles, and just laid there.

I had my tv on, with my ATHF dvds playing (yes I know this sort of stuff isn't suggested, but noise I'm used to helps me concentrate and meditate.) And soon I wasn't sesning anything but prickles, warm sensations, etc, and a few things that seemed to be mustle spasms. At this point I think thats just my body being fully relaxed.

After about 15-25 minutes of feeling those things, my right arm felt as if something were being slid over it, kind of like some cloth being pulled down over it, and then my whole body started to feel the same thing.

After that, sure enough, the see-saw thing (or at least I think thats what it was) started to play out. It felt like waves going across my body, gently, yet firm at the same time. I just laid there and accepted all of it, trying not to let my mind wander to much at all. The seesaw sensation kept up, and suddenly, I don't know what it was, I think it was my mouth drying out, I lost my concentration...and that was it. I knew I had failed partialy, and I finally moved my entire body.

As the article descirbes, my entire body felt extremely fatigued, and still does as I type, but that feeling is also going away.

My thought process was also entirely effed up durring the entire thing...when my mind reached it's climax, all I was seeing in my head were weird shapes, seeing my body expand, and become tiny, and microscopic, it was extremely weird...and that was all I could think of or see, was my body shifting size, I also felt this as I lay there, felt as if my entire body was shrinking, and enlarging. (What is this called/mean?)

It was indeed an awesome experience, and I intend to do it again when I'm actually planning on sleeping (Its summer, so that won't be untill 5 frigin am lol)

I suggest the article art-outline-to-shielding.html153 to anyone who wants to try this, it did help quite a bit, and I know if I didn't read it, when I started to experience the seesaw and other thing I would have immediatly been freaked the hell out and awoke from the trance like state.

So, I hope I can actually leave my body later on, I plan on it being pretty damn cool ^_^
