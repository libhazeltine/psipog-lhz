Did your skills in the psionic field ever made you think you were 'chosen'. Be it for what ever reason, did you think you were 'special' and therefore 'better then others'.

Did you think you were an individual who's chosen by something higher to come and bring a new light to this world or did you make sure to stay grounded (pun intended) and keep a sceptic eye on each and every step you make along this road called Psi.

In short; How firmly did you keep your feet on the ground?

But now I know that I'm just walking down the same path as everyone else, I just choose to step on some different tiles then others.

So yeah, I say that psi helped me become a better individual myself, but in no way more deserving then any other human being.
