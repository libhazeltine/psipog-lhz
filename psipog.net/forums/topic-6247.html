Quote:
To me, micro PK doesnt seem real. It seems like instead of you making the random outcome, you're actually predicting the random outcome.


Actually, those are two different things. With Micro-PK, you're not predicting it. You pick a single outcome, and continually try and force (I use the term 'force' lightly here) it to occur, whereas with Precognition, the outcome you have in mind changes every time. For example... Let's say you're rolling a 20-sided die. If you're practicing Micro-PK, you'd pick a single number, let's say 7. You'd roll it many times, and try to make the roll a 7 every time. If you're practicing Precognition, every time you'd roll, before the roll, you'd try to pick the number that you're going to roll..
