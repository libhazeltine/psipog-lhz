I know it's been a while since I shared much around here, but explaining this "aura vision" could be useful to several people.

For the most part, the experienced and "professional" psychics I have spoken with have a fairly well-attuned perception of psionic energy around them. As far as I know, it has nothing to do with magnetics, except that some traditions work with manipulating or correcting your body's unseen energies with powerful magnets. In general, the most common "aura viewing" of an individual is based on healing, where the viewer's subconcious breaks down the energy output of an individual into seven "chakras" of colored light that represent the individual's balance and health.

As far as answering your question, it depends what you define as "see". The majority of the time that I am "looking" at my psipet, I'm just mentally feeling it out to check up on it or direct it. However, after a while it has a tendency to cause shimmers and flares of light as it builds up energy around itself, which are generally startling if I'm not expecting them, as I "see" them just like anything else around me.

"Flaring" is generally defined as creating a visual output that is detectable by the human eye. A telepathic suggestion can simulate this, so the real question is whether you are actually creating light. However, what really matters is whether you can consistantly "watch" your construct, which is more being aware of its energies. This awareness of energy, creating a visualization if not necessarily and image for your eyes, is "aura vision" when you can extend this to "looking" at other energy around you.

So... yeah. Nobody's really going to read that, are they?
