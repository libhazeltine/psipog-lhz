Eye color change can not only be psychically done, but it can also be a serious medical problem.

Yes, DMILS can be used to change eye color. Eye color is represented by the ammount of melanin in your eyes. Blue has little, brown has the most. Traits for either Blue-Green Hazel or Green-Brown Hazel are more complicated.

Some hazel eyes change to completely one color in the presences of different light colors. I know with mine, extreemly white light changes them to blue. The more yellow the light becomes, the more amber appears.

Also, rapid change in eye color can mean a serious medical illness. Watch yourselves.

~ Stony
