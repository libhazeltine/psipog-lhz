Someone has asked me my schedule on practicing PK.

Sunday: 1-2 hours practice

Monday: 1-1.5 hours practice

Tuesday: None

Wednesday: 1-2 hours

Thursday: 1-1.5 hours

Friday: 1-2 hours

Saturday: None


Yes it does seem like a little much but hey, you need practice. That is my practice schedule.

I normally start practicing with a psiwheel (a couple minutes), move onto a pencil (about 30 minutes). I then move onto something that is difficult for me (the rest of the time). You can change that around though.
