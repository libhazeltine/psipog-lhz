Ah yes, the physical preference over metaphysical. I know this one well.

Pretty much all my life I've been looking for something more than I can touch, or more than I can physically experience. I'm not talking about religeon or spirituality (though it does come into play in a sense) - but rather a greater understanding of the universe that very few have found.

For me, my TK is still very much in its infancy. I cna spin a Psi wheel, but that's all. My next challenge will be rolling a marble. From there perhaps moving large objects and even levitating them.

But, why? What practical purpose could this have when my physical body is so much stronger and more adept at manipulating objects?

Because my physical body can't see into the future, or move an object from across the room.

Think of it this way, you're wrongfully put into a small town jail and the keys are on the table across the room and out of reach. A corney clicke situation, yes, but if you're about to be executed you'd be glad you could get those keys with PK rather than getting executed.

The world is a larger place with Psi in it for me. It's not just a physical manifestation of random possibilities, it's something more. A sea of energy interacting, with itself and with me.

So in the end, why PK?
Because it's out there, and because we can.
