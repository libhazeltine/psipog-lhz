I'm usually able to lucid dream every night very easily. But latey I've been so tired by bedtime that I'm to tired to try and induce a WILD. So yesterday when I had some time I went into "sound studio" and recorded 10 min. of silence. Then after that ten minutes I recorded my voice saying "I'm dreaming" over and over. I made it very soft and quiet so it wouldn't wake me. When I went to sleep I simply pressed play on my mp3 player which I had downloaded my "Lucid track" on to ealier. It worked great. You guys might give this a try.
