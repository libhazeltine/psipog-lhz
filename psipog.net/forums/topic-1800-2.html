Ok, here is my theory on this subject: If you're depressed, your sub-c is depressed. It develops negativity, sadness, anxiety, and all the other bi-products of depression. If your sub-c has been putting up with this for an extended period of time, it may stop caring. Even when your conscious mind is back in full health, you may still need to "coax" your sub-c back to health. Complimenting it, giving it mental hugs, feeding it happy feelings whenever you can, and in general being life affirming would probably help. Also, depression can lead to states of "not caring" (hardly a complicated term ) and maybe it doesn't see why it should do PK. Perhaps, looking at your memories, it associates PK with times before you were depressed, which brings back thoe memories of depression, and thus treats PK as a hostile notion. You could try explaining to your sub-c that this isn't the case, and that PK would make you happy, more confident. Even in the smallest success situations, try being very affirming, giving out compliments like its 1999

Oh, and if I sound crazy now, just wait However, I do believe that your sub-c is damaged and/or affected by all states of emotion, be they negative or positive. Perhaps it just needs a confidence boosting, and a little confirmation that the unenjoyable state of depression is behind you now. And also, congratulations on joining us in the happy crew again.
