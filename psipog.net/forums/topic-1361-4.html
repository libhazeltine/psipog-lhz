Sticking to the original topic.

What you experienced was a common sleep disorder (?) called Sleep Paralysis. It is quite common and 20% of the population admit to having experienced it at least once. I myself have experienced it and it was truly terrifying and realistic. I am not convinced however that I was attacked by aliens (as i thought, during the episode) or that i was being sat on and crushed by a massive force. My reason for not believing that it was real is due to the fact that it is a major part of the disease, Narcolepsy. People with the disease have episodes of Sleep Paralysis at least every day so consider yourself lucky that you've only had it once or twice.

On a side note: Everyone in the world is expected to experience an episode of Sleep Paralysis once, so wait your turn.
