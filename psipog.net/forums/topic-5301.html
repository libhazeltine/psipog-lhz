As soon as you pull your hands apart, just keep feeling the ball there.

But first, start off by making a PsiBall, then gradually make your hands go further and further apart. After some practice, you will eventually be able to make a PsiBall with your hands a few feet away from eachother.
Then, when you feel the PsiBall is strong, release it from your hands, feel all of the energy leaving your hands, and visualise. Visualising is good for Newbies, but once you've got the hang of throwing PsiBalls, you won't forget the feeling.
