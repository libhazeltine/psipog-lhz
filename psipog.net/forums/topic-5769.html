I've been playing around with psiballs, seeing what I can do, and what I can't. So, I thought you guys would like to contribute your ideas for constructs for others to try out, just for fun.

Dancing psiball: Psiball that pulsates to the beat of music playing on your speakers, radio, etc.

Psi Pets: Refer to this thread.

Psi Plants: Also mentioned in the Psi Pets thread, a construct that resembles a plant, tree, etc. that absorbs energy from a source and grows.

Hide-and-go-seek Psiball: I know someone that actually did this, he programmed his construct to randomly move around his house, and he'd try to find it. Excellent for working on sensing psi.

Temperature Psiballs: Create a warm/cold psiball on a cold winter day or warm summer day. Results are not great, but it helps a little.

Psi Person: Now this one is really advanced, where you make a life-sized, human-shaped construct (an advanced psi pet), and program your personality into it. Of course, I read of one person's Psi Person that went evil and litterally tried to take over another person's body with an evil grin on his face... so experience AND a destruction code might be a good idea here.
