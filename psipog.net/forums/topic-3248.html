I thought it would be interesting to see what layers everyone uses on a daily and emergency basis to their sheild network. Personally I always try to keep up a solid telepathic tower shield that I can regulate how much information comes in or out. Over that I have a 1-way mirror shield. Sometimes between the two I'll put up a marshmellow shield.

Most other temporary shields go between the tower and mirror unless it is a hedge or "cloaking" shield and you can probably guess that that goes on the outside. I also keep one or two "expanders" around in my field which are basically the size of psiballs but are designed to expand to the size of a shield when activated and providing a "sweeping" motion that clears out my field and gives me a few minutes to construct a new shield should any of my critical ones fail. Quite handy incase of a major failure. One other cool shield is actually an extension of the tower shield. I call it an antenna sheild as it basically enhances certian types of energy perception. I find it to be pretty useful

Also if you don't have any shields post that anyway and give a reason as to why you don't shield on a regular basis. I used to not shield, but the reason for me was only laziness and that I thought it didn't do much. But I found out that a good tower shield is very very quiet, and much more enjoyable to have at all times. Sensitivity comes with a price I suppose.

If anyone is unclear of any of the shield names I posted I'd be happy to explain just post to ask, though I think most are in an article somewhere around here.

Each shield is programmed to do something specific. Programming a single shield to do everything is very difficult in my experience. Also adding an order to your shields gives them a "logical priority". Such as there is no use to have a cloaking shield on the inside because it would not cloak your other shields.

Personally I also use a psionic "brush" that is pretty much just a construct that I use to brush or clean out my energy field. Not only does it feel really good (somewhat like a real hair brush), but I guess I just like the idea of having a clean and groomed field. I also find that built up energies tend to work like knots in your muscles. They build up slow over time and you almost don't notice the difference until they are gone, or until you get a massage.

Anyway I hope that explanation helps
