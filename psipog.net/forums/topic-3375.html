I think seeing the future is a very mixed issue on here because some believe it and others don't. Do you believe in the ability to see the future?

I have no problem believing that seeing the future is entirely possible but its effects are pretty hard for anyone to wrap their head around. Let's say that you see something in the future: You're going to get into a car accident on a certain day. What effect would that have? Would you take actions to prevent it?

So you take actions to prevent the accidents and you end up getting slammed in a car accident. Was seeing the future what caused the accident to begin with? Would you have gotten into the accident if you didn't know about it?

Let's say that you know about the accident and take steps to prevent it and nothing happens. In that case, did you really see your future? Did you change your future?

I could really make it interesting and cross fate and free will into this but I'll save it for another board.

It's a very interesting topic. What are your opinions?
