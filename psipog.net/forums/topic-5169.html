I listen to music a lot when I am on my computer, which is usually a majority of my day. During meditation, when I begin to clear my mind or focus on one specific thing I can usuallly hold it for a little bit, but after a minute to several minutes a song I have listened to recently will just start playing in my head. I try to not pay attention to it, but sometimes it can be distracting if I try to block it out.

Should I work on trying to block out the music, work on trying to accept it, or something else?

Any help or advice will be appreciated.
