Hi everyone.

I just wanted to tell you of my obe try last night.
I went to bed pretty early, as usuall when I try to get an obe.
I didnt move for some time and I felt that my arms were getting numb, its not that often I get to the phase where the body falls asleep and the mind is awake, so I told my sub-c:
-Whatever you do, dont make me fall asleep. I would be glad if you could keep me awake.
Right after I finnished the "keep me awake." sentence my vertebras started to "pop", it was a pretty nice feeling, not unplesant at all.
It kept poping for a few seconds and my body turned completely numb.
I thought that this must be it, I'm about to achieve an obe.
I started to fantazise how it would feel, if I would start to see in black and white and stuff like that.
Eventually I started to turn more and more in to a dream like phase, and right before I started to accually dream my sub-c sent a great chill down my spine.
It kept doing that every time I wandered off. Since I didnt have enough will power to keep my mind in one piece I kept wander of the whole night, and my sub-c kept sending me the chill thing.
At two am I felt that I just had to get some sleep so I gave up. Moved around in my bed a little, felt my body again and fell asleep.

Well, I wasnt able to achieve an obe, but I think that I was pretty close.
What do you think?
I'll try again tonight.
Oh, and if youve got any tips or something that you think could help me please tell me.

G'night.
