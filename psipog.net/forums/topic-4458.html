I think I may have dozed off, and I had a dream in which everything in my room was exaggerated (television was much bigger, also lower than normal) and felt like someone was by my bed and pulling at me. I tried to get a look at whatever was by my bed, and it was a real struggle to keep my eyes open and push the blanket out of the way so I can get a clear view, to find nothing.

At that point, I began rolling over, but couldn't go over the edge or roll back onto bed. I figure this was just in the dream. Then, the dream and atmosphere of fear were over and I'm not sure what happens between this and the next part.

My legs began to twitch very rapidly, and lifting themselves in the air. It felt very real and I sincerely believed that I was standing on my head near the end. Then, I woke up and I was unsure if that really happened or not.

Edit:
The leg twitches were kind of like climbing along a wall while lying down, but the wall curved above at the perfect distance from me so that they could just continue running along it. Very odd.
