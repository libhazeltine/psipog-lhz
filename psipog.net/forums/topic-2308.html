I think Dbizket is right, to an extent. If you teach an animal... Say a dog certain commands, they will know what they mean. "Sit/Stay...etc."

However, if you want to do telepathy with animals you'll have to "learn" about your pet, or the animal in question. Basic instincs can overcome telepathic suggestion (at least it does with animals, seems more like it does with them than does with humans). For example, I have a rabbit. I used to project to it that i have all this good green food and that if he comes to me he will get it... Eventaully, after I didn't have food he stopped coming to me when I did that.

Another example, which is kind of strange, is when I want to pick him up he runs away from me... That is unless I "LET HIM KNOW" he's going to be picked up and that I'm not playing and am not going to stomp on him or anything.

You have to learn to communicate with your pet and understand it. It usually requires a little bond to have an effective Telepathic link with an animal.
