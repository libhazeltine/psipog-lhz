personally-

i gained control of pyrokenesis by using lighters, filling my hand with fluid, then igniting it (gas/vapor fluid via bic) ad then tinkering with the fireball effect.

make a 'tube' with your hand, enclosed, except on one end where the lighter will fit snugly.
fill 3 seconds, one- two- three (sometimes a count less, or more ) then ignite as you pull the lighter away and sligtly open the hand.
hopefully your not new to this or it can take a bit to get used to.

if you want to pecker more at my knowledge-
i started by not letting it flame.
then letting it flame.
i then began to tinker with the size, brightness, color, properties (tactile feeling ) etc etc.


take control of that fireball!!!


now the only side effect of this is that your body becomes accustomed to this tinkering with fire, and sometimes your lighter might explode...
something about the gaseous fluid,maybe pores, and well my mind knowing theyres more in the bic... its happened twice to me. be careful.


either way the fireballs from your hand are low risk to the lighter UNLESS
-its NOT a bic!!!! keep this in mind, bic uses glass and metal sealers instead of cheap bimetalic and plasics

-either the: the lighter is hot, the 'hat' or flame protector is missing, or there is a weird hissing sound coming from your lighter.

-your mind has deducted the lighter is the better source for the fireball. in this case, dont bother. no amount of homestyle gear and enviroment protection will stop searing hot plastic from chewing through your skin. trust me.
