You, my friend, are just now noticing the effects of what I like to call "Cerebromuscular Distraction", or CD for short. I call it that because you're trying to do something with your mental muscles, and when it doesn't work, you get disheartened and stop trying. And then, suddenly, when you're distracted by something else, your desired effect takes place!

Peebs documented it a while ago with regular PK and a psiwheel. He tried so hard to make it move, and it remained still, then when he looked away, it spun like no tomorrow! It's also the main technique on fork-you.com, where you're told to do all the focus stuff, and then distract yourself to let the force get to the object.

It's a curious little effect. But when we can find time, STAG will be looking into it.
