Heres a cool concept plot:

1. Ushima's parent's have died when he was very young (like eight or something) and he has been raised by his grandfather in a remote area of the mountains. His grandfather taught him how to hunt, fish, and fight.

2. Ushima (about 15 by now) goes fishing for dinner. His grandfather has a premonition about an awesome darkness consuming the world, he sees an image of Ushima shrouded in darkness leading the evil behind him. At the end of the premonition Ushima's grandfather suffers a fatal heart attack. His last word is "Ushima!".

3. Ushima returns home and sees his dead grandfather on the ground, his grandfather's death echos that of his mother and father's. Realising he is alone, Ushima cries and runs off into the forest. He stays up all night remembering what his parents and grandfather had taught him and pondering what he should do next.

4. Flash forward 2 weeks as Ushima is about to eat a beetle. He slowly devours the creature than passes out from exhaustion. As he is passed out he dreams of darkness consuming the land and a demon points at him and says "Ushima!". He wakes up frightened and sweaty in a....bed...of all places! He is alone in a house. he walks around asking if anyone is there but no one responds. He steals a sword from the house and runs off. But as he is running he is stopped by a man grabbing onto his neck. He is a huge hulking lumberjack type man. He takes his sword back and tells Ushima that his daughter found him in the forest almost dead. He meets this mans wife and daughter and they talk for hours about...well...I dont know yet. Ushimas appetite and physical srength, which he proudly displays, impresses the daughter and she falls in love with him. That night she comes into his bedroom and tries to kiss him. Ushima, who has never even seen a girl his age (or any woman for 7 years) is frightened by what she is doing and he flees the house without ever saying goodbye.

5. He finds a monastery and runs into it looking for food and a place to sleep. He meets with the leader of the monastery who senses potential psionic abilities in the boy. He trains with them and several other children his age including one girl. He befriends the girl and one of the boys and the three of them become very attached. 2 years go by and Ushima has become a very advanced psionic, but for the past month the grand master of the monastery has been having the same dreams of the darkness, his latest dream ends with the face of the demon leading the darkness becoming visible, and it is Ushima's face. Realising the importance of these premonitions, the old man weilds a golden dagger and approaches Ushima while he is sleeping. In blind defense Ushima uses his psionic abilities to rip a hole straight through the old mans body. "Ushima!" says the old man before he quickly dies. Ushima is disgusted at what he has just done and confused about what has happened. Ushima wakes his best friend to tell him what happened and points to the dead man on the floor. His best friend, who had been raised at the monastery becomes enraged at Ushima for what he has done and tries to kill Ushima, but Ushima knocks him unconscious.

6. Ushima must once again run away and as he is lighting a fire he senses someones presence, using his ninja/psionic skills he apprehends the intruder from behind. It was the girl from the monestary. She tells him the outrage of everyone at the monastery but vows to stand by his side because she doesnt believe that Ushima is capable of doing such a horrible thing. She says that she believes that one of the other elders killed the grandmaster to take control of his authority. Ushima lies and agrees with her that the death of the grandmaster was a conspiracy. They pitch up a tent and sleep next to eachother, but when the girl tries to get closer to him he rolls around and pretends to be asleep. He is disgusted that he lied to her and that the innocent girl is being decieved by him.

7. The two of them make it to a town and decide to settle in for a while becoming hunters and selling the fur and meat of the animals Ushima has caught. Because the girl he is with is so beautiful, men from the town always come to by the fur from her regardless of whether they need it or not. One man in particular flirts with the girl (we'll call her Kay) the most, and Kay seems to flirt back. Ushima becomes jealous and challenges the young man to a fight. Unbeknownst to Ushima, this young man is also a psionic ninja who has trained at another monastery in the far east. Ushima is quickly subdued but the man (lets call him Kobayashi after that japanese kid who can eat alot of hotdogs) admires his courage and abulities and decides to let Ushima live. Ushima comes to respect the man and asks to learn psionic abilities from him. The young man agrees so long as they pay him all of their earnings from the fur trading business. Ushima and Kay meet Kobayashi's apprentice, a handsome and cocky young boy Ushima's age named Mokaru or whatever (Ushima, Kay, and "Mokaru" are all 17ish, and Kobayashi is 19ish). Kobayashi has them doing insane and weird training activities and the four of them sparr often. Kay, who is not the most talented psion usually only trains half way with them and then goes out of her way to cook for the boys and make them feel as comfortable as possible. Strangely, Ushima no longer feels much attraction to Kay as he now views her more as the motherly figure he never had.

8. One day a group of murderous theives plunder and destroy the whole village. Kobayashi tells the kids to run to a secret safe haven and spend the night there as he tries to deal with the bastards. The kids hesatate but eventually honor their master and run to the haven. The next morning they return to the village to see dead body upon dead body, one distinct body still moves in the distance. It is Kobayashi and he is slowly dying. Before he dies he tells mokaru tdig under the floor of their house and retrieve an essential item, he tells mokaru to retrieve the map from his pocket and after they dig up the floor boards and obtain this "special item" they must follow the map to a certain City. Kobayashi dies and the kids cant help but cry. Ushima cant help but notice the look that Kobayashi gives him. The same look that was on the face of the grandmaster before he died. The word "Ushima" is heard by Ushima himself, but no one else can testify that they heard it.

9. Mokaru digs up the floor boards and retrieves kobayashi's sword. With sword in one hand and map in other, Mokaru leads his two friends through many adventures to get to the forest. Eventually tensions rise and A huge all out war is waged between Ushima and Mokaru and the gang of ninjas.
