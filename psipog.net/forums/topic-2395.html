I heard a little something about PK, shortly after I first heard about Wiccan, so I decided to look a bit further into it, as it deals with energy, and makes a bit of sense.

I just decided to share a story, to kind of introduce myself around here..

I've been at the Wheel for about 2 days now, on the first day, I managed to get some movement, and dismissed any belief it was me.. And then it happened again, so I got out the webcam, closed the windows, put a towel under the door, and sat wondering "how in the fuck?", waiting for it to move on its own. But, nothing.. So, I've been doing it more often, and I got a buddy over. He made sure there was no air circulation, and we sat back and watched it not move at all. I sat back far enough that my breathing wouldn't affect the wheel, put a shirt over my mouth, focussed for a few minutes, and it got moving a bit.. By now, my buddy is trippin' right out, telling me to stop. But I kept on, when it got to spinning he yelled stop, and flicked the wheel off the thumb tack when I didn't.

I'm still not entirely sure it's me, but it feels like im getting a more concience spin, when I get it to start moving, so if my buddy believed there was nothing to move the wheel, I only have reason to believe PK is true, and I am indeed, moving the wheel..
