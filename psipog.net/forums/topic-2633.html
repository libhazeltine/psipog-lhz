Ooh, perhaps you're aiming too much for the 'getting back to where I was before' goal, rather than 'getting it to work at all' and then building up from there.

Try to drop all your expectations of what _Should_ be happening in your opinion, and lose the preconcieved ideas from your past experiences, etc. Just let it happen, try to give the 'power' over to your subconscious in this situation.

Take on the mentality of discovery / playful-ness, and being focused - rather than frustrated and annoyed with the psiwheel and yourself. Both factors lead to a whole lot of nothing!

As for the arguing with yourself over results, they should drop away if you take on a less frustrated mindframe. And getting results again will help quite a bit!
