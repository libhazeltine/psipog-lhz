So, one of my friends that i JUST asked about this earlier - According to him, when he lived in Europe years ago... He met an old man. Apparently a HIGHLY skilled psion... who had apparently delved DEEEEEP within his own mind, to uncover secrets that mankind isn't supposed to know yet. He wrote 2 volumes based on what he found. These books he passed onto Will (my friend.) Who didn't believe at the time. However, he DID try a technique (shielding) that the old man taught him. Later that year, a little boy walked up to him and said "Mister, why do you have a blue ball around you?" And he freaked out. He then memorized everything within those 2 books, and passed them onto that kid. Telling him NOT to pass that information on to ANYONE, except one person - who he would know when he met. Part of that information dealt with the "Age of Faith" and the "Age of the Mind". Apparently, we had been in the Age of Faith since the formation of the Church (religious, bla bla) and this age was ending... and we were passing on into the Age of the Mind. Supposedly, within this (short) age, us Psions will get muuuch better at what we do- and for a purpose.

Shortly after explaining this, i began to sense... things... around us. Ben and Will (the friends i was hanging with) both saw an Impish little psionic creature up on the ceiling... and then as we walked into the Teen Center, Will spotted (an apparently malevolent) construct/demon... and killed it with PSI. Which ben confirmed... and even I could see it sitting there. (both ben and will have Aura vision) I remembered Ben mentioning nightly battles with demons... which i blew off as Sleep Paralysis. But now i'm not so sure...

And another thing that Will mentioned after this - He said that in the old man's books, it said that when demons began walking the earth freely, in large numbers... the Apocalypse is upon us. Which fit everything I had been studying PERFECTLY. Explained everything, including this odd nervous feeling in which my underarms and palms would sweat overtime (this generally happened at the YMCA... which is a congregating place for psions. I believe that these demons like to hang out around psions.) This explained it, as it would happen when a demon was nearby. After i put up a shield... Voila, gone.

I also got to try out my psionic combat skills against a few demons while in Fencing class. On the way home, I was assaulted multiple times... And I currently have NO doubt that the end of the world is at hand. Thinking back on Titor's predictions, overlayed with (RELIGION WARNING) Revelation... the Four Horsemen (USA, China, Russia... and another participant in the war [the plan was for me and all my psion friends to hide in the mountains... and train to fight in the war. so maybe the fourth is us]) and so on... Surely the Antichrist could be anyone... but I sincerely believe he will show himself before long.

Took me half an hour to write that up, please read and honestly consider this warning. Religious or not, you can't possibly believe that this world will go on for eternity.
