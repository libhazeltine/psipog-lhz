There is good reason for this being illegal. Each person has an attentional capacity, meaning that they can only do so many tasks at the same time while putting forth an appropriate amount of concentration toward each task. We, of course, have the capacity to do both. However, using language (talking on the phone) usually takes up more concentration than driving does, since language is processed at a much deeper level. Driving, on the other hand, becomes an automatic process after a few years of practice. Therefore, most of your attention will be put toward talking rather than driving. That could have fatal consequences.
