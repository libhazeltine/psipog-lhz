If you are talking about a paper psi wheel then you could always try the tin foil psi wheel. The reason why alot of people start off with the psi wheel is becase it has almost no friction. If you were to try to slide or roll some thing you would encounter friction. That being said, here are some other things to try. Hang a straw of the side of the table and try to make it fall off. Float a toothpick on top of water in a bowl and try to make it move. hang a key on a rope hanging from the ceiling and try to make it move.
