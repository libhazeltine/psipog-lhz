Mind reading ability evidence

1. He started singing this song one time by Jimmy Buffet. I don't think Jimmy Buffet is that well known, but I am not sure, so that's why the poll is up. I listen to Jimmy Buffet, and was thinking about his songs earlier that day.

2. He only once asked me about video games, he was asking me if I played TimeSplitters. (Great game btw.) That's my favorite game. Basic Psionics-related evidence

4. Once I brought in a pendulum into school. I use it as a lie detector. As you can probably see by my set up and stuff, I do like to do detective work, so I like to have something to use as a lie detector. We were asking each other if we were stupid while holding the pendulum or whatever, just doing weird things. At the time, I was not a big psionics practicer or anything, I had some basic knowledge and a few minor skills. It's too bad I didn't see all this earlier. Anyway, I told him that these lie detectors could amplify psychic abilities. I don't know why I said this, it was pretty random, as I figured the chance of there being another psionicist in the school are pretty small. He said, "really?" in a very excited/ enthusiastic tone. This is a weird thing to say, unless you are trying to hide your ablilities. If he was a psionicist, he probably would have known this, if he didn't know anything about psionics, why did he seem excited at all?

5. At lunch, I saw two of his freinds doing something strange that could most likely be explained as practicing telepathy. Each had their hand pressed up against the other's forehead, and they had their eyes shut and it looks like they were concentrating pretty hard. Then they cayght me looking at them and resumed eating or whatever. Why a lunchroom though? That's a pretty noisy place to practice telepathy.

So, I hope you read all that. What do you think? Is he a telepathist or is this all a hideous fluke of probability?
