This happened to me years ago, I just want to tell someone.

It was right before sleep and I thought I was just trying to meditate, breathing in and out, thinking of nothing and waiting for the pictures to show up in my mind.

So they did, lots of entertaining but random images, and I was laying there keeping my mind's eye open, seeing but not focusing (if I focused on what I was seeing the picture went away and I'd have to start over)

Then I got the most fantastic image, I was flying over the ocean everything was so bright the sun the blue water it was so real.

I didn't feel any wind or physical sensation, I knew I was still in bed and I was awake and then the vision ended.

It's still so vivid. I don't know why I quit trying to do that.

I'm going to try again see if I can program my subconcious mind to take me back there again.

I'm so glad I found this site.
