'Ello. I have just done my first PK using my own Technique.
I call it "The Tent."
First: You get a napkin.
Then: You fold it into a "tent" shape.
After that: You place your hands around it and feel energy moving through your hands.
Even after that: Concentrate only on the "tent" pretend that their's a bear in the woods and it wants to eat you little "campers" in the "tent."
Finally: Concentrate even harder to make it move to the left, right, up, or down.

Funny, I did this in a public place just playing around with a napkin. Unfortunatly no body saw.
