What do you mean, it would require an extraordinary amount of programming? Basic cellular automata programs can have "lifeforms" that contain only a few bytes of information, but nevertheless exhibit all the emergent behavior that evolution has to offer.

Psi creatures with developed intelligence could be similar, but distribute their intelligence between themselves. So each one has a very small amount of programming, including the ability to replicate. They communicate, and duplicate effective emergent behavior. That's intelligence, right there. Self-aware intelligence, or sentience, could be created by simply allowing such organisms to evolve for a long period. Eventually, circumstances will make sentience a useful survival trait, and they will develop it.
