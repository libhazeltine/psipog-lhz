Hello. My name is Chris, I'm 17 years old and I am open to the idea of psi actually being real. I was brought here by reading The Psion's Handbook: Overview of Psionics written by Sean Connelly a.k.a. Peebrain. I was introduced to this handbook through a friend on another forum that I am a member of. At first I was skeptical and thought that it had to be a trick. But, myself being naturally curious, I decided to tryout the psi ball. After reading through the fist few pages, I began to try to create a psi ball. Being my first time and only having about 30 minutes of practice, I cant be sure if what I felt was legitimate or not. At first I didnt notice much. A tingling sensation in my stomach was first, maybe I was hungy, maybe it was psi, I dont know. Then I tried to make a psi ball between my hands. Then, to my amazement, it was like I could actually feel a ball between my hands, not warm, not could. Again, with only half an hour of practice, it could have been anything. From what I have read, it normally doesnt happen this fast. So, this is why I cant say for sure that it was indeed a psi ball. I will continue to practice and hopefully increase my skill. Thank you to anyone that was patient enough to read through this whole post.
