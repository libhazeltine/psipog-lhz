Since people are always randomly asking if they can get someone's help to RV something recently lost, I thought why not setup something where you guys can help each other out. So basically, this is how it is going to unfold : If you have recently lost something, create a new thread stating what you have lost which such a title as "Lost : objecthere". Then pm me with your information so I can post organized links to them all in here, to lower the confusion, rather than having a pile of responses to 15 different items in one post. If you want to assign coordinates yourself, that's fine, or if you would like me to, I'll do so when I post the information that's fine too. Also if you are going to ask me to assign the coordinates please explain the item to me as best as you can, so I can more accurately make the connection. Upon finding your item please pm me, so I can lock the post.
