Feeling energy of objects is your sensitivity to energy in the enviroment and everything around you. Some people are initially more sensitive than others. I'd wager that most everyone can faintly feel it, but until you practice with TK, you can distinctly recognize it. Before TK, I never really "felt" energy, but I could sense certain presences. It wasn't until I started practicing that I could sense energy everywhere often.

When using energy to move an object, remember not to be aggressive or commanding. You first problem is that you're trying to "force" it to move. Don't force or push. Ask and give. You're treating the object as if it's an obstacle, and the "energy" of the object is the goal. What you're forgetting, is that the object is the goal, because it itself already is energy. Focus on the toothpick, and build a connection. Imagine what it might feel like to have a skin of uneven and splintery wood. No arms, no legs. Things like that, just find a connection. I don't need to ask anymore, because I've trained my subconscious to "ask" the object for me; I simply request or tell me subconscious to move an object. When I forced or commanded the object, I lost concentration, and more importantly, the connection. So, don't visualize what you percieve as just it's energy; visualize everything. Let the object do it's own thing, but be there to guide it.
