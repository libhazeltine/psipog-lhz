Auras 101 by Raitaro

In this article I will explore many of the different ways the aura can be perceived and used. It is extremely important to remember that when reading and seeing auras there are no certainties in what they mean. You must interpret your findings yourself

Definition

Auras
A subtle, luminous energy field radiating within and around the human body as far as 3-7 feet. That part of the aura which surrounds the head is often represented by artists as a nimbus or halo to depict saints and enlightened beings. Usually people who practice energy or people who are more spiritually advanced have more spectacular auras. These are usually seen as a lot larger or brighter. However every living being has an aura. Seen psychically, the aura is filled with many colours which are reflections of the thoughts and emotions active in the nervous system and change according to the person's state of mind.
While reading an aura it is common to pick up various surface thoughts and sometimes pick up traces of peoples character. Auras are not actually physical in any way. They are simply a representation of


Sensing
Sensing an aura is quite simple but some people are uncertain if what they see is what is really happening, This is called analytical overlay and is caused when the conscious mind try?s to reason with your findings ?Is that just what I think they should feel? etc. To sense an aura I usually concentrate on something I associate with the individual I am reading. I build up their physical form round this so I can see their body in my mind. Then I would perform empathy, some people like to visualize a chord linking the people involved heart to heart (as this is where emotional is felt usually) then they would clear there mind and feel the other persons emotions, When I start to pick up feelings I get flashes of colours taste and even sounds that I associate with certain emotions. I would then mentally record this colour and envision it surrounding the person and build the aura up in layers. Before even starting to sense auras it is best to build up a ?library? of feelings sounds tastes and smells you associate with certain emotions. 20 minutes should be enough to get a good foundation. Bare in mind that people feel many emotions at once. Someone might be feeling love deep down but annoyance at the fact that they are not dating who they want.
Reading
When sensing an aura I actually feel them and get flashes of colour, tastes or smells in my head
Contrary to what most people think. The auric field has many colours in it the predominant colour is usually how the person is feeling however it is not as simple as looking at colours that just appear. To me auras ?look? like coloured mist and the way the mist moves is as important as its colour. For example if there were sudden violent bursts of red then it would be obvious the person you were reading was angry. I find that if someone is radiating pink then it is a sign of love because it is gentle and soothing. It is possible to hide your aura or change the colours seen in it (Certain summoners make themselves void of all emotion and feel what the deity they are summoning feels) Orthas describes the method as making oneself empty of all emotion using a technique similar to a void meditation. The reason that there is no concrete foundation on what certain colours mean is that people associate different things or experiences with different colours.
Manipulation
In auric manipulation visualization is an essential tool. The aura can be used for offensive purposes but I find it is more effective when used with empathy. For example if my friend was stressed or sad I might visualize a radiating pink aura
but to make this visualization effective I would have to will the aura to have the desired effect by concentrating on this effect, for example I might focus on what it is like to lie beside my dog and feel his unconditional love. At this point I would use my aura to give her aura (which I would imagine as blue seeing as she was sad) a hug .
On the other hand if I wanted to make an impression, maybe by making myself slightly more impressive or even intimidating I might visualize my aura towering high above me while focusing on how I felt when I was surrounded by a gang of people about to mug me or how I felt while getting into trouble from my head teacher. What is important as the colours only mean what you interpret them to.

Practice

This is a simple game that is good for increasing awareness in the auric field


1. Notice whether you can sense how far your auric field extends. Imagine
that your auric membrane is a big bubble that sends you messages, and notice what you feel at its outer perimeter. Look around
your environment, and see what is located at that boundary edge, Pay
attention to how you feel as your gaze falls on each and every thing around
you.

2. As soon as you sense someone might be staring at you who is out of
your visual range, stay calm and relaxed and turn around to check to see if
you're right. You need only maintain eye contact for a brief moment. Notice if
they were within the auric membrane range you established.

3. Keep a mental or written record of how many times you felt that someone
was staring at you, and how many times that actually turned out to be true...
and how often you also correctly knew where they were physically located.

4. When you try this more than once, notice how you can enhance your
accuracy by consciously extending your aura (or sense of personal space) out
to where other people are. If you find people aren't staring at you enough for
this game to be rewarding, you can make yourself more tempting to stare at by
wearing brighter colours or a hat, or changing your hairstyle.
