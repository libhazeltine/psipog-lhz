I do this for almost anything. if i forgot my glass of water on the counter, i "Will" it to come to me (i stopped doing that since the phone fell over, because i dont want the glass to fall over) but if u are doing any daily activities, just try to use psionics first and then go do it if it doesnt work. so far it has worked for me, and it can work for you also. It is a quick and easy way to practice PK every day.
