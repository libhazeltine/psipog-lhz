A few days ago I had one of the most amazing experiences that i've had in a while. I was sitting down and my mind was clear, I was just relaxing, I began to see images in the darkness of my closed eyelids (Also no it wasnt the micro organism that live on our eyes, I can tell the difference between those and the images), as if I was doing Remote Viewing unintentionally, which is nothing new since this almost always happens, but what makes it amazing, to me atleast, is what comes next.

I kept my mind clear and just observed the pictures, I didn't even give them thought, well time passed by and they got into more detail. During the more detailed vision I could see something, like figures moving in the upper middle of the vision, I kept my mind clear though. After a while the vision got so clear, it was as if I was actually there, it was three blackish birds, large ones, flying. The terrain was a mountain and some grass below, also a stream or river flowing through. It was so vividly detailed and it was like I was viewing this through my eyes, it was not just how we imagine things and can see them in our 'minds eye'. I just observed how beautiful it was, but after some time I snapped out of it, so to speak and the dark closed eyelids returned. I don't know what this was, if it was Remote Viewing, an OBE to the 'Astral Plain' or whatever it can be... One thing for sure, I want to have another experience like it.

What do you guys think it was?
