What do you mean out of range? Anyway... Try making a psiball and tell it to stay there. See how long it lasts. Now make another one and program this one to stay around longer and see how long that lasts.

It can be any number of things from a lack of good (or any) programming... too little energy... or... I'd say that's about it.

As for the protection from rain thing... you're looking for force bubbles. You have to be pretty decent at PK to do that. Go practice PK.
