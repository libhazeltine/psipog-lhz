I agree Corbin... I would suggest relaxing with motionless meditation or tensing and relaxing each muscle. Then visualize your self going downwards somehow. For example: On the exhale climb down stairs but on inhale stop. This is meant to bring you into trance. Instead of visualizing with eyes try tactile visualaztion. Also, you could visualize yourself in an elevator and on the inhale you stay still but on the exhale you go down.

Hope this helps!
