Psipog does not cater to psionic combat because it attracts the wrong element. We don't want people coming to psipog to learn about combat; we want them to come to psipog to learn about self-awareness and their mental potential. Learning to make a psi-nuclear-ball-of-fucking-death is, for most intents and purposes, childish. However, psipog does recognize the fact that combat does happen, and therefore we cover some methods of defense, such as shielding.

If you are a big guy, but don't feel like you're strong enough for a fight, there are a variety of options at your disposal that do not require nearly as much time, energy, and focus that learning combat does. Take a karate class, or study aggression-resolution tactics.
