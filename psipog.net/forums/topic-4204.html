you can put your own personal banner on your free invision board-

this is done by:

admincp > skins & templates > manage skin sets > then, choose whatever board you want to edit the banner on. After the edit, you will see an option that says html. Click it. > Click "Global html Elements" . > then, you will see a lot of things you can edit. Scroll down until you see "Board Header (logo, links, etc)". There will be a link beside it that says "edit single". Click it. > You will then see a whole bunch of code.
Scroll Down until you see what is pasted below. Replace what you see in bold with the direct link to your banner. You can upload your banner at www.imageshack.us.

</div>
