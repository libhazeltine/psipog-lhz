a) Putting a psiball in your face is like shoving a fist up your nose

and

b) it depends on the intention, on the person, how they perceive psi as, etc.

I'd like to throw in my theory that mixes those two up...sure, you can probably damage yourself with psi, but I'm willing to bet that it wouldn't be major damage...most people tend to 'pull back', so to speak. Try punching yourself hard enough to give yourself a nosebleed - I don't think it's a very easy thing to do. Hard enough to bruise, to hurt? I'm pretty sure most could do that.

Then again, your hand is something that's been with you for XXX years that you've been alive, and you know how hard you can hit, etc, etc. So it's possible that a newbie could accidentally charge up a psiball way too high and zap himself
