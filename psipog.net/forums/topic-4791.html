I wanted to share a little story with you guys. This is right from my away message. Kinda creepy.

"I almost got carjacked today. No joke. I was at work and took my lunch break. I went to subway and got the usual- meatball sub and some cookies. As I walked out, a guy came up to me and said, "Excuse me sir, excuse me. I'm from out of town and my car just broke down just up the road and I need a ride. Can you give me a ride just up the road here?" It seemed very shady, so I told him to hold on a second as I went in the store. As I went in, he followed. When I got to the counter, I said to a coworker, "Hey, this gentleman's having some car trouble and needs a ride." I just looke at my coworker, trying to hint that something was wrong. I didn't exactly get help, so I turned to him and told him I couldn't help and that I was on duty. He said it was ok, but then asked if I had 5 bucks for a cab. That sounded really shady, but I pulled out my wallet to act as if I had no money. As I did, he said, and I quote, "Just... if you have 5 bucks.... 10 bucks would be great though... if you've got 20 bucks thatd be really great..." At this point I got very nervous. I said to him, "Sorry man, all I have is a 20 and I'm not giving you 20." He then walked out.

Now you know damn well that that guy was going to carjack me. I don't know if he was packing heat or not, but yeah. Not exactly a good situation. Come on. For chrissakes, I'm 17! Cut me some slack! What the hell am I going to do?

Oh well. If you read all that, thanks. Leave a message if you want."
