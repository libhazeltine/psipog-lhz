Well, first of all, you need a white background. Whether it be a wall or a piece of paper. Colored background will influence the aura color.

Now, soft-focus on the object, whether it be a pencil or your own hand (I do this one a lot).

Then search for a color around the object.

First time I saw my aura I was singing in a bathroom, it was green...

The color should come out of the object and surround it.

It may take a while. I also suggest opening a blank MS Paint document and place the object in front of the computer screen. I found it easier to see auras because the pixels somehow break up the color to make it more visible. So, try it.

Best of luck to ya!
