well, it's kind of like an RPG. it's fun, once you get into it. you basically control an army of either humans, dwarves, ocrs, or elves. they each have their own bonuses: humans get extra money for attacking/each half hour, orcs have higher attack, dwarves have higher defense, and elves have higher covert. you start off with 50,000 gold, and (i think) 30 turns. you get one new turn each half hour and these are used to attack other armies. you can buy weapons, armor, attack/defence specialists, spies, and some other things as well. you also get a certain ammount of gold every half hour (depending on the ammount of soldiers you have, and i think your rank.) they have their own forums where you can get help, and create alliances. in order to get more soldiers, people have to click your unique link (mine is in first post), you can also upgrade to automatically get a higher ammount of soldiers each day (which should be the first thing you buy). you can join in as your own commander, or under someone who is already playing. the benefit of having a commander is that if someone attacks you, they will get revenge and you are less likely to get attacked if you have a commander. the game is seperated in ages, this is age six. each age is between 6 to 9 months long, and this age just started last week. i think it is a fun game, and if you get bored (or are interested) you should join the game (and click my link )
