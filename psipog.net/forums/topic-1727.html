1) Perhaps try some exit techniques once you've been laying for a while.

2) Ensure you have the proper mind state. Basically, you're normally thinking "blah blah blah blah blah" when you lay in bed. You can consciously not think of anything, which is sort of like "..."; you're not thinking of anything, but you're thinking about not thinking of anything. You need to be not thinking about not thinking about anything, which is analogous to "" going on in your head; absolutely nothing, not even white space. This is the correct mental state for OBE. If you can hold this long enough and often enough, eventually you will get an OBE.

3) Those noises are called "hypnogogic imagery" and are purely constructions of your subconscious. They are not real and cannot harm you.

Hope that helped a little bit.
