Ok, just to keep psychic link seperated from other stuff, I or one of the mods can continue this list here.

http://www.PsiPog.net - The like most amazing site EVAH.

http://www.PsiOnline.org - Energy Manipulation (psiballs, constructs, shields, etc) site only. Open forums, chat, and podcast. Good stuff.

http://freewebs.com/psionics_123

[URL]http://www.eldibs.sphosting.com/ [/URL]- This website does contain some personal views by the author that may or may not be suited for everyone. This is not a 100% psychic only site. Also note that the current color scheme makes your eyes bleed.

Active Psy - Online book intoducing one to energy and explaining the most popular skills.

Psi Palatium - Large resource of information on a variety of topics convering psionics, magick, and general psychism.

Dimensional Doorways - Contains a lot of good information and is where some of us started out.

Lucidity Institute - Very nice site focused on the topic of lucid dreaming.

Cognitive Sciences Laboratory (CSL) - Dedicated to researching psi scientifically and explaining it without relying on mysticism.

Does Psi Exist? - Replicable Evidence for an Anomalous Process of Information Transfer by Daryl J. Bem and Charles Honorton

Got Psi? - Cool online games to test how good your psychic skills are. Definitely worth checking out.

Bob Peterson's Homepage - Contains an online book pertaining to out of body experiences. Very insightful.

PPSociety.com - Lots of information on telekinesis, including exercises and theories. Also a message board.

The Psion Guild - Contains information on psionics, but also touches on other things considered "controversial" by some PsiPog staff members.

Psionic Social Club (PSC) .ORG - Contains chat seminar logs on a variety of psionic topics, as well as a public chat. Just a warning: fluffbunnies won't be tolerated there.

Dream Views - Cool site about lucid dreaming, with an active message board.

Astral Society - Cool site with a ton of information on psioincs, and other paranormal topics like magick, new age, and spirituality.

Coordinate Remote Viewing Manual - Awesome resource for information on Remote Viewing.

Rhine Research Center - Research organization aimed at understanding the fundamental processes that underlie psionics.

Institute of Noetic Sciences (IONS) - Large resource of scientific information concerning psi and the mind.

Foundation for Mind-Being Research - Has a list of editorials, including a great one on Psychokinesis.

Fork-You.com - Site focused on bending silverware with PK. Nice information and cool pics too.

PJ's FireDocs - Lots of information on Remote Viewing, and some other tid bits too.

Psi Links - More links to check out. Be warned though: there are a lot of fluffy sites too, so turn on your bullshit detector.

I know there's other places I should post or steal from links.php, but eh, you guys can add them. Just post under here and I"ll add them to this list!
