I have an Xbox 360. I can't get into the discussion as far as hardware is concerned, all I can relate is my subjective experience of the system as formed through playing it.

1) The graphics are absolutely outstanding. I started with an old atari system when I was a kid and I still can't fathom how much better the graphics are with each successive console generation. The only downside to the 360 as far as graphics are concerned is you aren't getting your money's worth unless you have a Hi-Def television.
