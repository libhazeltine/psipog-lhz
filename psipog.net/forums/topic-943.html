Many people seem to think they are being attacked by a psion or some spirit. If you think you are being harmed/harassed/attacked, first ask your self this:

For a psion:

    -Who could you have ticked off? Do you even know any psions?
    -Do you even know anyone who would be strong enough to hurt you or even make you feel uncomfortable (especially if you have a shield)?
    -Why would they be trying to harm you?
    -Ever consider that they may have better things to do other than sitting there trying to harm you?
    -If you were them (the person you think is attacking you), would you attack you? Wouldn't you think it would be a waste of time to try to hurt someone who you don't even know and have no way of telling if you succeded?


For a spirit:

    -Have you previously even believed in spirits/enitys/ghosts/(insert name here)?
    -Could it hurt you?
    -Why would it want to harm you?


Finally, could it be a physical ailment or just your imagination? Unless someone said they were going to attack you, just go see a doctor/psychologist. If the "attacks" persist then post on the forums. I might be able to belive you if you had a unique circumstance or could tell who it was that was attacking them but most people say they can't think of anyone they might have ticked off and they describe thier symptons as a headache or stomachache.

P.S. I didn't know where to post this so i put it here.

EDIT: I edited this post to clear up some confusions about its topic. The point of this post was to say that I am skeptical that someone would try to hurt another person for no reason (although there are a few exceptions) and that, most of the time what someone precieves as an "attack" (notice the quotations) is a psychological or physical ailment.
