The subconscious is an amazing thing. It has an eidetic memory (photographic memory). It could very well relate your experiences to past experiences, find hidden algorithms, or even calculate what day of the week it will be on July 4, 2043. It's a genius part of the mind that one must learn or maintain access to. An interesting thing is that it can be fully accessed as a baby. Some people maintain the ability to access the subconscious, while most people do not, and have to learn how to again to gain the ability. That's not to say that accessing it cannot be learned. I believe that anyone can learn to access their subconscious or even communicate with it without knowing. For instance, when one is planning to jump somewhere, they can predict where they are going to land depending on how much force they put into their jump, but they do not subconsciously calculate it. Also, when one wishes to ride a bike, they just know how, after being taught long ago. They do not have to consciously think to remember what position they need to be in, and one does not need to consciously think to pedal the bike. These are all examples of subconscious thought.

Savants have greater access to their subconscious, and many can do incredible high speed calculations and memory feats without having to consciously think. I've accessed my subconscious before, but I do not believe I?ve actually "talked" to it as if it was another person. I can make it do calculations for me as long as I know the rules that apply to the calculation. I have a friend that can multiply three digit numbers in his head using his subconscious. The subconscious is probably used in many psionic abilities as well.

IMHO, your experience was probably your subconscious telling you where something is based on a past experience. It could have found an algorithm that the people who made the game subconsciously use to determine where to place items, or maybe it has calculated a high probability of the location of various in game items. It is certainly possible that your subconscious is telling you where it knows an object is or anything for that matter using psionically gathered information.

The mind is an incredibly complex tool. Never doubt its power.
