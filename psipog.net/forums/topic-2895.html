Well, at least your subconcious wants to listen.

And by that I mean your mother's disapproval has been ingrained into your head as a Bad Thing? and something you shouldn't be working to gain. This is probably part of the reason that you're having trouble. And it's also something you'll have to make a decision about too - are you interested enough in PK to go against your mothers wishes and teach your subconcious it's ok to do so? Sure it doesn't sound that big, but if you do then it'll probably take more concious thought and consideration afterwards if she tells you to do something.

Another item is that when you sit down at the wheel, you hear your mothers voice telling you not to do it. That's a pretty big distraction.

I had something similar recently when I hinted at a friend to see if he was interested in Psionics and got the reply 'It's not possible. No - not at all.' and really had an impact - the nest few days I found it very hard to move the Psi wheel.

The only real way to deal with something like that is to prove it wrong in your head. You may think you know it's true, but if you're sitting down and hearing it all over again, it's just not resolved. You need to confront it, dissect it, and disprove it. Once that's done it's no longer an obsticle.
