I just ignore other sites and decided for myself what works and what dont.

Ok, well the very first OBE i had (self induced one anyway) i thought to myself i WILL come out tonight then as i lay down i thought about how cool it would be to be flying around out of my body, and how i would do it. the next thing you know i felt the vibrations, i imeadeatly began to clear my mind of all thoughts my heart was racing the sounds were coming into my head, i thought of one thing, exiting my body. I lifted my arm out of my body then my legs, (i had a play around with this and started to kick my legs in a bicycle motion) then i pushed the vibrations up to my chest and into my head. Then i thought this is it...

I sat up and felt a sharp sting on the back of my neck it wasnt painful or anything. the world looked exactly the same, i thought to my "self damn i didnt do it" then i looked behind my and there i was lying in bed i thought YESSSSS!!!! i did it, i got out of bed and thought of something i could do, i decided to have a feel around, the walls felt like jelly when i tryed to pass my hand through them, the windows were like air. I looked outside the world was exactly the same as the physical world so i thought this must be the astral plane.

Then i saw something really weird, my door handle was on the wrong way, my picture looked tilted, there was a square shaped object on my TV. I didnt waste any time thinking about what they were i wanted to fly!! so i thought hmmm itd be cool if i just jumped out of my window....bad idea i took a running jump and dived out of my window....i fell to the floor i returned to my body just before i hit the floor. I spent three hours awake that night.
