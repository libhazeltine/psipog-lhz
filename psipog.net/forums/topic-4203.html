a super short attention span is a really big problem. Know that how you act in one part of your life is how you act in all parts of your life. That means if you have trouble paying attention here you must also have trouble paying attention at other places. Trying to pay more attention everywhere and to everyone and everything will be good for you. If you need strong practice then start meditating. If you can keep yourself sitting in one place and concentrating on meditating for first 15 minutes then 30 minutes and then 1 hour then you will find that you will be able to pay attention better at all times.
