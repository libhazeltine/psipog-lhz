Well, don't tell it in a forceful manner. I guess you could say it aloud, but that usually distracts me. Say it in your mind to the wheel. You have to remember that your subconsience is the one who really moves everything, so you're communicating through your subconscience to the wheel. Thus, the more in tune with your sub-c you are, the easier it is to move things, at least, in my experience. But you have to give it more than a day's work, and be patient. If you get frustrated, take a break. If you practice when irritated, you waste time. I'm starting to use Zepp's method more often, because "telling" is so automatic it starts to feel like I'm just "willing" the object to move. But in the beginning, asking and requesting the object was a good start for me. It might be different for you. Just practice and try. I know it's annoying as hell to hear it over and over again, but it works.
