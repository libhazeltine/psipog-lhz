Hold on... you're trying to make physical bodies? Matter? Have you even heard of the Theory of Relativity? E=mc^2 c=299,729,458 meters/second. Therefore, just to produce one kilogram of matter would require 8.983774799*10^16 joules. Given the approximation that a person produces 1 joule of heat/hundreth of a second, we're already over one trillion times that. When you want to create an entire body, (I'll approximate this to be 85 kilograms), you get about 8 exojoules, where one exojoule is 10^18 times one joule, so 8,000,000,000,000,000,000.
(Read 8 quintillion).
