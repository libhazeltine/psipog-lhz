This is one of the coolest non lucid dreams I've ever had:
I was sitting in a yellow buss loaded with all sorts of people, most of them were guys except one little girl standing next to the driver.
The cool thing was that I could see through all of the passangers eyes, I could think and controll the actions for every single one of them at the same time.
I remember how I drived through this really weird forest and ended up on top of a big mountain full of snow.
The buss stared to glide down some snowboard track, we were sliding towards a really big jump with a long gap comming after. The little girl (I) shouted "Step on it!" to the driver while the others (me) just chilled. There we were, heading towards a big jump at full speed when my view of perspective suddenly changed, I was now a basball player at the end of the gap, while being filmed I started to kick on a brick wall that was standing in the snow, I could now see the buss flying in the air from the baseball players view ( I was still controlling the people in the buss).
The buss landed safe at the end of the gap, next to the baseball player and then I woke up.
