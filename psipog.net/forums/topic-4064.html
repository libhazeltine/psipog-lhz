I've read it. It's quite possibly the best book I've read next to the Carlos Castaneda set of books. I believe that all the insights are true and with each day more obvious to me.

First, I would like to mention coincidences. It's funny how I never thought about this the way this book describes it. Its like we're stuck in a space-time matrix, where every move of an object inside this matrix is directly connected to the entire matrix and every event that happens has some kind of meaning and a message for the observer. The book says the more you realize these coincidences and the more messages you get out of them, more and more coincidences will happen and strangely, you can almost know when something is going to happen and why. This is very interesting to me as I have experienced it several times, as did all the other creatures, knowingly or not.

Second, the part when he learns to observe "the energy". In the book it was desribed a bit too easy I think. There's no way one can see energy fields around him with only half an hour of practice, unless extremely gifted. I'm learning to see the same thing for like a year, and I'm no where near the experience desribed in the book .

Third, the "human future" thing. It says we will evolve into higher beings in the end of this century, but I just can't understand that as more and more people are only physically oriented and the entire world is going down to hell anyways. The image of an entire humanity spiritually evolved to the point where we draw a line between living and dead, well I just can't imagine it due to our stupidity factor. It does sound good however.

Anyways I'd like to hear your opinions on these things.
