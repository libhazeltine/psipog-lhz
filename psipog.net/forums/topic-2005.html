That got me thinking, could we possibly band together a bunch of really good remote viewers, and use data sheets (with info regarding the people who are missing), and find some of these people?

It would have unforseen benefits. Firstly, it would recover missing people. This would give the families unprecedented joy. Second, if it works, then it would probably end up on the news, so if we speak out about our method of finding these people (RV) then people would be more likely to take Psi seriously. They'll think "The US army used it in the 80s, and missing people are being found by it now, so it MUST be good for something!"

Anybody thought of this before, or have any ideas regarding it?

Hello sgtpsion,

Around two months ago I was asked to help my local police department with finding a missing child lost in the woods. One of my best friends father is the chief of police at the local police department. I received a phone call late at night by my friend's father asking me to come down to the police department and remote view this lost child. I agreed.

When I first walked into the office, I was greeted by my friends father and he told me the situation that they were in. He explained that a child was missing since 4:30pm and that they have not found him yet. The child was last seen in the woods with his family camping in the woods. I told him that I would give it a shot and sat down in a room where it was quite.

The session took around 30 minutes (I wanted to find the child fast). The pressure started to get to me. I knew if I blew this session, I would not only be made fun of but my respect from my friends and their families would be lost. I tried to brush it aside as best as I could.

I handed in my session and told everyone that the kid is no longer in the woods. I viewed him by a small yellow building by a busy street. Within 20 minutes, the child was found right were I viewed him. The child left the woods and walked near a Turkey Hill store.

Their have been many remote viewers/psychics asked and used by the police departments to find missing people. I was lucky enough to have to opportunity to help out. Only a few people knew about the session due to my friends father not wanting anyone to know that he asked a psychic to help out. I really didn't mind either. The last thing I wanted was to gain attention from people wanting to learn how to remote view. I really don't have the time to have that type of attention. I believe I told a few friends and my family members. A pat on the back and a "good job" is enough for me .

Take care.

- JoeT
