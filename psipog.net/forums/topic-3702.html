Hello everyone,

While browsing through the technique section of the forums, I thought of an idea that I would like to share with everyone. If you've browsed through the technique section, you'll agree with me that they're techniques for almost every major psionic related skill. My idea concerns more organization for our members.

If we were to provide sub boards in the technique section, I believe that our members can benefit greatly. They'll be able to access the material that they're searching for quickly instead of browsing through pages of random techniques. For example:

Psychokinesis Techniques: Personal techniques for Psychokinesis related abilities.

Manipulation Techniques: Personal techniques for Manipulation related abilities.

Perception Techniques: Personal techniques for Perception related abilities such as telepathy, empathy, precognition, remote viewing etc.

Dream Phenomenon Techniques: Personal techniques for dream state abilities.

Other Techniques: Any techniques that do not relate to the abilities above.

It's just an idea that I thought might help everyone. What do you guys think?

Take care.

- JoeT
