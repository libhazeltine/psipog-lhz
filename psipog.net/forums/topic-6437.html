I wouldn't play music. I've had some small success with binaural beat generators, but if I play it loud enough through headphones to drown out external noise, I get a headache after ~15 minutes.

I've had external noise to deal with nearly the entire time I've practiced OBEs. What I've done is a little bit of self-programming. I've programmed myself to "sink" deeper into trance whenever I hear an external noise. It took a while for me to master it, but creating and conditioning the reflex was worth it.

I originally started this at home, where I had my own room and the noises I had to deal with were random creaks coming from the house, or cars driving by outside, stuff like that. I'm at college now, with a roommate who snores. The self-conditioning work so well that, the past couple nights when I've had the room to myself and no snoring roommate, I actually had a little bit of trouble reaching the trance state!

So, that's what I reccomend. Condition yourself to go deeper into trance whenever you hear an external noise.
