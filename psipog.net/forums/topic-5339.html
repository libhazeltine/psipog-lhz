sorry to ruin everyone's fun, but that's actually a sleeping disorder. People wake up in the middle of the night to find that they can't breath and their throat feels like it's closing up. The disorder is called "Sleep induced swallowing syndrome." Really, look it up. Even if you're not entirely asleep, it can happen. It used to happen to me, and it's really terrifying. Maybe it is part of OBE's too, lots of people say it is, but I really don't know.
