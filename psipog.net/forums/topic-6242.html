You are invited to an online discussion sponsored by the Psionic Social Club and Research Library. Details are here and below.

On Saturday, 18 November 2006 at 9 pm Eastern Time, we will be discussing this paper on psionic research done in the former Soviet Union listed in the Research Library section of our site. The discussion will occur in the PSC Annex.

The goal in this group discussion is to expand our thinking in terms of psionic research. In the discussion, we will be asking questions about the article and sharing our ideas based on the information presented in the article. Reading and understand articles takes a certain amount of skill. Hopefully we will all increase this skill by discussing this and other articles posted in the Research Library.

The article must be read prior to the discussion, but you do not need to understand the article in order to participate in the discussion.

Hope to see you there,
