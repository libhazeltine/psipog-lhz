On Saturday, 9 December 2006 at 9 pm Eastern Time, the Psionic Social Club will be discussing this paper listed in the Research Library section of our site. The discussion will occur in the PSC Annex.

The paper is by Joseph McMoneagle and Edwin May and discusses the issue of "Intention, Attention, and Expectation" in regard to Remote Viewing. However, the discussion might well be applied to other areas of psionics.

As before, the goal in this group discussion is to expand our thinking in terms of psionic research. In the discussion, we will be asking questions about the article and sharing our ideas based on the information presented in the article. Reading and understand articles takes a certain amount of skill. Hopefully we will all increase this skill by discussing this and other articles posted in the Research Library.

The article must be read prior to the discussion, but you do not need to understand the article in order to participate in the discussion.

Hope to see you there,

Jael and The PSC Mods
