In my own experiences, stamina comes with time. Like running, the more you run, the more you are able to run longer without tiring out quickly. There is no instant stamina boost, but it grows with your practices. Don't push past your limit, that'll only hurt you. You are using "mental muscles" they have a limit, but they can gain more endurance as long as you keep working at it. I tired very quickly at the beginning of my first few practices but after a while of regular practice I got more "in shape" Just make sure you aren't straining too hard. Don't push, it'll come in time.

Hope that helps.
