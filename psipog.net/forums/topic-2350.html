HOW TO DO TK

I?ve been getting some PM?s lately asking my technique for moving objects. I?ll give you the way I first started out (I skip some of these steps now, however). This is what I do/did, as follows:

Step One:
Enter a Trance-like-state
I used to stare off into space. When I felt my mind was relaxed enough, I would begin.

Step Two:
BE the object
Put your awareness into the object. BE the object. Feel out what it?s like. Is it a pencil? How does it smell? How does the wood feel? Look through the pencils ?eyes?.

Step Three:
MOVE IT!
Move the pencil just as you would your own muscle, since you ARE the pencil. Your body should start shaking, if you haven?t put your ?brain? into the object enough. When this happens feel the object out more and try to relax the spasms, it might feel numbing. If you do this right you should have the object spasm.

My article is very similar to NI?s. Coincidentally we both started with this technique around the same time, or so it would appear from his article. We?re a very visual society. IF we do evolve we will probably gain bigger eyes (just my theory). I think we need to learn to FEEL more. For me that is the trick to telekinesis.
