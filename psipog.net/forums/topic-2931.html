These forums will probably be around for a while. But they will be updated eventually to a custom script. I will probably leave these forums up for a little while after that, as an archive, but eventually I'll delete them completely.

The main reason for updating to the new forums is better integration. You'll be able to use your PsiPog.net username, and the PsiPog.net mailing system. The phpBB mailing system is absolutely terrible and bass ackwards. The most important feature of the new forums (in my opinion) is that it will ALL be searchable. I.e., you type a few lines to search for, and it brings back forum posts, articles, Q&A, your own mail, and blog results.

On top of that, I can customize it more easily. I'll be starting with having a thumbs up/down system, where users can vote for how good a post or thread is. If it gets a certain number of thumbs down's, then it'll trip a report for a moderator to check the thread or post out. Hopefully that will motivate people to post QUALITY information, and it will help in choosing future moderators... just look at their thumbs up count.

phpBB basically sucks. I'm not happy with the software. But, it is free, and it's easily deployed, so they've succeeded in that sense. The features are crappy though. It works for now.
