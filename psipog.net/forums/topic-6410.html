What you call focus in your post, sounds to me more like forcing or straining. Be more gentle, you are too attached and dependent on the outcome of your attempt. Let things go a little, loosen up and give the body time to remember the feeling. Get back into that feeling you had when you used to move stuff decently. Just relax and spend a little quality-time in that sensation of your memory. Then try moving it again. Not with force or attachment to the result, but look at the situation as a new experience to explore right here right now. BE there and let the object move. Give it time.
