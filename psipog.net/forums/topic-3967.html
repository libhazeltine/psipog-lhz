Elliptic wrote:
Also, are you setting your own targets? That's a no-no.


Yeah, definatly have someone else set your targets. There's even places online to remote view, if you don't have a partner to do so, like gotpsi.

Remember, when you "see" things, it might seem like imagination. It's all in your minds eye. Don't expect to actually "see" whatever you're remote viewing. Most psychic related input comes in through the minds eye, IME.

When you're reporting, write down what you think you feel, any images, shapes, and so on (that appear in your minds eye). You might "just know" a small peice information, or you might just "see" some moving lines that pop in your head. However, try and refrain from using nouns - use adjectives to describe what's going on. That way you'll be less pron to AOL. Write down every piece of information you get, do not interpret it. That is for when you're finished.
