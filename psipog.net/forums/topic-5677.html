I used to be a true believer of psi ever since me and my friends got telepathy to work, but now I'm starting to give up on psi. I've made psi-balls and made psi-wheels spin, but I really think I'm wasting my time here. It almost seems like when a psi-ball flares, you are simply visualizing the light a little too well. Do people on here just say that they scared their cat with a psi-ball or made a pencil float to feel better about themselves? I know I'm about to get a ton of people yelling at me, but I don't see any other explanation for telekinesis. Every time I post on this site, there's always someone here to ridicule me, tell me I'm doing it wrong, or tell me that I will never understand how to do whatever it is I'm trying to do. When I make a psi-ball, sure, I can feel it. It feels warm and strong and everything, but that could easily be my mind creating a visualization for me. Whenever I mention something like this, everyone says that I will never progress in psionics because of my disbelief in it. Doesn't everyone have to question it at some point?
