I'd like to point out something to people trying for red eyes.

Yes, people with red eyes do exist. Most people with albinism have blue eyes but a few of them have red. This is due to there being nearly no pigment in their eyes (so they are truely a very very light grey), but because they are so thin the red you see are blood vessels. People with albinism are very susceptible to skin and eye damage, and many are nearly blind because their eyes are so weak.

So the next time you try for red eyes, think twice. You're probably trying to darken them when you have to in fact lighten them, which could explain why so many of you have been getting severe headaches . However, unless you want to blind yourself, I'd reconsider the colour you're going for.
