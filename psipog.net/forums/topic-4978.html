An analogy to my idea would be: Let's take the game of football, every football player has his own unique way of playing, and a unique strategy that works for him. But no matter how they play, they're playing football, and football has rules that any football player must obey.

Now PK is just like football, and PK practitioners are the football players. But the thing is now we don't know a thing about the rules of the game, and we try to make a guess by observing how the players play. If we make a careful observation of many PK practitioners across a long period of time, I think we might just figure out the rules of PK, it might be inaccurate, but it's way more intelligent than plain trial-and-error guessing.
