When I was 11 years old I started PK. At that time I didn't like anything about my life. One day, I was standing at the bus stop thinking about everyone and everything I hated. The thoughts overwhelmed me so I sat down on the curb to wait for the bus. Slowly everything turned gray, until I was staring at a gray wall. I stared at this wall for about 5 minutes (or so I thought) and then came back to full consciousness. When I woke up, I felt very calm and energized. I noticed that I had missed the bus and everyone was gone, so I walked to school. When I got to school, I saw the time and I found out that I was "asleep or something for 2 hours". My friends were telling me how they were yelling at me and hitting to wake me up, but I didn't move.

So... WHAT THE HELL WAS THAT?
