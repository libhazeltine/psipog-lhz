I haven't successfully changed my eye colour to a degree where its easily noticable. However I think I've changed it very slightly.

Anyway, my technique, (in case anyone cared) worked for me and might work for you.

I call it the eye-dropper technique. (Like as in any decent image program) Basically say you want your eyes blue, every time you see a blue object of a near shade to the colour you want, try to imagine an eye-dropper tool selecting that colour and putting it inot your eye.
