haha,

I am pretty sure I mentioned the benefits somewhere But that's no problem, I'll tell you now.

When you master your mind, the benefits range from the most simple things and aspects of daily life, to the greatest goals you might have in this life-time. You see, normally the mind is master over us. Whatever the mind thinks or desires or feels attractive to, we feel that sensation and the attachment to those things. If we walk by a hotdog-tent and we smell those hotdogs, the mind automatically tells you: I want some, I should get some. And in most cases you will get some because the mind has a strong hold on you. It is not only with food like the example above, but it echoes through every aspect of your life. You cannot experience peace and tranquility because the mind is chattering all the time. The mind is confused and doesn't know what it wants, it is one big pool of chaos up there and it limits our power and our ability to listen to our higher intuition greatly.

What happens when we master the mind? What are the benefits?

Too many to name. I shall name a few important wants.

When you become master of your mind, the mind will stop his useless chatter and you will be capable of experiencing an alertness of consicousness unlike anything you have experienced before. And you do not even have to sit still for it no longer. Once you mastered the mind, you will experience long periods without thoughts, but still with consciousness. For most people it is difficult to see the thinking mind distinct from consicousness, but consciousness is something very different than our thoughts. So when you master the mind, you will master your life and the way you experience it. you will never get angry at someone if you don't choose to play that role. You will never be aggresive to someone if you do not choose to play that role. You will automatically be capable of seeing deeper in other people. you will feel the intenseness of their soul in their hearts and hence, you love them like yourself. Besides that, you will be focussed and one-pointed. You will never become sick anymore (unless there is a unique circumstance or case in your life that requires you to get ill). You won't be suffering no longer either. Emotions are bodily energetic reactions to the thoughts in your mind. So if you master your thoughts, you master your emotions and much much more aspects of your being. Your psionic skills will greatly increase also and your ego will dissolve. You will be capable of experienceing constant peace and joy no matter what happens outside of you. you will be able to feel exactly what other people need and give it to them at perfect timing. You will be completely self-confident in all situations and you will be capable of entering samadhi quite easily. You will then rise to higher states of consicousness automatically.

Did I name enough benefits?

All these benefits are natural to posses. the only that is blocking that flow of divine energy that grants you everything you wish for, is the mind. So if you master the mind, you master life.
