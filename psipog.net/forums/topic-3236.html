I don't believe in any media-hyped "magicians" for two reasons:

a) If I had amazing psionic skill, I wouldn't use it just to get popular (well, I would at first , but here's a good analogy - say you somehow end up on another planet, with people who look human, but have no arms. They are amazed by your arms, and nag you to let them look at them. You hear "move them again!" every day. This would get really annoying really fast.

On the other hand, if you don't really HAVE arms, you'll like the idea of pretending to have them, because you don't really have them. In this case, you "fake" things for popularity.)

and b) The government pretty much controls the media. I doubt they want the general public to have knowledge about it, because if everyone was a psion, we'd quickly find new, more powerful ways to use it. More power = less control of the few over the many. Plus many people would probably use it to further their own agendas.

-And that's what I have to say about that.
