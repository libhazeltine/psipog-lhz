I'll be honest, I'm not a master at this stuff, I'm a bit of a psionics newbie, myself, but I can give you advice that I found to be helpful.

Patience, young grasshopper. Patience.

Start with be able to feel the nergy, and not mistaking it for anything else, it might be a bit before you can feel a psiball, maybe let alone create one.

As said multiple times on this site, Psionics is a skill. Like throwing a ball, when you first pick up a ball, and realize you can throw it, you probably won't get too much distance, maybe a few feet, as you were probably a child. But with more and more practice, you can throw further, picking up tips and techniques on how to get more distance, your skill develops with time and practice.

Same goes for psionics, if you would have "picked up the ball" when you were young, chances are you could do amazing things right now, but we're still at that infant stage of psionics where we can only get a few feet, which may be just feeling the psi. But give it time, keep throwing the ball, eventually you'll be able to throw much further, and accomplish all the areas your mind is capable of.

So don't get discouraged if you can't move things with your mind yet, or if you can't do TP, RV, or OBEs, keep a positive mind, practice daily, and you'll get the hang of it.
