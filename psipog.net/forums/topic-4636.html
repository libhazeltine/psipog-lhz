Ok well this was my third lucid dream. And I found them to be rather fun. This one started in a parking lot in the middle of no where. It was just a giant parking lot and nothing around. So I decided to fly around and I finally came to a gate. There was a guard there. I attempted to fly over the gate and he stopped me, he said, "I can't let you leave."

What was really weird is that I knew I was dreaming. I even did a reality check with the watch I was wearing and I said allowed "I am dreaming".

He would not let me past. Then I just tried closing my eyes and opening them to go somewhere else and he said, "I can't let you do that either, I'm sorry." and when I opened my eyes I was still there. No matter what I tried I couldn't leave.

What is this about? I don't even know why I started in a parking lot, but it was just weird.

Why would he not let me leave, even though I proved I was dreaming, and knew I was.
