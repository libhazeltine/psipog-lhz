Why are the first 10 posts of this thread different versions of "I think it's impossible although I have no practical experience with attempting this?"
Before you go saying stuff is impossible, try it. Then try it again, and again. After you've done all of that, try some more. So after you've given it the best effort you can, meditated on it for an hour a day for more than one day, and spent time actually attempting rather than thinking of how cool the success would be, tell me it's impossible.

So back on topic, yes this is possible. I have done it myself. My eyes started at hazel and I changed them over the course of about 2 1/2 weeks to dark blue. I don't have any pictures, so don't bother asking. And yes, I physically changed their color: no telepathic suggestion here. Simple really; I just meditated and told my body to decrease the melanin production in my eyes. Maybe that's too technical for some of you, so just stick with whatever works for you. I stopped telling my body to change its melanin production, so my eyes faded back to hazel after about a week. Pretty fast, but it was very noticeable - I had received comments from friends asking whether I was wearing contacts or not several times.
