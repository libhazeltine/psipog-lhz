I believe love can be interpretated in many ways, but roughly taken, there are two perspectives for me:

1: Love is a specific feeling (can be satisfactionairy but also disturbing) feeling of the ego of one's person.

2: Love is one with the source of life.


The first one is transitory and if you ask me, an illusion. The second form is unconditional love that is forever. It is the basis of our being. It is intertwined with the non-physical as well as with every manifestation.

If you are asking how to send love, I would say: Cultivate the feeling of love in yourself by trying to emphazise and feel connected with the person you are sending love to. Appreciate his being and his process in life and send this love in visualization or in intention. A smile can do wonders as well. So can a loving gesture or a nice flower or some sweet encouraging words. Love can be send on all different levels.

Nice to see this idea of yours Apollo. We should all remember it a lot more often I agree. And remember: If you send love to someone, some people fear to lose their own energy. But that is not possible, for if one sends love to another, the love first has to built up in your self before it can flow out to others. You will get more if you give more.
