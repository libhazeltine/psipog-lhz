Yup, psionic "umbrella" and a warm layer of air to dry my clothing quickly are two of my favorite constructs for bad weather. I've only practed the umbrella a few times with limited success, as I really enjoy the rain. The "umbrella" concept actually works quite well when it's snowing, as all you have to is whirl enough warm air around to keep the snow from landing on you. More for idle amusement than actual usefulness, but I've had better luck with that one.

Oh, if it's misty out, you can put a teeny shield around the lenses of your glasses. I've switched to contacts now so I've not done it in a while, but it was a life-saver during fall soccer games!

If you can think it, it's possible.
