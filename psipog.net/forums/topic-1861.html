If you do Bio-PK with a broad intention, then it's unlikely you would harm yourself. As Sheep said, if you hold the intention, "I want my eyes to safely heal so my vision becomes clear and percise", then you are sending a safe message to your body. Your body hears your thoughts - express what you want, and let your body find a way.

However, let's say you read about how the eye works, and you learn that bad vision is caused by imperfect sphere shaped eyeballs. So you send the message, "I want my eyeball to flatten on the left side", reasoning that it will fix your vision. Well, that's where Bio-PK becomes dangerous - because you've just told your eyeball to flatten on a side based on your research. Don't try and interrupt your body's processes - instead, tell your body which process it should use. The process of healing is already programmed in your body - you don't need to tell your body how to heal - just tell it to HEAL. It will choose the pre-programmed process that accomplishes your goals. The body understands.
