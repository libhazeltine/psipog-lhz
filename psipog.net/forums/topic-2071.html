I will say that communicating with your sub-c is something that can make you feel very borderline crazy. It really is just a form of talking to yourself- only with the perks of knowing certain things you can't exactly recall. It's hard to really get the hang of talking to your sub-c because usually it's hard to tell if you're just talking to another part of your conscious mind or to your sub-c. Either way, do it. Talking to yourself is, after all, a sign of intelligence. Eventually, your sub-c will start to nose in on the conversations in some subtle way or another. And when you notice it happening, just focus on the tone, the 'voice in your head' that seems more seperate.
