To move something using mind power hasn't really been explored that much, but there are loads of theories on it. For me, I "just do it", with a little bit of mental pressure on the object at times. Try to feel the boject and expect it to move. Also, if you spin it a couple of times with your hand then you can begin to understand what it looks like when it moves so you can incorporate that into your method.

To make a psiwheel, you need a piece of square paper ranging from 1 x 1 inches to 3 x 3 inches, preferbly 2 x 2 inches. When you have this, you need to fold it from corner to corner on each end (take one corner and fold it ove to the other corner, then the other way, as in length ways and width ways.) You should end up with a cross as a crease going across the psiwheel. Then simply take a tack or pin (mount on some blue tack if needed, so it doesn'ty scrape on the table/desk) and place the wheel on it and your away!

Hope that helped.
