Hello,

I would like to ask what's the best dream you've ever had?

The best dream I can remember is the following (it was so reallistic, the images were so vivid and the emotions had a lot of intensity...):
I find myself walking on a road (a downhill) with some friends, on my way back to home from school, on a bright afternoon. There was a lot of traffic (the road was full of stopped cars, as if they were trying to evaquate a city). A friend of mine and me were in front of the others and we were jumping literally the cars (!) as if we were as light as feathers.
Reaching home at night (the day passed very quickly) I noticed a huge black hole on the sky, that was sucking air! The image had such a great detail! The hole must have been very large because although it was very high, it looked quite big. I stared at it for a moment swallowing the clouds along with air...When I enetered my house I heard on the radio: "the black hole is very close to earth and scientists claim that humanity has 2-3 hours till its final destruction"!!!! At that time I felt extremely anxious.
Anyway not to tire you with all these details, I decided to go out and pass my last hours trying to fullfil my most wanted dreams...
Then I woke up. (I can crearly remember a lot of details but I would need pages to write them)

Usually I am consious of what I am doing in my dreams (lucid dreaming) and so every emotion was very strong.

What about yours?
