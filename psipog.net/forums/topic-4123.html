Teaching people in an online class chatting system is very ineffective. We hold seminars every once in a while, but even those only help maybe 100 people. In comparison, my psi ball article gets around 114 reads PER DAY. After a month, the one article has been read 3420 times. It's much more effective to post the majority of the information in the form of articles, and then help people when they have questions about the articles.

There are a bunch of other reasons online classes aren't a good idea. We ran telepathy classes a while back, and with only 10 students, we had a lot of schedule conflicts, lazy students, lazy teachers, and bad results overall. It's one of those thigns that sound great on paper, but actually DOING it is something else.
