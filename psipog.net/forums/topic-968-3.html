I can't recall any happy dreams (although I have had my share), and few of my nightmares come to mind. However, I do remember a dream that happened prior to my Sleep Paralysis. I wasn't exactly lucid, but it was one of the most vivid dreams I've had.

I was on this floating dome that could've seated maybe 50-100 people. A few of my classmates were there, as well as some random children I've never met. One of my classmates looked at me with a distressed expression on his face, and kept looking around the room. I figured we all weren't supposed to be there. All of our seats were arranged stadium-style (half-circles with descending rows, and an open area in the center). The roof had a clear-glass dome, and we were floating through a wrecked city. Vines were intertwined with all the buildings, and we were near the top of a few sky scrapers.

At one point a large crystalline structure began to float into view from behind a skyscraper. My vision started to blur, and I heard a faint ringing in my ear. Then this giant shadow figure began to follow it, and I blacked out. I woke up with SP, and the ringing/blurry vision was still present for a few moments.

Sorry for the long post, and I know this isn't "my best dream." I'll never forget it, though. I thought it was some riddle that I had to solve, bothered me for weeks, haha.
