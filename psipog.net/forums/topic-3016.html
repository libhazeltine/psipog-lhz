\Only problem with otherkin, is that it opens you up to a completely different set of beliefs that get wierder and wierder. By otherkin, I'm not too familiar with the word, but you're referring to human versions or dragons, or werewolfs or cat demons and such right?

To believe in them, you must believe that either they exist in a type of astral world, or believe that they exist in the way that humans take after them. Or that one of those mythical races would be reincarnated into humans.

It sort of crosses together fact and fiction.

I personally don't disregard anything as impossible until I see some solid proof that it's a load of crap.
