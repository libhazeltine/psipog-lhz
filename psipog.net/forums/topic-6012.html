see-saw
close your eyes, visualize a suspended bridge with a cube connected to the end. try to tilt the cube, as if you where tilting your head. watch the tilting bridge and cube, then imagine your laying on the bridge... keep tilting it from left to right and you should easily get the see-saw effect

psi-ball
look at the ground, visualize water, or some kind of water surrounding your vision. let the water flow to your feet and make the water go into your feet... push the energy (numb feeling) up to your shoulders then down to your hands.. visualize blue light or liquid coming from your palms and force that into a ball... if you did it correctly you would feel a cold wet feeling (thats my feeling at least, you might get hot or something else, anything thats odd)
