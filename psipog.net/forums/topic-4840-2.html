It's not like you're coming out of the closet doing cart-wheels and wearing streamers.

If you're 8~15, your parents are more likely to not take you seriously if you tell them you practice Psionics. That's not to say they won't interfere, they'll just believe it's another crazy phase. So if you're going to tell them, make sure you can at least roll something.

When you approach your more "adult years," and you tell them you practice Psionics, they may be more open to it. They'll trust by now (hopefully), that you're mature enough to make decisions on your own. So even if they don't believe you, there's not much they can do. If you can't do a thing, however, Lord knows what they'll think.

All families are different of course, that's just a common trend I see. If your family is really close and open about things, you've probably already told them (or should). I showed my closest friend, and now I'm moving on to my brother. Eventually I'll show the whole family. Just tell whoever you think will be the most accepting, and move on from there.
