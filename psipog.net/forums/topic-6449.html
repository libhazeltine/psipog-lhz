I discovered that I 'am learning in a much more constant way when I change only one parameter in a training exercice.

For exemple, You spin a wheel 2x2 inches in paper. Try a 2x2 tin foil psiwheel of the same shape.

I think it should be easier to learn rolling if you use the same object you are the most familiar and proficient with. The psiwheel !

Take a piece of paper 2x1 inches, roll it around a pen for it takes a half- cylinder shape. Put it on the side of an eraser which as a slipping cardboard. Have fun.
