First I would like to apologize if this idea has already been suggested.



Sometimes I think that although articles are helpful, an additional resource is needed, the resource I have in mind could be an area specifically for personal experiences.

This may be in the form of a dairy for example, one which documents psionic experiences on a daily or even weekly level.

In the past couple of days a lot has happened to me (psychokinetic wise), I think it would have been helpful to have read someone else’s experiences to see if my own experience was common, and maybe to gain a greater understanding of exactly what was going on with my body. Also, the person posting their experiences can gain a lot from the replies they are given.

I guess what I am proposing is some sort of psionic blogging community. I’m sure this idea has a few problems that would have to be sorted out or prevented, but overall I believe it could work and be a hugely beneficial resource.

If such a community exists, PLEASE point me in the right direction and POST THE LINK.
