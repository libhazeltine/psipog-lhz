The PsiPog articles are awesome for newbies but just telling yourself and the object things in your head works fine. Personally, I say things like "the ___ and I are one", "the ___ is like another limb", etc. Afterwards (when actually moving the object), I do one of three things: attempt to command the object, move the object as a limb, or ask it nicely (suprisingly, this one is the most effective for me!). Moving the exact same object on a regular basis works better, I find, because it's like I have a "pact" with the object. Anyway, good luck! Happy to help out a budding Psion!
