The distance does not matter, your mind may think it is harder from a longer distance but it isn't called Tele(far,distant) Kinesis(movement) for nothing.
It does not matter how far away the object is, as long as you are connected with it you can move it, even if it is on the other side of the earth.

Edit: read this topic by apollo: http://psipog.net/forums/viewtopic.php?t=4066
Apollo wrote:
There is no limit of distance. What i mean is that you can create a psiball on the other side of the earth. You can do telepathy on a person on the other side of the earth. You can move a psiwheel on the otherside of the earth. It is a big problem when someone has the sense that they cant do PK from more than a foot away; or they cant do telepathy to someone they are not looking at. That is a huge mental block and can restrict your success. People use their hands when making a psiball or doing PK to delete some of the mind's automatic sense of distance. When your hand is in front of the object you are trying to move, your mind knows it can move it by touching it. That is a good place to start rather than having your brain thing there is no way of moving it. Hope this helps people
