You got to learn how to focus, instead of thinking. Thinking is often interpretated as focussing. Thoughts are no focus at all. Focus is really being there with your presence, your awareness, your consicousness. Experiencing the psi-wheel in it's fullest and not thinking about it. When you do this, it doesn't matter if it moves, because you will still be present with your focus on the psi-wheel and you will just be there with your conscious energy all over those molecules. Therefore, you cannot think: Oh look! Its moving yay! It is impossible to be in concentration, ?nd using the process of thought at the same time. This is why the psi-wheel stops when you start to think about how it is moving. The moment before, you experienced a moment of focus, no-thoughts. Because of that little moment of focus, the psi-wheel moved, but you could not remain focussed, but started thinking.

Next time the psi-wheel moves, notice how your thoughts start to come and how excitement starts to come. Then when you notice that, bring your presence back onto the psi-wheel. As if you teleport your entire being, your entire body, onto the psi-wheel...and experience that. Experience the shape and structure and texture of the psi-wheel again. Fully be there. Then you won't think about it. And even if it is moving...if you are in real focus, that would not matter, it still remains the psi-wheel, just in momentum now, nothing special about that. You will be able to maintain focus. this is the key-element in PK. Focus. For me, it's all about that.
