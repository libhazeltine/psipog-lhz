You don't need pictures of each other; all you need is a pre-existing location in the dream world. A popular one is the lucid crossroads, because it?s well, cool. It has a detailed site about it so that you can envision it correctly and get there. http://www.lucidcrossroads.co.uk/

From there you can just all go through a door to another place and mess around, or stay at the crossroads. Try not to screw the place up too much though, other people use it. As far as being lucid, all you guys need is one person to be lucid and "wake up" the other people. Although if you incubate your dreams so that you start out at the lucid crossroads, you'll probably be lucid anyways. Some people claim that shared dreams don't depend on time, but I think that?s false. So I would recommend that everybody is dreaming at the same time, however you want to accomplish that is up to you, but I would recommend a Wake Back To Bed at the same time. And make sure to take time zones into account.

And last, to make sure you were actually there, everybody should do the old password swap, so that when you wake up, you can compare it on the forums by PMs. That is if you weren't doing that already.

If you don't know what wake back to bed is, I suggest you read up on
it at http://www.dreamviews.com/forum/viewtopic.php?t=6147
