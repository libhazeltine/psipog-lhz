Practice this exercise once per day before sleep. Take a blank sheet of white paper and draw a dark colored circle in the center. Fill the circle in. Place the picture under a lamp and stare at the picture for three minutes. Then look at a blank wall and see the picture projected there in reverse colors. Hold your hands up, palms facing each other.

Absorb
Sit up with your back straight and your chin up. Place your left palm on your right calf and your right palm on your left calf. Be aware that there is magickal energy all around you in the form of a colored snowy cloud and you are going to breathe it in. Breathe the color of your choice for the snowy cloud into your solar plexus for 4 heartbeats. Hold your breath for 2. Breathe out for 4 heartbeats but not the color and hold your breath for 2. Do this for 10 repititions. Your body will begin to rock a little involuntarily and your solar plexus will get warm. You may even feel butterflies. This is normal and is a sign that you are absorbing magickal energy.

Collect
Place your fingertips together near your solar plexus as if you were holding a softball. Look at the space between your hands. Sense the warmth in your solar plexus and feel it going up to your chest as you move your hands up. Then fork the warmth down both arms and out through the palms of your hands shooting into a softball size sphere. Your palms will start to tingle. You may see a white grey or blue light flow out in waves or shoot out in straight lines toward the center of the space between your hands. The texture and material looks similar to a snowy television screen. Your hands will feel warm.

Shell
If you move your hands away your eyes will be able to see this snowy sphere. The sphere will last for only a few seconds. Move your hands away only slightly and focus on the rim of the magickal sphere that you have created. Imagine you harden that outer rim into a tranparent like glass that contains the light. This will not last unless you use some sealing techniques as described below using sigils, gestures and words.

Genekinesis
Visualize the sphere from the last exercise materialize between your hands.

Telekinesis
Visualize moving the sphere with your magickian's will. Move the sphere from your right, to your left, back to your right palm. Center the sphere between your palms again.

Cryokinesis
Visualize the sphere become more solid, then turn it into a ball of ice.

Electrokinesis
Return the sphere to its previous texture and polarize it. Visualize the sphere warming up and sparkling. See electricity envelope the sphere.

Morphokinesis
Make it hot. Reshape the sphere into a cube, and then reshape the cube back into a sphere as if it were metal in the fire.

Pyrokinesis
Visualize the sphere getting white hot and igniting on fire.

Oxykinesis
Visualize the white hot sphere of fire turn into a star and see it dissolve into dust.
