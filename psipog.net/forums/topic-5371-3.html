Hi, all. I rarely read the threads in this category, and came upon this thread when looking for something else. Seeing as I seem to have been misquoted once or twice, I figured that I might as well comment.

My chest has been phased through objects, more than once, and I am still alive. It was decidely unpleasant, but not fatal.

It has been suggested, in this thread and previously, that it only hurt because I am unskilled in phasing. This is entirely possible. I am unskilled in phasing, and make no claim to the contrary. Other people have said that it doesn't hurt when they do it. Truthfully, it didn't _always_ hurt when I have done it, on a small scale, such as a hand going through the counter-top...just most of the time. And the large-scale stuff hurt a lot.

If I were going to make a practical study of phasing, I'd start with inanimate objects. There's probably less risk that way.

Bear in mind that psionic phasing is, by definition, psychokinetic. Therefore, it will probably affect your blood chemistry. I'd advise that you keep a snack and something to drink handy, while you are conducting your experiments. And don't drive or operate heavy machinery immediately afterwards, for those of you old enough to drive.

I look forward to reading about your progress.
