Now if only that had a 100% success rate... www.ld4all.com has lots of information on it. So as to make this not a spam post I'll give some help too.

When learning to ld (lucid dream), doing the above helps. It can get you an ld on it's own, though this is unlikely. Keeping a dream journal is a given, just jot down your dreams when you wake up. There are various ways of falling asleep that can help, though you'll probably only have a use for one or two. These would be:

MILD - Mnemonic Induced Lucid Dream - Basically just fall asleep repeating mentally something like "I will dream. I will remember the dream." or "I will wake up in my dream." over and over and over until you fall asleep. Helps dream recall.

WILD - Wake? Induced Lucid Dream - Basically fall asleep but still be conscious when you do. Hard to explain. Is usually only useful after you are fairly used to the basics.
