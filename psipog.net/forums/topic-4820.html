First off, remote viewing is a military-devised protocol for focusing attention in such a way that a clairvoyant effect is produced. Remote viewing would NOT be used to communicate, it would be used to view people/places/things in close or far away areas. Telepathy is what would be used to contact an alien, if said alien contact were possible.

Secondly, your friends have probably been reading too much David Icke material. For those that don't know, David Icke believes that there is a race of reptilian aliens that currently hold power in the upper echelons of international society, such as presidents and politicians. If I'm not mistaken, David Icke's belief's are derived from the theories of Zacharia Sitchin who, in his book Chariot of the Gods, put forth the idea that human DNA was tampered with by extraterrestrials to make us the sentient beings that we are today.

I would remain highly skeptical of these theories, and of the claims of your friends.
