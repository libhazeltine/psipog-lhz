Recently, when I have been lucid dreaming I tend to fall into a waking dream. That’s where you think you’ve woken up, but are actually still in a dream, for those of you who don’t know this term. Strangely these waking dreams are sort of lucid as they feel real (i.e. I can feel things, etc) but I am not actually aware that I am dreaming, until something happens to make me realise I am dreaming.

I’m finding these occurrences rather intriguing at the moment and would be interested to here if anyone had had any similar experience.
