Okay there are three types of vampires that I've experienced

Poser Vamps: These are your typical middle-upper class kids who decide to be all rebellious and are attracted by the lore and romance of the vampire life, as shown in movies and Anne Rice books. They have weak psionic powers at best, and are basically total vampire wannabees. They do it because it is "cool"

Sanguine Vamps: I'm not really sure about this one because it is extensively hard to prove, but I know a few people who are pretty sure they are ones. They feel for whatever reason that they need blood from time to time. They don't die without it, they just get sick and blah and depressed and generally not fun to be around. They aren't immortal, they can't transfer it to other people, they don't have overly enlarged canines (though some have somewhat larger canines just by chance).

Psychic or energy vamps: These people are definitely for real, because I am one to a very small degree, and I've gone out with two psi vamps. For whatever reason, their basic energy imput (chi or what have you) from their surroundings isn't enough to support their everyday activities, so they end up having negative pressure so to speak, when it comes to energy. Energy is drawn in from everything around them, especially people (we're basically walking sacks of energy). That's why many are drawn to crowds or social situations. Many psi vamps are very social, flirty, gregarious, and physical at times. Think about it, it kinda makes sense. If human life-force is similar to other forms of energy in that it obeys the inverse-squared law, the closer you are to someone, the more you can absorb.

Energy vamps follow this particular pattern. Let's say Joey is a psi vamp and Susie is just a regular person. Joey is kinda down, but Susie is very energetic and cheerful. If Joey and Susie spend a lot of time in close proximity (not in a dirty way) eventually Joey will become very energetic and Susie will mellow out.


I have figured out a very simple and effective way to determine if someone is a real vampire or a poser. Posers will think that it's the coolest thing since sliced bread that they are able to live off of drinking blood or steal peoples energy, while the legit ones shun that fact. The real ones often wish they weren't, and seriously look down upon the posers. Think about it. If you have diabetes or any other deficiency disease, and you needed to take supplements (insulin or hormone replacement or whatever), that is a large hassle, and you don't feel well unless you take your supplements.

Artful Dodger: Very insightful. I like your analogy on psi vamps "nibbling" on others energy fields, because that is very much what they do. Often in passing, a psi vamp feeling down will just skim some energy, consciously or subconsciously, off of passing energy fields.

And yes there are people who are like energy fountains. They either have too much energy and don't know what to do with it, or they just don't shield very well and leak energy like a sieve. Psi vamps like these people a LOT for obvious reasons.
