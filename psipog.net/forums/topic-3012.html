i have always understood that a basic resistance sheild was the most basic...oh well yes, we are already capable of filtering out these emotions, but with some sensitive empaths, filtering isn't enough. they need some other layer of protection to help them deal with overload. in a way, they are already filtering out some emotions, the sheild just helps block the rest. and in some cases, dealing with the emotions without sheilding (for long periods of time) can cause a "delayed overload" which most empaths don't want to deal with later, so they sheild to prevent this. the sheilding is different from person to person, and some people are better at sheilding than others. empathetic sheilds are sort of like an air bag: you already have the airbag to protect you, but in some cases you need the seat belt's added protection to keep you safe and unharmed.
