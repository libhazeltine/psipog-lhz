Ok. Since there does seem to be a debate on the validity of this picture. I'll tell you guys the long version of the story.

Ok. Our group, Age, PazarX, Vedran, and myself were in #projection, we were just messing around, trying a game that PazarX wanted to try that none of us had done before. The game was pretty cool, it worked for all of us, our first ball that we sent to each other was hot, like hot enough you didn't want to hold it on yourself. This was all cool, but no offense to Vedran, he's a sensitive as a rock, so we decided we should try and get him to be more sensitive so we were throwing things at him, stuff like that. After a while I said that on AIM one of my friends should be getting on soon [he never did, lol], and that we should send a really cold ball to him and I would try and get his reaction, so we made the ball and passed it around. It finally got to Age. He had thrown something at my face earlier, so I was slightly annoyed with him, so I threw the psiball has hard as I could, I told him this before I did so. We aren't really paying attention to it at this time. We thought it was some fluke with the shield or something, but after it was still visible for about 10 minutes, we figured we had something. I had the only camera in the group so as I got my room to complete darkness, or near enough not to see my hands infront of me. I snapped a picture.
