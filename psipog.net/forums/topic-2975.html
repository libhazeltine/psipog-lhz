Here's a funny story that I tell sometimes...

Back in my college days, I worked part-time in this office where I basically helped out the secretaries there. Usually they didn't have any work for me, so I would sit alone in the copier room. I used that time to practice PK.

So one day, I had like a 4 hour shift, and practiced PK the entire time... playing with the pinwheel, and thinking about how it could work, and what's going on. Finally my shift was over, and I went downstairs to go to my next class. I was walking towards the door, when I thought, "Screw the pinwheel, I should just try moving big things!" Seeing the door ahead of me, I walked up to it while focusing on opening the door. To my surprise, the door actually opened! For about an entire second I was freaking out... until I realized that it was a motion sensor door .
