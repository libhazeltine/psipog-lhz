There are a few topics on flaring, but I'll tell you what I've gathered. First, you create a psi-ball. Then, you compress it until it is as dense as possible. Then, you put more energy ito it, and compress it down too.

Keep adding energy, and compressing, and when it is powerful enough, it MAY flare. Apparently, it's not always 100%, and the more you focus on flaring, the less chance you have.

Also, flaring is not essential to make a psi-pet. The energy used to continuously flare it would drain it of it's energy, and it would become useless pretty quickly. Since this is (presumably) your first psi-pet, how about sticking to the basics of creation, programing, and sustaining it.

Good luck! I hope your attempt at a psi-pet is succeful. I can't wait till I can make one.
