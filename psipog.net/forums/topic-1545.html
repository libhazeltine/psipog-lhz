Salutations,

Another subject that I have seen to be rarely touched on is Lucid Dreaming. So in an effort to try to bring all the topics into play i've formulated this post.

The question here is how often do you have lucid dreaming, the type of lucid dreaming, and if you wish to tell us about the lucid dream or rather one of them.

Now then I do have a tendancy to spread Lucid Dreaming into two topics, I dont know if they are official or not but it helps me atleast spread them apartl, my apoligies to the Staff if this is offensive just as the false Kinesis terms are. Though let me explain the difference in my point of view.



Lucid Dreaming
This is the state in which you are in total control of the dream and have realized it to be a dream, you still feel all the things with your normal senses as it does with any degree of lucid dreaming.


Semi-Lucid Dreaming
This is the state in which you are conscious in a dream but you are not aware it is a dream, all your senses are plugged into the dream though so you can still feel and touch things and get a response.
