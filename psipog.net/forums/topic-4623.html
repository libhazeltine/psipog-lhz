I've seen a few forums with this phpBB hack. It works like this: You type in [hide]hidden text[/hide] and it comes up with

Click to reveal hidden content

which you click on to show:

Click to reveal hidden content
hidden text

You can also configure what the hide tag says in place of "click to reveal hidden content" by typing [hide=hidden text]hello[/hide], and you can hide images ([hide][img]URL[/img][/hide]).

I think this would be a good idea for posting large images or movie spoilers. What do you say?
