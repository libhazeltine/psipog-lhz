I too have had, what I call, the PK Hangover.
When I first started I spent over an hour at the Psi wheel, and getting results too, but developed a minor headache. I didn't stop.

Stupid of me, really.

After the end of the session my minr headache had become a thick throbbing. I went to bed and figured I'd sleep it off. Boy was I wrong.

All through the next day my head felt like it was splitting open. I was also unable to do any PK becuase I couldn't focus. Again, stupid of me to try.

The moral of the story?
Don't overdo it. If you feel a headache coming on, STOP!
It may be due to overexersion, or a rise in blood pressure (brought on my the added brain activity), or even just a random 'my hear hurts' headache. Whatever it is though, if you feel it coming on you need to stop until it's completely gone.

And no taking an aspirin is not a good way to keep going.
