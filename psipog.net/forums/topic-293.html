Zemeon,

First off, I'm sure that if you can access psipogbb.net, you can also access wikipedia.com.

But in case there are unforseen circumstances that prevent you from accessing wikipedia, yahoo, or google, or perhaps any other search engine or relevant website on the Internet, I'll help you out.

H.P. Lovecraft is a prolific writer of horror/fantasy short stories. He's generated a terrific following due to his detailed and wildly inventive mythos regarding "the old gods", supernormal beings that lie dormant until they can rise up and conquer the known universe as we know it. Although dormant, these old gods still have a profound impact on people who either explore ancient cult lore or who, in one way or another, fall under their covert influence and this usually manifests in abnormal or psychotic behavior. My description will not do justice the wide body of Lovecraft's work. There are paperback books that contain dozens of his short stories. Also, I've found several websites that reprint his short stories, free for you to peruse at your leisure.
