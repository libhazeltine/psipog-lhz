This strange experience occured many times while I was in China doing a program called "GYLS" (look it up in your own time, but its completely unrelated to psionics). I would look over to someone and think about asking something, then they would turn around and ask me to repeat what I just said, or, in one case, actually answer my question. These happened most often in a place where it was hard to hear, which leads me to believe that in trying to hear what someone is saying, you actually listen telepathically as well. I think this is an interesting theory, and might be implimented to try and help people learn to communicate telepathically (mainly helps in reception I would hypothesize). Give me your views.
