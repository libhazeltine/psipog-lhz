Telepathy really isn't that hard, just takes a bit to get used to.
I only know basic telepathy, which is sending a message from one person to another. There are two main parts to it. Recieving, and Broadcasting.
For Broadcasting, you first need to make sure you have a link of energy between you and the Reciever. Next, figure out what you're going to send. Once you have your idea, think "towards" that person. Try to imagine your thoughts being projected outward, and make them loud and clear.
For Recieving, once again, make sure you have a link with the Broadcaster. Once they start, let "them think for you." Try to ignore your own ideas, just accept whatever thoughts come up; they're probably not yours.
One trick to have a better chance of getting a message through is to first make sure that both the Broadcaster and Reciever have their "shields" down. A sheild is a mental block that may not let out your thoughts, or take in others. Just try to relax and visualize the shield dissolving.
Hope this helps
