1. To see how far I can go.

2. I think there are many factors that limit us. For one we have to believe that we can do it, but regardless of belief the act of PK still consumes energy (imo). So even if you had perfect confidence you may not be able to move a giant boulder unless you have built up your "psychic muscles" enough to use the needed amount of energy.

I disagree with the whole "size matters not, unlearn what you have learned" concept and will only believe it when I see someone levitate and throw a truck (or equivalent) without considerable mental "weight training".
Simply put, I think that if we could live forever, then we could eventually build up enough psychic strength to move anything, but a 75 year life span just doesn't allow for enough training. What I don't know is how far we can go within our limited lifespan, and that is exactly what I would like to find out.

P.S. If there is any research that actually proves that size doesn't matter in PK, then I would love to be proven wrong.
