The only complaint I have is your definition for the MILD technique. While the MILD technique does involve repeating to yourself that you will have a lucid dream, the reason it's called Mnemonic is because of the memory exercises that prepare your mind for the lucid dream. The memory exercises are essential to the definition, in my opinion.

Perhaps change:

"This method is effectively repeating to yourself that you will have a lucid dream."

To:

"This method is effectively training your memory to prepare for a lucid dream, and then repeating to yourself that you will have a lucid dream before you fall asleep."

Other than that, thumbs up.
