I pick up random stuff from people's minds all the time and have to keep shields up if I want to shut it out.

I'm never able to shield all of it, so I always have the background noise of people's thoughts in my head, but it really is something you get used to after a while. I've been empathic/telepathic for as long as I can remember.

I do have some conscious control over my telepathy/empathy, even though I pick up random stuff all the time. I can focus in one one person and pick up their thoughs, or send to them. I really found the telepathy manual on this site very helpful. One of the things I had a lot of trouble with for a long time was figuring out which thought and emotions were mine and which were'nt, and alot of that is just knowing yourself better too.
