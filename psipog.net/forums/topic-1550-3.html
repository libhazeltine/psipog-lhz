Alright, here's my story.

I was sleeping one night, I think 4 years ago, and suddenly light was coming in through my eyes. I opened them and was staring right into the face of a reptile-looking humanoid. The skin was brown and scaley, it had slits for a nose, and the eyes were yellow, black and red with elongitated pupils. They almost seemed to glow.

I realized I was laying on a table or something, and this thing appeared to be around 6 to 7 feet tall. It had been using some tool (can't remember what) to mess with something underneath my heart. I was in intense fear, and apparently the alien got a bit freaked out aswell. It's eyes widened and I got the feeling that it wasn't expecting me to wake up. Suddenly there was a bright flash and I was back in my bed.

I explained this to myself as being a dream for around 3 years afterward. Then, about a year or two ago, I met someone who claimed to have seen the same thing, and described EXACTLY what I had seen. I know there are coincidences, so I looked into this further.

About 8 months ago I found a researcher by the name of David Icke who heavily investigates these creatures. He has talked to thousands of people who have seen the exact same thing I have, which I believe discredits it from being a hallucination/dream paralysis story. What I learned from his books is staggering...

These creatures are called "chitahuri" by african shamans, and are commonly referred to as "reptilians" by researchers of them. They came to the earth thousands (possibly even millions) of years ago. Today, they take up the main positions of power in the world and you can see this through their bloodlines.


David Icke (although commonly the target of ridicule) is, imo, a genius and his books and ideas explain EXACTLY HOW things like psychokinesis work among other things.

He touches on pretty much every topic you can think of, from conspiracies to the nature of the universe, and they all intertwine to create the world we live in today.

I would definately suggest picking up one of his more recent books (children of the matrix, infinite love is the only truth; everything else is illusion) at a local borders, although that choice is yours.


It's none of my business whether you believe me or not, but I had to give input on this thread since it is one that applies greatly in my life.
