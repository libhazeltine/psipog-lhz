This is for all you new psi-students trying to get this energy thing down. I didn't create this method, but read it off another website.

I know how hard it is trying to visualize that seemingly 'mystical' force called energy. The best way to simulate the sensation you will need in order to begin to move energy around is to do the following:

1) Sit down somewhere comfortable and quiet place (Duh! )
2) Take one of your arms and have the palm-side facing up
3) with your other index finger, begin to gently rub up and down the length of the arm in the same area over and over for about 1 or 2 minutes.
4) After about the 1 or 2 minutes have passed, take your finger off your arm and experience the "persistance of sensation"
5) Now try to recreate by visualizing that same sensation without having to run your finger on the arm.

Soon after you have got that down, you will begin to feel a "pressure" that isn't your heartbeat. Keep on creating that sensation and soon after that you'll feel the occasional tingle.

The tingle you felt is the stimulation of (certain) nerves in your body much like the eletric eel has specialized nerves for building and releasing a charge. I'm sure the eel can do more if it had the mental capacity, but the eel is working pretty much on instinct only.

Keep on going. Soon that tingle will have more tingles, and eventually you'll be able to stand a couple of hairs up on your arm/hand/whatever.

Hope this helps-
