Not really Psionics, but when I was young, about 4 or 5, I was playing in my room. Then, just in front of my window, there was this tall, black figure. It had a hood of some sort on it's head, and it had HUGE claws on it's hands(kinda like Wolverine's, I suppose). I ran SCREAMING to my parents downstairs. By the time we got back up, there was nothing there, and the window was open. The reason that the window being open was freaky, was because: 1. We never opened my window. It had no screen, so my parents never opened it when I was up in my room alone, for fear that I'd fall. 2. The window was practically painted shut anyway(or at least it seemed like it to me, as it was very heavy). I never could open that window.

My parents remember this, too(well, the me running to them, and the window being open part at least).

I won't name it a demon or anything(not here, at least) as that's borderline religious.




Something that WAS Psionic, though, happened when I was around 10 or 11. Me and my friends were fooling around, when someone brought up DBZ(you heard me....). We talked about how cool it would be if we could do DBZ-stuff. After a while, we began to pretend we were doing that stuff, and then when I did one, someone noticed that something actually went into my hands. And so it continued for several months like that, all of us creating(very basic) psi-balls(though, I'd be willing to bet the first dozen psi-balls were just our eyes playing tricks on us). Fairly quickly, we grew out of it. Except for me. I continued doing basic psi-balls(without the DBZ-edge, thank goodness), even starting some basic programming(making it fly around on a pre-dertermined path, making it explode, sticky, etc.). For several years, I just went on, practicing my(DBZ-free ) psi-balls.

About a year ago, I stopped it all together, figuring I'd just been tricking myself over the years. Six months ago, I got the bug again, but this time, to see if anything like what I'd experienced had happened to anyone else. That's when I found PsiPog. I read about manipulation, and it was EXACTLY what I did. From the feeling/movement of psi, right down to programming(I used to call conceptual programming "Blasting").

So, in an odd, fluff-to-non-fluff sort of way, I discovered Psi Manip. on my own.
