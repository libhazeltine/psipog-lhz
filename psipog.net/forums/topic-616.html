this technique was an accident but it apparently works for me.

if i want to get a temporary boost to your level of psi sensitivity, (just feeling. i have almost no psi vision unless i have my eyes closed) i first draw up the image that my hand is alive with purple fire (feel the flame and the heat). purple's just my colour of choice cause to me it represents power. once the fire is strong to my likeness, i then set the rest of my arm on fire and have it proceed to my shoulders and then to the rest of my body.

once i am incased in a fireball, i then start sending out tendrils of fire in every direction. and have them return. kind of like a sun doing sunspots. after about 1 minute of flaring, i extinguish the flame all at once. if done correctly your arms and face will tingle as if you have just got out of a cold shower into a desert. (odd explanation but it works for me.)

this sensitivity can last for as long as 30 mins. maybe longer. but you will be able to feel close proximity psi. i had a friend make and toss psi balls at me. i could feel them coming and caught them and sent them back.
the most use i got out of this is sensing my shields. i like to feel my psi working. i can rarely feel my shields but with this, they are as clear as day.

according to my friend, others can feel the fire tendrils. i've caused a bit of confusion in my class with this.

one word of advice. the technique works best if the flame is a flame of your favorite colour (or very similar)

mine's black but a black flame is very hard to visualize for me so purple works just fine.

questions? comments? ask away!


EDIT: i've had a comment that the tingly feeling also is combined with a feeling of weightyness or tiredness. (scuse my bad spelling.)
