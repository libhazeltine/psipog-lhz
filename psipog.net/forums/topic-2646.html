This topic comes up a lot. Believe it or not, magicians do things that you can't figure out. They DESIGN their tricks so you can't figure them out! So don't use the fact that, "Gee, I can't figure out this trick" to mean "it must be real!!!".

You are being fooled. That is the POINT of magic. To fool you.

Both David Blaine and Criss Angel are wonderful performers and entertainers. They are magicians. All of the tricks you see on TV actually do have an explaination that doesn't involve psionics. Even though you can't figure out what that explaination is, doesn't mean that it doesn't exist.
