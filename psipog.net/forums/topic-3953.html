You are creating a mindblock. Don't do that. You are being too attached to your ability to perform psionics and because of that attachment, there is fear of loss. Because of that fear of loss, there are thoughts of fear with a potential power of becoming a mindblock. Negative thinking and negative programming of the mind is what results. Thus, you block yourself from performing that what you desire so badly.

Try to let go of your attachment to psionic abilities, and soon these mindblocks will fade because they lose power. You feed them no longer with energy in the form of self-doubt and negative thinking. Now you say you are not doubting yourself, but somehow I doubt that. If this really is not the case, then there must be another reason why it doesn't work anymore. But it doesn't make sense to lose those abilities just like that. Only your own mind can stop you from doing so. Goodluck finding out what the problem is.
