So last night was attempt #5 or 6. (I've practiced more, but several months ago and recently started practicing again) I got to the point where most (all but a part of my neck) was completely numb. My heart would beat at the intensity as if I were doing a lot of stressful cardiac exercise. This isn't normal when I am just laying in my bed! Are these the vibrations discussed in how-to-OBE articles? I noticed the heart beat slowed down when I cleared my mind, but I can literally only hold it for a fraction of a second.

Are these normal effects of an OBE? Am I close to having one at all? Any advice?
