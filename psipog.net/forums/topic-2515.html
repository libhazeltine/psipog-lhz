I think this is because you are still concentrating (takes effort), instead of meditating upon the object(effortless, automatic continues stream of cognition towards the object). If you look at Nina her vids, you'll see that she looks very stressed and her way of doing tk seems very unhealthy to me. My guess is that she concentrates too hard, instead of letting the attention flow continues towards the object. That way your heart won't get excited, instead you will become even more relaxed and enter a deeper state of mind.; hence the body will relax more as well. Excitement is not so good for tk, because then it is likely that you are using adrenaline. Real concentration comes effortless and automatically.
