Ok, I've been practicing pk for 2, 3, maybe 4 days... but not that much, just thought it would be good to start practicing since I could create psiballs. Well I know it isn't much, but I was finally able to wiggle the psiwheel! Just sort of, tilt it back and forth. Shake it a little. As soon as this happened, my heart leapt and my confidence grew, which I think opened the doorway to me being able to do it even more. I admit I was skeptic at first of course, but now I'm amazed. I covered my nose and mouth with my shirt, and afterwards I thought it might have been several things. I was resting my hands on the desk, so I thought since I was so excited by doing it I was shaking it. Shook it a small amount without really "focusing" and it didn't move at all, unless you had some kinda, microscope. I thought maybe I was breathing through my shirt, debunked that idea luckily. I thought my small hand movements might just have enough wind power to move it, and nope for that one too. My AC was off, too. I'm just so happy right now I'm shaking, gonna go to bed though.
