(I was advised to put this post here since it will eventually be advertising a community)

I know its a little ways off before Pog actually closes, but I wanted to start getting people thinking on where we're going to go. I think creating any more new communties is illogical, we should go to either one or a couple of the existing communities and bolster them.

After all a ton of small communities can do a lot less good than a couple big ones. Everyone agrees that part of what was special about Pog was its size, we should try to preserve that by adding our talents to another medium sized community and making that place special through their current base plus our added base. Also its a lot easier and time saving to go to somewhere that's already established than going through the joys of starting a new community.

Anyways, if we can get enough people interested in leaving as a group I wanted this to become more of an actual commitee, where we'll eventually vote on the options and the bulk of us will end up in one or two places.

As far as I see it here are the main canadates in terms of what would be semi-compatible to Pog - (I'll edit the post if someone thinks of more):
