I'm a terribly uncoordinated person when I'm trying to throw something. Just over a month ago, I was with some friends up at a cabin, and there was several feet of snow still around. I sat with my girlfriend and chatted, exhausted, and tried to throw snowballs at a tree. In trying, I couldn't hit it at all. My snowballs kept going to the left or the right.

When I stopped trying, relaxed, and didn't even pay attention to the snowballs I was throwing, I realized I was hitting every one dead center.

Perhaps telepathy is the same sort of thing. Your subconscious is really good at it, but when your consciousness tries to take the reins and do it intentionally, it fails miserably. Of course, there are still ways to make it happen. You just have to "ask" your subconscious to do it for you.

Or at least that's what I gather. My own moments of accidental telepathy have been enlightening, but trying to make them happen has never brought me any closer to recreating it.
