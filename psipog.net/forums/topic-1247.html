Hello everyone,

When I first started to learn/practice PK, I did keep it to myself. As time went on, I had gotten better and better. I slowly decided "Hey, I can do this and no one else can, why keep it a secret?" and started to amuse people during class and at work.

Last summer, students personally asked me to start to teach them how to practice PK. I gladly accepted and started to have practice sessions with the members after school. As time went on, more and more people started to come and wanted to learn.

A teacher of mine approached me and asked me about this. After telling him, he asked me "Why don't you start a club?" I replied "Great idea!". To make a long story short, I started to teach PK in a classroom every Wednesday. I did so for around a year until I needed to stop due to lack of time with school and work.

So no, I did not keep it a secret. I started to teach students willing to learn. I was thinking of beginning teaching again, however I really don?t have the time. Perhaps next year in college, I'll start something similar.

Take care.
