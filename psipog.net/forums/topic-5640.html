I found a link to this site the other day and I'm glad I did. Months ago I saw a show on TV where this guy did a bunch of tricks. Real or not, one thing he did was fold a piece of paper and put it on a pin and cover it with a glass. Suggested viewers try it, so I did. I tried it for weeks........nothing. Found this site last night and the psiwheel minus the glass. WOW. Within moments it had moved and slowly spun. I don't know if it was removing the glass that also removed some mental thingy or the rest from trying. I've been trying to roll a toothpick with no luck, but a tiny scrap of tissue suddered a couple times when I tried to move it today.

Some people seem to ask what other thought and what not so I'll answer before you ask. I read a NI's articles and found them useful. I get much better spin when I imagine the giddy feeling of success.

Along with my story I have the ritual question. I spent a lot of time during the day trying to move that tiny scrap of tissue. About noonish I really started to feel sick, kinda like being car-sick but not exactly. This seemed centered just over my stomach rather than in it. I stopped and it sort of went away, but when I got home from work I started playing with the wheel and the feeling returned. Am I pushing too hard to fast or is it probably badly prepared cafe. food? If it's me letting my excitement get the better of my judgement I guess I can pull back, but I'm just SOOO riding a wave right now.
