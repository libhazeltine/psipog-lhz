I presented my evidence in this thread: http://www.psipog.net/forums/viewtopic.php?t=1394

But you're lazy. You're not going to click that link. So here's a summary.

I was in middle school. Around thanksgiving time in 2004, when I was in eighth grade, was when I found the wonderful world of the paranormal.

There were a few people in middle school who seemed like they could be psionicists, but it wasn't like, "OMG I think there's another psionicist in the school!" No, more like "Ha, that person seems like they could actually be a psionicst, but there's no way in hell."

But I realized that it wasn't just personality that made the case. It was physical evidence. Mostly mind reading. Click the link if you want examples. And unfortunately, I realized this at the end of ninth grade. I never saw any of the "suspects" again after eighth grade.

Now what? I eventually sent a letter to one of them, actually my best friend in middle school. Yes, my suspicions were true. All too much. But he wasn't the only one. There are five others, according to him. But he's not saying much.

But I was always too determined to find the truth. I asked him who they were, but he didn't answer, of course. I told him I wanted to know so I could find out if any of them are in my high school.

I had no evidence at all for anyone in my high school. But I'm always on the lookout.

Now, I don't practice psionics much, that's not my job. But sometimes I just know the answer to something inexplicably. Sometimes I will know exactly what someone is going to say. It doesn't happen often, and it never actually matters. But this time it did.

I knew that someone in my school was one of them. I don't know how, I just do. Not necessarily someone from my middle school, just another psionicist.

Last Saturday, 10-28, was when I think I figured it out. I was lying in my bed, and I closed my eyes. I saw a bunch of pieces of paper with a single word written on each one, all jumbled together, overlaping, unreadable. But there was one piece of paper that was apart, the other papers moved aside for it. It was the only word I read, and I only saw it for a second.

It was a name. The name of a person I know at school. A person who went to my middle school. A person who seemed like they could be a psionicist.

Now, my logic is sure to be biased, maybe the word just looked like a name, maybe this isn't the person, who knows. But at least I have something to work with. Now I just have some research to do. What do you all think about this?
