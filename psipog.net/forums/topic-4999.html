I have never believed in any sort of superhuman, psychic power. Most members of my family are very religious; in fact, my father is an evangelical pastor. It is a begrudgedly accepted part of family lore that our now-deceased batty old great-aunt had some sort of psychic connections, having predictive powers over things like the deaths of two of my cousins, in addition to some form of telepathic power she used to mentally communicate with her sisters. Although our family writes off psychic power, we have never bothered to explain old Aunt LaVerne.

She lived upwards of age ninety, and as she was already in her mid-eighties when I was born, I rarely met her. On one of the rare occasions that I did, at a family Christmas gathering at age six, she clasped my shoulders and stared ferociously into my eyes, telling me in a hollow, ghostly voice that I would have the most powerful mind our family had ever seen, putting a ten-year timetable on my "awakening." The event was traumatic, but we wrote it off as old crazy LaVerne making up stories again.

I put the incident out of my mind, and had a fairly normal childhood; with a couple of minor exceptions, there were almost no unnatural experiences. All of that changed about a week ago. Last Monday was my sixteenth birthday. Ever since then, I've had a mindblowing array of experiences, all dealing with psychokinesis. I have moved a number of objects without touching them, such as cell phones, glasses, and on one occasion, a dictionary, and I seem to have very little control over these experiences. None of this makes any sense to me.

I did a little bit of research into my family history, mainly courtesy of the older "family historian" matriarchs, and found that a number of my long-dead relatives, a large clan of Chicago/St. Louis Swedes, have had psychic histories. Considering this, it seems that either many of the members of my family are insane, or my family genuinely has psychic capabilities.

It has been only a week, and I am already starting to feel more control over the objects that I move. Pardon me if I am somewhat ignorant of psychic terms; I have been a skeptic all my life. I want to know if there are any other cases like this; I want to know how to control these "powers", if they exist; I want to know if I am really crazy. I am still very skeptical of this entire process- ideas like "astral planes" and "constructs" seem very far-fetched to me- but I would still like your help.

Thanks,
-H.M. Peters
