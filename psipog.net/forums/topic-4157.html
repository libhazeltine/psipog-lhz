My advice: don't overdo it. Instead, try figuring out when are you most comfortable with doing it, and try to figure out what kind of feelings are you experiencing while practicing. Your feelings vary from day to day and so does your ability to control them so your PK practice is different from day to day. But don't worry once you reach a certain state you will be able to recall the same feeling every day every time.

When you lose passion you lose everything.
