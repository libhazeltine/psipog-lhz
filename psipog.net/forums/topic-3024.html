Hi everyone
I quit my psi training almost 4 months ago because I just didn't have enough time to continue.
Today I got addicted again and started with making psiballs I didn't have any problems with those.
After that I tried the psiwheel it didn't move at all the first time just some little shivers and twitches.
I decided to make an alufoil psiwheel because I heard it's easier to handle.
Well the result was better I could move it but I couldn't control it at all.
It spun to the right, to the left, faster, slower, it even jumped off the needle once but I just couldn't control it.

It's been about 5 hours since that and I still didn't progress.

However the first time I used a (paper) psiwheel I could control it instantly I didn't need any training I even made a video of it.

I'd be glad to receive any hints/help on how to solve this problem.

Edit : Sorry but it seems like I solved the problem...
You'll never guess how - MUSIC was the key to success.
I couldn't concentrate in silence I just had too many toughts in my mind.
Therefore I concentrated on the beat of the song I was listening to and it worked, I went into a trance like state and it just worked.
I can't really control it yet but I managed to spin it to the right for almost 8 Minutes (that's exactly the duration of the song).
If you have similar problems, give it a try.
