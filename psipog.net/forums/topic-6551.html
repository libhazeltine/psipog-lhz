Well, as all such things go, it depends on the situation. However, I would take out the part about "weaken what it can't," since that could hypothetically cause you to mentally associate failure with the shield, and you can guess the possible results of that. You could, however, word it like this: "Weaken all negative energy that comes this way, and absorb all the energy it can." At first glance, it may seem like I just said what you said, but notice how I didn't use words like "can't..."
