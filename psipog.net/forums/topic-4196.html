I know you have good intentions, but this idea is very bad.

Nine times out of ten, a newbie will come along and feel the slightest prick on the back of his neck, and announce to the world that he's being psychically attacked and needs immediate assistance. Nine tenths of the remaining one tenth actually have a real attack... but the attacker has no clue what they're doing, and isn't accomplishing anything other than making the target paranoid. So 1% of the people who claim to need help actually might need help. In those cases - that person probably did something to piss off the attacker. I'm not saying they deserve being attacked, but chances are neither person is "innocent".
