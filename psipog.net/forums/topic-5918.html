This is definately weird. I think you should think about your dream. I guess dreams are something that your sub-cunscious makes up, just to reflect stuff or maybe to tell you something too. Of what i'm sure is that you can learn from your dreams. So in your dream you had to find the right way, else it wouldn't stop. Seems pretty obvious for me. It's like in many life-situations, too. You can't go on, until you've found the right way.
I can understand you pretty good because i had similar dreams. They wouldn't stop until i found out what i was supposed to do.

After some time the dreams stop or they evolve. Try to learn from them.
It's what i would do.
