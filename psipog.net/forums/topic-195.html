From my observations your body is somewhat like a cup inside of a cup with a valve between them that can open and close. If you use none of your ?stored? energy then both part of the larger cup are filled with much heavier better quality energy. While if you use a lot of it the upper part of the cup gets drained of the quality energy and you can meditate to fill it back up, but only with regular psi. The problem is that your body can?t use normal Psi to function, it can?t keep your heart beating. So that?s why there is the section part of your cup. It stores the energy that keeps you alive. If you needed all your larger cup?s worth of energy, you would die. I believe that the lower portion of your cup contains the same kind of energy in the upper part but it?s used to run your brain, your heart. While the energy in your upper part of your cup is used to run your immune system and your muscles and such. So overall You just have to make sure that you don?t push yourself too hard beause you can hurt yourself.

The uses for this technique are as follows (as far as I know):
1. Healing ? you can pull this kind of energy to wounds to make them heal faster.
2. Body strengthening ? you can use this energy and pull it into your muscles to make
them stronger and more efficient.
3. Charging your body ? Not only do you make your muscles more efficient but you
Bring your whole body to a more efficient state.
4. Dulling pain ? self explanatory

Downfalls ? the problem with using this energy is that you don?t have it afterward. Its pretty easy to understand. You are basically using your psi to make yourself stronger for a shorter period of time. So while your strength goes up, your energy goes down. That is the downfall. Warnings ? DO NOT push yourself too far. I?ve gotten very sick for pushing myself too far. Make sure you read the beginning first.

The technique ?
As I said before the idea is that you are pulling the energy that your body stored into your muscles.

Step 1. The basic thing to do is to meditate until you can feel ever muscle in your body (not the heart but like your arm muscles and neck muscles etc.) Don?t feel them with your hand, or flex them. You need to have your mind go along each muscle and feel with your psi. You have to feel the flow of psi move throughout your body and touch each muscle. Once you?ve done this you have to try and feel all of your muscles at once. Understand the flow of psi in and out of your body. Understand how your body works. After this is completed you can go onto step two.

Step 2. The next simple thing is being able to feel where the energy comes from. The best way and most efficient way is to feel the energy coming out of the base of your back/spine. Feel the energy relax and flow out through your body. Feel the energy fill you.

Step 3. Try to control this energy. Try and direct the energy to your arm. Feel it build up in your arm. Put it into your finger and feel how it feels as though your finger has a better feeling. This is how you heal your body. You let the energy sooth over a wound or a tired body.

Step 4. Now try and Flex your arm and try to pull that energy into the flexed arm. It?s hard but imagine it entwining with your muscles, making them stronger. Feel you arm getting stronger. Push as much energy as you can into your arm. Then stop pushing the energy and feel how much stronger it is. It should feel less pain in the arm and it should be able to do more than you could before. This feeling will wear away if you do not maintain it.

Step 5. You could try and do this technique and push the energy throughout all your muscles while you flex to make your whole body faster and stronger. This is hard and dangerous.

Step 6. If you did the prior step. Take the time to let the energy sooth back over your as if it was a warm blanket comforting your tensed muscles. You need to relax your body.

Step 7. Is bringing your body to a higher state. You need to relax and then feel the energy surge throughout your body. Do not flex your body as you did in step 5 but surge through and awaken you. This is hard to imagine but its almost as if you are entwining your psi with your muscles but without the flexing and ?all over your body-.

Step 8. If you combine step 5 and 7 you will have fully charged your body. Its easier to do step 7 before 5 if you are going to do them together.

Step 9. Meditate and feel the energy sooth you again. Relax.

Step 10. Eat and don?t try and do something too strenuous today if it was your first time.
