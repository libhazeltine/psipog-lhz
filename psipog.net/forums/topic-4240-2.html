I stopped beleiving the whole "you die in your dream you die in real life" myth long ago. Simply put, goign through the experience is the best proof you can get

I've died at least FOUR times in my dreams. Three of them QUITE gruesome and all tactile sensations simply too vivid for words (very creepy)...

The first time i died while dreaming, It was from a sawed-off shotty blast to the face. My whole head was literally obliterated... I FELT the initial impact and tearing of my skin and skull, but as that was happening i felt myself sucked out of my body into an "ethreal" body, invisible to everyone else, but i could fly around...


The second time I died, I was ripped apart by Velociraptors (dont ask... long story ) but basically, i'd been pinned up against a cement wall that i juuuust couldnt get over before one nabbed my leg, pulled me down, and proceeded to feast on me with the 2 others in the hunting pack that was chasing me and some friends down... It was fantasically gory, blood pouring out of my body, the intricate detailing of my ripped innards being tossed around and munched upon... Upon this death i merely floated above my body after the first four or five bites finally pulled the life from my physical body. A strange feeling indeed... I'f you've played any sort of multiplayer first person shooter games, kind of liek the effect when you die in those, you just float up out of your body and spin around slowly, looking in the direction of yoru corpse....


The third time i died in my dream, was actually in a dream-state re-creation of Team Fortress Classic (this is one of said first-person-shooters). I was literally participating in a "real-life" version of TFC, and i died about 4 times in this dream alone, each time respawning to go forth and fight some more, lol.

The fourth and last time i recall dying in a dream, was very detailed... I was a prisoner of sorts in some foreign dungeon that was apparently up high somewhere... large, light-tan stone walls about 30ft deep with a man-made leaf-canopy covering the top so as to mask the presence of the dungeon. At one point i was laying down, and a strange, invisible presence basically gave me an ultimatum: Kill myself or be killed. I opted to kill myself, and a small, green blade appeared in my hands. i tried to slit my throat and it just couldnt penetrate. After a whiel i was given a large, insanely sharp butchers-cleaver. That worked. I felt the blade sink in across my throat, i felt the warm blood run down my neck, i felt it fill my throat and start suffocating me. I felt the adrenaline kick in, i felt my body tense up and try to cling on to life, and as it was all coming to darkness i felt/saw a white, angelic presence over my body, it whispered something to me and then i could no longer see, all there was was a speck of light in the distance, and i hurdled towards it at an alarmingly fast rate, and just as i almost "hit" it, i woke up flying out of my bed (was laying on back, and sprung forward as if i had literally "hit" my body at 100mph).

Very strange indeed.

I have died many a times in my dreams, and has yet to have had an ill effect on me

As far as the waking up just befor falling-death: yeah i've done that countless times... If i were to count near-deaths like that i could raise my in-dream-death-count to something in the double-digits
