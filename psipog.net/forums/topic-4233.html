The general consensus seems to be that empathy is easier in the sense that most psions have it or start with that ability. I'm not much of a fan of empathy because the simple ability to read body language can delude a person into thinking they're empathetic. In high school, I've been able to predict the break-ups or hook-ups of my classmates solely by scrutinizing their behavior. Depression is also something that a person can easily pick up on since it has a pretty blatant set of behavioral displays.

I'm not saying you're not empathic, just letting you know that you could be really good at understanding body language and not know it. Discerning the two is a matter of time and practice.
