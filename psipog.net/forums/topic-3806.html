That magnetic feeling, like it's opposing the two hands, is NOT a psiball. When you leave your arms sitting like that for just a minute, it feels like something is there. In a psiball you are looking for tingling feelings and heat, or coldness. That magnetic feeling happens to anyone that keeps their arms in a position for so long, try moving your arms, usually that magnetic feeling will go away.
