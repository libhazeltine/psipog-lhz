I'm pretty sure that depends on how sensitive to psionics your friend is. The more sensitive, the easier it is for him/her to pick up. Does your friend practice psionics at all? If so, there's a good chance he will pick it up. But don't think that by just thinking at him telepathy will work. It takes practice, and time. You may need to start off simple with practicing shapes and colors...then by the time you get good at that, you may be able to send thoughts with more ease, enough to which your friend will pick it up (if he is sensitive enough.)

You could also ping him, which is sending something simple ("hey!", maybe a pinch on the neck, ect...) with a burst of energy...We need some more information in order to answer this question fully.
