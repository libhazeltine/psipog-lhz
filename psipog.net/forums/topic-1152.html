Here are some exercises that I thought of to practice your psi skills.

Key =
1 - Newbi
2 - Advanced
3 - Are you human?

Level 1

(These are for people still practicing psiballs and moving toothpicks.)

Vibrating a guitar string

Set off a mouse trap. (Quite simply, put psipressure on the trigger mechanism.)

Moving a cat toy. (One of the wool mouses.)

Flicking a lightswitch

Level 2

(For those who have completed those above.)

Opening a pair of scissors

Open to a random page in the dictionary, put your finger on a random spot, then guess the word.

Trace a bottlecap on a piece of paper, then practice moving it to the tracing. You could do this with eyes closed, but that's a level 3 type thing.

Level 3

Open a window or door

Type letter on Microsoft word without using hands or keyboard

***For bonus points make me lunch lol
