PS: To psi_ninja

im 22 years old now (just turned 22 today in fact). And I considered the 12 year thing might be in regards to just myself. In fact, when i was 10 years old (maybe 11, not positive) I encountered some kind of entity while i was swimming at my cottage. Everyone else had already gotten out of the water and started heading up to the cottage, and I was playing in thwe seaweed crib (catching minnows with a net. I used to do it all the time at that age).

While I was underwater, I heard this EXTREMELY high pitch screeching sound. At first, I figured it was a boat (boat engines sound very high pitched underwater) but then I noticed it was really loud and that the pitch was consistantly altering somewhat. I lifted my head out of the water and looked around for a boat and saw nothing, and I noticed I could hear a huge rushing sound (of water) above the surface, and looked toward the sound.

Hurtling towards me at a fairly high speed was this big thing. Ever see a submarine when it first surfaces? all you can see is a rounded off blast of water, before it clears the sub. Well, that's what this was..... except that there was nothing underneath the rounded-offness of whatever was surfacing from the water, and the water didn't clear it, it just stayed the way it was, churning this way and that. It was moving toward me, quite quick, and I was paralyzed. I had no clue what to do.

Then it smashed into me. I felt a weird hard/slickness rub up against my body at that moment, and I was sent sprawling around in the water, my head nearly hitting the rocks of the seaweed crib. I literally turned upsidedown underneath the water, doing like a 720 spin before i could regain control of myself. It was still screeching, really really loud, underneath the water. I looked over in the direction it had gone. I could still see it, a little ways off. Suddenly, it turned out toward the center of the lake and submerged.

To this day, I still think about this event. It's not even the only time i've encountered an entity during daily activities... it's just the only one where that entity actually came into physical contact with me.

So, chew on that one, peeps. I gotta tell yas though, i don't care if any of you believe me. Those who do believe me can rest easy knowing they are NOT believing a lie, though.
