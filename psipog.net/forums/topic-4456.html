K, to be frankly I don't dream much at all.
When I was little I had a classical falldream (firstperson) where I were to be smashed at the end of the lion king cliff.
But as I fell closer and closer my view switches to a fly on the wall and I see myself land on my feet and walk away, dream over, nomore dreams for three years.
Then after about three years, not sure if it's exact, but I begin dreaming again, like I know that I'm dreaming, and it's kinda funny cuz it's like you're watching a movie or something, dont remember the dream unfortunatly.

And this other time, which I dont know was dreaming or anything I was going downstairs to take a leak, I was prolly 50% asleep or something cuz I kept seeing my mom and her friend in bedrobes jumping out from several corners and trying to scare me, the corners was about like 4-8cm wide, so noone could've hidden there, but I surprisingly just didn't care about getting scared or anything, since I knew the mind was playing tricks with me, I guess it was like a hallucination of some sort.

I've had lots of smaller experiences, but not that worth to mention.
But I still remember one lucid dream if you may call it that.
I had a dream where I went back into the past and to the future in an elevator, and to different destinations in a jiff, I was aware it was a dream. but before I woke up I just like got sucked up into space somehow and went to some star figures. dont remember more.
