It's that simple, but it seems complicated and vague because there's no explanation. But not everything does have an explanation, this being one. There undoubtedly is one for this, but until we learn it, we don't have one. Why don't we question how we move our arms? The answer lies in impulses from the brain to another place, but we don't question it; we just do it. So, quit thinking about all the complexities involving psionics. Let them go for now. Get your energy and send it to the object. And then you just move it. The more you practice, the more you will see what we're talking about.
