Well, yeah, it DOES depend on the scenario. But then again, nothing's perfect.

And also, there are countless variables for just about every major incident. Using Judgement Day from Terminator, destroying the labs was one big variable that got changed, but the people who crafted the SkyNet that caused Judgement Day in T3 were another big variable that wasn't changed. If that variable had been changed, another one would've caused Judgement Day.

We've been using simple examples to convey the point, but complicated scenarios (such as Judgement Day from Terminator) are complex problems with many, many variables. Some variables can be changed without affecting the result, while others will delay the result, and still others will stop it altogether. It really does depend on the scenario though.
