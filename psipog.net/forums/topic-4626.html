9__16 wrote:
I can make it go one way with no effort at all by just puting my hand by it but i dont know how to stop it or reverse it


If it's only moving one way with your hand next to it, chances are it isn't psychokinesis. Static electricity or warm air currents or little invisible winged creatures, or whatever other explanation for a psychokinetic effect besides psychokinesis could be at play.

The best you can do with the psiwheel, insofar as proof to yourself and proof to others of psychokinesis is "intelligent control". Intelligent control is simply being able the move the psiwheel in a manner and direction that is consistent with your "will". This can be moving it one way, and then stopping it and making it go another direction, as well as making it stop completely and keeping it very still, and then making it spin as fast as possible.

I say keep at it. Focus on moving it in one direction, and then the other. Remember, you're developing a non-developed ability. Neurological or mental or physical (or all of them) transformation/adaptation is required and it usually doesn't happen immediately. The only sure way to make it happen is through repeated practice. Good luck, 9__16.

Also, I've noticed that when I have my hand near the psiwheel, I put little to no 'oomph' into spinning and it spins, and I almost always feel a 'cold breeze' feeling on my hand, be it energy or actual wind..
