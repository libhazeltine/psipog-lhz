Anyway, I was just in this dream (didn't know it was a dream at the time) and for some reason there was a small hole at the bottom of my door. I look through the hole and can see down the hallway but then I close my eyes and notice I can kinda still see. Now, the only few times I DID have an OBE, they always started like this. I never got them when I tried to do it (like putting each body part to sleep) but just out of no where I would be looking out at my room when I can feel my eyes are shut. I *think* it was like the start of an OBE, IDK, I woke up directly after.

So, while im in this dream I begin to see with my eyes shut. I tried to do whatever I could to make this one last because the other times I would just wake up (as you can tell, I didn't know this was a dream) so I try and focuse on what im looking at. As soon as I do that, I get this incredibly weird feeling over my entire body, every part kinda feels like its numb with tingling vibrations kinda waving through. All the sound stops and I hear something kinda like a voice. It sounded like someone struggling to slowly breath in... thats the best way I can describe it. The sound stops and the sound of my fan kicks in. Then I realize im laying face first into a pillow (I had woken up).

I was just wondering if anyone can identify what happend, it was probably one of the weirdest things thats happend to me. It felt kind of eerie though... almost like I shouldn't have been able to do that or something... idk.
