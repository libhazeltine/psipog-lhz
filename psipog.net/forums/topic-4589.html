A few months ago my boyfriend complained about pain in a specific area of his body (I think his hand). Although I had never tried to heal before I decided to help him feel better. So I drew energy from the earth and began to pass that energy through my hands and to the area. I concentrated on the energy using repetitive thoughts that the energy would be healing/positive and that the area would be healthy. It worked and since then I have helped him, used this process on myself, and my mother (a fellow psion and the only one I know of besides my boyfriend). It works for various applications like making bruises or cuts heal faster using the same basic technique.

Here's an outline of the basic pattern I use:

1. Find energy source yourself, a tree, etc.

2. Channel the energy to your hand if the area can be directly touched by it. If you can't hover your hand over the area then just focus the energy to the afflicted area. I find it easier to use my hand but technically it makes no difference.

3. Program the energy like a construct to heal or perform whatever biological task. The difference is that I make the energy flow like a river slowly into the source and keep track of it.

I hope this information helps people.
