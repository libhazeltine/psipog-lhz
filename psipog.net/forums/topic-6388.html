Well, this morning I had my first OBE (partial I guess)!

It's kinda ironic, me and my friend were talking on the bus yesterday and he said he had gotten his first OBE that morning. So I said that I hadn't really tried achieving it that much but that I'd start get more serious this weekend. Who'd have thought that I'd be getting one the day after?

Anyways, it was like this, I just somehow woke up from a dream or something I can't remember, and everything was all vibrating and I heard a loud ringing sound. I could see the TV that is in front of my bed, but it was like blurry, maybe like a double image even. Everything of this happened in just a few seconds and I somehow just knew I was going out. Then I felt my hands going through the blankets and I got freaked out, and it was like everything went into my body and I woke up. I sat up, and after a short pause I said out loud "Damn, I should have continued!". Ah well, I hope I'll go out completely tonight No I know I will
