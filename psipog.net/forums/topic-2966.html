My best advice is to stop expecting it to work (unless you use the Gnomish method as I do). If you expect results you're not open enough to possibility of another outcome - something less that total and complete success. I find when I simply 'try' instead of expect, I do much better.

I would also recommend moving to another object as stated before, it may help you get over the bump.

You may also be drifting from your initial breakthrough technique - this can happen because you focus too much on the elated feeling you get when the wheel moves. I recommend going back to basics - reread the articles, be confident that the wheel will move, and stop beating yourself up when it doesn't.

Relax and have fun!
