I noticed something in the corner of my eye. It was a plant, and it seemed to be moving a little bit. When I looked at it, I got an intense pressure in the middle of my forehead. I felt connected to it instantly.

I made it turn left about an inch, right about an inch, and the most significant of all, I made it move up about an inch, and alot of the leaves seemed to go up as if they were under an air current. I could see the slack in the rope as I lifed it up. I turned it and lifted it a couple of times. This is after a session of trying to move a small crystal with no success and moving the psi-wheel with limited success.

It was.... weird.

I don't know if the pressure should be that strong, and even though I've stopped influencing the plant, or so I think, the pressure is still there. This is cool, but unsettling at the same time. I do, however, consider it a step forward in my development as a psion, and ultimately, a better person who is in control of their mind.

EDIT: About 5 minutes later and the pressure is mostly gone. Although, for a second there, I was focusing on using micro-pk to try and make a web page load faster, there was this fairly thick line, offset a little to the left of the center of the monitor, and it repeated from the bottom to the top. I can't seem to see it anymore.

EDIT 2: I've been making much progress on the psi-wheel today. I am pleased with my progress. You can't try too hard, and that's what I was doing. I was focusing so hard and trying to make it move. I kinda just let it move, if you know what I mean. If your sub-c is in control of it, then it is a very subtle mechanism. I'll learn how to control it yet.
