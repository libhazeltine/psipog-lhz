Ok, here it is......

STEP 1
Stand up, take a few deep breaths and relax

*NOTE: If you want, before step 2 you can absorb energy from something else.


STEP 2
Picture a ball of energy around your bellybutton area spinning around and getting denser and denser and denser and denser and denser and denser.......and denser. Just about as dense as you can make it.


STEP 3
Now shoot some energy from your dense ball of energy out the bottom your feet, and picture it coming out like how energy comes out of a rocket booster. And picture yourself slowly lifting off the ground.


STEP 4
To get higher after you've lifted off the ground just shoot more energy through the bottom of your feet. The higher you go the more energy you'll have to shoot. Do this until that once dense ball of energy is gone or until you notice that you are starting to lower to the ground because that ball of energy you formed earlier is almost gone. And once it IS gone, you'll land(or fall)to the ground. Obviously.



Well, that's MY personal technique for levitation, I hope it helps you to levitate or to find your own technique. And as I said earlier, you can absorb enrgy from the sun or something if you don't think your own energy alone can do it. I personally like to use my own energy, so absorbing energy from something else to give you a boost may make it a little easier, but I wouldn't know since I've never did that. Well, anyway have fun with it. ^_^
