I had this same problem when I started out, but I found a few ways around it. The simplest one is to talk to yourself. I know, sounds crazy.

If your falling asleep while your relaxing your body, talk out loud (not to loud though, or people will think your mad), and even better is to talk to your body and walk it through going to sleep (I find this works very well).

If your falling asleep while your body is asleep and talking out loud isn’t a viable option, as it would wake up your body, talk to yourself in your head. You can even use this to re-enforce the idea of going OOB to your subconscious (for example; talk to yourself about going OOB).

I would write more, but I really don’t have the time right now, so good luck and I hope this made sense.
