I hate all the different telepathy terms for the same thing. For what we're talking about here, these are the words I've came across to describe it:

hacking
scanning
probing
mind-reading
telepathy
linking

wow. I'd say hacking is reading thoughts from other people's links.
scanning is reading surface thoughts
probing is the right word for this, searching through memories and/or surface thoughts
mind-reading is the same as scanning
telepathy is all of the above (and below)
linking is making an energy path between the target and you.

I've heard scanning being used to describe searching through memories, like probing, many times. So I just wish that there was an article that officially defined all of the telepathy terms.
