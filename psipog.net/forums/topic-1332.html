I have been practicing TK for 10 days now for about 10 minutes a night. On my 9th day I am almost 100% sure I actually moved the psi wheel (with no hands) via TK. But it was weird. It seemed like it had a mind of its own yet seeming to move coinsidentially in the desired direction. And something else strange was that I have could only do it for a minute or two and then I had to relax for a few minutes to do it again (I didn't feel consciously tired though; maybe my subconscious need a little time to take it all in?). And when it went in the wrong direction I just kind of visualized it stopping while I willed it to stop at the same time and the wheel just stopped instantly like it slammed on the brakes. Then I would try to make it spin the other way and it would wobble a little as if acknowledging my command and would proceed in the desired direction. And when I got "tired" I visualized energy forming near a corner and pushing it and that worked too. The next day I tried it in the morning and it took a little longer but eventually it moved violently as if I had created a gust of wind making me believed I had actually performed aerokinesis. And when I tried to do it in front of my brother I couldn't do it! But then again I had to hold my breath which made it hard (I usually just breath with my hand over my nose or breath in such a way as not to disturb the wheel). Then that night (I have to practice covertly!) I tried again with no success. Then I thought I would try the hand method. It worked better than I expected. It moved faster than usual and with a lot more reliability. I basically moved my hands very slowly (there's no way that could have created wind) in a given direction which caused the wheel to spin in that direction or the opposite direction. I even tried combining that with the no-hands technique to stop it or influence more and that worked quite well. I used my hands to amplify the power. That night seemed like it was actually made for me to experiment with my hands. And, sorry to bring up religeon, I prayed I would be able to perform TK within 3 weeks practicing for at least 10 minutes a night (I brought it up because I thought it might have been a factor that lead to my success .) And the night after I got bored of my hand TK I prayed that I would be able to do no hands TK once more and I did but that was pretty much it. I would appreciate any advise on what to do to enhance my skills or any info. about my experiences.

It seems you are doing quite well yourself. You don't need alot of advice, just keep experiementing and practicing as you do now. Though watch it that you do not intellectualize and label every experience you have. By that I mean, what you were telling us about you seemed to be "tired" and then you had to wait a minute before it worked again. If you label this too much, and if you get attached to that exp4erience too much, then you might be bound to that "tiredness" in future practices. So While practicing and experimenting like you do, just observe what is happening, but don't label the situation too much, before then it may become a mindblock later on.

Besides that, I want to congratulate you, and perhaps you could try moving on next week to spinning a balanced straw, or some other excercise that you like.

Keep up the good work, and the praying as it seems to help you.
