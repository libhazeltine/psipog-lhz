Congratulations rob .

No I don't think the pain is due to telekinesis. Though when you are starting out with exploring and trying out your paranormal abilities, you can indeed get sensations around your hear. Like a slight pressure or a tingling feeling somewhere around your head. This is normal as your body needs to get used to the use of this extra sense you are now using. These sensations will in most cases go as you get used to it. The only feeling that might get stronger is the pressure on your third eye region. But that has always been a positive experience for me, so don't be afraid of that.

But these sensations I am talking about that you got when you still need to get used to it, should not be painfull. So if it is real pain that you are experiencing, then I would not write telekinesis off as the cause of it. This will only program your mind in thinking that it is due to telekinsesis. Then the chance is created that next time you perform tk, you get exactly that same feeling again. So I would not decide that it is due to TK if I were you.

Goodluck on your further progression, sounds like you are doing quite well .
