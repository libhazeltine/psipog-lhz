This is just a random thought from some boards at AVEN. It's a rather unknown group of people, as well, so in order to try to come up with some statistics (male/female ratio, etc), there's a poll section with every possible question imaginable.

We could set that up here, as psionics and psionicists are equally unknown in quantitative formats. Any question from "If you have made significant progress in training empathy - What is your gender?" to "If you have moved the psiwheel succesfully, controlling direction - how long did it take you?" would work. Naturally, it wouldn't be hard data, only anectodal evidence, but eh. Better than nothing.



EDIT: It occurs to me, this is just a section of the forum on AVEN, but there's no reason we can't have a survey-format type thing on psipog.net itself. Of course, a forum has the advantage of user-posted questions.
