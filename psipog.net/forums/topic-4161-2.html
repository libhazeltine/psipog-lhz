People do it because in most other psionic communities, the psions use terms like those. Therefore, when they come here, they use them. A lot of the terms come from video games, comics, or Sci-Fi movies. People think they sound cool (ie "Hey, i can do xxxxkinesis"). They also think, as it has been pointed out earlier in this thread, that it catagorizes things into smaller groups. In reality, it just makes things more complicated. Instead of saying "I picked up some dirt and some water using PK yesterday", you are saying "I did some aquakinesis and some geokinesis yesterday." Well, it might sound the same but listen. There are different terms for the same thing (ie. geokinesis and terrakinesis). Not only that, but using "PK" is more specific. What are you moving? Dirt, a rock, etc. What is happening to it? Did it levetate? did it roll? did it do anything (you still didnt say you suceeded). i hope this helps clear up some things.
