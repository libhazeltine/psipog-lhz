And might anyone know a why to kind of decrease the spread range? Then I could like double my TK powers!!!!!!!!


2 things:

1. Keep your focus on the object, dont let your mind wonder, like (incoming example);

NeoPsychic:' Hmmm, this bloody psiwheel doesnt seem to be moving much.'
Sub-c: 'Getting bored already.'
Neopsychic: 'Still wont budge'
Sub-c: 'Sooooo bored, hey look, over theres a challenge, a big psiwheel'
Neopsychic: 'Holy shit, that big psiwheel is moving!'
Sub-c: 'Who da man, who da man, *points at self*'
Neopsychic: ' Now back to the mini wheel'
Sub-c: 'Dude, are you insulting me? i could move this with a mere thought'
Neopsychic: 'Good lord its moving, with more control!'

The more focus you have on your particular object, the easier and faster it will be to move, since youve moved the big psiwheel, your sub-c thinks, 'oh that was f*cking easy, i can move this mini wheel with a mere thought.' As our mind seems to think that if youve moved something larger, moving something smaller will be as easy as pie.
Also, if you did focus on a particular object, i shouldnt imagine that your Tk abilities will double, that comes with time, practice and focus.
