INTRODUCTION
This a useful technique that I found on some website (I forgot where it is).
After using it I could produce a psiball in an instant and I could move objects that I couldnt move before. This technique only took me five minutes to master after my first try.

TECHNIQUE
Go outside on a cloudy/ sunny day and look up at the sky. Hopefully, you will see clouds in a variety of sizes and shapes. If it is possible, focus on a small cloud that is away form other clouds. Make an image of that cloud in your head. In your imagination, make the cloud slowly dissappear. When you focus on the real cloud again, you'll notice that it is slowly dissappearing until its gone.

WHY DOES THIS HAPPEN?With psi techniques it is bad to question why something happened, because even after it works your mind will begin to think it isnt possible.

HOW DOES THIS WORK?
Without knowing it you used basic PK to separate the individual water molecules in a cloud. The water molecules in a cloud are not very dense so they are easy to move, even for beginners. The cloud did not really dissappear; it's still in the sky, but it is not whole anymore. This is similar to blowing the seeds off of a Dandellion (I know I spelled that wrong).

DONT'S
