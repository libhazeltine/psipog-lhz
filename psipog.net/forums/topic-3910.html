Last night I had a nightmare. Before that night, I had empathically read someone close to me and she was going through a tough time.

Guess who I dreamt about; that someone. I don't know why but I was in Hell. And you know what else, I thought it was real, too. The dream must have been during my entire sleep.

Usually I only dream for about 10 minutes before I just wake up. I hate it when your alarm clock/parents wake you up when you have the best dream ever. Like once I was dreaming I finally got my Gibson Les Paul, I woke up. Realizing it was just a dream, I punched my pillow. But back to the subject:

To be honest, it was the most depressing dream, ever. I was crying in the dream alot, well actually, I cried pretty much through the entire dream. Imagine crying for 9 hours straight.

I think this will take me a while to get over. The dream felt so real, the scene was dark.
