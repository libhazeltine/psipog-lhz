I don't know if anybody else has noticed this but you're body can so easily fool you into thinking you've got a really strong magnetic pulsing feeling from your psiball when actually its just the sensation of your hands moving with your body as you breathe. The pushing out matches your inhaling and the pulling in matches your exhaling.
Similarly sometimes the (blood) pulse in my hand feels like the magnetic pulse.
Or even if you hold your hands in front of you they tend to waver slightly around the position you hold them in which can simulate the pulse sensation.

Don't get me wrong I'm a firm believer in psi etc I've just noticed that the pulsing sensation can be attritubuted sometimes to your own movements. And please don't flame me for this....its like with any psionic thing, rule out any other possible cause - like a breeze on a psiwheel.
