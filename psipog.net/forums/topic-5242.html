But just tonight at dinner (I'm 16) After a couple failed sessions with the spinning paper on a pin thingy, I was zoned out wondering why most people can do it and someone as smart, athletic, and cunning as myself couldn't even come close. Then without even realizing I pulled the salt shaker into my hands with my own willpower. Now THIS scared the crap out of me. Noone else witnessed the event. My brother and parents were in a heated argument about not doing chores or something.

So I tried to do it again and again but couldn't. nothing moved, not even my napkin or a hair I pulled out. Why can my unconcious self do it so easily and my concious, thinking mind can't? It's all so frustrating
