I think beliefs affect your practice too. This relates to how NI posted about our concept of stable and instable objects. But if I'm working on rolling the can, and I clearly can't get a full roll, but I'll imagine that I've already rolled the can and just moved my hand up closer to it to get it to roll again. That way, in your mind, it doesn't say "I can't roll", it's "I already have and I'm trying to do it again". It seems to bring me more successes with movements. I figured that one out from the psiwheel because I realized that it was easier for me to continue the spinning motion once it already was going, even if it was going slow at first.
