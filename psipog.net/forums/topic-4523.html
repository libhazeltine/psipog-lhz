I never have actually practiced to get better at it, but sometimes I have lucid dreams anyway. The last time I did, and some times before that, I haven't had complete control. The last time, there was a locked door. I though, this is no problem. This is a lucid dream. I can just walk through it, right? No, I couldn't. I couldn't get through it. After that, I either "forgot" it was a lucid dream, or I woke myself up. I think it's weird to be dreaming and know you are dreaming so sometimes when I have a lucid dream, I'll be like, Oh, no, I'm dreaming and I can't move! And then I'll breath really loudly to wake myself up, because I've found that works really well. And then I'll think, Darnit! I could have tried dreamwalking or something! So, anyone else have weird things like this happen in lucid dreams?

Wow, now you probably all think I'm insane or something.
