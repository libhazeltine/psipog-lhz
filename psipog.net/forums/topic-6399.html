Hi

Here a nice meditation technique:

Length: 1 Hour

Manner of execution: Sitting, eyes open, in complete, pitch black darkness.

Definitions:

Complete darkness – nothing can be seen. Waving a hand in front of the nose can’t be seen.

Recommendations:

A - Sitting in the most comfortable place available so no need to move will arise.

B – Better be done at the beginning of the night, when the darkness is “fresh”.

C – It is allowed to blink, but try keeping the eyes open.

Warning: As a meditation technique, it alter your "energetic structure", so it is highly recommended to follow it exact simple instructions. If one think it would be difficult for himself doing the technique, it is advisable not to start.

Enjoy!
