OK. Where to begin?

First off, my first word of advice is: dont expect an OBE right off the bat (i.e. even possibly a year) yet 'expect an OBE'. Know what I'm saying? Keep your expectations low for experiencing it, but high for knowing you will do it...I know it doesn't make any sense, but honestly 10 nights is a short amount of time for one of the hardest skills to master in 'psionics'.

Second, there are techniques in the e-book for mastering mind awake/body asleep. It is not nearly as hard as it sounds. Just deep breathing, slowly relaxing each muscle group and telling that muscle group, one at a time, to "go to sleep" and forgetting about it!

The best time to practice is in the morning, after you wake up naturally (not to an alarm). Try that out! No guarantees either.

Affirmations are a big help. Go throughout your day saying/thinking "I am going to have an OBE". It helps. I had my first OBE after doing affirmations all day long, and that was the first time i had done affirmations all day long

It sounds like you are on the right track though. Keep practicing, don't lose hope! Keep telling yourself you can do it and expect to have one everytime. You don't have to necessarily float yourself out every practice session. When it is time and your subc is comfortable with it, you will float out on your own, with most likely no visualization of such.

Good Luck!
