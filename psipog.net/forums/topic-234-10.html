Hi,
I have found a good way to make constructs and easy too. You see, I have satellite TV (the one with a 60cm dish,works on KU band ) at home. The signal gets disrupted when there is rain. What I do is make a construct with a connection to the earth, with a sort of a diode attached to it so that energy flows only from earth to construct and not the other way . So this construct will make a clear path for the signal till above the clouds. I have tried it many times and it worked.

I found that it needs some motivation. If I am not very interested in watching the programme currently running, it may not work. But if the loss of signal was during a programme I really LOVE , I go mad...really... and I think the emotion makes it more powerful. I usually get good response in some 20 seconds.

Cool huh? I also make a 'thinking cap' -style construct. Basically it covers the entire head other than face. This one also features a 'wire' to the earth with a 'diode' in it to get energy when needed.

I used it before my recent exams. Basically I program it to get the questions for the exam in the next day and make the answers for them fixed very clearly in my memory. I do study of course... .I am waiting for the results now... Will see... But the exams were relatively more easy than usual... dunno if it was due to 'thinking cap' . One thing the cap really helped with is waking up. I am a late riser (need 10-12 hours sleep ). Last day, I programmed it to make me wake up at 6 AM and I woke up, fully refreshed, at the exact time!!

I am thinking of improving this 'cap' further as it is very helpful.
