Hey everybody, I discovered a new psychokinesis method. I read it in a book that taught psychokinesis ( I forget which one...). Basically, what the person trying to do psychokinesis does is try to "feel" the object as much as possible in their mind. The person should try to sense all the different qualities of the object. Then, he/she focuses psi intensely for at least 5 minutes straight on the object, fiercely thinking about how he/she wants to manipulte it. The last step, which makes this technique unique, is that the person should try to defocus their attention from the object. In other words, the person attempting PK should completely forget about the object after the intense focus. Of course, this technique is used most well with a fully relaxed mind beforehand. I have so far found this technique to work well, at least when I was relaxed enough to forget about the object. So try the technique and tell me how good it was.
