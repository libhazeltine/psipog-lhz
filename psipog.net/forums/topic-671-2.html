It's not powering anything yet, but I'm sure I can think of some stuff for it to power.

If you're going to make one, make sure you can personally distinguish between psi "dirt" and psi inside people, so you can program the tiny constructs to only take the "dirt".

Make the storage construct compress energy as it is put into it, and to expand itself if needed. Make sure the stored energy is not programmed at all.

Lastly, for the links that carry energy from the storage construct to the others for recharging, make them separate from the link that carries energy to the storage.
(Don't forget to program the storage construct to recharge itself too). And make sure the energy flow is regulated, you don't want all the energy to be transferred to the other constructs at once, perhaps causing them to fall apart.
