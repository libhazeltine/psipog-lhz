well it was just something I heard somewhere and yes I finaly levitated a psiwheel (took me long enough though). I got it up about 2 inches above my psiwheel. I didn't lay it on my hand because it didn't feel comfortable to me. here's how I did it. You know you sometimes focus on one of the corners to make the wheel spin? Now you try to make the corner go up instead of sideways. After a while you'll be able to knock the wheel off the needle by pushing one corner up. so the next step is: apply the same thing on all four corners ( + the object as a whole). It'll be really hard at first. but patience and a clear head can pay off. I had to teach myself how to do this and it took me almost a year.
