Hmmm. There's too many possiblilities to be sure.

First, since you mentioned the window was open, it's quite possible that it blew from the shelf and to it's position, regardless of what was in the way. Didn't necessarily have to happen in one fel swoop either, could have taken a few gusts to get it that far.

You also mentioned feeling another presence - well it's quite possible there's another person living in the house, or at least visiting. Have you noticed anything else like this before?
I don't discount this possiblitiy mainly because of my experiences. A short story to illustrate.
When I was in my late teens and starting college, I moved into my parent's basement. Our house was built on the spot where we took down an old stone cottage, on the shore of a lake. It wasn't isolated and was a small community with a fair number of people in the summer.
Shortly after moving down into the basement I started hearing some strange things - odd creeks and groans which I just wrote off as th ehouse settling and me being a little jumpy. I don't believe the house was settling at all.
I clearly remeber several different nights when I would hear the basement steps, coming from the main floor, groaning and creeking exactly like someone was walking down them - though noone was at 3am - I know that for sure. The steps would continue down and around the corner, through a short hallway and into the main room of the basement. Form there they would shuffle across the carpet and into the bedroom, which was raised and also creeked when walked on, then all th eway up to the side of my bed.
I was terrfied - hell thinking back now it STILL scares the crap out of me. I did, on occasion, get the courage to goo looking for the source of the sound, which I never did find.

And, of course, you may have actually moved it with PK as well. While I doubt it was done while on your bike I wouldn't doubt that you may have done so when you felt the other presence, or perhaps while heading to your room.

There's a few options and I wouldn't discount any of them. Unfortunatly due to the number of possibilities you'll probably never be 100% on what happened and will either have to remain undecided, or pick the answer you're most comfortable with.
