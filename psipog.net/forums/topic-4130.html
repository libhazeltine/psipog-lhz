You mean connecting or linking with objects?

This is my method:

Place the object in question before you, try to feel the object, try to feel its structure.
I use my hands to feel the object, take it in your hand, feel it from all sides.
Feel that you are becoming one with it.
Once you?ve a good idea how the object feels, place it in front of you.
Don?t think about anything, keep relaxed and clear of thoughts.
Now try to feel the object without touching it, feel it in your hands, as if you are holding it again.
When you can feel the object in front of you in your hands as a small pressure you are starting to get a connection, the more you practice the stronger the feeling will become.
When you can feel a magnetic kind of pressure in your hands in the direction of the object, you are connected with it.
