I had a dream a few days back that I was outside my mom's garage and watching her pull into it. And as I was messing around with my eyesight, letting it fade into a trance-sorta look, I started seeing little lines vaguely over where I was looking. This somehow made me think "Oh, I should try to precog" (just one of those random somehow knowing things in dreams, such as 'I have to get this pie to the window or else aliens will destroy the earth', that sort of thing).

So I dazed out in my dream, and I sort of felt a weird 'vrooshing' feeling, a mix of an odd feeling of transportation at high speed, and the feeling you get from goosebumps all over your body. As this was happening, my vision started blurring over a bit, clearest at the center, fuzzier at the edges, and time was speeding up extremely fast. I saw a bunch of different cars go back and forth inside and out of the garage, all in high speed.

Though I'm quite sure it WASN'T a precog dream, it was just ABOUT a precog dream, it's still interesting.
