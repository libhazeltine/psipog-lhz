The memories can be clouded, but in my experience I always remember at least one part: Becoming aware in the dream. If you remember thinking to yourself: I'm dreaming, then it is by definition a lucid dream. It sounds like it probably was, because most people don't usually fly out into space in their normal dreams (at least to my knowledge) but unless you realized you were dreaming, it wasn't a LD.
