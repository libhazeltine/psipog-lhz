(thinking to myself) ME "Ooh come on move you f*king pencil!"

(suddently) "Man i'm tyred of this"

ME "... what's this, it'wasn't me thinking.."

SC "yes it was you, your inner yourself"

ME "... ... the subconscious?"

SC "Bingo man, bingo!"

ME "Cool! Now listen, if you are real tell me why the pencil is not moving"

SC "What the f*ck, u really think u can move things with your mind?Are you kidding?"

ME "Wait a minute you really think you don't believe in the Psychokinesis?"

SC "I don't know"

ME "Well then listen to me, if you don't know believe in me when i teel you that it is possible! We already moved that psywell last year, remember?"

SC "Yeah i remember"

ME "So trust me if i tell you that we can move this pencil. Are you with me?"

SC "Right on man, but i really don't remember how to move that"

ME "Don't worry, pratice will take care of that"

SC "Ok, let's try it!"

What to say, the answers came out of my head by theyr own, it wasn't me thinking, it was like someone else was talking from my inside.

It was strange but fun and usefull, now i know that i can talk to my subconscious and correct him when he is wrong ( yeah i know it should be the opposite, but hey! No one is perfect!)!
