It's an interesting concept. But you're talking about tearing an object. If you have one half of paper moving one way and another the other way, it tears. It's trying to pull apart the pieces with force. Cutting it in two neat pieces without ragged and uneven edges, like with scissors, you'd need to displace the atoms at the "line" of where you'd want the paper to seperate. But "cutting" this way seems incredibly difficult, at least, more than tearing. With a piece of paper, you'd just need push and pull one way. Even that is difficult, and I'd wager only a very skilled psion would be able to do that. Really neat thought, good topic I'd also like to hear some other thoughts too.
