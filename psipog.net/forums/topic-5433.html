After about 2 weeks of trying to, i've finally had an LD. It went...rather wierd though.

At first, i was walking into my kitchen, and for some reason there was a Jerry Springer reject in there too (must've been because i was watching Springer earlier). So I'm looking at the guy, and im thinking "thats not quite normal" and then the words "IM DREAMING" shot into my head.

I realized i cant get too excited or I'll wake up, and then felt as though i was being brought back in a tunnel. Then i saw myself being pulled through a gravitational tunnel. I'm sure you've seen it in some Star Trek show or game (Prey).

So I'm falling and i yell to myself "Increase Lucidity", hoping it'd work. It did, and i landed in my kitchen. I turned around and saw my sister on the couch. I decided i no longer cared for her since this was my world. So i thought i'd shoot her. Shes a pain in the ass anyways. But i had a problem, i couldn't really do anything.

I tried to have a gun appear in my hand. Nothing.

I tried turning around and having the gun be there. Nothing.

I tried transforming my hand into a gun. Nothing.

And then i woke up confused and unsatisfied, like most of my ex's

Question: Am i supposed to lack control the first time? Is this kind of how it went for others?
