Well, Ill get straight to it.

I've meditated a few times before and although i entered an extremely relaxed state, it was barely fruitful. Today i started reading more detail into psychic phenomena etc.. Id read some before but never in as much detail as today.

After reading, inspired, i decided i would try and meditate again. I led back on my bed, having done it before relaxing became a fairly fast process. I led back and closed my eyes and focused on relaxing each limb and area of my body until it felt like I was 'Un-attatched' (for need of a better word). Anyway I started saying to myself in my head 'My psychic intuition is growing everyday'. I said it about 4 times, taking deep breathes and remaining in my state of relaxation.

All of the sudden from seeing nothing but black I was falling out of the sky and towards a brown building then I came to with a jolt, covered in gose-pimples feeling like i was actually just falling from the sky. This was far more than any day dream, or ordinary dream, it was so real and i was still awake. My heart was racing and my breathing was heavy.


Id read before something about a falling sensation but i never expected this. Needless to say what doubt i had is fading fast.

I want to try it again soon and try to control it, from what i read I believe I may be close to or in fact doing astral projection.

I really want to know if anyone else has experienced this and if they can tell me what it is? Im begining to fully believe and im astounded as how quickly something like this has happened to me, perhaps im lucky enough to have had the ability naturally and now im just training it further. Whatever the case, any help would be useful.

(I apologise for any grammar/spelling omissions, Im still a bit shocked as this is the first time i have actually truly experienced something like this, im having trouble believing it happened now which is why if I know it has happened before ill know its not my mind playing tricks on me)

Err, doubtful. It's probably either strong hypnagogic imagery or an instance of conscious REM (rapid eye movement) causing a dream state to generate. I've been flitting in and out of sleep, or in a light sleepish state (i.e. sleeping in class), or in a deep trance, and have had various things like you've described, and have suddenly awakened and grabbed on to something to catch myself. I've never associate that with OBE/AP, just with dreams on the verge of sleep.

That's not to discourage you at all from further practice in OBE! It's just that this particular occurance can't necessarily be attributed to psioncs.
