Note: None of the following links contain illegal software/services.




1.) FireFox.
FireFox is a 100% FREE Web browser that is ALOT Faster, Safer, and Easier to use than Internet Explorer. It contains a buil-in pop-up blocker as well as many other fun-filled features. FireFox actually helps protect your computer against Viruses/Trojans/Worms/Spyware and it updates itself.

You can download the latest version of FireFox for Windows Operating Systems, MAC OS X, and Linux (for a couple of different languages) here: http://www.mozilla.com/firefox/




2.) Proxify.
Proxify is like a search engine that allows you to browse the internet safely and quickly. It is FREE to use, but if you buy a subscription you can unlock more features (I myself do not have a subscription). It also allows you to choose the content of the website you are going to by disabling Cookies, Scripts, Ads, Referrer Information, and making the page text-only. All of these things will help the web page you are visiting faster and safer to load.

http://www.proxify.com/




3.) Web-Warper.
Another search engine like Proxify. However, it is always FREE to use... meaning I don't think it is even possibly to purchase a subscription here. Web-Warper usually lets you use its services more often too. Like, with Proxify, sometimes the service is busy due to payed subscribers... Web-Warper is never busy. It doesn't let you MODify as much, but it does make loading faster and safer.

http://webwarper.net/




4.) Grisoft Free Services (For Anti-Virus and Anti-Spyware protection).
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Have Viruses/Trojans/Worms/Spyware or are concerned that you might? No problem. Just use AVG Anti-Virus and/or Ewido Anti-Spyware. Both are FREE from Grisoft (the people who made the programs), unless you want to buy a subscription for more features, but you don't have to (I haven't). Both programs are easy to use, and they are (I believe) only Anti-Virus/Anti-Spyware service out there wtehose programs are not infected by Viruses/Spyware themselves. Plus the programs dect just about every type of Virus/Trojan/Worm/Spyware out there; and then they remove it for FREE. Plus you get free updates.

http://free.grisoft.com/doc/1
