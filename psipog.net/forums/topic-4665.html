Learn how to relax the body. If the body and breath becomes deeply relaxed, the mind will follow. So if you are new, first start with relaxing the body. Then comes the breath, then the mind will follow. So do a little excersice if you will, just to get the body and the blood-circulation going. This is not nessesary but might help you in the beginning. Then sit down in a comfertable position and take a few deep breaths, as if you sigh. Then gow with your attention through your entire body, and be alert on any tensed muscles. Relax every musscle of your body. Then you will automatically be in Alpha state once you did this. Then you may want to continue focussing on the breath, it will take you deeper and it will make your mind relaxed and still.
