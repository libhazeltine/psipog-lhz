I've been working on astral projection for about a month now and i've came across quite a few ways that really help relax your body. Though the first thing you need to keep in mind, something i've discovered for experiance, is that sometimes your body will not relax. It just refuses to (well i guess if you layed around for like three hours it probably would, but where's the fun in that?).

Here's a few little tips I've picked up over my practices.

1. Before you even start trying to relax make sure your body is in the most comfortable position possible. I really dont think there's a universal "OBE position", nor logically -should- there be one. Whatever makes you comfortable will work. This takes me awhile sometimes but save me from alot of fustration.

2. I picked this one up from one of the articles here on psipog (cant remember which one right now, though.) Basically you pick a number and start counting backwards, all the while imagining your body becoming more relaxed. You might have to loop the numbers a few times, but at least this gives you something to do, right? ;D

3. I can't remember exactly where I read about this one, though I would like to give credit to whoever came up with this (if anyone knows it would be appreciated). You pick a song and "play" it in your mind. It's good to pick a simple song that disnt take too much consicenceness to orcestrate(sp). I'd suggest the "Song that never ends" song (i know its a kid song, but it loops constantly and is very simple.)

4. And as you already know, visualization. But the trick is finding a visualization that works for you. Honestly, whenever I try to use someone else's visualization, it seldom works. Every person is different and we all have our own preferences.


Well, that's all know. Hope it helps you out.
