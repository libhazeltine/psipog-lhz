Well, I can only speak from experience, but....

When I first started making psiballs, I got the exact same feeling. Why don't you try an experiment. Hold your hands in the exact same position. Now, move them back and forth like you did before. I would suspect that you will get the same feeling. What happened with me, is that I relized that it was the same feeling which meant that my muscles were just tightening up. Keep at it though, and I'm sure that in time you will make a real psiball.

Also, some advice is to not move your hands back and forth. If it is a real psiball, your hands will move apart with out you moving them in first!
