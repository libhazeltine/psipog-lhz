Warren writes:
"Hi, I just wanna ask what is happening to me, it is empathy? or
coincidence? Sometimes I know when my friends are sad or happy
and they dont tell me their state; I see them and I just know
their fee1ings. How I can practice and improve Empathy and how I
can manipulate their feelings? cause somethimes I know their
feelings and I cant do anything to change that. Thanks."

My comments: Yes, that sounds like empathy. One exercise I do
to practice empathy is to just read random people on the street.
Go to a mall and find a comfortable seat in the middle where
everyone walks around, and pick out people and read them as they
walk by. Look for their general emotion, but also try to pick up
on specific things. For example, someone might be happy, but why
are they happy? Is this happiness originating from seeing
something funny? Or maybe you can sense that they have recently
released a lot of tension and stress from a relaxing activity?
Perhaps they just saw a movie. Figure it out, and try to hone in
on specifics. The best way to change someone's emotion is to
talk to them and see what's up.

The Game Man writes:
"I'm having trouble even getting a sign of moving the psi wheel.
Whenever I do it I just can't even when I'm trying my hardest, so
is it REALLY possible to actually move a psi wheel? Not to put
down practicing telekinesis, but I've read an article that shows
if you position your hands right (in the same spot other people
put their hands in movies) the psi wheel will spin because of the
heat rising. This is really putting me down because now I really
don't know if its rea1 or not?"

My comments: Heat rising is a problem you have to deal with. I
suspect some people out there think they are moving the psi wheel
when it is really just heat from the hands. BUT, there is a
large number of people who have tested against the heat theory.
For example, some people (including myself) have moved the psi
wheel without having our hands close to the psi wheel at all. I
have done it from across the room. Others make sure they are
truly moving it by focusing on different directions for the wheel
to move. Heat can spin the whee1, but if it was ONLY heat, then
you couldn't control what direction the wheel spins. The
majority of people who have spinned the wheel have done tests
like these to see if it was heat or not.

Blueish writes:
"What is your opinion on deja vous, or precognitive ability? Is
it possible to see future events or does free will mean that by
knowing the future you have already changed it? Is there free
will or is the future already defined in stone?"

My comments: Well, although I have few experiences with precog,
it seems obvious when looking at the evidence that precog'ing
something is possible. How does the past, present, and future
all tie together to produce reality? I have no idea. How does
deja vous work? Again, no idea :-P. I do NOT agree with the
scientific/psychological explainations for deja vous (like the
brain misfiling an experience under long term memory, instead of
short term memory). If I had to guess, I would say the
phenomenon of deja vous is probably a lot of different things
clumped together under one umbrella. Some people might
experience it from a forgotten dream they had about the future,
while others might experience it because they saw something
similar a long time ago. Good question to ponder :-P.
