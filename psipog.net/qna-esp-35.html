Bhodili123 asks on November 10, 2003, 3:49pm:

I initially asked this in the chatroom. However those present had no idea, so I'm sending it here.

Here is a question. Premonition a psychic fortelling of events? Or the subconcious picking up subtle hints and leaping to a plausible but not always true event?

Rainsong replies on November 12, 2003, 6:33pm:

A premonition is a "feeling" about something happening in the future, like a hunch. The word usually implies psychic foretelling, but it is often very difficult to distinguish between an an actual psychic premonition, and a nudge from your subconscious as a result of more "mundane" clues picked up, but not consciously recognised. In either case, the subconscious is nudging the conscious mind.

Quite often, "premonition" refers to vague foreboding, rather than detailed foretelling. However, the specific usage varies with location and context. In any case, it falls under the category of "Precognition", literally "knowing before".

I have seen a lot of people talking about "aura vision" what is it?

Rainsong replies on November 4, 2003, 8:54pm:

"Aura vision" is the ability to see the so-called "aura". Depending upon the source (book, site, person), an "aura" might be anything from the electromagnetic field surrounding the body to some kind of "energetic double". Some forms of "aura vision" have been more-or-less disproven.
