Rainsong replies on April 11, 2005, 11:54am:

That's entirely normal. Start by sitting quietly (or standing, if you're waiting in line at the bank or something...) and close your eyes. Observe the thoughts as they come through, and don't do anything about them. Just watch them.

Next step is to breathe, and just count your breaths. Count them silently as you exhale. 1, 2, 3, 4, etc... There will be random thoughts wandering through. Ignore them and count breaths.

Or, build up a picture in your imagination. Add details, bit by bit. Eventually you can take this exercise as far as playing video games in your mind.
