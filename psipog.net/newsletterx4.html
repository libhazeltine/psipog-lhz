Hi everyone :-)

If you didn't know, I plan on archiving PsiPog.net on the 26th of
January (Friday). This means that all interactive portions of the
site will shut down, including the forums and chat. As a final
event, I decided we should host an online PK Party on that day, in
the chat room. I put a poll on the PsiPog.net homepage, asking you
guys which time would be best for everyone. Please vote in the
poll if you want to attend, so we can get a good time for everyone
in different time zones.

During the PK Party, I want to release the URL of my next project.
The ideals are pretty different than PsiPog.net's, so some people
might not like it as much, but at the same time - others might
like it more. Oh well - that's how change goes! :-P

I'll send out one more message to everyone once we have a clear
indication of what the best time will be for the PK Party. Thanks!

- Sean Connelly
