I am Peebrain, the webmaster of PsiPog, but I don't OWN the articles on here. I personally only own the articles that I wrote (the ones that say "by Peebrain" ). My personal policy, about MY articles, is you can copy them if:

1. Proper credit is given (i.e., "this article was written by peebrain").
