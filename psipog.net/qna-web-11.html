SKainuiG asks on November 12, 2003, 7:41pm:

What is the symbol on the first page called, and what does it mean? I'm talking about the one before you even enter the site, it's the fork thing.

Rainsong replies on November 13, 2003, 8:21pm:

That is a Greek letter. It is spelled out as "psi". It is used as a variable, in this case as the "unknown mechanism" which allows such effects as psychokinesis and telepathy to happen. It was used to coin the word "psionics", in parallel form to "electronics", "nucleonics", and so on, in the middle of the twentieth century, in the United States.

By contrast, by the way, "psychic" is indirectly derived from "Psyche", the Greek Goddess who personifies the mind and soul. This is the same root as is used in "Psychology" (study of the mind), "Psychiatry" (which is medical practice relating to the mind), and "Psychokinesis" (movement effected by the mind).
