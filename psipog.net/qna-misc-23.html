Rainsong replies on November 8, 2003, 8:54pm:

Flip, you ask a very good question. Somehow the White-lights-and-fluffy-bunnies brigade have managed to establish a myth that "psi can only be used for good and selfless purposes". The myth in question is totally bogus. Some of its _possible_ origins are:
