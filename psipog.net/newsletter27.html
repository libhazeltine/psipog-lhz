Lucifael wonders:
Ok, first off I came here because I wanted to learn how to make
shields to keep my empathy from getting out of hand. That was
my only interest, however as time progressed I began to become
more interested in other aspects of psionics such as
psychokinesis. However there were certain abilties I wanted to
avoid altogether such as telepathy. However it seems that one
can not pick and choose which ones they learn or pick up. I
found this out becuase one day I was talking to my friend and
eventually I already knew what he was going to ask and would
answer him before he did. At first I had actually thought he
asked and I had just forgotten he did. But after a while it
became apparent that wasn't the case.

Why is it that one can pick up abilities they dont want like
that?? Or is it because I tried to avoid that I picked it up.
Which if the latter is the case why would that be? Oh and
another thing is if I dont program my shield to prevent that
from happening it will happen at random times. That has me
very concerned so I've been extra careful to program my shields
to keep telepathy signals from leaving or entering me because
I'm not really sure which that would be.

My comments: This is common for most people just getting into
psionics. For the most part, you became interested because you
had some degree of sensitivy at first and needed to find the
reason for it. Others just getting into psionics are recognizing
the existence of this extra sensitiviy, and are often bombarded
with it in many ways, due to not having a clear idea on what
they want to accomplish in the way of psionics, and basically
they are still open to most forms of it. If you are still
getting any unwanted input later on in your practices, then it
is probably best to shield as you stated. This way, until ready
to deal with what else is going you can avoid it interferring
with your daily routines.

Peebrain's comments: Also, this general sensitivity which brings
about empathy can also be used for telepathy. My guess would be
you picked up telepathy just from already using your empathy
skill and developing a little bit more sensitivity through some
basic exercises. Again, the solution is to shield.

BloodTrail shares:

Hello, I've been practicing TK and psiballs for a few months and
avidly reading all the articles on PsiPog and the newsletter.
While I struggle with TK, I think I've got psiballs "under my
belt". So I'd like to offer my suggestions and tips.

1) Learn anchoring commands. That was the best singular advice
I've ever followed through on. It's easy and I've already
securely programmed it to ground myself (I say "root") on command
as well as instantly make a fairly well formed and dense psiball.
Be sure to read up on it on PsiPog.

2) Don't always rely on other's techniques. After experimenting
a lot with psiball visulizations and techniques, I found the best
way is to really use those as "guidelines". The best way is to
make you're own sequence of visulizations and ideas to help. For
example, I use a Final Fantasy sort of idea to take in energy
and make a psiball (without anchoring). I imagine swirling around
in the pyreflyes that appear in the series, and then absorbing
those as "white" energy, then, absorbing a "dark" energy that
swirls around the white energy energy in my body. I push both of
those into a psiball and make a ying-yang sort of effect in my
psiball. It works great for me, but I still stand by making your
own. Anyways, good luck.

My comments: Most definately right, it is always best to follow
the basic methods to a certain point. But in the end, you are
going to want to form visualizations that mean something more
to you than just the generic terms which only get you so far,
due to your lack of connection with them.

Rahmid asks:

I got into TK maybe a year ago and didn't have any success so I
would stop and go. But I really got into it, looked up
exercices, and found PsiPog. I was trying to spin the psi wheel
with only random success. Then I went to a party sleep over at
friends house and it was about 3 and I was just able to do it.
It was amazing. Total control. I believe it was the many videos
I watched earlier that day on google video. And my friends were
able to do it instantly after watching. So, I can still do it
sort of currently. But when ever I would try and show someone to
try and prove to them that TK was real, I would bomb. I would
eventually get a few spins but nothing much nothing to change
their mind. I was able to do it under the table, but now I
generally don't do well. It has to do, I believe, with doubting
my skills. I dont understand it I believe, but I KNOW I can do
it. I go and try it, but it doesn't happen until i just stare
and focus for like 10 mins. Then I can get a really good spin but
even then I cant stop it very good, let alone change directions.
Its bad, in the very least.

Any advise, or similair instances, please share!!!

My comments: For the most part, it sounds like doubt is most of
your problem. I say this because you mention being able to do it

you will probably never be fully convinced even after you pull
it off a few times, it does help when the doubt pops up less
often. So work on the doubt as you also work on Telekinesis, and
it should prove helpful.
