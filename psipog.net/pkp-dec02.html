December 14th, 2002 PK Party

As far as I know, this is the first time anyone has ever done anything like this. A normal PK Party is a gathering of people, at a physical location, where they have a small party and try to bend silverware using psychokinesis (hence "PK Party"). The original idea comes from a guy named Jack Houck, and PK Parties have become pretty popular.

Since we can't really have a bunch of kids from all over the world meet at one spot, we decided to meet in the PsiPog chat room and see if we could reproduce the PK Party, but online instead.

I led the event, but really I have no idea what I'm talking about when it comes to bending silverware, so all I did was summarize the method on Fork-You.com and hung out with everyone else. RetroKat (the webmaster of Fork-You.com) came and helped us out, and gave us some tips too. A few people had results, and the images are below.
