I open the client and press the lightning symbol, and it says "*** Connecting to United States: tuath.ny.us.darkmyst.org (6667)..." then almost instantly it says "*** Connected. Initializing..." then I wait and wait and nothing ever happens. When I try typing in the text field it displays " *** Please wait until the client has connected to the server completely." Peebrain replies on December 14, 2004, 4:26pm:

This bug happens when you enter in an illegal nickname in the first box that comes up. To fix it, type:

/resetnick

Then try to connect. It will give you a random nickname that isn't illegal, and then you can just use:

/nick TheNicknameYouWant

After you connect to set up a new nickname.

russianspy3141 asks on November 28, 2004, 5:28pm:

In one of the old polls, you asked what new things people would like to see at PsiPog, and one of the choices was "a way for people to enroll in psi classes". Is there a possibility of that happening?

Peebrain replies on November 29, 2004, 1:02pm:

I don't know... I have mixed feelings about it.

1. It requires a teacher. And teaching something like that is VERY time consuming.

2. Classes benefit only a small group of people. If a log is posted online, it helps more people, but sometimes that's a conflict of interest for the teacher.

3. Choosing students is always a pain in the ass. People get pissed because they aren't "chosen", and all that junk.

But, 4. It really helps that group of chosen students.

I don't know if it's a fair trade. Whenever I think about it, I always think: maybe my time would be better spent on writing more articles, and answering the Q&A, where EVERYONE can benefit. We have over 1000 hits a day... teaching a group of 30 students doesn't seem like it's worth it in the long run.
