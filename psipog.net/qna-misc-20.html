Creature asks on December 22, 2003, 1:54pm:

Will Drugs weakin you abilty to use and manipulate psi ?


-Shadow Creature

Rainsong replies on December 22, 2003, 11:16pm:

Rather a broad question you have there. Some drugs do dampen psionic ability. Some don't. You would probably be surprised at how many have been tested.

In some cases, drugs prescribed for the treatment of certain neurological disorders appear to improve the psi abilities, as they stabilize brain chemistry or bring seizures under control and so on, making mental activity a little easier. Of course, some conditions which require medical attention would make psionic activity unwise. An obvious example would be hypokalemia, because psionic activity will make it worse.

I suspect that the "drugs" to which your question really refers are the "recreational" sort. There have been studies of the effects of a wide variety of drugs --legal and otherwise-- on psionic abilities. With the exception of caffeine, the effects common drugs were consistently negative. That is to say, when studied objectively by people who were looking for something to make people's psionics work better, the drugs in question were found to be unhelpful...in some cases, _very_ unhelpful. Caffeine's effects were variable, and appear to make some people more vulnerable to telepathic reception.

Think about it, though. "Recreational" drugs affect the nervous system. If you are trying to learn psionics, you need your nervous system to function correctly. Mucking it up is stupid. Long-term effects of some popular "street drugs" are still unknown. Long-term effects of drugs that have been around for a while are being discovered now. In many cases, the long-term effects have been different from what was expected.

Anyone who says they get better perception while "high" is to be regarded with suspicion unless or until the alleged improvement in the perceptions is demonstrated by comparing records of the accuracy of perception when "high" as compared to when the person is not drugged. When a person's judgement is impaired, that person cannot judge the situation clearly....That's what "judgement is impaired" means, after all.
