icefiremark asks on July 30, 2005, 7:59pm:

can anyone use psi wiith training? specificly cryo K and pyro K and TK

Rainsong replies on July 31, 2005, 11:39am:

There is a certain amount of evidence that indicates that most --and possibly all-- people can use psionics for the ESP side of the house: clairvoyance, telepathy, precognition. It is not likely that everyone can reach the same level of proficiency, but that is also the case with such mundane skills as writing and playing the guitar.

As for psychokinesis, the evidence is more sketchy on this count, and some researchers express doubt that it is a skill which everybody can learn. However, there is no concrete way to determine which people can learn it, and some people have gotten it to work after trying for several years with no apparent progress in between.

Peebrain replies on July 26, 2005, 11:48pm:

The first thing I suggest is a reality check. That means make sure you aren't fooling yourself. Everyone fools themselves from time to time... I've fooled myself a lot, and made a jackass out of myself in the process... but I've learned . So, reality check.

Second, what you are describing is certainly possible. What you MIGHT be doing is mistaking your own psychic impressions of them for THEIR thoughts. For example, if you are communicating with your own subconscious, and you think it's THEIR mind by mistake... then things might get confusing. Find a way to figure out "who" you're talking to.

Third, any time you do something really strange, find a way to confirm it. That means try to prove that it really happened some way or another. For example, if you can project to others and make them say things, try to get them to say something really random (use uncommon words). Get them to say "banana" or "fantabulous", or something random like that.

So: Reality Check, Investigate, and Confirm .
