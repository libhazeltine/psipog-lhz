blackwarrior asks on December 18, 2003, 12:54pm:

what is a Lucid dream...

Rainsong replies on December 18, 2003, 8:58pm:

A lucid dream refers to one in which you know you are dreaming, while you are dreaming. Quite often, you can control the progress of such a dream, but the control does not define the lucidity. Awareness does.
