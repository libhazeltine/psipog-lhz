SKainuiG asks on March 28, 2004, 11:57pm:

What are the differences of Psychometry and Telekinesis? It seems to me that they are both transferring thoughts, except psychometry is through imprinting items.

Rainsong replies on March 29, 2004, 9:27pm:

Psychometry is the reading of the impressions imprinted on an item's field. By extension, it is often regarded as including the act of deliberately imprinting the item with such impressions. Neither of these actions affects the physical structure or location of the item, so far as can be observed.

By contrast, telekinesis is the movement or alteration of the item, by psionic means.
