Some people work different ways. The first step is always to relax. If you're trying to force yourself to feel something, then you're trying too hard. Relax. Breathe. Ahhhhh.... good 'ol oxygen .

Try some different things. By this I mean, try visualizing different things, or just try different techniques all together. I'll give you some ideas to try:

3. Ummmm what else. I find that it just follows my focus. If I think to myself "I want psi in my hand", then focus on my hand, and ignore everything else, it'll start to pool in there pretty good. Don't focus on a million things at once - put the Gameboy down and focus on what you're doing.

Also, if your sister got it to work, maybe you can ask her for help?
