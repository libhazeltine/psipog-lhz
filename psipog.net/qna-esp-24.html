fooneyguy asks on May 1, 2004, 9:53pm:

1)I was wondering if there are any general rules of edicate for telepaths.
I have read many of the articles and more and more of he q&a and many times there is mention of things bordering on combat and thimgs that are starting combat. I was wondering what kind of things might be considered rude or a challenge. As I practice and mess around with psychic skills I'd rather not anger a skilled psion.
2)I was also wondering if, as my awareness and sensitivity increases is it easy to tell a psion from a non? If so how? Also if so, is there a way to program your shields to make you "appear" to be a non?

Rainsong replies on May 2, 2004, 12:53pm:

1) There are no absolutely-established rules of etiquette, which apply everywhere, no. However, if you want to avoid ticking off other psionicists, ask permission before doing anything with or to them, including scans. Beginners' scans often feel quite rough, for one thing, and in any case, such "reconnaisance" is sometimes considered a prelude to assault. Any close telepathic contact is intimate in nature, and such contact can be similar to rape in its effects on an unconsenting subject. (Loud broadcasting and the like is disconcerting to "regular folk" and annoying to other psionicists, but tends not to be viewed quite so dimly as telepathic scans, probes, and so on).


2) This one qwould depend on your definition of "non". If you are using it in the Psion Guild's manner (local usage was borrowed, very loosely, from there), then it is supposed to be possible to detect differences in "tone" between "nons" and the other sorts of folk. I, personally, cannot do this with anything remotely resembling accuracy, nor can I vouch for the accuracy of anyone else in this question.

If you simply mean "someone who doesn't do psionics", then looking at their field will _often_ --not always!-- tell you. Best way to learn, is to look at folks's fields (with permission, see above), and compare appearance/sound/feel.

Of course, the easiest way to determine whether or not someone is an active psionicist is to watch them "do" something. ;)

It is possible to make your field look like that of an "inactive, not-psionic" type of person. For a lot of folk, dropping their Shields, dirtying up the field outline, and letting a few months' worth of debris accumulate will do the job. For other folk, careful Shielding in a not-reflective mode, and covering the shield with uncleaned stray "psistuff" and assorted debris (think "hermit crab") can get the job done. Some folks' fields are difficult to conceal.

SNESster3 asks on April 16, 2004, 1:48pm:

I have been attempting Remote Presence and had plenty of success in only a day or so. Yet sometimes even when I follow the procedure, I RP somewhere only to find I am fully aware and am "imagining" being there. I understand that's a no-no, and I'd like to know what to do to stop myself from "imagining" the place I am RPing to. What is the best method of getting your body to loose awareness?

Rainsong replies on April 21, 2004, 1:22am:

Don't worry so much about getting your body to lose awareness. Choose target sites that you have not seen, but can get verification of details about. For example, RP to a museum which you have not visited, and _then_ either visit it or check out their website to check your accuracy. If it is accurate, then why worry if it is "imagination". If it isn't accurate, then at least you know where you stand, and have a place to work from.
