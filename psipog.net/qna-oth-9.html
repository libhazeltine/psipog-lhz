gabriel asks on May 9, 2004, 10:38pm:

hi,
could some one please give me an actual definition of biofeedback because im confused with the hole thing, i can't find an actual meaning of it in reference to psionics.

thanks.

Rainsong replies on May 10, 2004, 3:58pm:

Strictly speaking, biofeedback is a method of training a person to take control of certain subconscious activities (such as blood-pressure) by using audible and/or visible responses from a machine which measures the "subconscious activity" (such as blood pressure). There are various such devices on the market, ranging from simple "lie-detectors" from Radio Shack to elaborate medical devices worth many thousands of dollars.

More loosely, the term is also used to describe any method of taking deliberate control of subconscious processes. Psionicists tend to favour the "visualisation" approach, mainly because it is simple to explain. Make a "mental video game" of a relaxing experience such as chilling out at the beach, and your pulse and blood pressure will lower in response to it....eventually. This is a "skill" rather than an innate ability, so the time it takes to effect such changes varies with the amount and effectiveness of the practice of the individual.
