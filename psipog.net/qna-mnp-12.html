MHR_75 asks on February 11, 2005, 10:36am:

My question is about a part of the Psi Ball Visibility Summary (art-psiball-faq.html#visib9). If someone else can't see your Psi Ball, then how do you know that your not imagining it?

SunTzu replies on February 16, 2005, 9:41am:

Psi-balls are not always visible (in fact, most of the time, they are not visible at all). However, you can still feel the effects of a psi-ball after you have made one, and so can other people. For instance have you felt a hot/cold sensation? Have you felt a "magnetic" attractive/repulsive force?

One of the easiest ways to see if you have created a psi-ball is to have another person feel it, and see if they feel anything 'weird' or 'out of the ordinary' and then ask them what they have felt. If you have successfully made a psi-ball, they will tell you that they felt either a hot/cold or magnetic feeling, as well as some of the other things in that very article you linked to. ( art-psiball-faq.html#feeli3 )
