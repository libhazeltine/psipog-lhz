Auto Pilot writes:
"I'm new to psionics, and I wanted to know.... why does it take
learning the basics to do other things quicker? They seem to have
a little related, but not very much. How does it make it easier?"

My comments: You don't have to learn the basics to learn the
more advanced skills. But it helps. Think of it like any other
skill - when you first start to learn to play soccer, you don't
start with complex psyche out moves. You start with kicking the
ball with correct form, passing accurately, etc. In the same
sense, a lot of psionics requires the ability to focus your mind
100% on a task without distraction, strong visualization skills,
and the ability to alter your state of consciousness. If you
learn these basics, then you can build off of them in different
directions. You can take your trancing skill and have it help
you with Remote Viewing - or you can take that same trancing
skill and have it help you with Telepathy.

Truekimbo writes:
"Hello, I thought I'd share some of the things I've learned NOT
to do with psionics.
1) Don't link to people that are obviously crazy or poeple with
obvious emotional disorders. I've gotten short term disconcerning
effects from looking too closely at crazy homeless people.
2) DON'T TRY TOO HARD. If you are experiencing more discomfort
than you would when doing something simple you already know how
to do, stop and practice on something more simple.
3) Be careful with mirror shields; I've gotten weird effects
from making them inside out, or from trying to send stuff out
through them.
4) Always practice whatever shield you want to try out on a
smaller psiball level scale first. If you can't accurrately
program a psiball to act like you want your shield to, don't
attempt anything on a large scale.
5) Lastly, just focus on having fun. If you're trying to do
something that is serious or has too much emotional importance to
you, you increase the chances of going temporarily overboard."

My comments: Thanks :-). Great tips :-).

Gabi writes:
"In newsletter 8 there was a letter that was talking about how to
tell if a psi ball is really there and not just blood rushing to
our hands. I think I might have found a way that works, though
it is a bit inaccurate if you are looking for numbers. Basically
what you so is: Test the air temperature with your hands and
make sure that all the air around you feels the same. Then you
hold your hands away from your body and away from each other,
that way you won't feel the heat from anything but your psi ball,
if it happens to be heat that comes. Next you make your psi
ball. Since your hands are apart it may be harder to do, but aim
for the center of the gap between your hands. No, you don't need
your hands but it helps with visualizing a psi ball for some.
Now you remove your hands but try to keep the psi ball in place.
Move one hand around and feel the air around you before moving to
the area where you made your psi ball. There should be a change
in the way the air feels around your psi ball, whether it be
warmer or colder."

My comments: Wow, this is a really good idea. I can't believe
I've never thought of it! :-P I hope you don't mind if I
recommend this test to people in the future :-).

meangelnow writes:
"I've been praticing psi-wheel for 3 or 4 weeks but still I don't
understand what is the problem? I've done psi-ball, meditation
and even though I try my best I still have no luck. My first
method was just to look at it and visulize it moving while I
relax and that didn't work so now my new method is to just see it
in my head like you would see it in the tunnel. I haven't give up
yet even though at time I just wanna break the damn thing. My
question is how long does it take to see reslut on the psi-wheel
or should I just forget about it and move on to othere things.
Anyway I hope you can help me figure out what's the problem is
and make my day little bettter. Thank you!"

My comments: The first problem I see is you've been practicing
for only 3 to 4 weeks. Granted, some people get it quicker than
that, but others take a lot longer. It took me about 1.5 years
of on and off practice. If you are bored with the psi-wheel,
give yourself a break for a week or two and try something else.
Personally, I usually go between TK and OBEs. When I get bored
of TK, I move to OBEs... when I get bored of OBEs, I move back
to TK. If you're feeling upset and angry, then it isn't going to
work. Stay relaxed, try to have fun, and keep upbeat about it.
