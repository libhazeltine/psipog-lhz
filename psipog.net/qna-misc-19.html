Rainsong replies on January 21, 2004, 6:06pm:

"Psi" is whatever mechanism allows such effects as psychokinesis and telepathy to occur, in simple terms. Therefore, if you can make those effects happen using a spell, chaos magick, ki, or your own personal army of imaginary purple dinosaurs, then you are using "psi". If your spell, chaos magick, ki, or personal army of imaginary purple dinosaurs cannot/doesn't produce such effects you are not using "psi".

Psi is not specific to any religion, nor is it a religious concept. "Wicca" is therefore not part of the equation at all.
