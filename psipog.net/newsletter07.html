Matthew asks:
"How do you use psi to health yourself of any ordinary sickness?
Eg. cold, flu, fever etc."

My comments: Since I'm not the brightest guy to ask about
healing, I pasted the question to annie, and this is what she
replied with:

- - - - -

This is a great question as we in the northern hemisphere enter
into the "wheasy sneezy season". I, however, have some bad news
for all of you. There is no cure for the common cold. I'll give
you a moment to digest that bad news.

Cure or not, there are things we can all do using psionics and
other homeopathic methods to alleviate some of the more annoying
and painful symptoms of the common cold and flu. As always, psi
is awesome for healing, but doctors are better. If you have any
preexisting conditions, or your symptoms don't go away in a
reasonable time frame, SEE YOUR DOCTOR.

Reducing your fever should be your first step if you have one.
While a fever is the body's natural reaction for healing
infection or virus, it also causes most of the uncomfortable side
effects of said. This is simple Bio-PK. Relax. Breathe. Get
into a state of comfort and rest. Slow down your heart. Follow
your breath. Sense and feel your blood - how it moves, it's
warmth, what the current task is. Focus on the affected areas of
the virus or infection. Say to yourself, inside your head our
aloud, "It is time to cool down. It is time for each of my
organs and for my blood to relax, to cool off, to be at its most
optimum for the most beneficial healing of my body." Visualize
if you want. Make something up that works for you. Cool breeze,
a shot of ice water through the veins, whatever. See my other
healing articles for tips on healing headaches, stiff backs and
necks, and other cold/flu symptoms. Keep up the vitamins and
fluids. Your multivitamin should include high doses of A, C, and
E. If you have an iron supplement around the house, that will
help too. And as always, water water water!

-annie
