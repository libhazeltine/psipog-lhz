I know that a lot of weird things happen for untold reasons and people I talk to might be hiding the fact that they can hear me through ESP, I mean I hear just about anyone I come in contact with, and they respond (either through physical movements or through their thoughts), BUT the fact still remains that I hear people's thoughts, on a different level that you might think. QUESTION am I crazy???? What do you think!

Peebrain replies on September 14, 2005, 1:13pm:

You might be crazy. But you might not be . Take serious time to examine yourself to see what is so crazy about what you're saying. Think logically. Don't think of just your perspective - think of other people's perspectives too. I doubt people would hide facts from you on purpose. Relax and take it easy.

One good way to test whether you're crazy or not is to try and VERIFY what you hear. For example, ask someone to think of a fruit. Then see if you can hear what they're thinking, and guess it correctly. If you think that they'll lie, tell them to write it down before you guess. That way they can't change the answer after you tell them what you think. Some people do lie - but if you can get them to write it down FIRST, then they can't pull any tricks on you.
