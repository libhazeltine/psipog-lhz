Last week's Activity:

While attending daily life take a minute to pause and look at
your surroundings. For example : Look out a window and try to
obtain as much detail as you can of the area you see. When
ready, close your eyes and take as much time as you need to
re-create the image in your head. Try to include as much as you
can. With practice you will more easily be able to recall
objects in places you have already visited and thus increasing
your ability to visualize.

Veno asks:

I had only started working with psi about last year. I
had made one psi ball in my room and in an elevator I
could feel the energy moving through my arms, forming the ball
and felt the ball in my hands. Now I can barely feel anything
at all whether it be the energy traveling to my hands or the
energy in my chest and the ball in my hands.
What could be the problem? Does the fact that I
started during a time when it was raining outside play
any role at all?

Also only after I had started working on psionics my
hands have been doing this thing in which they start
tingling in the pores. I want to know if this has happened
to any other people and if not why is it happening ?

Thank you for your time

My comments: At first most people are more sensitive due to
the fact that it is a new stimulus, such as when you first
go swimming in cold water, and eventually you don't feel
the temperature of the water any longer. Or when you first come
in to an air conditioned room after being in the heat all day.
Unless it is something different from you have normally been
experiencing. Physical feelings are a bit harder to ignore
rather than such things as hot/cold stimulus. But after a
constant of application of such physical stimulus, we don't
focus on it as much as we would a newer one. Such as,
you do not focus on your clothes all day long, but if you
think about them, you can feel them. The same works for
energy, the more you focus on energy, the better you can
feel/sense it. There is a guide to sensitivity you can find
here : art-psiball-source.html7 That should help
alot on getting your feel for energy back. As for rain having
to do with anything....it seems the only affect the rain had
was to keep you inside long enough to try psi out.

Also, it is common to feel energy in your hands, or tingling
when you start working with energy manipulation, as you
are getting used to the whole new feelings and control.

Kiss expresses:

My weridest attempt for an OBE occured about 5 days ago.
Here's what happened: I went to sleep and stuff. I had a lucid
dream and I wanted to leave my physical body, but got stuck. I
heard whirling sounds, and wanted to know what was behind all
this. So I asked "who are you?". Suddenly, VERY heavy
vibration were induced and my heart beat faster. I was resisting
and finally I woke up. Very werid experience. Words can't
express what I mean. Had anything similar to this?

My Comments: The only thing I've had similiar to this would be
what I classify as Sleep paralysis. I would normally wake up
from the middle of another dream, and then I would be laying
there and all of a sudden my mind would shift to maybe ghosts
or the fact of it being dark in the room. At this point, an
overwhelming feeling of fear/another presence would sweep over
me, usually followed by horrible sounds of various types, from
such things as tv static to slow voice like sounds. Normally,
I would recognize it for what it was, and let it pass, since
in reality it seemed to be a state of being half awake while
in a sense dreaming at the same time. I've never worried any of
it as malicious, and have probably had some that would make most
people assume something evil is out to get them. In my
experience I would have to say, that it is simply a point
where you don't fully wake up, and the part that does is
normally bombarded with dream-like images and sounds, creating
the illusion of something real.
