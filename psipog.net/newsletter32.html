My Comments: Well this is certainly a good combination for
finding some things out. You have one guy who can OBE, and
another who says he can see the other in OBE form. If you
are wanting to get more information about what is going on
personal experience is always the best for teaching ourselves.
You should work on being more passive, and maintaining a
calm state throughout the OBE process. If all of them happen
while you are asleep, work on telling yourself before you go
to sleep, that when such things happen, you are going to
remain calm, and try and associate with the events going on.
This way when the actual event occurs, your brain may decide
to relate your messages from earlier to what you are
experiencing, and this may help you remember to stay calm. As
for the hot/cold issue, not all ghost cases involve a drop
in temperature. This is just a cliche event most people
try and associate with all ghost, when in actuality
in some cases people report a rise in temperature. It all
depends on the situation, really.

Mortal Abbattoir wonders:
I was wondering what to do about excitement during OBE's. Alot
of times, I will lay there.. get the minidreams.. then find
myself floating above my body, and something I find or see that
I was unable to in my physical body excites me to the point of
returning back to my body. For example.. once I was floating
above my body while my back and head lay against the wall. I
get closer to my head and move the hair out of the way and see
an extremely bright pea-sized thing right in the center of my
forehead. The moment I saw it, I exclaimed "My third eye!" and
came back to the physical.

Any techniques you know of on how to not get so excited?

My comments: As I mentioned earlier, you can work on telling
yourself you will remain calm. Also since you seem to be
working more with them from being awake, it will be a natural
reaction the more you have for the novelty to wear off of that
initial excitement. After this happens, you will notice that
they will last a bit longer. At this point OBEs normally tend
to end due to random unexplained reasons or you get frustrated
because of something that happens, such as once when I got
stuck in a chair. Extreme emotion tend to be the reason my OBEs
end. So just work on staying passive, and it will eventually
come with time.

but it doesn't seem to be proof enough for me. I just can't
figure out why it won't work, then I get frustrated and give up.

On another note, I notice that I have my own way of
meditating that I've never seen anyone else do, but...people say
their meditation doesn't work as well as mine. Obviously, this
is either because they're bad at it, or because my method works,
but I was wondering what you had to comment on it. What I do
is I try to make sure I'm in a quiet place, and I either sit
cross-legged or I lay flat on my back with a few inches between
each of my legs and between my hands and body. Then, I close my
eyes to fully minimize sensory input, and visualize my body in a
third-person view. I see colors flowing through my body, because
this is how I visualize emotion (red is anger, yellow is
happiness, blue is sadness, etc., etc., etc.) and I will it
to come together, normally in my hands. Then, I merely will it
to leave my body, leaving me devoid of emotion, then I can focus
on whatever task I have at hand, normally talking one of my
friends through a problem, because those are the ones that
frustrate me the most.

My comments: First off, I myself, still don't truly believe
half of the things I do. I'm not speaking figuratively from
some other perspective like I am amazed at what I do. I'm
saying that truly when I think about it, I don't accept it
as easily as I do that this or that exist. I don't think I will
fully accept it until I do something so utterly amazing that I
myself cannot deny. This here is where personal opinions come
into play. While bob may simply need to spin a psiwheel before
he believes in it, Jim an avid believer in Science has many
ideas of why it spun on natural causes rather than of his
ability. It all comes down to personal beliefs, and overcoming
the ones that you don't favor. But with all this said, and you
mention yourself that you are still able to accomplish PK
without fully believing that you are doing it. I see belief
as an acquired thing, at first they assumed the world was flat
but until adequate proof, and a few years of idea being around
did they accept it was round. So don't let the lack of belief
stop you from doing something you have seen yourself do.
