Having PK work a time period after you have stopped practicing is not unusual. For a period of 3 weeks or so, it doesn't matter much, but as you give up practicing for longer and longer periods of time, the TK ability starts to decrease (although you mentally know what you need to do, you are psionically unable due to lack of practice). The mindset for psionics is one of "paradoxical compound of detachment and excitement" to quote one of the books I'm reading at the moment (a scientific study on telepathy and ESP in relation to micro-psychokinesis).
