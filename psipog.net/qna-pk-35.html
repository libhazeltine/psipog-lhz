stevetb2001 asks on December 13, 2003, 2:59pm:

Has telekinesis to do with Poltergeist?

Rainsong replies on December 14, 2003, 6:09pm:

When a person produces telekinetic effects which they are not controlling, by accident or on account of "stress" or during convulsions, this causes a variety of "poltergeist" phenomenon. Overloaded telepaths and teenagers with -ehem- "hormonal problems" are the usual culprits. "Poltergeist" comes from the German term for "noisy ghost", and the experience was once attributed to --yes, you guessed it-- ghosts. Whether _all_ poltergeists are attributable to out-of-control pk is still open to question.
