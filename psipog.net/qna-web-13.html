Peebrain replies on November 1, 2003, 7:33pm:

Actually, I'm not as anal as I used to be about the usernames. Back in the day there used to be a large community of DBZers who went around thinking they were Goku and whoever else, and thought that the show was real, and that you could fly around and blow up buildings if you NEVER GAVE UP!!! Etc... Basically, a bunch of kiddies running around with over-active imaginations. With a lot of effort of KMiller and his snide-snipering, he converted a lot of idiots into normal-thinking human beings (and pissed a lot of other people off). So, that rule is a reflection of the "No DBZer idiots"-concept. Now, it's not so easy to find a DBZer, because of the strict rules and moron-bashing that we used to do. So the rule seems a bit odd. Back in the day it was useful though, now it's not so useful. So to answer your question: yeah, go ahead and make up any nickname you want. Just don't make it DBZ .
