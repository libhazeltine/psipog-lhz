Rainsong replies on June 4, 2004, 5:01pm:

Typing telekinetically _Can_ be done, but it is very slow, and awkward. Typing accurately is difficult, because the pressure must be placed precisely, to avoid hitting neighbouring keys.

Hacking directly into the computer, bypassing the keyboard entirely, can also be done. Strangely enough, this tends to be the easier route. You do run the usual risks of any telekinetic activity, of course, plus the added "bonus" of the possibility of frying your computer.

It is also possible to imprint programming directly onto a disc (CD, floppy, etc) using psionics. It must be said, however, that typing it in the old-fashioned way is a _lot_ easier.
