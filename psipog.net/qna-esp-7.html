Block_Head asks on June 12, 2005, 1:02am:

I was wondering about meditation. I find it very hard and wanted to ask a few questions.
1)what method do YOU use to meditate?
2)could you explain it in detail?(position,thoughts,breathing,ect.)
3)how long it took YOU to get good at meditation.
4)any advice to a begginer at meditation.

SunTzu replies on June 13, 2005, 1:12pm:

I use a simple count-down method of meditating. I usually find a comfortable position (easy chair/couch), sit down, close my eyes and take a few deep breaths. After that, each time I take a breath I will count a number (starting at 20 and counting down to one). Example:
*breath in* 20 *breath out*
*breath in* 19 *breath out*
at each step, clearing away more thoughts, and focusing only on the numbers.
After that, I go through the colors (Red Orange Yellow Green Blue Indigo Violet) picturing each color as clearly as I can, as a visualization exercise. Ending with white, I am in a meditative state.

Took me at least a month to get a decent meditation out of it (but I was doing it on a bus-ride that I had to take every morning)

Advice? Stick with it, it can be a very helpful tool in your psionic ability list. A meditation before a practice can make a practice be much more worthwhile.
