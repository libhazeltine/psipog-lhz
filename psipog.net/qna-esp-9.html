shadow_born07 asks on April 26, 2005, 6:48pm:

what is the difference between "remote viewing" and "remote pressence"? also, which is harder to master? and which is more useful?

SunTzu replies on April 26, 2005, 7:19pm:

Different people master different things at different rates. Additionally, remote viewing is more of a set procedure (with explicit standards that were set by the government) for viewing long-distance.

Remote presence is more of a free-form technique that allows the viewer to have more of a 'feel' of the place that remote viewing does.

In short, in comparison to actual senses, Remote Viewing would be seeing and Remote Presence would be seeing+feeling+(sometimes other senses).

Remote Viewing, I feel, is more accurate (and proven). Remote Presence is faster and can potentially be more accurate (with other senses). However, it all depends on the viewer and his/her skill with each technique.

(in short, do what you do best )
