Rainsong replies on August 1, 2004, 2:16pm:

In common with most psionics terms, the meaning of "Empath" varies somewhat with the experience of the writer/speaker. Generally, it means "someone who experiences other people's emotions" and/or "someone who can cause other people to experience his/her emotions". The "vibes" thing tends to be associated with psychometry, also known as retrocognition. That said, all the perceptive abilities overlap to some degree, and may be lumped together under "psychic phenomena".
