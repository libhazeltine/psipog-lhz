This has been bothering me for some time now.

If I telepathically 'talk' to a dog, will the dog understand what I am saying? Could it understand and know what every word I am saying means? Could the dog talk to me back? I have read about how some scientists think dogs have some forms of Psychic stuff, like seeing spirits. Could this mean they CAN do Telepathy?

Rainsong replies on June 30, 2005, 2:03pm:

Some dogs are pretty good at picking up stuff, telepathically, and others are not so good at it. They aren't that different from humans, in that regard. Whether or not s/he _understands_ what you are saying will depend heavily on how you are conveying it. If s/he understands all the words when you speak aloud, there's a good chance s/he'll understand them when they are Sent, presuming the Sending is clear. If s/he doesn't understand them, the method of communication won't make much difference. This is why it is generally easier to use images, "movies" and the like when communicating telepathically with someone who does not understand your language, regardless of species....of course, if the species has no concept of vision, images won't avail you much, either.

Hi people,
I just like to ask if anyone had come across the distinct similarities between psi practices i.e. remote viewing,telepathy and the symptoms experienced in schizophrenia or extreme psychosis i.e. hearing voices and experiencing auditory and visual hallucinations. Is there really an obvious difference betwixt parapsychology and psychology or are they in fact one and the same?
yours wondering,
Sonny

Rainsong replies on June 28, 2005, 11:43pm:

The simularities have been noticed, yes. In fact, many people who are afflicted with certain kinds of mental illnesses believe that their symptoms are actually psionic in nature, rather than symptoms of their illness. Indeed, some of these people refuse to believe that they are ill. There are some differences between the "hearing voices" experienced by a telepath, and the "hearing voices" experienced by a schizophrenic. The first, and most obvious, of these is that it is possible to verify the telepath's perceptions. Difficult, sometimes, but possible. On the other hand, the question is complicated by the fact that some telepaths are also schizophrenic.

"Parapsychology" is more properly a province of Physics than of Psychology, these days.
