PSI41 asks on December 6, 2003, 9:52am:

Before I knew about psienergy my parents and even myself noticed I had the ability to sense an emotion another person was feeling, they also said I was very good at it ,my first question is can you still be a psionic when born and have parents who are non Psi. My other question is that after a while my abilty started to get weaker and now i can barely do it anymore, is their a way I can retrain my mind to do it as well as It used to be?

Rainsong replies on December 6, 2003, 3:14pm:

As for your first question:

1) "Sensing" the emotions of other people is not restricted to psychic/psionic ability. If you _don't_ pick up on the jist of what other people are feeling, at least occasionally, you're likely to be a sociopath ("path" in this case referring to "sick" rather than "feeling"). So, you do not give enough data in your note for us to say whether you are empathic in the "psionic" sense of the word.

2) It is possible to be born with psionic talent, to parents lacking such abilities, in theory. In practice, the question is more complicated than that. One of the complications is that there is considerable disagreement over how much psionic ability needs to present in a person before that person is considered "Gifted"/natural/(insert semi-derogatory term of choice here). A goodly number of supposedly not-psionic people have had evidence of some small degree of psionic activity.

Concerning your second question, the simplest answer is to do as other folks do who wish to acquire or re-acquire a skill: practice.

Rainsong replies on November 26, 2003, 12:23am:

Feeling like you're reliving an experience like that is often a case of deja vu. One of the common explanations for the psychic version of this is the simple expedient of half-forgetting a precognitive dream, then being startled when the scene unfolds in real life.

The cats crossing the road at night probably indicates the cats' desire to be on the other side of the street, perhaps in search of mice, or feline companionship. Black is one of the more common coat colours.

"Negative energy"? Interesting concept...Energy is the capacity to do work. *shrugs* Making lights blink or fade is fairly common with minor pk, intentional or otherwise.
