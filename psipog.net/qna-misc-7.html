shadow_born07 asks on April 24, 2005, 2:08am:

hi, a couple days ago i walked into my bathroom and i turned on the light and both the light bulbs blew at exactly the same time. this has happened to me several times over the last few years. is there anyway to prevent this without shielding? i'm a noob and can't quite shield yet.

Rainsong replies on April 24, 2005, 4:16pm:

First of all, you might want to get the wiring checked, if the bulbs are burning out more than they should. The quality and type of bulb are other obvious factors. For example, there are light bulbs designed to better withstand power-surges and brown-outs "out in the country", than the standard (and usually cheaper) city bulbs. Hardware stores generally can get both kinds. You might also consider that you changed the bulbs at the same time, and they would have approximately the same "lifespan".
