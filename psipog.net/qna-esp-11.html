Actually, this is not as uncommon of an occurence as you may think it is. In fact, we even have a name for it here. We call it "telepathic (or empathic) overload)". This is defined as when you are being bombarded with the thoughts (or emotions) of those around you to the point where it is difficult to feel your own thoughts/emotions. As Sean (peebrain) says, the benefits of using these abilities far outweight the risks and inconviences, so instead of being discouraged about your burst, be encouraged that you are getting better. And, of course, practice shielding when you are in larger crowds now ;)
