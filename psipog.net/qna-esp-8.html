Heolstor asks on May 7, 2005, 7:09am:

hi.. dunno if this has already been ask but here goes... can telepathy be used to find out if someone is psychic or something?? and how would you know if someone is reading your mindwithout permission? thanks in advance :)

Rainsong replies on May 7, 2005, 12:18pm:

1) Not as far as I know, unless the person in question already knows s/he is psychic. Can it be used to ask someone if s/he is psychic? Yes. Can it be used to determine whether someone who doesn't know if s/he is psychic is indeed psychic? I don't think so, but someone out there may have figured out a way to do it.

2) There are too many variables. Generally, if you have to ask this question, you probably _won't_ know if someone is reading your mind, with or without permission, if the person is at all competent and being careful. If someone is doing it rather clumsily, most people would feel it, but they might or might not know what was happening. A surface scan of the field pattern done particularly clumsily can feel like a (slightly less clumsy) reading. On the other end of the scale, a particularly careful and experienced telepath can read all but the most sensitive of folks with no discernable trace of their activity.
