Mahgirb asks on April 21, 2004, 7:50pm:

I have been trying to make a psiball for a couple of days now and it just is not working! i am now getting frustrated and so i calm down and try again but it isn't working what should i do?

Rainsong replies on April 21, 2004, 9:36pm:

We get this question rather frequently, and there is very little variation in the wording.

First, re-read the instructions for making a psiball. In fact, read several different sets of instructions for making a psiball. The little spheroids have become very popular over the past several years, and you'll find many (slight) variations on the general theme.

Then, once you've read them. See if you understand them. Try paraphrasing the directions.

Third, try it again.

On the other hand, _making_ a construct and _perceiving_ a construct are two separate abilities. Have you checked to see if anyone _else_ perceived your results or lack thereof?
