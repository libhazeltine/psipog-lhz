Rainsong replies on February 3, 2005, 3:26pm:

As far as I can tell, from personal experience and the reported experience of friends and co-workers, it makes no difference. I've seen a few websites that claimed that the crystals must be flawless and shaped, but those same websites were selling shaped "crystals", and usually plain glass "crystals", at that!

Personally, I favour beach pebbles. However, that is a matter of convenience; I live near the ocean and beach pebbles are easy to come by. Quartz is very, very common. Most beach sands are mostly --or entirely-- quartz.
