It doesn't work! What am I doing wrong?

 There could be a million different reasons why its not "working." Most likely, it IS working and the Psi Ball just isn't strong enough to feel yet. Other reasons is that you don't have enough practice. Don't read the How-To and automatically think it will work the first time. Practice for one or two days - if you don't feel anything after that, you might not be doing it right. Read the step-by-step part carefully and do exactly what it says. Maybe its not working because your visualizations arn't solid enough, maybe because you arn't trying hard enough, maybe because you have other stress in your life that is distracting you or maybe because you arn't waiting long enough. Sometimes I work on ONE Psi Ball for 45 minutes. Try it. Just dont give up!

Why can't I feel it?

 Because it's not strong enough. Keep practicing and keep compacting the ball and make it denser and denser. Try not to break your concentration. Don't be distracted by things around you. Close your eyes if its to distracting with your eyes open. Play some soft music to realx you, or don't play any music at all if that will break your concentration. Practice and it will come. Also, you might be feeling it, your just ignoring it thinking "Naa, that wasn't it." Trust yourself! Trust your feelings!

Why can't I see it?

 Oh, probably because its REALLY hard to make a visible one. If you are a beginner, don't wait around for it to be visible. The ones I make that I can see are made like any other one and happen when I least expect it. Sometimes when you have more emotion behind it, it will be stronger and visible. But don't think it is going to look like a big plastic ball in your hand. It can take on a lot of different forms:
  • Sparks*
  • Wavy, colored, worm-like lines*
  • Fog (like when you look at a popcicle and you see the fog coming off of it)*
  • Wavyness in the background (like when you look over a hot stove, you see the background gets all wavy)
  • Ball of light
The ones with the stars are the types Ive made. Im sure there are more. Why can't you see yours? Because you dont have enough practice!

What are the sensations I should feel?

 You should feel? Nothing is written in stone. You CAN feel a lot of different sensations. You don't nessicarily HAVE to feel them. Heres some I've read/felt:
  • Cold breeze*
  • Soft Warmness*
  • Pulling/pushing on your fingers*
  • Pulling/pushing your whole hands
  • Extreme heat (so much, you must stop)
  • Extreme cold (so much, you must stop)
  • Just a presence (hard to describe)*
Again, the ones with the stars next to them are the ones I've personally felt. If you've felt something a little different than the ones here, please tell me.

What do you mean "Just a presence"?

Oh, its like, umm... ya know when sometimes you just feel that someone is staring at you? Kinda like that but a little different. You just know for some strange reason. Intuition, your gut feeling.
