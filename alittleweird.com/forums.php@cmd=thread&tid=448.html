You are assuming that desire is what triggers a win in the lottery. The (few) cases I've found, there wasn't a strong desire.

posted by Mindful:
But what if the general rule is that the world IS random, but because of human conscious/desires/thoughts/emotions, the 'random' aspect becomes influenced and thus less random?
"Random" is a term used to mean either one of two things: 1. it's something we don't understand, so it just looks "random" to us, or 2. it's something with so many factors, that it's extremely hard to influence and predict, so it might as well be "random".

#2 isn't true in the case of the lottery, because I've won, and so have other people. #1 sounds like it would be true... and if so, then the solution is simply to understand the process, and it will no longer look "random".

Also, what do you think about the whole emotion aspect when it comes to IM? Has it been extremely important in your experience with LoA.
With the lottery? No. With IM in general? I think emotion plays a role, but I don't think it's the only factor. Intensity of will power seems to be more important - and emotion can play into intensity. But you can be calm and intense at the same time - so emotion doesn't always play a role. But it's still very unknown.
