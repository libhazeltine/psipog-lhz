Raathuus
Wow, Subliminal... that's a lot of stuff!

Anyways, you wanted some personal experience? Alright.

I'd recommend thinking about what you want to do most. Try what you want most first and see how well you are. If you find something easier and/or more fun, you can do that. Basically, the thinking of your priorities is a rough sketch that gets fine tuned when you actually go through with the things.

While I wouldn't quite say it's absolutely necessary to start with Energy Manipulation, I did and I found later that it was the most enjoyable thing to do just for the sake of it, especially since I went after psionics mostly for telekinesis. You shouldn't worry about being too skilled at EM. Just go until you get a sense that you're good enough for you. You, ultimately, are the judge.

I don't find it necessary to meditate to do stuff. Never have, don't plan to, either. I just go for it. You should do what works best for you, meditating or not.

To find time on when to do things, just find any moment that you can. If you can make a whole practice schedule, that's fine. I personally just find random moments throughout the day to just do what I can. If I'm waiting for something, I check out how the person over there is feeling. If I can't sleep and I'm bored, I move an impromptu psiwheel. I do what I can.

Hopefully I've helped. Nice to see new people around here :)
