Remembering Deja Vu
Author Message
SheepKing
Something strange that I notice happens to me is that I will be in a situation and not have the feeling of deja vu, but then moments later, I will remember what I had just seen and then get the feeling that I have seen it before, in the memory.

So, as if I was the person in the memory saying "Wow, this feels like deja vu."

I think I'm jacked up in the head :-P

Anyone else experience this? I find it very strange when it happens, because I'll know that the situation I am remembering I did not have the feeling of deja vu... And then all of a sudden it's as if I had missed something the first time around, and I saw that memory before. As if I've held it twice. It's actually kind of cool, just weird.
