I need to learn how to shield before school starts, or I'm gonna hurt...(raging hormones aren't the most fun thing in the world at a big school...)

I'm assuming that the reason why your constructs are not working must be due
to you thinking too many thoughts or something. This is why meditation/trancing is
important in psionics. The only tips for holding it I would say is just keep meditating
and trancing, and maybe self hypnosis too. As for building energy, I have read that
apparently if you work with your own energy you can hold more. Try shelling your
constructs too, and program it to hold in all the energy, and maybe block out all
thoughts not related to adding energy to the construct.
