Once a long time ago during a Ouija board session, I asked the "spirit" or ghost, whatever it was, to give me a sign that it was really there. I had two candles lit (both identicle and fairly new), one on the right and the other on the left. I asked the ghost to blow out the one on the left. The left candle started flickering a lot and the wax started to melt down faster than the one on the right. I held my nose and mouth beforehand to make sure it wasn't me accidently breathing on it. The candle never blew out but it was halfway shorter than the one on the right from all the flickering and melting before I told it to stop only about 5 minutes in.
I have a hard time believing in ghosts so I think maybe it was psychokinesis.

While writing "I thought I hadn't met an ghosts", I remembered this from when I was only a few years old:

I went into the old and dark barn house. I closed the door, turned around and saw something that looked like a lower half of my own reflection moving around in the dark room. I freaked out and escaped quickly.

While shopping in the department store this evening, I remembered this:

A year ago or so, when me and my father were among the few people in the store, I saw the shadow or reflection of a hand picking an apple from the back of the shelf (about 10 m away). It failed and the apple fell down from the shelf, onto the ground and rolled almost half a meter towards us. At first I thought that some shopkeeper was picking out stained apples and that we had startled them. But when I walked closer, I realized that in the back of the shelf there was a wall. It was weird - I was certain I had seen a hand coming out from there just a few moments ago..
