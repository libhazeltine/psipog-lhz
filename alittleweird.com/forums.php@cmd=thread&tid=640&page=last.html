Does your room reflect your mind?
Author Message
joke-lain
So, I am sitting in my room, on my bed, typing.

My room is a complete mess, but it has only been this way for a little while.

My mind has been in complete chaos since the last few months, and it has started to reflect itself in my room. I have a whole bunch of boxes in my room which represent the objects in my life. My dog and his bed takes up quite a bit of space in my room. He represents my family. My dog is actually represented by a stuffed dog I have on my bed. My bed represents my dreams and nightmares. I have a small collection of jewelry which represents vanity in my life. The Icons in my room represent my spiritual life. I have my first stuffed animal sitting in an Indian baby carrier in the corner of my room, that represents my childhood. My closet represents my secrets. My windows represent the outside world and the closed curtains on them represent me shutting the world out. My bike hanging from my ceiling represents travel, and the wolves on my walls represent my personality. And since my room is really dirty and messy, it represents how messed up I am in the head right now.

So, anyway. Tell what you guys think.

Interesting.

Some would say our environments area reflection of what's going on inside of us, but at the same time we are a reflection of the environment. It's a cycle that slowly happens; We affect the things around us with what is going on in our heads, and then the things that are going on outside affect what's going on in our heads continually.

Seems to me like you've let yourself affect your environment to the point that it is affecting you :-P.

Time to stop analyzing and take back control. Clean up your room to the point you would like your 'life' to be cleaned up and take it from there.
