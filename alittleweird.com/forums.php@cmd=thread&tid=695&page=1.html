During the last summer suddenly out of nowhere I saw two weird dreams.
In one of them, my best friend had passed away and I remember asking him for the reason while I was talking with his ghost.
The second dream: I saw myself waking up in a really beautiful graveyard. It was a foggy morning and I remember seeing all kinds of flowers everywhere. Because of the fog I could see nothing but the graveyard. While I was enjoying the graveyard and its beauty (I had (in the dream) and have (in reality) never seen such a beautiful graveyard before), I suddenly sensed my father coming. It was then that the fog lifted and father took me with him.

About two weeks ago I saw a dream where first our dog went crazy followed by other family members. When I was walking the dog, it killed someone I wasn't able to recognize in the dream. Then my grandfather killed it. After that my sister went crazy and tried to commit suicide - fortunately she failed. Some cities were in total chaos (full of people trying to kill themselves). <- the whole dream was a total mess..

Well, that may have been the result of playing Stalker. However, I usually memorize only the dreams that differ from the others (and I didn't see anything abnormal in the dreams before nor after the dream).

That was until yesterday.
I was slowly walking towards our tent in the woods when it began to rain. However, it wasn't a normal rain - it gave off a disgusting smell of sulphur & ammonium - and it could easily penetrate tree leaves and other thin materials. I arrived at the tent where others were hiding. They had somehow reinforced the walls of the tent so that it was safe in there. After the raining stopped, I saw a satellite recording the event and felt that the catastrophe had actually been caused by humans.

These have been the recent dreams that remind me of something weird coming. Actually, it feels weird that nothing seems to have happened yet..
