UltimateBlob
I too have experienced being conscious but unable to control anything in a lucid dream. I remember waking up (in my dream) and getting out of bed thinking, "hmm, this feels like I'm dreaming still" so I figured I'd prove it to myself by levitating myself - it didn't work so I went back to "sleep".

It's happened more than once and I think the reason you can't control anything is because you don't believe you can. I was pretty much convinced in my dream that I wasn't dreaming, but just to check I tried levitating. However when I'm completely aware that I'm definitely dreaming it's much easier to affect things.

So take a moment to say "I am definitely dreaming" and find a way to be sure of it before trying to make cool stuff happen, 'cause if you try and it doesn't work, you'll probably just convince yourself you're actually awake.
