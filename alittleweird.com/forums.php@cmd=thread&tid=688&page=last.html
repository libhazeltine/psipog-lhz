Creating Vibrations
Author Message
Saiya
So on Saturday, I was making another attempt to astral project. I was laying in bed for about any hour until my mind started to blank. I somehow felt like I had no need for a body, so I sort of subconsciously imagined my astral body, being light blue, and pulling my astral body out of my bodies arms. Then I started feeling vibrations overcome me. Now these vibrations, though supposedly normal, was totally different than usual for me. I felt like my entire body was violently shaking as if I just been struck by lightning. Normally the vibrations for me are a more soothing falling/floating pressure sensation. But this time is was abrupt, and unlike normal I didn't see a bright light. This leads me to believe that because the other times, I was trying to reach the astral plane, I saw a bright light, but this time, I was trying to have an OBE, which is why there is a distinct difference. However, I didn't have time to think logically at that moment. Since the feeling was so new to me, I couldn't keep a passive mind, so the vibrations began to fade. Then I remembered reading somewhere that this feeling was normal in trying to have an OBE. So I was like "I can't give up now!". Somehow, I created vibrations. At first I thought I created the vibration from my throat, or somewhere near there. I don't know WHERE exactly my throat is. However, after I explained this to one of my friends, they showed me this image of the chakra map, and he asked "This? The blue one."
http://www.rickrichards.com/chakras/chakra_man1.jpg

That was exactly where I felt the vibrations coming from! So I sort of just "knew" how to create vibrations, like moving my fingers and arms. It was internal, and natural. A natural control over energy I guess. However, after a while the vibrations began to die down again. I guess I became too excited and totally lost my passive state of mind. I felt so close! :[ However, this brought lots of hope back for me, made it seem a lot less "impossible" to have an OBE. Infact, I'm pretty confident I'll atleast have one within the next three months :). I'll keep on practicing.
