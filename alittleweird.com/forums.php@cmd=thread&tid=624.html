Akio
Personally, Micro Pk isn't difficult, I have done it, can do it, etc. As for the big stuff, I often doubt it's existence simply because of lack of proof. In theory, however; I believe that it requires a total of 3 things to work.

1.) Guidance (something to guide the force, i.e. you)

2.) Energy (change requires energy, it's physics.)

3.) A focal point (Some way for you to focus all of the energy onto one object.)

A good analogy that would explain why this should work is a magnifying glass. Normally, you can Stand in the sun and effect what gets more heat and less heat based of your physical position. This doesn't mean that you can light something on fire, unless of course, you have a magnifying glass. The Magnifying glass adds a focal point to the equation, which means you can focus the suns energy onto a single point, and cause the fire.

The same Idea applied to Psionics would tell you that you can effect slight degrees of change (i.e. chance), but to really do damage requires a Psionic "magnifying glass"

I do believe that Macro-PK is possible, I just haven't had the opportunity to see it or experience it for myself, so I can't say conclusively that it works. So much to do so little time, I guess it all comes down to whether you believe or not in the end of things. Meh... to each their own.
