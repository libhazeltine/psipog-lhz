Raathuus
I personally never trusted the "orb" phenomena too much. One point I would make that has been said by many a skeptic of the orbs is that there may have been something on the lens. What were the weather conditions? It looks a bit cloudy since there's a lack of bright sun and it's cool enough to wear a jacket. If there was some moisture, there could have been condensation.

If it isn't real, I wouldn't call this an outright hoax; I doubt it's anything intentional. PizzaBoy's friend, as far as I know, would have no reason to deceive us.

I'd call this inconclusive. Just as many other things like this, there's isn't enough evidence available to us to weigh solidly in either direction.

The closest I've come up with after a quick Google Image search is this: http://www.greatdreams.com/orbs/east_cem_spirit_orb_big.jpg. Obviously there are some differences, such as that this one appears to be more pinkish/purplish than yours, which is whitish/grayish. On the same page as this, there were "orb" pictures that are generally the same as every other "orb" photo. Interestingly enough, they try to debunk that the "orbs" are just dust, but only test if the dust is floating around. They tossed dust in the air and took a photo, which obviously doesn't look like the "orbs". They did appear foolish to me when they took a picture with some dust within an inch of the camera, looking just like orbs ( http://www.greatdreams.com/orbs/100_0121.JPG), and stating:

"So, its pretty obvious that the dust has to be practically on the lens of the camera to show orbs of light from the camera flash.

"One has to admit that dust creates orbs of light, but in photos of other people, the cameras are many feet away from the orbs, they are not close to the camera at all," which completely ignores the fact that the if the dust is a bit smaller, it will look like it's farther away.

Back to yours. One question I have that would be hard to answer would be why only that picture out of all taken would have something in it. And why your cousin? If it were your recently deceased grandfather, did they have a better relationship with that cousin than any other people?
