For those of you who don't know about the Enneagram (Most of you, I bet), the Enneagram (meaning 9 cornered figure) is a system of 9 personality types. They are arranged in the Enneagram itself. Everyone has a personality type and a wing (one of the two types adjacent to your type)
Here's what the Enneagram looks like:

Each number represents a personality type.

The types are as follows:
9- The Peacemaker
1- The Reformer
2- The Helper
3- The Achiever
4- The Individualist
5- The Investigator
6- The Loyalist
7- The Enthusiast
8- The Challenger

You can find out more type specific information as well as a free sample test here: http://www.enneagraminstitute.com

There's a decent amount of information about the Enneagram on the web, and in my experience, the Enneagram Institute is the best source.

I don't know if this really qualifies as weird, but it certainly is interesting.

I'm a type 9 with a 1 wing, just by the way. Take the test, and post your results, if you want.
Use this thread to discuss anything Enneagram related.
