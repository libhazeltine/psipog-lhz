The Purple Bishop
Author Message
Psikillion
During my work in a Boy Scout camp in Northeastern Pennsylvania this week my friends and I have heard of this ghost in another Boy Scout camp about an hour away from our camp called the "Purple Bishop". From the details I have heard so far it apparently is purple in color and shows only from the torso up, kills people who are not Christians, and will only ask if someone is a Christian if they are wearing a crucifix. It lives on the eastern side of the camp, which happens to be across a creek and that section of camp has been abandoned due to "insurance reasons". This area of camp also tends to have other minor apparitions, and rumor has it there are Satanists that also frequent those parts as well. About 80% of the staff apparently have had some sort of paranormal encounter.

Unfortunately we have not been able to find out more about the "Purple Bishop" and his origin, and google has not been very helpful information wise about it.

Ever since hearing the story we have decided that we want to remove the ghost from the area, so we are making a plan of how to get rid of it.

Just wanted to post about it to see what you guys think about it, what sort of ideas you might have for removing it, and whether you are interested in coming along too when we get around to doing this.
