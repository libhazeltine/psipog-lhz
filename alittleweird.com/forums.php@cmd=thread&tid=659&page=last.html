Strange spontaneous meditation
Author Message
Linked
I just had an odd spontaneous meditation. It started and I was standing at like a little Japanese shrine on a snowy mountain or something. Then I realized I was floating in the air and started to fly around alittle bit. Then something in a small canyon caught my eye so I flew down and ended up in a cave. Every wall of the cave was completely covered in large shards of emerald. I floated to the back of the cave and saw a huge piece of emerald jutting from the ground, it was taller than me and I'm about 5' 6" or something. I touched the emerald with both of my hands and got this weird sensation that I was more aware of my surroundings.
Anyone know what the emerald cave could be?
