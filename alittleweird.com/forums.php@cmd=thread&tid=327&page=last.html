Mmm, I had a rather interesting dream last night...just a dream, no vision, but it had an odd...feeling about it. Sort of mysterious and mystical...

It was dusk in the artic, or someplace like it. There was a small little camp on a sort of icy plateau, and behind the camp was a steep, vertical wall of ice, sort of like a glacier I guess. The light was fading fast and the aura was shimmering between various hues in the sky, though the remaining light of day and the light from various fires made it pretty easy to see what was going on.

There were eight people, I don't remember their features too well, but they were all adults and were wearing heavy pelts and furs, sort of like what you see Eskimos wear. They were lined up in front of the glacier, parallel to it. A small crowd was watching them. It was dark over by the glacier, but the eight people seemed to give off a faint cyan glow...they weren't glowing, but the air around them sort of was...it's hard to explain. Anyways, they all did something, and floated up into the air, stopping maybe eight feet up. Some of them appeared to be straddling the air, but most just floated there as if they were standing on solid ground. There were some cheers and someone declared "...successfully levitated 0.6 meters off the ground", though they were definitely higher up than 0.6 meters...

I don't remember much after that. I think they just floated there for a while, then the scene flashed to some kids playing soccer on the ice, and that's all I can remember. I don't know how I know this, but I know that the people in my dream were conducting some sort of psionic experiment and it had gone well...it also seemed they were all trying to connect with something...spiritual or other-worldly (again, I don't know how I know this).

Pretty freaky dream. It was quite vivid and I've been thinking of it all day... >.>
