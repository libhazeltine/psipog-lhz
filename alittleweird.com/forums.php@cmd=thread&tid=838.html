Insectoid3
I think the reason that people would support this at all is just because we like to have social classes. It provides another facet to some people's ego, while it gives other people a convenient excuse if they're not very good at a particular field of psionics.

Imagine that you're a gifted telekinetic. You've been practicing for years, but you still make it plainly evident that telekinesis runs in your genes. Even if it took you years of practice, you now have that "special gene" that a lot of people don't have. You have the telekinetic abilities to back up this claim, and so other people will believe you, and they'll think that they won't be as good at telekinesis because they don't have this gene.

Basically, it's a way to explain certain people's inherent psionic abilities while making skilled people feel good about themselves and the poor psionics feel like they don't need to be (or shouldn't try to be) as good.
