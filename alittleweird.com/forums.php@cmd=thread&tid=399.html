So last night I tried a binaural beat that was designed specifically to help induce an OBE. i've tried other binaural beats with some interesting results so i though why not?


the whole beat was 30 minutes long

it started off quite similar to my normal technique when i have always tried (and failed) to induce an obe. my body slowly went to sleep and became numb then the beats frequency dropped and some weird things began to happen.

I had random muscle twitching and i became a little light headed. the frequency then began to pick up again and it felt like my whole body was rotating counter clockwise and after awhile it felt like i was sliding to the left it became so intense that i became a little nauseous (i get motion sick easily)

the frequency changed again and the motion stopped. then my body felt like it became tense (couldnt really tell because i was completely numb at this point) but then for some reason my eyes began to creep open and what i saw kind of startled me.I was certain that i started completely flat on my back looking up at the bed above me ( i have a bunkbed) but instead of seeing the bed i saw a small bright square and i had no idea what it was then it slowly began to move down and become larger then i releaized it was my window (i realized that i was lying with my head slightly angled so with my eyes straight forward i was staring atmy window) but this distracted me and i lost concentration and lost any effect from the end of the beat (there was about 7 minutes left)

itwas quite interesting.

any feedback?
