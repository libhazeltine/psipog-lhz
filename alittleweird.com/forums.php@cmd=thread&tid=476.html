Fun practical ways to practise psionics
Author Message
Claudius
For those of you who wish to practice psionics in a more measurable and applicable environment, I thought i'd make this thread.

For example here's my idea:
Say you got bored of making psi balls. You already know what a psi ball may feel like: warm, cold, etc. Now take that feeling and apply it somewhere. Get a bunch of Ice cubes, and a stop watch. Determine the time it takes for an ice cube to melt. Throw a bunch of psi balls or a really strong one, and try to make the ice cube melt faster then it normally does. Or, if you're good at cold psi balls, try to make the ice cube last as long as possible before it completely melts. Practice until you get good. Its a good way of verifying how strong you can actually make your psi balls.

However, thats just one idea and there are many others. Please post your ideas here. And they can be ANY area of psionics, as long as they are reasonable whether it be OBE's or telepathy or TK or w/e.

A common test done in laboratory experemints is to roll a dice 100 times to get normal results, then roll it 100 more times while trying to make a certain number come up. Then compare the results and see how many more times it came up than the first set of rolls. Then you can even roll a third time and try to get better than the second.

I tried that idea a while back. However, I did it with one of my calculator functions since I couldn't find a dice. The function works the same as a dice, you just keep pressing the equal sign and a random number from 1 - 6 pops up. So far I would say about 80% of my tests proved to be successful. I managed to get one of the numbers to pop up more frequently then others, although not by a significant amount.

Also, a while back during school, me and a few friends were bored so we decided to play the dice game. Basically you pick a number from 1 - 6, then if you land the number you win. Its very simple. I took note of the fact, that during one trial, three of us picked the same number, hoping it would come out the most out of 100 rolls. Somehow, we kept getting it to come out the least out of all the numbers. I'd say there was only one trial where the turnout was significant. Out of 100 rolls, the number we picked only came out 5 times! The rest of the trials weren't as significant because there was not a large difference between the number we picked and the outcomes of the other numbers. We did however almost always manage to get the number the we picked to come out least or greatest, so i am guessing there must be something going on there.
