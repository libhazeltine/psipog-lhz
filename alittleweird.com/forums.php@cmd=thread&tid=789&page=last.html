Near-OBE experience!!!
Author Message
AngelsFall
I just had the craziest experience.

I was trying to trance out to get comfortable with the hypnogogic hallucinations that I always freak out over and I ended up almost getting out of body... as soon as the session started I knew I'd be successful on some level, and I got extremely deep in meditation when I started pondering what my "ethereal" or whatever body would look like should I look at "myself" in a mirror while out of body...

And while I was pondering this visually, I made some kind of indescribable connection, and I felt this really profound sense of "alignment." I looked myself in the eyes in the mirror [mentally, in this visualization that I was completely lost in] and all of a sudden I was "back" in my body having easily the most intense vibrations I've ever experienced. There was a very strong sense of heat, but none of it was painful or unpleasant, just intense.

I felt my bond to my physical body "melt" or something in the heat and I heard/spoke simultaneously this voice in my head [it's hard to describe, but no, I'm not "hearing voices," I was just voicing a deeper concept I became aware of]... It was something along the lines of "...You sure you're ready to do this?" and I affirmed it to myself, and started rising, in some sense, completely out of body...

I have no idea how to describe it, but it was very, very vaguely like a plane lifting off the runway, when you become acutely aware that the ground beneath the plane is now WWWWAAYYYYY beneath the plane, waaayyy down there... that's what it felt like in regard to my physical body... I felt like I would eventually just get "tossed" out of body completely if I held it long enough, but my heart was pounding and the adrenaline ultimately woke me up all the way. Mentally, I wasn't scared, although I was a tad nervous. Apparently my body was scared shitless =p

Anyway sorry for the rambling, I figured I'd share this before I lost the words completely.

I must say though, the feeling of freedom while in that state is profound and exhilarating. I only hope I can achieve it again and take the next step further so I have something more interesting to report back.

If I can ever get out consistently, I'll try some experiments and report back. Anyone have any good ideas about things I should try? [psiballs, flying, looking in the mirror, telepathy, interacting with "physical" people, telekinesis, morphing my ethereal body, seeing in 360 degrees, and such are stuff I can think of offhand...]
