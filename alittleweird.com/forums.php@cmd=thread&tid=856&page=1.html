posted by f3licks:
What's Hemi-sync?
Hemi-sync is a binaural beat thing, which is what Neuro-Programmer 2 seems to be at a glance (though I can tell there is definitely more to it). I haven't tried NP2 yet, but a notable thing about Hemi-sync is it has some sort of "training" feel to it, and has Robert Monroe's voice in it as he guides you through it. It also recommends a lack of visuals/dim lighting. After using it the first time, thinking sort of "felt" different, and I felt subtle vibrations in my hands and arms. Thinking still feels different than it did before, but I haven't gotten the arm vibrations I felt when I first tried it. This only happened on the first recording, which was about a half hour long. The lesson, so to speak, was about "resonant tuning". Vibrations and such are a theme in his early recordings (I haven't gotten far in the program). There are also preliminary exercises which you will get into the habit of doing in the recordings. You do have to "get in the zone" though. I've screwed up before, realizing that I had to restart, because I wasn't in the zone. I think the exercises are there to help.

In "Hemi-sync - The Gateway Experience", there are 36 recordings. I enjoyed using it, especially with the kind of "play with this state" attitude to it.
