CALLING ALL SCANNERS - and #Psifight Explained
Author Message
Subliminal
Experienced scanners preferred.
All interested welcome.


Psifight is a channel that specializes in energetic combat.

There are many spar types.
These include: shutdown, skirmish squads, vamp duel, mass armies, cloak and hit, heart rate manipulation, fox hunt, storm the fortress, physical pain, system hacking, system corruption, hex duel, telepathic combat, construct battle, construct hacking, fencing duel, tag, and electric battle.

There are other spar types that are done that are not listed, such as shield and field battle.

A spar takes place between two or more people.

The opponents agree on a spar type, and get a ref.

Refs watch over the match, track damage, and make sure things don’t get out of hand. They also call the match and declare the winner.

This is done by means of scanning.

After the match, healing is available to anyone who asks for it.


Note: This is NOT roleplay.

Unsure? Go there and experience it.

Benefits to sparring:

It’s fun.

It also offers you a way to practice energy manipulation in a way that’s exciting, as opposed to other tedious ways that while they can be effective are slow to yield results and difficult to keep up with if you’re a person who tends towards boredom easily.

Sparring can improve your energy manipulation skills, you gain finesse, your regeneration improves as does your generation, your energy capacity increases, abilities such as scanning develop faster…many things.



If you want to try reffing, join #psifight and seek out RBTOT-ICTX. Tell him you’re interested in reffing. If he isn’t around, speak to Neeros.

Come to:

Channel: #psifight
Server: darkmyst

This can be reached through an IRC client, or the darkmyst chat mibbit.
