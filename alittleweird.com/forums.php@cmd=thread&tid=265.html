I had an idea for a game involving cards with colored shapes on them. It's a two player game, or single v.s. com, and each person draws five cards with different shapes of different colors. Then using a turn based system you have to guess what color/shape card the opponent has. If you get one right they discard that card. First person to run out of cards loses. Just a thought to maybe jumpstart some ideas. An advanced version of the game could also be made involving random pictures of things or even colored numbers 1-20, 1-50, ect. Maybe a difficulty setting could even be adapted.
