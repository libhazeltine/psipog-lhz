FreedomBud
From my experience and reading around this is pretty common. For me it seemed that the one way was just easier to visualize. I know how it can get frustrating, for me if I did manage to make it go the other way it would fall down >.<. The only thing that will help is just more practicing, although it isn't the answer most liked. I've also found if I think that it's going to work a certain way (It will only spin in one direction until I come back and try again) it limits your potential for practicing. The easy part is doing it, the hard part is convincing your mind that you can do it. :D
