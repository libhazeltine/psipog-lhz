My thoughts on the "Energy-Vampire" topic:

I am of the opinion that the mind (external from the brain) requires energy to operate intellectually and semi-emotionally. A properly nourished body has the bio-chemistry needed to power the physical workings of the body and mind-body interface.

By our own nature, we unconsciously cultivate from our environment the energies needed to operate our mind on a level beyond just "existing." Just as we eat food to fuel our physical bodies, we "channel" energy into our mind to fuel our consciousness. Whether we are aware of it or not.

Now, energy is not just some quantity of power coming from nowhere. It has a history. All of it. Just as a person or object has a history that describes its passage through time, so do environmental energies. This encoded information is consumable just as the energy itself is. Once the energy is converted, that bit of history will co-mingle with the receiver and extra information can be perceived.

This process is very passive and to the average person, however the experience of this can be right in the front of their mind.

Those who become more aware of subtler energies and begin to consciously manipulate them on a daily basis, work out the "energy cultivating mechanisms" to a greater degree; ESPECIALLY those with the attitude that "more energy is better" or "I am energy deficient and need more." On an unconscious level, you could be pulling more energy from your environment (and other people) to do your energy work.

Now, I speak of this not in the tone of a rogue psion sucking energy from various victims, but as a normal human being. This is a normal thing. This is part of being human. As a matter of fact, I feel, that this mechanism is actually the CRUCIAL "everybody has it" device that we use for manipulating energy and any other mental energy work. It connects us to others, our environment and the universe. Dare I say, without this, our mind would not survive (or exist).

I have a longer rant about links, information and energy but I'll save that for later. ^__^

But being an "Energy Vampire" is like being a "popcorn hog;" it is just a potent simile describing an observation. A charismatic and open person is energized being around interested listeners. Just as a room of mopey people could bring a chipper person down to their level.

People who become more aware of energy and how it affects them can react positively and negatively to their environment more than others. Often overreacting.

The one example of "energy vampire" that is the truest for the namesake is the person that feels the conscious need to specifically extract energy from a specific target over fear of depleting their own supply. A theory I have is that they, have too much conscious control over the gathering of energy and see the need to control it consciously all of the time perhaps they have even "damaged" their ability to do it naturally and NEED to control it. I don't know.

In any event, I personally feel that over 99% of the cases of "energy vampires" are just normal people slightly more aware of their energy and are complaining about the effects they perceive from the quality of the energy they receive. Crappy job with a mean co-worker, polluted energy being absorbed, mental and emotional effects observed.

That's life.
