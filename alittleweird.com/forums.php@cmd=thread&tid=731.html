The following interview is with a scientist for the US Government who came forth anonymously (Henry Deacon is not his real name), to explain some of the things he's seen.

I can't say I believe every word he says, but he seems quite sure of himself, and he seems fairly intelligible. So while you take this with a grain of salt with the knowledge that the US Government cannot contain every leak and that if it looks to the rest of the world as 'crazy-talk' then they don't have to worry about the populous believing them.
