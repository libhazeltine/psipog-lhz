Akio
It works... though I would recommend you first learn what the colors mean, then learn how to see them (I prefer the exercises found here, with slight variation: http://www.thiaoouba.com/aura_eye_exercise.htm )

The second bit is to relax, adjust and just examine as many people's auras as you can. The practice should help you get more accustomed to seeing them, till eventually it will come with little to no effort.

You need to be relaxed, and comfortable with what you're doing, but also observant. Like most energy sensing/working, aura-sight is a subtle skill and requires a keen eye.

Protips:
<> If you wear glasses or anything of the sort, I'd recommend removing them while you practice/read auras. Glasses tend to strain the eyes, and corrective lenses (glasses that actually fix your eyes) work by straining your eye to adjust your vision. Either way, it's strain that you probably don't want on your eyes if you're doing this often

<> Relatively dark rooms, without drastic backgrounds (white is best) makes it easier to see an aura, as well as heat (moister heats are better, okay, maybe that's a bias since hot dry air gives me nosebleeds). I recommend you practice outside of such environments first, or at least ignore those advantages while you practice. It will only hurt your abilities if you use conditions as a crutch.
