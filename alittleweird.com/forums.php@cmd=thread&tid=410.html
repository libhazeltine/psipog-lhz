"What is your favorite method?"

Well, the type of meditation I use most of the time, is a no-mind meditation.
Basically, not thinking or feeling in any way at all; very similar to the border of sleeping and waking, or when you "click out".

I've been doing it for a while now, so it's easier for me to just go straight into it in a calm environment.

The method I used though, since I was in the middle of forest, was to listen to the sounds of birds and animals around me.. slowly cutting out each group, until I only noticed one group of chirps. Then finally, cut that group out too.

After that, I just hold it until I come out.
The point of no-mind is what you could consider "pure" meditation. The other parts, where you still feel etc, is still meditation, but I wouldn't say it's a pure state personally.
