I believe that shapeshifting is completely possible, it being one of the reasons that I got into psionics originally. There are probably people that can shapeshift easier than others, but I doubt that there are complete "skinwalkers" who have never needed any training.

There are a few methods to change your eye color with psionics, which is considered one of the first steps towards shapeshifting. I wouldn't advise googling shapeshifting though, you will find many sources that are a bit... sketchy at best.

Do this until you know that your eyes are changed. Try this a few times. The more you do it, the more effective it is. What this does is it tricks people into thinking that your eyes are a certain color. People who know you well may not be effected but people who don't know you as well should see the color that you want.
