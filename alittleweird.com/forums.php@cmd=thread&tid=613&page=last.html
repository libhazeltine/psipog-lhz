Eldibs
You could take a close friend, and introduce them to psionics. Then you'd have a practice partner. Just make sure it's someone you feel comfortable with.

I myself have the same problem. I get distracted easily. There are two methods I use. The first is meditation. Simple and to the point. If you can clear your mind and keep it that way for extended periods, focusing on your practice is cake. The other one is to only practice for like five minutes at a time, but a lot more often. This works great for me, because it fits my daily activities a lot better, plus it keeps me from getting bored. I can't guarantee that it'll work for you, and I recommend the meditation over the split practices, but that's just because meditation is useful on its own.
