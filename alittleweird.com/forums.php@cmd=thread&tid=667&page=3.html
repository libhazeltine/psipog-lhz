Just a suggestion on getting rid of irrational fears. Rationalize them away.

For example, if I were afraid of getting stuck in a refrigerator, It might go like this.

Irrational Fear: Be careful when you open the fridge door.

Logic: uh... why?

Irrational Fear: You might fall in and get stuck inside.

Logic: OH NO!!! *thinks* wait I'm too big to fit inside a fridge.

Irrational Fear: uh...

Logic: and even if I was, I could simply push the door back open.

Irrational Fear: *implodes*

So if you're afraid of hell, meditate on things like: "a loving wouldn't send anyone to hell" "hell doesn't fit any crimes that can be committed." things like that.

Just whatever you do, don't repress your emotions, that will go like this.

Irrational Fear: Be careful when you open the fridge door.

Repressor: ROAR!!! SHUT UP FEAR!!

Irrational Fear: But you might fall in and get stuck inside.

Repressor: ROAR ROAR ROAR *digs hole and buries fear* Hurray, for now I am brave and shall have no fear, well I'm going to go play in traffic.
