Sean
A lot of my experiences have been blind.

Or even better, when I open my "eyes", my physical eyes open. So I feel myself walking around my room, but I'm looking out of my physical eyes! Very cool :-).

It took me a long time to figure out how to fix this. Basically, I just had to calm down, and maintain my state until my vision eventually faded in. In the past, when I got out of body, I would be so excited, and tried to run away from my physical body as fast as I could. When I slow myself down, calm down, and relax, and focus just on calmly brining my vision into focus, then it slowly begins to work. It usually takes a good 30 extra seconds to get my vision working now.

As a last ditch effort, if it still isn't working after that, sometimes squinting worked for me. I have no idea why.
