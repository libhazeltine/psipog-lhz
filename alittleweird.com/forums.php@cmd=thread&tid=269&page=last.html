Alright, first a test:

Either put your left hand on your right hand or put your right hand on your left hand.

Which did you choose?
Either way, you chose, right?
You had the ability to choose "left on right" or "right on left".
In fact, some of the people that read this probably didn't even perform the test.
That was also their choice.

Whether or not your choice was foreseen by God, or something else, you still made the decision. Just because there was another possibility doesn't mean you didn't choose, it just means you went down one path and not another.

If you look at it in the sense that time has already progressed and we are just acting it out in accordance to what choices we've already made (in the future), then it was still "us" that made the choices, we still had the control, why would we make them different if time was a loop like that?
(According to some physicists, time isn't a loop at all btw).

The day when I choose to put my left hand on my right hand and my right hand goes over my left, then maybe I'll question my free will.

I might even freak out a little.

By the way, if you have someone with excellent precognitive abilities standing on one side of you, and someone who is a very skilled telepath on the other, and you ask them:
"Which one of you guys am I going to punch in the face?"
They would technically both be able to tell you the correct answer, right?
What's the point?

WARNING! RELIGION!
Maybe God doesn't see your future and your destiny isn't set in stone, but rather he can read your mind, or he just knows what you'll do.
