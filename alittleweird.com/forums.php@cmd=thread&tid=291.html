Author Message

I've been practicing making psi balls on and off for a while now, and as a test I decided to see how much I could heat up the thermometer in my bathroom. It was quite stable, I watched it for some minutes without doing anything, and it stayed at the same number (22,3 or so, I think). I then put my hands around it as if making a psi ball, but not doing it, to see how much my hands would heat it up. After about a minute it went up by 0,1 degrees. After that I tried to make a psi ball and put around it, and I managed to get it up 1,1 degrees. Now, I'm wondering, how good is that compared to what you guys can do? I'm not that good with psi, but I just want to see how warm psi balls others can make.
