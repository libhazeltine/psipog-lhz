Winning the Lottery with Peanut Butter
Author Message
Goggle Hat
I've decided to practice being lucky, in a manner.

It started when I noticed that my best results on the lottery game (on this site) were "auto pick" blind luck sorta stuff.

Tuesday was the control. No trying, forcing, or anything. Buy an auto-lotto ticket, and look at the results. $0.

After that I began trying to associate eating peanut butter with winning the lottery. I I have since ate 2 PB&J sandwiches, and 2 pieces of peanut-butter toast.

Friday, auto-lotto, Kicker yes, 3 balls correct $7 winnings. I plan on using this money to "reward good behavior," like, buy more pudding. And also restore my bread and peanut-butter stocks.
