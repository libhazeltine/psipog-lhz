Multiplicity and the scientific proof behind it.
Author Message
Linked
I recently came across a book titled:
Multiplicity: The New Science of Personality, Identity, and the Self
By Rita Carter.
This book is an amazing bit of information from an actual scientist who's work was published in a science journal.
The first part of this book deals heavily with the history of Multiplicity, misconceptions in the past about hypnosis, possession, and similar superstitions.
Along with providing a wealth of knowledge about the way the Human brain, the real brain not the mind, meaning neurons and other physical things, actually works.

Anyone who has read this book please post your thoughts here.
Anyone who hasn't, you should seriously try finding a copy if you are interested in the human mind, as well as with the way peoples personalities work, how we can use this to our advantage as people.

I myself already have at least two distinct "me"s. One is what I would define as myself, my "Major", the me that goes by my given name. And another "me", another state of mind and feelings, that "we" have named Mie.

This is not MPD/DID(Multiple personality/Dissociative Identity disorder), it is a completely normal and healthy way for out brains to function. And gives us the ability to adaptively change ourselves to fit situations.

"I" am a very sweet, caring, and considerate person. Who is easily hurt but also easily swayed into a securing comfort of love.
While "Mie", my other "self", still sharing my memories and still being "me", is much more prone to being excitable, hyper active, and fun loving, but doesn't seem to be as considerate of other people. Not that that personality tries to be mean, it just doesn't think as much before it acts.

Wow, this was a long post, and the first I've made in a long time on this site.
Responses are very welcome and indeed why I posted this to begin with.
Thank you if you managed to read through all of that and sorry it was so long. XD
