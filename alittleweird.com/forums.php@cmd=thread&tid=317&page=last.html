the way i understand it (and i am by no means an expert) is that the mayans believed in set amounts of time that had names. like we have months (~30days) they had these sections of time, based on their astrological calendars.

A baktun is 20 katun cycles of the ancient Maya Long Count Calendar. It contains 144,000 days or 400 tuns or nearly 400 tropical years. The Classic period of Maya civilization occurred during the 8th and 9th baktuns of the current calendrical cycle. The current (12th) baktun will end, or be completed, on 13.0.0.0.0 (December 21, 2012 using the GMT correlation). This also marks the beginning of the 13th baktun, as such a term is usually used among Mayanists.

J. Eric S. Thompson states that when a Long Count of, say, 9.15.10.0.0 is placed in the 9th baktun, we are almost certainly committing an error. However, that practice is so well established among Maya epigraphers and other students of the Maya that to change it would cause more harm than its perpetuation.
perfect excerpt from wiki. from all the information ive seen on the subject the mayans did not predict what would happen or not happen on this date. more over, it was believed that they simply did not know what was to come after that time, only that their calendar completed itself there. 13 being the final number in one of the basic calculations of their calendar system (also correlating to the completion of the astrological cycle - all 12 houses/zodiacs).

long story short, ive come to believe that they used this date/time as a marker for a changing of "eras." just like the changing of baktun to baktun before it, except that this is the first time (according to them/that calendar) that the calendar has completed. i would assume that means the calendar would start over, only at a higher octave/resonance/level. this mirrors the setup of their calendar system as a whole... one cycle repeats as it rolls over into another cycle that repeats in kind.

should you expect anything crazy to happen on that day? probably not, save possibly some interesting\unique astrological events. more than likely you should be aware, that as we approach and move past that date, of what changes are occuring in the world and your life and in what direction they are going.
