I usually got a headache when I tried to move a lot of energy through my head.
Then I thought that maybe going really slow with these things is the right way to do it.

Yesterday I decided to do something about it.
I traced the psi flow and found that it just crashes into some kind of shield or something without actually getting out of the head. I don't know what it was, I'll just call it a wall since now.

I visualized opened doors wherever this painful crashing seemed to take place.
The headache lessened with every door that I opened.

Finally the whole head was full of open doors and I thought: "What's the point of this wall anyway. Oh, well, I'll just leave the doors open for 1 night and see what happens."

There were 2 round lumps of something in my neck that also seemed to block the energy flow and flushed those too.

After that I felt a lot cleaner during psi practice. There also seemed to be a difference in the amount of psi I was able to feel.

On to the questions:
1. How and why are psi blockages formed?
2. Is everything that blocks psi called a psi blockage?
3. Is the "wall" just something that everybody has and that is there as some kind of default protection gear or could I have made it myself (and forgotten about it)?
4. What happens when one "shuts down" from psi practice (could it be related to the blockages)?
