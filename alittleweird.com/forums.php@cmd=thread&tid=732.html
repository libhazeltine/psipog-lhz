Heh, Don't know that much about paranormal science I'm afraid, I know a lot about the so called 'occult laws' and stuff (I'm a magician of the Hermetic science) but pure science just isn't my thing. I'm more of the empirical and spiritual way.

However, though most of my practice and knowledge is centered around the Magick paradigm, I have a few sources about 'Psionics' (And yes, I am at VSociety a lot, hence the links):

- The True Nature of Psi, a theory on what we call Psi is really like.

- Articles on the 'Dynamic Psi' system, a system of Psionics made by kobok, I recommend this to everyone who really wants to get good progress with Psionics, it isn't just a series of skills, but rather a good manual on how to gain mastery over Psi. (Though it takes TIME and PRACTICE to do so.)

- How to NOT Practice Psi, this is a great article as well, actually, it is more some sort of rant about people who practice Psionics in some very weird way.

Thought the last two articles aren't really that 'scientific', I felt it necessary to provide them here as well, since they are the real gems when it comes to Psionic practice, in my opinion.

~Falcon
