Wandering Wolf
I had four (very) strange experiences (that I can recall at the moment) and only one of them involved psionics. For that, I apologize in advance.

1. On one occasion, when I was testing out manipulation for one of the first times, I absorbed energy and could have sworn that it made hundreds of hairs all over my body stand on end as if I was producing a massive static electric field. I remember it lasted for as long as I was absorbing energy. I was never able to duplicate this effect thereafter.

2. Another experience that I had dealt with what I believed to be some kind of symbolic clairvoyance. I was taking a shower and I suddenly saw, in my mind's eye, the door to the bathroom slowly open by itself, only to reveal darkness behind it. I was disturbed to say the least.

3. Throughout my sophomore year in high school, I always felt that one of the girls in my gym class was extremely evil and sadistic. She showed no outward sign of being such. In fact, she often smiled and seemed like a friendly person that I would otherwise have not had a problem being around. I just always felt it deep down that she was evil, so much so that I avoided speaking and being near her at all costs.

4. The most intense and probably the strangest experience I had was my first (and so far only) (supposed) experience with an apparition (ghost). I saw the (supposed) apparition with my peripheral/indirect vision and saw that it vanished in an instant by the time I had raised my head high enough to look at it directly. The strange thing was that it actually had a strong presence like that of a person right behind you breathing down your neck, but it wasn't even that close to me. It's presence was so human that I irrationally thought that it was my brother who had just entered the apartment. The moment it vanished, I left my irrational stupor and remembered that my brother wasn't a blue vaporous substance shaped like a person! Upon realizing that it wasn't my brother, the hairs on my body stood on end in fright instantaneously. Following that encounter, I was extremely weak and depressed for about two weeks straight. The weakness and depression ended intantaneously during my English class. It happened when I began to more attention to the teacher's lecture (I always had a problem paying attention in class when I was young and suffered the consequences). Right when I had resolved to pay attention for the rest of the period, I could have sworn that I saw what looked just like cigarrette smoke (no joke) come out of my mouth and disappear from my vision. It was as if the ghost had been inside me the whole time (probably feeding off of my energy) and had decided to leave. Not too surprisingly, my depression ceased after it had left my body. Shortly after that, I experienced a feeling of intense euphoria (bliss/genuine happiness) that lasted for several days straight. During that time, I was strangely able to do my homework faster and I felt a strong desire to learn everything I possibly could. It was completely unlike me and my family was curious as to why I returned to my normal self so quickly, as they thought that it was a permanent change of character. I was mad that I lost that wonderful bliss so quickly, but I was interested to see the effects that the paranormal experience seemed to have on my academic performance. I was somehow able to focus much more and I was able to raise my grades from C's and D's to A's and B's over the course of several weeks. On top of all that, my memory seemed to improve dramatically, so much so that I was able to bewilder classmates by consistently and accurately rattling off boring statistics and facts during class trivia games. I was like a one man army in trivia that everyone wanted on their team. To this day I am still puzzled as to what the hell happened to me.

Any thoughts on any of these crazy experiences?

EDIT:

I wonder if that supposed apparition was actually someone having an OBE. Just food for thought.
