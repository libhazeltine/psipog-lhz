oh, and i have a question, does anybody here have the paitiance to teach me how to do meditaition?
The way I learned was to sit still in a position that's less than comfortable (so as not to fall asleep) and just notice your thoughts pass by and try to stop them when you realize them. You won't be able to shut out all thoughts from the get go, but as soon as you notice them you just silence the thought. If you're having trouble silencing your thoughts replace it by focusing on your breathing. This takes a lot of practice for people with A.D.D. like me, but anyone can get the hang of it if they try hard enough.
