Wolfram, Again »

November 11th, 2009 by SeanLarger Than Mind

Happy 11/11!

I don’t really have anything to offer today.  I don’t know… for the past two months or so, I just haven’t been interested in “truth” or the “paranormal”.  I think it’s related to the crystallization of Wolfram’s theory of everything.  I just don’t feel the same passion that I used to.

I mean… Wolfram solved it.  He figured reality out.  Of course, there are an infinite number of details… but the big picture is complete. (Well, technically the big picture isn’t complete yet, but Wolfram has pointed in the correct direction, and it’s just a matter of time at this point… and I always reserve the right to be completely wrong.)

It’s funny, because I think we hold on to this illusion that God is some sort of personal being.  Like a human, sitting and making decisions, and interfering with our lives, helping us along, etc.  I see a different God now… neither personal nor impersonal.  Larger than mind.  I can’t contain the idea in my head anymore.  I can’t label it, and parse it, and move it around… it’s too unknowable.

Inherent unpredictability.  What a beautiful thought.  What a sigh of relief.

Anyways…

There is so much to learn and wonder about in the world.  I really love this world… it’s such a curious phenomenon.  No box is big enough to contain it.

And the sneaky part is when I say “larger than mind”, our mind immediately grasps the concept.  But what a slap in the face!  “Larger than mind” loses all meaning once mind thinks it understands.

Like I said, I have nothing to offer you today… just random musings on life, the universe, and everything.
