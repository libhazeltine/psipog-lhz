Larger Than Mind »

October 28th, 2009 by SeanHuman

I would like to think that my “search for truth” is some noble venture.  I would love to inflate my ego, and declare that my pursuit is a result of higher thinking, or that I stand on principle, or philosophy, or some other big idea.  All lies!

My obsession for truth originates in my strong sense of survival.  I don’t pursue truth for some higher purpose - I pursue it because my biology wants to survive, and my programming is betting that this abstract concept of “truth” will give me an evolutionary advantage.

And with that, my obsession is vanquished.  Awareness is a bitch.

See, now the real questions start.  The real hard ones.  Such as, How does one live a fulfilling life?  What matters?

These glowing rectangles are killing me.

Human.
