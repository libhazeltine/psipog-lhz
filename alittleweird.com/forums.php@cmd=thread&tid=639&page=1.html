Did you know, the big bang theory has already been disproven?

How, you ask?

Newtons first law.

"An object at rest tends to stay at rest and an object in motion tends to stay in motion with the same speed and in the same direction unless acted upon by an unbalanced force."

If this statement is true, then the world couldn't have been made by a giant explosion. Something outside of our universe would have had to have cause the explosion, something like a higher being.

What are your thoughts on this.
