Trick
Telepathy was by far, the hardest psionic skill for me to learn. I've got some advice that will hopefully speed up your progress.

1)Meditation, daily thing, if your not already.
2)You should probably practice with other people, also. Like shapes and colors and things. One session a day is fine.
3)The most important one. (This is pretty much the same as one of Seans blog posts) but you need to be aware of YOUR thoughts. Get into the habit of always being aware of what your thinking, all the time, until you always are. It should be something your always doing, second nature. Like breathing. Once your to that point, go over to some people, in the conversation youll notice thoughts will pop up, but youll realize their not yours.

Basically, just always be aware of your thoughts, and eventually youll notice thoughts that arent yours (other peoples)

Use that in telepathy games, too. When receiving, dont try to ELIMINATE your thoughts, just try to be aware of your thoughts (like always) and when one pops up that doesnt seem to be yours (youll learn to tell wich ones are yours) then its thiers.

Hope this helps.
