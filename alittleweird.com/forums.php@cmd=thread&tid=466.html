Feeling of pressure on my forehead
Author Message
Sussch
Hello,

Sometimes when I lay down in my bed at night, I begin to feel something rushing through my forehead into my brain. It's a quite uncomfortable feeling - having something pushed through the skull..

I have experienced this maybe 2-3 times only. It usually happens after I have been willing to learn something fast.

The first time I had wished for being able to understand math good enough for school. I was lying on my back with my head towards the window. Then when I was almost dreaming, suddenly I felt a slight pressure on my forehead that woke me up. The pressure was gradually increasing. I also began to feel the stream that flowed through my skull. After about an hour or so, when the pressure was already hard to tolerate, I turned my head away from the window (I felt it coming through the window), but the stream slowly followed my forehead. I then panicked and closed the stream (I don't know how I did it, but I felt the pressure slowly dropping).

With every experience the stream seems to have broadened and the amount of pressure decreased. While at the first time the area that the stream penetrated, was about the size of cm x cm. Yesterday the area encompassed almost my whole forehead. There is another thing I noticed yesterday - it was the first time that I felt the stream flowing through the front of my brain and being focused somewhere near the center of the brain.

Well, I hope it's information or psi that is flowing through and not some symptoms of some disease and that it isn't somehow dangerous to my health.
Yesterday, when I had this feeling again, I began to worry about it.
I think that I worry about it because I can't verify, read or decompress that "something" that I feel. I don't even know if this really is extending my knowledge or improving any skills.

I wonder if anyone has experienced something similar..
