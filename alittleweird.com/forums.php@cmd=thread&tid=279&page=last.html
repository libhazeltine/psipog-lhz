I've never had any luck with intentional psychokinesis, but I do seem to speak clearly with my subconscious from time to time, as well as living my daily life with frequent synchronocities. Those aside, the only real concrete experiences I have had are OBE vibrations and recently an unexplained incident. These things said, I wanted to address a few things here:

Are OBE vibrations a natural part of sleep? If they are, they don't validate a paranormal event for those who haven't had a successful attempt.

I continously try to 'feel' an outcome and then look at the result to see if it's true. The problem with this is, I get it wrong as often as I get it right, and many of the times when I'm right there really is no distinction that I TRULY did anything at all. Of course, I have had my days where I just know something real has happened. I think one problem is that I choose things that are too arbitrary.

Synchronocities are essentially odd coincidences, but they have happened to me as of late to such an extent that I can't really trust that they are real in any way. Sometimes I feel like It has become second nature for me to take anything I see and make it a 'synchronocity to me.

Basically, all of my supposive experiences are very deniable, so I often feel it is hard to advance.
