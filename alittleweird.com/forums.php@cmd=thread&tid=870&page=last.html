Trick
posted by f3licks:

Any advice/tips?
Two big no's:

-Dont put your hands around it, or even near it. This causes it to move from the heat/and or static from your hands. Usually just heat.

-Cover it. Not with a plastic jar, because plastic has a static charge that WILL move it. But use a glass cover.

-If you want, do it from across the room too.

A lot of people might thing this is a bit much. But I tell you, I made every mistake possible. I went years doing PK much like a lot of PKers do today, only I wasn't doing PK. I had to restart training countless times after finding out over and over again that I did something wrong, and what I was doing wasn't PK. IMO, the best thing to do is to skip all that trouble and just cover it, and no hands.

(I know I probably sound like a broken record with all this stuff, I say it all the time. But I cant stress it enough, I see too many new guys fail miserably off of it.)

Besides, its very rewarding, (at least at first) to be able to move a psiwheel with a glass jar over it, and show your friends.

Other than that, nice video.
