Communicating with your Subconscious
Author Message
Renodin
Hello, everyone. I'm not really... new, to this site. I've had an account for a while, I've just never gotten around to using it. But I figured, hey, why not? The people here seem nice enough. Sorry if this post is really long, though.


I've read about psychic abilities for a while, around three or four years. Some of them I have tried, some of them I have not. Some of them actually seem impossible to me. I've practiced some of the big things, like creating Psi balls, psychokinesis, telepathy, clairvoyance, etc. I can't say I've had very much progress with those at all, heh heh. While I have no real skill in any of those, I mostly enjoy coming up with theories on how they may be possible and how to test them.

One ability, though, I have had great results in. And, really, I don't even call it a "psychic ability". Communicating with my subconscious is what I do best out of all the weird things I've practiced, and it's what I've enjoyed the most as well.

When I first began, I did those basic little exercises. Imagining a pool of water, and the ripples being subconscious messages. That type of thing. Sadly, just like my previous experiments with skills such as these, I failed. I couldn't get anything. I had given up for quite a while.

One day, I decide to try again. But I though that since I was getting no where with these methods, I'd try something of my own creation. This method of mine seemed a bit advanced, but I tried it anyway. I thought up a look for a person. After completing the look for this person, I created a little peaceful tranquil home for him.

Afterward, I got used to how this place looked, along with the man inside it. I got it memorized so that anytime I wanted to, I could imagine it, just like that. Before long, I decided it was time to work out my method. I tried to use this humanoid figure as a link to my subconscious. I would ask it questions and attempt to get responses.

At first, this was a bit difficult. I couldn't keep focused to long, and I was mostly limited to asking yes or no questions, getting nods or head shakes. But, what kept me going was that most of the answers I got seemed to be true. I was a bit discouraged, thinking it was only my imagination, but I decided I had nothing to lose and everything to gain. I kept practicing.

After a few months, I could focus for a long time. Around thirty minutes! That was great for me. But I was still limited to simple yes or no questions. But then, I thought of something. This humanoid image was able to nod and give head shakes, because it had a head to nod. However, it never talked. I did some thinking, and created a voice for it.

To this day, I'm not sure why or how, but the voice ended up being a female's. So, I changed the humanoid's body to that of a female to match it.

I was extremely happy! I had successfully created a link to my subconscious in a way that I could easily relate to as a human! I was able to ask it more complex questions and get vocal answers from this little image in my head! However, it required a bit more focus, which I needed more practice on. And, sometimes my focus would lack, and I would just get back to movements. Ah well.

Eventually, I could keep up this person all the time, anytime I desired. It was a little bit of practice every night before going to sleep well worth it! I was a bit doubtful at first, thinking the entire thing was just my imagination. But when damn near everything I ask it is true, I start to believe I've succeeded in completing the link.

Now, about only a month ago, I started getting more involved with my subconscious. I asked it to give me advice throughout the day, without needing to focus and ask a question. This worked quite well. Every now and then, I might just hear that little voice I invented tell me something, usually something of good nature. It seems to really like me to help people out all the time, but whenever I do those random acts of kindness it suggests I do, good things always end up happening.

Finally, about two weeks ago, I went on a trip and fell behind in school. I needed help to get caught up. Bad. So, I decided to ask my subconscious for help. Oddly enough, it really couldn't help me too much when it came to catching up on my schoolwork. Well, I decided on something else that would help me, and help me study a bit more about my subconscious. I went through the first process and created a second link to my subconscious. I made a new person, a new voice, and it didn't take even a day for me to be used to this person. I was already trained on that. I tried a new thing to experiment. I gave this new person a different personality. I made them intelligent, pretty booksmart-like, and a bit rude. This second link I used to help get a lot of schoolwork done. I got about five days of schoolwork caught up in about a day and a half.

I've had a great success with my subconscious, and, I'm thinking, with it's help, I can move on to other psychic abilities and try to research them better. All it took was a few minutes before going to sleep every night to practice this, so why wouldn't it be the same with other skills?
========================================================
So, the point of this is to discuss communicating with your subconscious. How you do it, if you do it, if it works for you, benefits from it, etc. etc.

Before anyone calls me crazy or anything, these links I've created aren't any way insane. They don't try to manipulate me or tell me to "burn things" or to hurt anyone in any way, shape, or form.

Sorry if this is really long!
