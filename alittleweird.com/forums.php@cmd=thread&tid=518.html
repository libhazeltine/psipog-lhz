11:11 and Strange Dreams
Author Message
SheepKing
So, have you heard of 11:11?

I've read on the internet that its the sign of 'light workers', or it's the sign of spirits trying to get in contact with you, or it's the sign of who knows what... Aliens even? I do not know the significance of it.

However, I have been seeing it, almost everyday for the past four weeks. At work I just happen to look down at the clock at 11:11. I get home at night am working on my computer and then bam 11:11 . Today I was driving home from a friends house and I looked at my clock. 11:05... I then look up at a digital display that is out front of restraunt/club, and it reads 11:11.

Do you think that it's appearance, or my looking at the clock at these times has to do with my subconscious recognizing that I am attributing it to be significant, and then when I see it in the peripheral vision, I am cued to look? Do you think the numbers have any significance at all?

Strange dreams now. As you all know (probably) I have some pretty freaky dreams sometimes. Like just straight up abstract weird dreams, that I don't particularly attribute to being significant. But there are two dreams that I've had in the last three weeks that have particularly left me remembering them and thinking they are caused by something other than myself.

Both of them involve some type of "Ghost", or spirit, appearing to me in some form. For the purpose of brevity I will only make a synopsis of the dreams.

Dream #1: Sitting in the living room of my old house, there is a woman infront of me who identifies herself as a medium (someone who talks to spirits). She tells me a spirit of someone I used to know wants to talk to me. The spirit whispers to me, you're going to see a ghost now. Don't be afraid. Everything will be okay. As this is happening I start to feel strange vibrations, and get very cold. My lungs and chest feel paralysed and I wake up cold, very cold. End.

Dream #2: I am at my office and I am taking pictures of myself. And the lights go out. The last picture I take on my camera has a spirit standing behind me and I start feeling cold/having trouble breathing and I wake up. end.

What do you think?
