How effective is a shield really?

For someone who knows what their doing, getting by shields is a fairly easy process. In reality it seems that a shield is at best just a filter. Of course, there are ways to make it more difficult (adding more shields, making more complicated shields, etc.). This is what I'm here to cover.

Simply put, this thread is meant to be a discussion on how to make the "best defense". Here I also would hope to cover idea's such as "a good offense is the best defense" and idea's alike.
