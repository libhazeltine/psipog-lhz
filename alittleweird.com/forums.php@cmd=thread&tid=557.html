Sean
I'm going to go out on a limb here, and try to put my finger on why I think you perceive lack of progress. I could be wrong, or maybe this will help:

The current lack-of-progress you see is a result of the mindset of the "community". There hasn't been progress because people - in general - don't want progress. They want the status quo.

So what is the status quo? Self-delusional magical thinking.

The "community", as a whole, has made an unconscious compromise. That compromise is this: If you believe what we believe, then it's ok if you don't believe it for the right reasons. Just join us anyway - it's all good. In fact, we aren't even sure WE believe it for the right reasons, so who cares.

This mindset is the root of the lack-of-progress that you witness.

So, what are the "right" reasons, and what are the "wrong" reasons?

Right - discover truth about reality.

Wrong - everything else. (Ok, maybe not everything else, but for the most part.)

As long as most people are content with their self-delusional magical thinking, then you won't really see progress.

~Sean
