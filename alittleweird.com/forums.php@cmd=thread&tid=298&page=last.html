Jeffrey
Hah. :-) I have a very, very, bordering science-fiction story for you guys. And since this is the weird discussions, I guess it won't hurt. Now, gather around children, old Jeffrey gotta story to tell:

Well, this was awhile back. A few months, one or two. I was sleeping in my bed one night and I oddly for some reason got up. I don't know why, but eh, I haven't been able to sleep lately. Too many things on mind.

So yeah, I wake up in the middle of the night, laying on my side, just thinking... Thinking about how stressful my life is getting. Than, I hear a crack. Not like breaking crack. Like crackles. I just ignore it and continue thinking. I keep hearing it over and over, and I'm wondering, "What is that?". My arm was itchy, so I moved my arm to scratch it (The one I was laying on.).

Okay, this is where it gets freaky. What I see, is not my imagination, but real. I see static electricity under my shirt. I didn't believe what I saw, so I lifted my shirt up. And literally, there was electricity coming from me and to my shirt. I jump up and freak out. I got off my bed, and I was about to touch my shirt again, but when my finger got close, electricity came from my finger to my shirt again. It really freaked me out. (But, than I started doing it over and over again. It was kinda fun. P-:)

But yeah, I just wanted to let you guys know about that. It could've been my shirt, but it never done it again.
