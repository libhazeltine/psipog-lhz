Author Message
Kube
This is probably some kind of hallucination from being tired, but here it is anyway:

The other day (A few weeks ago, actually), I woke up, as usual, and I looked at my feet while still in bed. On the bed, I saw a cat rolled up by my feet and looking at me. That's no unusual sight, as my cat often sleeps there and is black, like the one I saw, except it has white spots on its paws, but I thought my vision was blurry because I had just woken up. Then I looked outside my room and saw, standing in the hallway, my black and white cat. So I thought "What the hell?!" and looked over at my feet where the other one had been and it was gone. I was not dreaming and I keep thinking about it.

I'm not superstitious, but black cats are supposed to mean bad luck... Which hasn't manifested in any way since, except for the fact that I hit that part of my training where I have lots of trouble doing PK, but that could mean nothing. Also, I'm not sure about it just being a hallucination, because when it looked at me I had that weird, intense feeling you sometimes get when you're being watched. I don't know if you've ever felt that, but it can't really be mistaken for any other feeling.

Anyway, I wanted to know your opinion on that. I'm not sure what I should make of it and I can't get it off my mind.
