I onced asked that question myself. The reply I got was that the creator just was. Makes sense, since the creator is infinite and so therefore has no beginning nor end. We can only have a concept of a creator as an individual being, giant ivisible being in the sky. Mostly because infinity is not a concept that can be easily grasped, if at all, by humans.
That's an interesting way of answering the question.
Humans have a problem with the concept of infinity because nothing tangible is infinite. For there to be an infinite being, it would have to be so vastly different from us that we could never understand it.
Think of it this way. If you look at a person for a single second, and only that second they are infinite. They have no beginning or end in that second, they just are.
None of that is really in support of the existence of some sort of god figure, but I just think that saying something is infinite is tantamount to saying it's magical.
