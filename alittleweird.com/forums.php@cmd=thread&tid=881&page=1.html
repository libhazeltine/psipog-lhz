The Psion School
Author Message
Kube
Here are all the main ideas we've gathered for the psion school. I'll edit in new ideas as time goes by. The red text means that idea's been scratched out for the time being. The blue text means we've approved of it and, for now at least, it's making its way to the final result. So I'll divide this into a few categories. When you get an idea, post it in this thread and we can start compiling everything and discussing the ideas we have so far.

[Name]
Psi Academy (Proposed by Kube)
New Beginnings (Proposed by mrj)
Psinapse (Proposed by Reniiji)
The Academy (Proposed by Shaffertyler)

(Ummm... Guys unless everyone wants this, we need other ideas and put it to a vote XD)

[Plan]
- 2-tier plan (Proposed by Instectoid)
1. Open an IRC or other chatroom.
2. Find dedicated psions willing to help.
3. Start giving lectures and seminars to form a base community and for everyone to start knowing each other more. Also used to determine who does what in the school.
4. After we've determined a teacher for every main subject, we create a forum.
5. Start giving the classes. Also open an AP class for those already familiar with psi, kind of like an intensive program.
6. Once a student graduates, they can become teachers.

- Library plan (Proposed by Dash)
1. Start with a library with a bunch of articles on different subjects
2. Teach in the forums, but formal classes will have to wait if we lack resources.
3. When we have enough teachers, they can start taking a few students under their wing and teach in more organized classes.

- Forum-only plan (Proposed by Falcon)

(This isn't an A or B thing, I just listed them like this to be more organized, but they do seem similar...)

[Progress]
- Students are given grades according to assignments, either individual or in group. (Proposed by Dash)
- Students are graded on their knowledge, not their skill. (Written exams?) (Proposed by Dash) (SEE 4 POSTS DOWN)

[Who's who]
- When a student graduates, they get to apply to become a teacher. They need to be judged not only on their grades but also on their personality, skill as a teacher, etc. (Proposed by Insectoid)
- Ranking system: The classes have different levels of knowledge, so we have beginner, intermediate and advanced students along with teachers. Create a tutoring program so the more experienced students can help out newer ones advance and help out the teachers. (Proposed by Kube)
- Ranking system with special privileges for higher ranks based on credits. (Proposed by Dash) (SEE 4 POSTS DOWN)

[Motivation]
- Achievements. Create an achievement board where everybody can compare each others' achievements. Bragging rights, in other words. (Proposed by arzhul)
- A teacher of the month system. (Proposed by mrj)
- Similar to teacher of the month, have a student of the month for every class. That student gets bonus points and/or an achievement. (Proposed by Kube)
- Take note of the students' study in independent fields. (Magic, Qigong, etc.) (Proposed by mrj)

[Quality]
- Interview the students before they can be admitted in the school to uphold the quality of the community. (Proposed by Kube)
- If we require an admission to get a school membership, guests and applicants waiting for their admission should have a public section where they can have a sample of the school's content. (Proposed by mrj)
- Interview teachers to see who's a hoax and who knows what they're talking about. (Proposed by arzhul)
- Create a detailed application form to verify the teacher's skill, knowledge and know more about their ways of thinking/teaching. (Proposed by Kube)
- Get an empath to interview applicants and would-be teachers so they can pick up the other's feelings. (Proposed by Dash)

[Staff]
Dash: Organizer, Teacher (Energy Manipulation, Healing, Sensing)
mrj: Advisor, Organizer, Possible teacher
Kube: Advisor, Organizer
Trick: Teacher (Energy manipulation, PK, Empathy)
f3licks: Teacher (EM, Empathy, PK)



That's it for now. If I missed anything, write it signal me and I'll add it in. Also be sure to check if the names are right, I wouldn't want to give someone false credit :P

Like I said earlier, this isn't an A or B thing. All those things in the list could very well be merged together, but for the sake of organization, I separated everything.

I think I've decided a ranking system to be considered..

We would work off of credits, given by the teacher to the student upon completion of tasks.
When they get enough credits they would be considered for a promotion in rank, and given further access to the site than lower ranks.

This will prevent spammers getting too far into the site without having to actually learn some things.

It would be set up like this:
--------------------------
Level 1 Access
Library
--------------------------
Level 2
Read class logs
Blogs
Access to newb forums
--------------------------
Level 3
Aide teacher
Post personal or unofficial blogs, articles and experiments
Access to more of the forums
---------------------------
Level 4
Access over the whole site
Able to mentor or tutor (Also can be considered for a teaching job)
---------------------------



Access | Rank | Credits
----------------------------
Level 4 | Graduate | 30 or higher
-------------| Senior | 20 - 29
----------------------------
Level 3 | Junior | 10 - 19
----------------------------
Level 2 | Associate | 0 - 9
-----------------------------
Level 1 | Unsigned | No teacher
----------------------------

Reward system:
--------------------------------------
Successful class project | 1-2 Credits
We use your essay as an article | 2-3 Credits
Be a -helpful- teachers aide | 2-4 Credits
Successfully prove a class experiment true | 1-5 Credits
--------------------------------------------
