Weird event that happened in my sleep
Author Message
vondur
Hello everyone,

About a month ago, while I was sleeping a very strange event happened. I opened my eyes, and I was laying in bed, but unable to move. (sleep paralysis, I am assuming) This happens to me way too often, but this time it was different.

I look up, and in front of my bed a figure is standing. The figure is about 6 feet tall (1.8 meters) a very deep black color, huge white eyes, and no visible mouth, nose, ears, etc. It also seemed like the figure was not solid, but instead was made up of energy. The figure glances at the ground, and laughs. I cannot hear the laugh, it was a silent laugh, but I somehow just know that it was laughing. Then the figure looks up at me, and moves closer to me. It does not "move" in the same way you or I do, but it was as though it was not limited by space/time, it could move from one place to another in the blink of an eye. Then the figure holds me down, and I feel more paralyzed than just being "paralyzed", if that makes any sense. This whole time I am extremely terrified. I feel like more happened after that, but I do not remember it. Also, if it makes any difference, this was not night time, it was the middle of the day (I'm nocturnal).

I am not asking for someone to explain this, as I do not know if that is possible to do, but if you have any idea at all of what this could be please do tell me. I have a few ideas myself, but I do not want to say so that I don't influence anyone else.

I do not know if I was merely hallucinating, or if it was real, but I can say it felt very real indeed.

Also, I have many other "weird events" that have happened throughout my life. I will be sharing those later.

Hmmm...believe it or not, what you're describing is also a very common effect of sleep paralysis. You're halfway between sleep and reality, so sometimes what should be a dream appears to be real. The whole tall, dark figure standing over you and keeping you "pinned down" thing is common to a lot of people who suffer from this.

There are prescription drugs you can get that eliminate the problem of sleep paralysis. If you don't want to have to deal with this sort of thing again, you shoudl probably get some of those.

I went down there in January to participate in an Ayahuasca ceremony and a number of us went for lunch and bounced different ideas off each other, exchanging reasons for why we came. One guy told the story of being a kid (around 5, one at around 23) and suffering from sleep paralysis. Steve was in his room, everything seemed normal but he couldn't move and he noticed a shadowy figure with a weird head. standing nearby and he freaked out. He went on to say that within the last few months before coming to peru he started seeing it again.

He describes the sensation of being woken up out of a dream and this think standing on the bed over him. Its thin muscular leg touching his. He tells of it kinda falling backward and deflating, he trying to touch it but finding himself unable to move. She wakes up and finds him reaching across her and his eyes are open but hes unable to say or really move much. Then he says he wakes up in the same position but its gone.

He mentions that he then had 2 experiences of wrestling with one of them and Yamo another guy on the trip butts in, Entity Attack.
