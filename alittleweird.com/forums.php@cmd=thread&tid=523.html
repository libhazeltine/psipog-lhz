Does anyone ever question their sanity. I’m not insane; I’ve not been diagnosed as being insane, I look at psionics in a pretty rational way. However, the first time I demonstrated a small amount of psychokinetic skill I still couldn’t believe it. There is no air movement in the room, and I tend to hold my breath when I’m doing it too. I’ve filmed it and showed the film to a friend, I’ve even managed to make about 6 pinwheels and moved them all at the same time, I also filmed this, and I do believe....

But, I still question my sanity sometimes, it’s sad but true, I quesion everyone’s sanity on this site and many others like it, I wonder whether we’re all crazy people who are unaware of their insanity and have managed to form a community that allows us to perpetuate our own craziness.

I’m not sure If I’ll ever shake this feeling, or whether it’s to be expected, healthy even...maybe it’s crazy to not question your sanity...I would be interested to hear your points of view.
