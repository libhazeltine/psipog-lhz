Falcon
*grins* Let's say I'm an active member there. (Not under the name Falcon though, that's someone else.)

Personally, I don't think making psiballs is even necessary, with a good basis you will find yourself able to do Kinetics (all sorts of Psychokinesis) and forms of Scanning. (Telepathy, Empathy etc.) Energy Manipulation is not something I would encourage to beginners, though it is often thought of as the easiest practice, I think that's not the case, and is most prone to problems such as biofeedback.

So, start with increasing your focus, being able to meditate for long periods of time, (about 20 minutes is okay, don't START with 20 minutes, build it up slowly!) and try to become aware of everything you do. That is, don't eat without reading comics, watching tv etc. I would consider this last step to be optional, but it will greatly increase your overall focus in life.
After that, start working on Kinetics and Scanning. And AFTER having the basics of at least Kinetics under control, I would advice starting on Energy Manipulation.

~Falcon
