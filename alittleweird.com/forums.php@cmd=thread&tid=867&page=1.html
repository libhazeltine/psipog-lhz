Okay, this is not much, but for now, here's what I have:

As I said earlier, one thing's for sure: Shadow People are starting to be seen, some think intentionally, more and more often. Or at least, the reports are increasing, maybe because of the ease of communication that we now have because of the Internet.

Here are a few theories as to what they could be:

Some think they are ghosts. I'm not sure if I like that one; ghosts don't usually appear as pure darkness, they are more like a vaporous white form with often distinctive features, while a Shadow Person doesn't seem to have features at all.

Others think they might be demons, but if so, why wouldn't they act like regular demons? Why let themselves be seen like that?

Another theory suggests they are indeed interdimensional beings that have managed to cross over, if only slightly and briefly, to our dimension. This could eb explained using quantum physics, as it suggests that at the base of all things in the universe is vibrations and some are beginning to think lernate universes are "emitted" on a different vibration frequency, kind of like a radio signal. Some think our dimension's vibrations could slowly be merging with theirs and that is why we are seeing them more often.

Another one is about tie travel. Some think they are people from the future who haven't yet been able to fully appear in a physical form and can only observe from their shadowy form.

Another theory suggests they could be people having OBEs. Some have theorized that everybody goes OOB when they sleep and only a few remain conscious during that OBE or at least, remember it. Those two go hand in hand very well, but the descriptions of our ethereal bodies during OBEs don't really fit the Shadow People description.

That's it for now, I'll try to find more. Any help is still appreciated, let's remember we don't want to single out Shadow People just yet, this could very well be something else. Please don't stray too far from the topic, though, I really don't think my reflection or the world's, the ambient light bouncing off a polished surface, could begin defying every law of optical physics and create a haunting. I did say there was something with my reflection, but I had been feeling a presence for hours, which might explain the weird feeling I got from looking at the mirror.
