posted by Vimick:
In short psipog lost its purpose and became diluted.
That is part of the reason. Or at least, part of the reason why I don't feel bad for closing PsiPog. I wrote about this idea here: http://alittleweird.com/2007/11/14/i-dont-miss-psipog/

The main reason I closed PsiPog was because it was rooted in disbelief. But even those reasons I don't fully agree with now. Basically: we all grow and change. I started PsiPog when I was 17 years old, 8 years ago. PsiPog feels like de-evolution to me, at this point. It's moving backwards.

So, I will definitely NOT be re-opening PsiPog. I think metalforever has done a great job at PsionicsOnline, and her community seems to capture the essence of PsiPog (and improved on it!). If you want that sort of psionic community, then hang out over there.

I think the community here is cool. It's big enough so we have some cool ideas floating around, but not so big that I have to have a team of people moderate everything.

I am at another cross-road in my mind concerning the best way forward with psionics. I mentioned some of my current thoughts in this thread: http://alittleweird.com/forums.php?cmd=thread&tid=501 . Once I finish pounding out the ideas, I may change ALW around a bit. The Blog format is getting on my nerves a little :-).
