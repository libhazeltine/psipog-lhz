Okay, so I'm just wondering, has any one else ever pondered this?

I'm thinking that eventually we are going to advance in psionics in maybe lets say
1000 years or something SO MUCH that we will have universal techniques, and be able to
do the most ridiculous feats ever (like levitating our selfs, or sending really big
things flying) and everyone will know about it. There won't be any "skeptics" because
everyone will actually believe in it. So anyways, since it will be a skill almost as
common as walking, aren't we going to eventually kill each other? I mean seriously, if
we do manage to advance this far, aren't we be going to have wars in witch we send
each other flying across the streets and make our heads smash into someone's windows,
people remote viewing on others in their private moments, sucking our energy dry, etc.
If we end up advancing that far...I swear were going to kill our selfs. This is why
I'm glad that we most likely will not advance that far anytime soon, I'm also glad we
do have skeptics because of this too.


There's also another fear I have.
Since we have skeptics now, what if lets say in the next 50 years,
we advance enough so that we have universal techniques for things like PK
and we are easily able to grow and advance in it...there probably still will
be many skeptics, but there will probably be a lot more believers since we most
likely will be able to do something beyond spinning a piece of stupid tin foil on
a thumb tack, and maybe even dangerous. Like what if we could send a pencil flying at
a fast enough speed to literally stab, and kill someone with it...

The point that I'm trying to get to is, eventually we are going to advance at least
that far fairly soon, and eventually someone may use PK to deal physical harm to
someone, so basically it's going to be the salem witch trails all over again,
because obviously people are not going to be able to accept this, they will
assume it as some sort of devils work of some sort...
