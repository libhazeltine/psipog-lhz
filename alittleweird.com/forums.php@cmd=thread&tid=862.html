(all done online)
-First we gather about 25 of us willing to try it.
-Then we agree on a lottery to predict, like the Powerball.
-Then one of us makes a document with all the lottery numbers on it.
-All participants stare/meditate on the document for a few minutes.
-Then we all choose our numbers.
-The numbers are added and divided to find the average.
-Then we wait to see if they match.
