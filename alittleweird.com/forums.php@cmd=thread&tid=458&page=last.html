Favorite animal and color? Test
Author Message
chillpill
Alright, first off, answer these simple questions:
What is your favorite animal and why?
What is your favorite color and why?

***Very important! Before reading on, have answers ready for both of these questions!!
It might even be better to write the answers first, before reading on!
If you don't follow these steps, your answer might end up being invalid.
I'm hoping for mature honesty.

Example:
My favorite animal is a tiger, because I see tigers as strong and independent.
My favorite color is blue because I see that as a very relaxed color.


Did you answer already? Alright, here we go:

Your favorite animal represents qualities you would search for in a life partner.
In my case, I search for a tiger-like person because I want someone that is strong and independent, not weak and clingy.

Your favorite color represents qualities you see in yourself.
In my case, I see myself as blue in that blue is a relaxed color and I'm not super edgy all the time.

Not having a favorite animal because you can't decide could mean that you don't know what you look for in a partner.
Not having a favorite color because you can't decide could mean that you don't know how to view yourself.

Post your stuff and give me feedback!!

Disclaimer:
This doesn't work for everyone folks, especially if you are younger.
