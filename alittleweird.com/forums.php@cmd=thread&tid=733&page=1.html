Two nights ago, I had a headache that was preventing me from getting to sleep. I didn't have access to aspirin or anything, so I decided in exasperation to try some of the healing techniques I read about on Psipog.
Basically, I gathered psi in my normal way, but instead of my standard purple misty/wind like energy, I visualized a soothing blue energy, kind of like antiseptic ointment, but, you know, energy. I moved it up to my head, and sort of envisioned the painful parts as red, with the energy going on top of them and draining away the 'red' pain.
It worked as long as I kept my attention on it. If I would get distracted, the pain would return, albeit lessened.

Does anyone have similar experiences, tips, or really anything to share?

I have a cool story to tell!! (finally..)

I was experiencing extreme discomfort in my (ahem) gonads. Then one day, someone who I deeply respected said three words to me which sent my attitude into a bit of spiral which inspired my incredible recovery.

I had heard about chakras, and thought Id give it a shot.

I focused on my sacral chakra by visualizing my lower stomach with an inner burning ball of bright orange light. This would usually cause me to tremble, kinda like having a really good pee.

Also, when I was in pain and could not focus on this burning ball of energy, I would realize my fear and anxiety towards the pain, and instead turn it into love and compassion.

Weeks later I was feeling much better. The pain only showed up every couple days, and was mostly brief when it did.. but then.. one night.. I thought I was going to die.

My lower stomach was on fire, and it was the most painful thing I have yet experienced in life.

Then I began to expel all kinds of bright shit.. bright red.. but not blood.. it did not even murk up the water.. it was solid.

After the first two hours, my clothes began to suffocate me, and I needed to lie down on the cold ground..

So there I was.. bare ass nekked on my bathroom floor.. crying..

Now this is an awkward enough situation to be in all alone, but the drama aspect is juicy as heck.. (oh god) My mother had decided to take in a foreign student for the week, and this was his first night away from his parents and Japan for all his 15 years of experience.. his room had a walk in closet which was linked to the bathroom I was in.

I was fucked up on the floor.. bare ass naked, puking and shitting so violently.. sobbing.. basically dieing..

Every now and again, I pick myself up and try to get some sleep in my bed downstairs, and I'd hear him go use the washroom.. this is like 4 in the morning at this point.

Anyways, since then, I have had hardly any problems..

I imagine this is an ongoing battle, or theme in my life.
