OBE at first try - Yes,it's possible!
Author Message
Vira
So,I was way too lazy to train for OBE so I didn't.I was always like "Meh.Some other time maybe" and I never tough I'll actually have an OBE (Stupid me)
Untill...
One night,since I was already in bed I decided to...try and have an OBE.So,I told my body parts to fall asleep (and it worked like a charm) and then I was just waiting...I wasn't thinking of anything (well,it's not THAT hard :P)But I got too impatient so I just fell asleep.
And at some time I woke up,just to find myself floating above my body.I was...(I'm not english so I'm not sure how to say it)in a position like Jesus on the cross thing.I couldn't see anything,it was pitch black,and I felt so tired that I couldn't open my eyes but I did moved my head to the left.And then I woke up.
That was the most awesome thing in my life...
Also,I'm sure I've had other OBEs,because I can clearly remember a few dreams where I've switched to OBE but I don't remember an OBE...So for me the best method is trough dreams.
(I'm very sorry for my spelling mistakes as I am a 12 year old and English is not my main language.)
