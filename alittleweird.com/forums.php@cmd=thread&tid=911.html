OK this night i had the worst expierience since i started out in my psychic developement.

First of all a warning for everybody! DO NOT practice in any magickal/psychic activities while u are on drugs (INCLUDING ALCHOHOL).
because this is what happenend to me last night.

Last evening i was sitting relaxed with some friends together, having some drinks.
I was not badass drunk but had some beer of course.

Then i went to bed and started to play a bit around with some Energy Manipulation/ drawing,and storing etc.
Thinking of nothing bad I went to sleep.

This is where it started. It must have been somewhere on the astral plane. when i had an encounter.
I was locked in an house and met something. I dont know what it was but it must have been some kind of evil female entity. I dont think it was a Demon because I wouldnt be able to sit here and write this if it would have been one.

Whatever it was it was, it locked us in this place and was trying to get control over me.I was trying to escape but it was impossible.
I was fighting against her will of controlling me with all power. but she was already starting to control parts of my body.

Then i suddenly woke up in horror and panic.I felt really cold my heart was beating fast and I had goose-skin all over my body.i wasnt really noticing that i woke up so i fell back into sleep after ca. 1-2min.

And again i found myself in locked in the same place.she was still there but i never could see here directly, she was always hidden and blurred.

She attacked me again with even more agressivity than the first time,but i could refuse her to posess me. she was raging and again I got snapped out of sleep.
The same feelings appeared,Heart-beating,fear,coldness and waves of goose-skin.
Then I started to focus and calm myself.
I knew that these entities gain power through fear because it attracts lots of energies. So i avoided to get scared and started to get a clear mind.
must have been something around 3am. At that time I wasnt drunk anymore so I meditated and found her.
I had a another fight with her in my mind but I was starting to get control back.
The bad thing was that i wasnt able to get rid of her completely and still felt her strange presence.

Finally my subconcious took control and care of me.
Suddenly I started to radiate incredible nice soothing warmth and heat that i actually started to sweat.
It was so strong and I knew I will win the fight. And then i felt all panic and coldness leaving my body in a rush and knew she was gone!

She tried to come back 1 or 2 times but this heat was so protective that there wasnt any chance.

After that I decided to "clean" up my mind and also the astral place where i met her, to erase all traces she left behind.
Then i fell into a deep restorative dreamless sleep.


That was one really intense expierience and I wish noone to have it.I learned my lesson. So again DONT practice while drunk or whatever, it will alter and open your mind and body for anything which is waiting on the other side.

Please tell me what you are thinking about this, or if anyone else expierienced something similar.

Thanks
