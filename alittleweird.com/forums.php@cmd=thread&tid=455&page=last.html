Trouble with visualization - can only visualize with my eyes open and concentrating on something else
Author Message
Liza
Hi, all, I'm new to this site, but not to psionics. I have been studying it on and off for about 7 to 8 years now, but am only advanced in theory, not practice. It's been more of a hobby than a life journey of mine.

However, I like to try little things and practice and such. I am also very spiritual and what I believe is ultimately linked to psionics and energy.

Ever since I started studying psionics, I've had a particular problem with visualization. When I read an article explaining a scene to visualize or a story describing a situation or even start daydreaming with my eyes open, I can literally see what I'm visualizing clearly, but in the background.

However, when I close my eyes and try to visualize something without another 'mind process' working in the foreground, it doesn't work nearly as well. The image is distorted and blurred and feels very unreal. When my eyes are open and I'm mostly thinking about something else, I find that my visualizations, though being in the back of my mind, appear as much sharper and clearer images.

This gets annoying because while I can 'see' things clearer with my eyes open, I can't -focus- on them as much as I would be able to if I set everything aside, closed my eyes, and visualized something. But when I try, everything is blurry and feels very unreal. Any ideas on how I can overcome this?
