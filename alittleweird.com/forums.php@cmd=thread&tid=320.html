Waldo
Any time I do meditate (not often) I simply sit somewhere quiet, look at something brighter than the surroundings, relax and clear my mind. Then I try the best I can to keep my mind on nothing, just gently bringing my mind back to that if a stray thought gets in there somewhere. The bright whatever is just something to focus on - it's often easier to keep your mind on one thing that think about nothing at all. Hold it for 5-10 minutes, and you've meditated, basically. I can't really help you with focus as it pertains to TK, because I'm still trying to get it consistent myself. Sigh. Hope I was of some help! =)
