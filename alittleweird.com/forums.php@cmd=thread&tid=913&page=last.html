While on the subject of dreams...
Author Message
EyeBall
Last night was a rather interesting night. Actually, the experience didn't happen while on a dream, but during a bit of time in which I woke up randomly. Now, it had been a long time since I had those "wake many times a night" sort of thing that lead to many sleep paralysis, and last night was a small case of that.

The important thing here is not the sleep paralysis. I woke up with my eyes closed, leaning to one side, I felt awake, definitely not a dream. I did not want to move, I don't even know if I was able to move, but suddenly I could see myself on the bed. I was half uncovered. I saw an entity standing behind me, facing my back, holding something which I figured to be a blanket or something. Dark blanket thing.. or something.

At that time, just by seeing that entity I became sorta freaked, but I've learned to stay calm (experiences with sleep paralysis), and quelled that freaked out feeling. I just thought to myself that the entity had no harmful thought against me and he was just going to lay that "blanket" looking thing on me.

Needless to say, the entity approached, and as the "blanket" was laid, I felt an outburst in tingly sensations and vibrations in my physical body, and then I was back in my body and soon afterward fell asleep.

Not too sure what to make out of it.

(and then I went onto dream about my cat, who's been gone for 4 days. Very hurt, with injuries that seemed real. The cat usually returned after 1 day or less. And I'm not the type to worry, I usually think stuff like- "eh, it'll come back eventually" but after that dream, I'm not so sure.)
