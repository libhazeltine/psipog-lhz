It depends on what you're trying to practice, really. If you're trying telepathy, then you can do it virtually anywhere quiet. No-one's going to question you both not really talking and trying to concentrate. But if you're doing psychokinesis, it's going to be hard to find anywhere that's quiet enough and has no possible outside influences that could ruin your experiments.

One suggestion is to try and get someone *else* involved in psionics, and see if you can use their house. Possibly not the easiest way, but it could work. Otherwise, try and find a public place where you can meet and will not arouse suspicion particularly. I've had a few practice sessions in the library (trying to do some work, getting bored, starting PK practice...), but it's hard if you can't find a secluded spot (luckily, my library is full of them). I mean, the worst that can happen is someone asks what you are doing. And then you can lie. If you chose your practice area right, people might not even walk by you. It's not like your average randomer is really going to know about psionics anyhow. =)
