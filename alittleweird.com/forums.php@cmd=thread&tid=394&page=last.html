I also usually find that music can disrupt meditation sometimes. If, however, you can focus on the actual sounds of the music as they present themselves to you, it can prove useful if you are good at concentrating and need something to concentrate on to keep yourself from thinking.

If I had to listen to some kind of "music" to meditate, I would listen to one of my ambient music sound tracks. These include rain drop sounds, the sounds of a "babbling brook", and the sounds of ocean waves hitting the shore. These sound tracks can either be a distraction or a hindrance depending on the circumstances and the preferences and skills of the user.
