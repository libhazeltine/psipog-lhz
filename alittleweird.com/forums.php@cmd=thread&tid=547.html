Akio
It's all up to you, but time and time again I see people fail because they never bothered to learn the basics.

Actually, if I were you, I would start with meditation. During the meditation, breath deeply, In and out. As you breath, feel the energy coming up into your body from the earth. You will probably notice as you breath a sense of heat running up your legs, into your abdomen, and out to the rest of your body.

Once you feel yourself full of that energy, breath in and out slowly before you open your eyes. You may be able to see your aura upon opening it, maybe not (I did but you may experience something different). go ahead and get up, stretch a little, get used to the feel of the energy in your body. After you are satiated, let the energy you have drawn in flow out with your breath, and feel the energy in the air. You will feel at peace, and at the end of it all, you will have a heightened sense of everything around you.

Do this from time to time, when you are frustrated, or need to relax is probably the best time, because it will have a calming affect on your body. Some have reported that this exercise can heal, although it's up to you to decide that much.

Once you've done this at least once, then you can start playing around with energy. You can manipulate it, form it into constructs, and then just let it go. bring energy into your body from the air, and vice-versa. Get a feel for moving it around, in, and out of your body... This can be done whenever you want, it can be done right after a meditation exercise, or later on. The Idea here is just to play around with the energy and get a feel for it.

As you begin to develop your skills, you may find that you can "sense" things about other people. This can be good, and this can be bad, it's really more of what your sensing and whether you need to be able to sense it.

If you are getting negative affect from this new sense, it is probably good for you to learn how to shield. I can't really help you with shielding because it would be too complicated to teach you specifics. In this case, I would differ to http://www.psipog.net/cat-shields.html and tell you to read all the articles. At this point you should be able to understand them and figure out what works for you.

After a while of following this loose guide, you should find that TK will come a little more naturally to you. Once you've gone that far, then it's time for a strict practice schedule for Tk. Keep in mind that you should continue to follow the other guidelines I've given you even during the strict TK schedule, as this will be helpful to you in practice. (believe me on this one, it will help you maintain yourself as you practice)

And as a Final note, good luck.
