I have a huge whiteboard in the room where I sleep and I am planning to try and write on it the next time I'm out. If the pen is too heavy, perhaps using a large frame filled with sand might work. I also thought of getting one of those toy drawing boards where you draw with a magnetic pen. There must be something like that that would work.
