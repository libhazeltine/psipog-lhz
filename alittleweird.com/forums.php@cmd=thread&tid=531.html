I have encountered a few instances where I could sort of see through my closed eye-lids as well. But, I always considered it to be fake in a way. My explanation is that my mind predicted surrounding objects around me according to other senses, memory, and logic. For instance, if I were to move my hands in front of my face while my eyes were closed, I would see them moving because I know they are moving, so my mind just fills in the blank. I have also looked around my room with my eyes closed, and in a way I also saw the room, but once again, my theory is that my mind just used it's memory to draw a picture of what is there.
