To me it seems that with each comeback, it takes less and less time to get it going again.

Don't stick with the psi-wheel if you're interested in more. Experiment and try pk on all kind of stuff, practice sliding, bending, levitating and pulsing stuff as well. It's amazing what can be done with pk, whereas psi-wheel is one of the least practical, in my opinion.
