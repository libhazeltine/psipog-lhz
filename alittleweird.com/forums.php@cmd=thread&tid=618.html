Ubiquitous Consciousness
Author Message
Psikillion
I was fooling around in my programming class today when I suddenly remembered this one class where I learned about ubiquitous computing. For those who are not computer science majors or not very computer savvy, ubiquitous computing is basically a network of computers that, when a computer needs more processing power, gives said computer more processing power from the other computers on the network.

So what does this have to do with the paranormal?

After remembering about ubiquitous computing I wondered, "The brain is a computer, and in ubiquitous computing computers can give other computers more power through a network, so could it be possible to connect our subconsciousness/consciousness together in order to undergo greater psionic feats like levitating large objects, more accurate remote viewing and precognition, and possibly even teleportation?" Okay maybe I didn't think all that, but the question I ask to you is the same.

"Could it be possible to have our subconsciousnesses and/or consciousnesses to work together in order to perform greater psionic feats?"

-Psikillion
