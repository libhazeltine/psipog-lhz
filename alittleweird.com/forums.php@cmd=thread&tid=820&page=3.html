Of course, this could be a valuable mechanism to see if past experience were "self-created". For example, if you "shifted" and believed yourself to be a "werecat" or whatever you want to call it, and had perceived better eyesight, then we could conclude that was a delusion ... a self created experience.(Or you could say you are a "were-eagle" :-/)

A simple desire to "be" something else.

So I suppose my advice to you is to simply check what it is you experience, and see whether it corresponds factually to things.
