Boom
If your going to read this all I ask is you that read the whole thing. This post touches topics on spirituality and progression of the soul.

Thought Process - Positive thinking brings about positive action. Positive thinking reinforced with positive emotions brings about change. I'm talking about life changes.

The Garden Of Eden is a state of mind. You might even say Heaven on Earth is a state of mind. Its ALL a state of mind. The particular state of mind grants you new opportunities. New perceptions. The universe might attract someone to you that is looking to give someone an opportunity of a life time. Most likely it will lead you to one person who in then will lead you to another or you will listen to what they are saying intuitively. Its a little like a wild domino effect.

The challenge in this world is to think positive in any situation. Its ok to think negative, because time is constantly moving. You thought negative then, what are you thinking now. If you start thinking positive the universe will put you in situations that promote your thinking and attract people to you. You are the company you keep. As you get older the people you keep by yourself for the longest are most likely the ones that know where your coming from when you speak.

Domino Effect - You think positive to knock one domino over but you must keep thinking positive to knock them all down. It makes us human to be mad but it becomes a problem if you stay mad. Thats the point. Its ok to FEEL the good and bad but its not ok to let the bad vibes linger. Consider the dominos spiritual boundaries.

Intuition - Heaven is a place on earth? Remember that song? My taste in music does not include that genre or that, but my intuition likes that song. It clicks with it. More specifically the 'heaven is a place on earth' part. Listening to positive music is great. Things can click in any aspect of life.

Love is a force, like solar rays. When you feel love for yourself other people can feel that.Keep knocking down the dominos and you get closer to heaven on earth or to a feeling of heaven on earth.

-Theres two type of people in this world. The ones that realize and the ones that don't / good people / bad people / negative people - positive people. Everyone else is the in between.

But were all still connected. Negativity just disconnects us.


The Indigo

The indigo, spiritual warrior, crystal child they are just titles to people that are born to realize. Kids, Adults that know who they are or have a desire to know thus not taking a life time to find themselves thus enabling them to make change happen quicker and in more peoples lives as the more people you come in contact with (another domino effect).

Emotional Knowing

If you feel good, embrace it and remember what it felt like. If you feel bad question why you feel bad then counter it with a variation of love. Have Hope & Faith.

You dont have to be flying on cloud nine but you do need that desire to progress. Accepting 'this is as good as it gets' can be..not so great. It can always get better. Its not your place to know how, just believe that it will. God will take care of the rest. Even if you dont plan on leaving your house for a month. Remember that saying "where there is a will there is a way". Your 'will' is your thinking/emotions combined. The 'way' is the force that is the universe. The universe is always responding to your will. Good and Bad.

The Ghost and The Darkness

Darkness doesnt always mean darkness. Concept.

The Evil in this world is the ghost..cause if you don't pay attention to it, it goes away. Attention is another way to give something thought - or power.

The Ghost is evil or 'negative' the darkness is the universe and you are the light. We were made in his image, the light. Light = Love

Good thing about faith and hope, nothing has to exist for it to be there. Both are branches of love. Love being god. God being the universe. The Universe being you.

2012

The whole concept behind the 2012 hysteria is to bring about this form of thinking. You raise earths vibrations by raising your own. You raise your own through what was previously written.

Theres no way a human being that is dominantly negative to raise any vibrations cause the individual is not yet able raise there own vibe. You can do positive things and go about them in negative ways. That negative way is just that, negative. The road to hell is paved in 'good intentions'.

I also believe that "2012" is going on now.

You see this philosophy is a hidden message in things we see on a daily basis. Because all things are derived from negative and/or positive emotions.

"If this is wrong, there is no right."
