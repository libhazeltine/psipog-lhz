"Measuring Psi" is one of the great challenges we face in the scientific investigation of psionics.

One factor is that "Psi" is a blanket term that is used in various ways that are not always "quantitative." One person will talk about "needing more psi" while another will state that "it was psi that did it."

As was mentioned previously in this thread, how "psi" behaves is multi-faceted and is very difficult to standardize.

Personally, I regard "psi" in the same I regard "wind." There is no "wind" as a thing. It is moving air. We measure wind by how it affects another instrument or object. We can measure the speed, the pressure and the density of air and that gives us indicators to how waves on the ocean nad the patterns of weather and such behave.

From my research, I have reasons to believe that "psi" is simply the observed effect of another more fundamental system of energy making changes to the physical. I feel it's safe to say that "psi" is not a special energy all its own but as air in motion becomes wind, this energy put to action becomes what we call "psi."

Without going in to too much detail, research indicates that the energy at work here is potential energy stored in the environment (universe). Pressure on this energy (scalars) creates stress energy (potential) that can be programmed with information (thought) to do work.

I have secretly been working on a unit of measure for "psi." It's okay enough for my work but it definitely needs a lot more refinement to be useful to others. It's a unit of measure for "psi influence" on a measured quantity of material.

The point of reference is a sphere of platinum-iridium alloy suspended by a wire from an axis two feet from a table surface.

The material is very pure and consistent. If indeed my theories are true that PK is a passive effect that interacts with an object's material rather than an active force that "pushes" an object, the sphere will require a consistent amount of "influence" to move it a specific distance. A laser measures the degree of movement and that corresponds so the amount of "influence" "psi" has over the object. Thus far tests have been positive.

In my mind, "psi" as a “stuff” is only present with energy manipulation and psychokinesis. Telepathy, empathy and other thought-based skills work purely on an information level and are not related to "psi" as a substance; basically the distinction of Psi-Kappa and Psi-Gamma.

"Psi" as a classification encompasses it all, but it not measurable as such. It's just a classification.

My method for measurement seems to be consistent enough for PK measurement. It has an unchanging reference point and seems to be repeatable between testers. I have a tentative designation of "PKI" for "unit of psychokinetic influence." I used platinum-iridium alloy because it is directly related to the determination of the International Prototype Kilogram. Though material densities differ, with marginal accuracy I can weigh an object in kilograms, measure its dimensions, estimate its volume and know how many "PKI's of influence" are needed to move it before resistance.

The interesting component here is that a "field of influence" comes in to affect. Roughly calculated it takes 1pki of influence to get 180g of platinum-iridium to travel 1cm in one second; but only if you envelop the entire object with influence. If you are "tugging" on only a small portion of the object you will need much more influence to move it. The same goes the other way. If the object weighs the same but is larger in volume (is less dense) it will take far less total influence to move provided you can distribute the influence across the entire object.

It's very neat stuff.

I'm working with some colleagues to fix some of the broken math and hopefully have a usable standard by year's end.

Now this is all well and good for PK but what about energy manipulation? Well, at the moment, we can only measure what is interacting with physicality. Once energy interacts with the physical it has become psychokinesis.

I’m working on a way to measure how energy in energy manipulation behaves by creating physical fields that are modulated by the energy passing through them or around them but for obvious reasons that is much more difficult.

My goal with PKI is to give us a physically grounded way to measure PK influence in a reliable way. I think it achieves that. The next (big) step is to see how energy manipulation like constructs, shields and such can be “measured” using understood and reliable reference points. But we have to leave the physical to examine that.

While PKI gives us a reference of physical effect, it still doesn’t give us insight into the energy used to fuel PK as well as other skills and abilities. But we are getting there. ^__^

Heh. Wish me luck!
