I tought it might be handy to have some sort of list about all paranormal abilities on this forum. so I decided to make one.


Kenisis

Telekenisis/Psychokenisis
Probably the best known form of kenisis. Telekenisis or psychokenisis is the ability to move objects with the mind.

Pyrokenisis
Having the ability to control fire: make a flame larger, smaller, make it turn left, or even put out a fire!

Cryokenisis
The control of ice. With this ability you can make water turn into ice, or make something freeze.

Aquakenisis/Hydrokenisis
Controlling water, or creating it. With this ability you can make whirlpools, waterballs, soak your enemys or even use water as a sort of whip!

Aerokenisis
Aerokenisis is controling wind/air. You can make it blow the way you want, or make tornados if you're skilled.

Terrakenisis/Geokenisis
The ability to control earth, rocks, sand, mud, and so on. (more info coming soon)

Florakenisis
Having control over the growth of plants and trees. Need some roses for your girlfriend? No problem! Plant a seed and use your florakenisis to make it grow alot quicker!

Electrokenisis
Creating or controlling electricity. Shoot spark from your fingers to stun people or give your gaming system energy!

Technokenisis
A not very famous ability. Gives you control over technological stuff, like computers or your Nintendo DS. Free level skipping 8D

Photokenisis
Being able to manipulate light.

Tempokenisis
The ability to change the temperature in a room, of your body, and so on.

Magnokenisis
Capability to manipulate magnetic fields.

Chronokenisis
Being able to control time.

Biokenisis
Manipulating genes inside a body. But be carefull: if you mess up while manipulating yourself, you might end up sick (or even dead O__O).


Other psionic abilities

Levitating
A very populair skill which make you, well, levitate. By levitating you can actually "fly".

Telepathy
Sending messages from mind to mind. You can also use telepathy to play fun games.

Empathy
Seeing the emotions of others.

Clairvoyance
Receiving things you don't know by using your mind.

Psiballs
Making psiballs is a cool and famous ability to have, because you can do lots of things with it. In example let it move something, make it tell something, playing paintball using psiballs, and much more fun things.

Shields
Creating shields to protect you from energy-vampires and other bad things from outside. (note: this is no physical shield. A human can't feel it, so it won't protect you against physical damage)

Constructs
Constructs are made out of psiballs. you can make cubes, globes, all kinda funny things, and more! you can even make a *psipet out of it!
*A psipet is an animal made of, well, (a) psi(ball). you can make it act like the animal it looks like, or make it act completely different! It can also gather psi-energy and give it to you.

Healing
A handy skill to have. Using this skill you can heal your wounds, or if you're good, bug diseases! You never have to pay for medicines any more :)


Others

OBEs
OBE stand for Out of Body Experience. This is when your spirit comes out of your body and travels around. See this article for more info on OBEing.



Be sure to tell me if there are any typos, missing abilitys, or misplaced abilitys in this post. or is there info missing? please tell me!
