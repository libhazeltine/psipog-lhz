There are alot of dreams that I dont tend to remember. And the dreams I do remember, make sense and are very creepy.

I had this dream that a stranger took me to a temple to get something. When I got to the end of the temple, there was a dead end with the stranger drowning me in sand. I died, and served him as a ghost untill some goddess that I dont think really exists, appeared. Then I woke up. :P

Just a basic summary.

I then go onto google to look up my dream meaning. Looking up various parts of it like "Stranger, Goddess, ghost, drowning, death, temple, etc."

Pretty wierd, but alot of them have similar meanings.

Drowning:"If you drown to death, then is refers to an emotional rebirth."

Death:"To dream of your own death, indicates a transitional phase in your life. You are becoming more enlightened or spiritual."

Temple:"To see a temple in your dream, represents your spiritual thinking, meditation and growth. It is also symbolic of your physical body and the attention you give it."

Sand:"To see sand in your dream, signifies a shift in perspective or a change in your attitude."

And stranger meant "a part of yourself that is repressed and hidden." while the goddess at the end had something to do with being feminine, but I dunno how that fits in with everything.

But do you see the common element here? "Growth, shift, transitional, rebirth" Things are going to change dramatically, I would think...But how?
