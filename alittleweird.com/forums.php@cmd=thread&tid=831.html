I was always curious about this one encounter i had at work.

Use to work at a wawa gas station. My boss came to me and said someone has been stealing the oil and he wanted me to find out who. I thought nothing of it. Months went by. I was very sensitive this particular day I was working, energy wise. As I was working something inside literally stopped me from what i was doing and every single ounce of my attention was focused on this one guy, a younger scruffy looking guy that was putting oil into his girlfriends beat up boat of a car.

I said did you pay for that oil? He said yea, he said he paid inside, he told me to ask the cashier Dominic. So i radioed inside and ask Dom if he sold this kid some oil and he said yea. So everything is all good. Right...no...something inside me is still saying something is not right to the point where i went inside and asked Dominic personally yet again if he sold this kid oil and he said no, Dominic thought i said gas. Gas and Oil do not sound alike.

Either some manipulation was going on or this kid was one ballsy liar or Dominic is an idiot and scruffy is still one hell of a ballsy liar...he actually sounded confident when he told me to talk to Dominic but i still detected a shady undertone.

Just trying to look at it from all perspectives. What do you think.
