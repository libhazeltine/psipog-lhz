Darned homework... well, im still posting this...

So, ive had my fair share of experience with sleep paralysis, and although the first time was somewhat scary, it started to get interesting. In fact, 2 days ago, i had many (about 5) successive sleep paralysises. Yeah, as in one right after another.

First off, scaring me is extremely hard, which is why after my first sleep paralysis i was no longer scared of it. Weeks later after that, i had another one, and i was interested in this one; i had all of my senses aware and tried to examine it. Though, it was a failure because i got too relaxed and got out of it. Noow, some days after that (2 days ago) i had another sleep paralysis, and i thought "Ah cool" (now im just gonna list them and say what i did in each)

On the first one- i did what i did last time and tried to examine it as much as possible, but it didnt last long.

Then, i got back to my random thinking, and suddenly i went back into another one. This one, i remembered that getting too relaxed got me out of it. So, i tried to keep a healthy level of 'omg' in order to make it last longer. I heard what some would describe as: being extremely close to metal that is being crushed, being under a low flying plane, or even some beast cries. Then i snapped out of it.

On my normal self, i noticed that i wasnt thinking really, but i was having some sort of self controlled thinkage =d. Just like watching a movie in my mind (about a guy trapped who couldnt get out of a room and kept pounding on the wall...) and i went into another one... In this one, i tried to control it. Tried to make it sound louder, as loud as possible, and succeded, but i couldnt control what the sound was or put the volume down. Tried to move too, but you know how that turns out--

I got out of it, and now i was seeing some ant like creature trying to escape a complex tunnel... mildly entertaining i shall say. Then i went into another one, (i wasnt surprised at the frequency of them til next day) this one, i actually remembered i could OBE out of it. So, i tried what i could, but i could only 'feel' my hands out. Felt them just waving around... I couldnt OBE though.

Last one was pretty much like the 4th one which is a shame because ive gotten real close to OBEing but never could.

No, i dont have any serious health issues and i havent had any in the past. Except for the occasional cold and those. Also, i dont make up stuff :l. I joined psipog when i was 12 or so (got banned from the chat once for being underage too, woo) and i check up on this site often, i just never post.. eh

Anyways, this was a really interesting event, hopefully i can recreate it.

comments anyone?
