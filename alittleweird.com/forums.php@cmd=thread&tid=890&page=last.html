OK, this is going to sound a bit odd. But, have you come across people who are ...let's say "more variable" than others in terms of their looks?

Occasionally, I try pinging when I am at the store, or wherever, to see what crops up. Sometimes I notice people who are 'different' somehow. When I find these people, of course, I do not stare directly at them, but I do pay attention to them with my eyes slightly out of focus, so that I can see them without acting like a complete weirdo.

During these observations, of those who seem unusual somehow, I have found that some are more "fluid" than others. For example, I can think "that guy has really long, thin fingers" and the result will be that the fingers appear longer/thinner. The change is often noticeable, but not extreme. The result is still within the realm of normal. Not before long, the guy will rub his hands or put them in his pockets and start looking around.

I tried this once with a classmate. I thought "That is some really blue eyeshadow. I have never seen such a color on a human before. That is downright hideous." Everytime I looked at her, the color would become more intense. I was not looking at her, just a few side glances around the room. She immediately took up a makeup mirror and started looking nervous. These are just two examples of it, but I suppose you get my drift.

It could be that our perceptions are not that accurate and the minor adjustments are internal. It is curious, though, that the people I observe should respond the way they do. Perhaps my body language is too noticeable? But, I don't think so. Also, it only happens with a few people, the ones who have that odd noticeability about them. I have tried this on people that do not stand out, and then nothing happens at all.

Disclaimer: If you feel a bit unstable, please do not try this! Chill out and wait until you feel like your feet are solidly planted.
