I once did the battery charging thing. My mp3 ran out of batteries, so I took them in my hands and started charging them with energy, when I put them back in it worked perfectly. I did this a couple more times that same day.
Then (sorry if this dissapoint anyone) I realized what really was "charging" the batteries was the heat from my hands. Batteries with little charge usually don't work well when they're cold, so heating them up a bit makes them last a bit longer.
Maybe try heating the batteries using only your hands to see if it's really the heat that's making them work, then try charging them up with energy and see what happens.
