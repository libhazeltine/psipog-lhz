Here's a training schedule I follow during my training months, I would recommend cutting down on the training time for yourself ^_^.
-Deep Meditation (sit down meditation): 20-30 minutes
-light meditation/trancing : 3-7 hours total per day
-Concentration and focus drills : I train this every day, but when not on vacation I call it "school". lol. Outside of school I focus while doing deep meditation about twice a week, I bring up my meditation time by 10 minutes.
-Energy manipulation, This is something I work on 5-10 minutes every day, give or take based on my schedule, sometimes I'll Have 3-6 hour serious sessions. This I would not reccomentd doing.
-Rest, 3-8 hours reccomended, I get about 5 per night during school, on weekends, I get 8 hours sleep, and during summer it can be as .little as 3.
-Researching Psychic/paranormal Phenomena, symbolism, the tarot, numerology, biorythms, etc.

I really don't Set a schedule, But I do do all of these things ussually in a day ^_-
