For those of you who may be confused, the dan tien is located 2 inches below the belly button. The solar plexus is located where that breast part is right between your ribs and a couple of inches above your belly button. Guess what else is in each of those locations? A chakra.

From what I have been able to gather, most people seem to have open sacral (dan tien) or navel (solar plexus) chakras since energy seems to flow more freely in these areas. However, I have seen in some psipog articles and questions that you should also try and discover YOUR personal energy storage center. Some people have reported being able to draw their energy more easily from their head, or their heart, or many other places. I believe it is due to the fact that those people have chakras that are more open than the recommended sacral and navel chakras, or that the sacral and navel chakras may be closed for them.

So if you are one of the people that seem to have trouble drawing energy from the solar plexus or dan tien, it may be because those chakras are closed. You have two options: you could use different chakras in your body that are open, or you can open the chakras that are closed for you. In my personal opinion I don't think it really matters which one you choose, however someone more familiar with chakras would say that you should try to open them to try to "balance" your self.
