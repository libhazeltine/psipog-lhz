Amaroq
I've been lucky all my life when it comes to games of chance. I'd always get favorable coin flips or dice rolls just when I needed them most, probably because I was really competitive and just HAD to win.

I never believed in lucky charms or lucky items. In my opinion, everyone made their own luck. A TV show had an episode about that very thing, and for some reason I started to play with the idea of lucky objects.

Being passionate in my love of wolves, wolf objects became my lucky items. I had wolf shirts and even a bunch of resin carvings and books about wolves on my headboard. I was in my mid-teens now.

One day, I began to wonder where luck comes from. There's gotta be some source of power that lucky people get their luck from. So one day, I imagine my wolf items have this energy within them, just for me. I place my hand on one, and 'will' the energy out of it, up my arm, and into the center of my chest. Once it got there, it flooded outward all throughout my body. It felt really good and intense, and even left my heart beating faster than it had been before. (This particular part of the story usually causes psychically knowledgeable people to tell me I'm an energy vampire.)

I began to make a habit of doing this with my wolf objects, getting to the point where I didn't need to touch them anymore, but rather just hold my arm out to them and will energy out of them all.

Probably a year or two later, I begin to wonder, "I can take in energy. There's gotta be something I can do with it once I have it." Inspired by Dragon Ball Z, I sat down on my bed one day and tried to make a ball of energy in my hand. I could feel the energy that I moved out of my chest and into my hand, and my hand would kinda hurt. I may have even made one. But I couldn't see anything with my eyes, so I figured it was just my imagination and gave up.

A few years later, I come across Psipog. I'm 19 or 20 at the time, and absolutely intrigued by the idea of psionics. I read a bunch of articles and forum posts, taking in all the information I can. But even while I did that, a lot of the information just felt... inaccurate. I took every tip and trick with my own interpretation and went with my instincts as I developed my abilities. A few months later, psipog goes down and I eventually stop paying attention to my abilities. I still knew they were there and casually did the occasional thing or two with them, but other than that, I didn't do much.

I'm 22 now (my birthday was a few days ago), and here I am. After searching for a few hours to see where Sean went after he closed Psipog, I finally found this place.

I'd been trying another forum in the past few days, and while it seems at least decently legitimate, they seem to have a strict interpretation of what can or can not be done. The general feeling there is that there's a right way to do psionics, with little room for instinctual freedom. After I saw Sean's post here about The Truth About Psionics, I knew this was the right place for me.
