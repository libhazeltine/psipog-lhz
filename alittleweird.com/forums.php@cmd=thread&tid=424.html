Problem with OBE
Author Message
Silly Bear
Hello, (BEWARE: Short introduction ahead! You may skip to the *)

I've always been interested in the paranormal. May it be ghosts, alien encounters, psychic abilities or magic pancakes. I've been into psychic abilities for around 5 years now, while having little to no success in most areas I still find it fun to mess around with.

*One among many things I enjoy doing is trying to get an out of body experience. It's really convienient to do at nights when you can't fall asleep. :)

It feels as I'm close to having one too. I get to the point were it's like my whole body has fallen asleep except for my concious mind. I can feel a weird sensation in the body as I imagine something like a dark hole sucking me in (don't ask me why, I recall reading about that technique somewhere, and it's proven to be effective on me). It's here were I face a problem. After a while I can feel my heartbeat slowing down, and it feels like it's close to stop beating at all. This throws me off-focus and sometimes causes a small panic to me, resulting in me failing. It's quite annoying as I feel kinda like I've reached the last step before successfully having an OBE.

Is it just a normal thing not to worry about? Or have I just found a new stupid way of holding my breath? Anyhow, it's a great obstacle for me.
