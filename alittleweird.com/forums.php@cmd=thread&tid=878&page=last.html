The thing is, there has to obviously be some element that enables psi to take place. I am convinced it is dimethyltryptamine since it is the most potent hallucinogenic in the world and is found naturally in the pineal body and the spinal fluid. Now then, what is a kundalini awakening? That's right, the spine and the "third eye".

Toddler's brainwaves are in the theta state (4hz-7hz per second), and many of them can do remote viewing without knowing there is any such thing - Ingo Swann tells us that RV must take place at the theta brainwave state. Get the picture?

There is, without question, some scientific way one can examine and utilize psi itself as of which. I did a report on DMT being connected to psi activity through the research of Dr Roney-Dougal years ago. Now even if DMT wasn't how psi begins, something else certainly is responsible for it. Electrical activity seems to be present in the people with poltergeist activity surrounding them. And, of course, some people whom have received a near-fatal electric shock have had strange movements of objects around them from time to time.

Some people are born with this core of psi activity where the intensity is obviously higher and thus do psi without even knowing it has a name. The ones who "learn" it, I'm convinced, are controlling whatever strength the "psi core", we'll call it, has. Others don't have such strength because not enough of the psi source's energy has been "charged" like a few people in the world have.

That's what I think it all comes down to. Very few people have so much "psi core" charge that they can move chairs, but many people have just barely enough to move a paperclip one millimeter forward if they really make concerted efforts to do so. My goal is to find out what this "psi core" is and how it is charged/discharged using science.
