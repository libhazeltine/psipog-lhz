deusxmachina
I think being able to keep your distance is very much dependent on how strong your willpower/state of mind is to begin with. If you are emotionally unsettled at the time for whatever reason, or you are rusty on your shield upkeep, or your blood sugar is low, etc., it is going to be more difficult to pull away.

Thoughts have momentum. If you are attempting any sort of energy task, such as establishing a link from a distance, and you don't feel any sort of resistance, you are probably just thinking about it, and not actually doing anything.

With that in mind, it should at least take as much effort, if not more, to undo a link than it did to form in the first place. Unless they are shielding or something, in which case it's a struggle to get in, and you will be forcefully kicked out.

But if you or your target are both emotionally receptive, that link is going to be harder and more complicated to break, especially because you two seem like friends. People naturally form links without even realizing it. Chopping the link, while sometimes effective, might actually cause harm to one or both of you.

I think slow and steady is the message here, like pretty much everything psi. What you probably should do is really fine tune your shields, so that you get just the information that you need in the first place, without the emotional baggage. Then, work on slowly "pinching" the undesirable links. Or leaving the link in tact, but filtering/blocking the majority of information passing through it.

Some people like to be isolated however, and may want to just shut everything out completely when they aren't using it. It's all a personal choice, and you really have to take the time to relax and get in a state where you can get information from your subconscious, and give it information to make the right shields etc.

Good luck!
