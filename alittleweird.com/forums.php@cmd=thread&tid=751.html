My thoughts are similar to Falcon's. I view "Magick" and "Psionics" as generally the same thing, I assume they're both a manipulation of energy. As I see it, Magick is a practice that involves using physical items as symbols... a physical manifestation or representation of the idea, belief, or energy. And psionics seems to be more upfront and literal, approuch to manipulating or harnessing the energy.

For example, say you were trying to win the lottery (since that's a popular subject here), if you were practicing Magick you may light a certain colored candle or use items that symbolize or "attract" money to you. A practicing Psion might focus on winning the lottery and attempt to change their odds by simply visualizing and focusing on their goal.
