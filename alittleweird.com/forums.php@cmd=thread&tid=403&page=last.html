Thought I'd post this, though some of you already know about it, maybe some of you newer guys will find it interesting.

http://coasttocoastam.com

Turn on your radio at 10:00pm MST/9:00 PST to listen.
or plug in to this site at that time: http://www.knrs.com/pages/player.html
(I used to listen to knrs, nothing hugely special, but it's a free online source.)

I don't listen to coasttocoast as much anymore, but I used to. They have some pretty interesting stuff come on. Lately it's been mostly UFO stuff.. I personally like to listen when they have stuff on OBEs, or astral etc, but I know a lot of you are interested in the aliens too, so there ya go. Fun to listen to and its on from 10pm to 5am in the time zone mentioned.
