I fell asleep during my OBE
Author Message
.Dreamer
Most awesome and horrible experience at the same time.
I was in a dream (I don't even remember what was it about) when I gained enough awareness to become lucid and wake up.(this is my technique, and pretty much the only one that works for me)I was back in my physical body.I rolled out of my body (I've never done that before, I always float up) and I felt a cold breeze and a sensation of moving.I remember thinking to myself "Nothing can hurt me, I've got my blanket" because I was still feeling the blanket over me. I then landed a few inches away from the bed, in the center of the room.I couldn't see anything but after a few tries I "opened" my eyes.I tried to pass trough the floor but it didn't work...I wanted to go to the first story and as I was walking down the stairs I tried to float up.It didn't work.I jumped a few times but nothing happened.
I don't remember the rest of it, but I clearly recall returning to my body at will and going out again a few times.It was amazing...Until the last time I returned to my body and realizing it was just a dream.There were random people and I was at random locations, alot of "weird dreamy" stuff. I don't know when exactly did I fall asleep in my OBE.
Maybe I didn't wake up from my lucid dream and had a "false awakening".Maybe I traveled to dreamland with my blanket.

Weird stuff.. 'Nuff said
