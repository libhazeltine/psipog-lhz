The Basics of Psychokinesis: A Brief History of Psychokinesis Research (Hosted By: Metalforever)

About:
This class session will review what was learned during the technique design lesson. We will go into more detail regarding technique design, and will be answering any remaining questions regarding that area. This session will serve as a "progress" checkpoint, where we discuss progress analytically. Finally, we will focus on the main topic for tonight: research. What is it within the context of Psionics? What types of things do psychokinesis researchers study? What controls are commonly seen when designing these experiments? Which individuals are famous within this field and why? The last question we will answer for that night involves how the students of PsionicsOnline can contribute to psi research. I also hope to get everyone off on the right footing for any experiments they may hope to conduct, and discuss common pitfalls. This class session will feature 2 exercises. Although everything explained during this class lesson may seem detailed, it is merely an introduction compared to what can be found in our research section, which curiously, is merely an introduction towards what is it out there regarding psi research.
