Hmm, interesting I suppose. Although, I generally do not believe that 50 questions or so can truly determine your chakra statistics. Maybe a thousand questions can? Maybe no questions can?

I lean more in the direction that no amount of questions can determine this kind of thing. My main reason for thinking like this is: Who says that ____ question is really connected to ____ chakra? I understand that it seems logical for a question saying "Do you have compassion for everyone you see?" to be connected to the heart chakra, but who really knows? Maybe it doesn't have anything to do with the heart chakra, maybe the question cannot truly even relate to chakras in the first place. It's like a color-- you can't describe a color, you can only see it. I believe that it is the same for chakras in the sense that our thoughts and emotions may not be able to be translated into "chakra language" so to speak. That's the best way I can describe why I don't trust websites that ask questions and then give you details about something.
