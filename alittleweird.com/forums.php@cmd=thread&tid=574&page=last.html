Leftover empathy?
Author Message
Corpse
Before I was into psionics, I remember that it was "career day" at my school. There was two people that came in who were in the EMS truck drivers who rescue people and who have to rush them to the hospital. They gave us a tour of inside the EMS truck. The entire time I was inside it, I kept feeling extremely nervous, and very negative for no particular reason. I then thought "wait this is an EMS truck so maybe..." so I asked one of the guys there if a lot of people died in there, and he replied "OH YEAH most definitely!" I went around asking if anyone else felt nervous or negative in any way, but apparently I was the only one.
=\

Back then I thought it was ghosts, now that I think about it, it may have been left over empathy? Because if you think about it, your obviously going to be very worried/nervous when you are in an EMS truck and either you are in a severe condition or a loved one. I find it interesting how emotions can be left behind. Or maybe I was picking them up from a different time/space?

Thoughts/comments?
