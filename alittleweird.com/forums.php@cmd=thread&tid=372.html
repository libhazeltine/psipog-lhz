Lucid Dreaming - Weird or normal?
Author Message
ThePantsThatBe
The topic of lucid dreaming has often been associated with New Age, psionics, OBE's, etc. But is there really anything that should put lucid dreaming in this category? Lucid dreaming has become the focus of some scientific and psychological research over the past few decades. Ever since the discovery of REM sleep in the 50's, dreaming in general has been studied more in depth. Lucid dreaming is merely becoming aware that you're dreaming while you are, and potentially having the opportunity to take control of your dream. Whereas some other topics of "weirdness" may assume breaking laws of physics, lucid dreaming really doesnt seem too out of the ordinary. So what do you think- is lucid dreaming a "weird" topic, or has it been misappropriately labelled and is actually quite normal?
