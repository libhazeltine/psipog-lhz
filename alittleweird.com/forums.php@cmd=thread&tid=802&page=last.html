Yeah, that happens.

The trick I use is to clear my mind, remain passive, and just sit still. My experience has been they fade out eventually, but only if you clear everything.

As a side note... when I first started OBE'ing, I used to THINK I was remaining passive. I used to THINK my mind was clear. I would get frustrated and think, "wtf? my mind is clear! leave me alone!" etc... completely unaware of those thoughts disqualifying my clear mind.

So I would say, "Watch yourself carefully, make sure your mind is clear!"... but that's incorrect as well. If you're in a state of "watching" then you're not in a passive state.

It helps to not give a shit. :-)
