Author Message
NewAgeAlchemist
I'm wondering if anyone has heard of this place before. I learned of it in January but just yesterday started looking up info on it.

Supposedly Aramu Muru was an Inca Priest who did something with a golden disk (powerful artifact that was held in the Inca capitol) before passing through the doorway after the Spanish arrived.

The Doorway itself is carved from solid rock and the local people tend to keep away from the area. Its said that people disappear through it and others have come through followed my glowing orbs of light. Jerry Wills runs a expedition company that offers guided trips to various places in peru and claims to have gone through the door.

Wills doesn't give tours to the door I don't believe, he leads them to Marcahuasi (also spelled: Markawasi, Marka Wasi, Marca Huasi) which is the other area i mentioned, he believes the area functions in a similar fashion on a larger scale.

The door itself isn't that large. its just a carving out of the rock face thats a foot or two deep and a bit shorter than most western doorways. Does apear to be another carving laying over the top that resembles someone laying down. Its located north of Chucuito, a town very near the lake, but i don't believe near the amazon since the lake is at such a high elevation. Aramu Muru's Doorway:

Marcahuasi is known for a number of stone sculpture that the jury is kinda out on if a number are man made.
