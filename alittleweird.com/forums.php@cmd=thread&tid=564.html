You are not entirely correct. A black hole is formed when a star not a planet collapses in on itself.

here is how it happens:

As a star reaches the end of its life and fuses all of its elements it begins to expand. if the mass of the star is close to or less then the mass of our sun then the star colapses but because the mass is not great enough to over come the electron degeneracy (the force it takes to press electrons extrememly close together) the star then rebounds creating a super-nova.

Now if the mass of the star is 1.3-3 times the mass of the sun then when the star colapses at the end it overcomes this electron degeneracy (able to compress electrons so they are extremely close together) but is not able to overcome the neutron degeneracy hence a neutron star is formed. These are extremely dense and extremely small.

NOW if the mass of the star is 3.1 times or more than the mass of our sun the collapse of the star is so great it overcomes the electron AND the neutron degeneracy after this there is nothing to stop it so now it collapses in on itself forever and ever.

The black hole itself is a single point with ZERO volume and infinite density.

what people dont understand is that black holes dont "suck" anything into them. merely when things come into range they "fall" into the singularity.

Another thing people don't understand is that the event horizon (the point of no return) from the black hole is quite small. There is an equation called the Schwarzschild radius that shows the correlation between the mass of black hole and how large its event horizon is.

In the case of the smallest black hole POSSIBLE the mass of the black hole HAS to be AT LEAST 3.1 times the mass of our sun or there is no way it can overcome the degeneracies to colapse in on itself to form itself.

The Schwarzschild radius of the smallest black hole possible is about is equated out to be 10 kilometers. YES KILOMETERS. it is much tinier than you think.

thats means you can come within 10 kilometers of a black hole whose mass is 3.1 times the mass of our sun and still be able to escape from it no problem. BUT once you cross that line, that event horizon, NOTHING can escape. not even light because the escape velocity from within the event horizon is GREATER than the speed of light. and nothing can move faster than that.
