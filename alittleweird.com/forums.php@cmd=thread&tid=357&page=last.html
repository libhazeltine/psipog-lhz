Here's some tips from myself. I've had problems with the same things, and I admit they are still persisting. My advice is to try NOT to put so much importance in not moving. If you do, moving your body will completely break your attempt and you won't care to try again. Instead, let your body move and just accept it as 'not being there yet'.

As for stop thinking. Have you ever noticed what it feels like to fall asleep? That suspended, 'not caring' kind of feeling? There will be a point where you stop consciously thinking and just start perceiving. Try to get to that point. Also, try laying down as if you're going to sleep (choose a different position than usual if you feel like you will actually fall asleep otherwise) and try and pinpoint that moment and suspend it.
