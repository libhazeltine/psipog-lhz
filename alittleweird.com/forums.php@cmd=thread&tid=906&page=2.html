It's not how to do it that's important. Once you acquire enough control over your own mind, you can do it in a million ways. "With meditation, there's nothing that you can't do," like a yogi who once visited us had said.

Psionics is certainly not dying. To me it even seems like it has just been born. There are active communities out there, people who do really amazing stuff. There's a forum that I have advertised on this one for .. I guess 3 times.

For inspiration and motivation:

I once requested my father's attention, when his mind was stuck in a while(1) loop by taking the feeling of not being answered and forming a construct in his head with this feeling imprinted. His reaction was pretty interesting =D. He quickly turned around and asked: "What???" with a great surprise in his eyes. Well, so far it has only happened once that I have had to force something like that.

Usually I just visualize what it feels like to be the person I want to connect to. When I get their feeling and feel mine degrading, I can browse stuff related to their feelings. After that, I suddenly understand what they're saying if it didn't make any sense before. It probably works both ways, as they usually begin to understand me better as well. Haven't tried to plant any feelings this way though.

Sometimes I just wish for someone to appear somewhere and usually they do, with a slight delay of a few seconds or a couple of minutes. Making a construct, imprinting the feeling of "I'm waiting" and sending it to them has also worked the same way for me.
