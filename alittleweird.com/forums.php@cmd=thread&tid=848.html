f3licks
@ Kube:

Yes, you could program a construct to carry a telepathic message, but I think it would always be easier to just talk to them.

@Cerberus:

1. While you can program a sensation they give off while touched, generally constructs have no feeling aside from the general tingly-like psi feeling.
Generally, a construct is defined as any programmed psi you create.
2. To make a human sized/shaped construct, just visualize it forming as whatever shape you want it to be.
When you make a psiball, you are programming the psi to be a ball. No shape is inherently harder to make than others, it's just that some are bigger and more complicated. Making a psi-cat is the same as a psiball- it's all a construct programmed to hold a specific shape.
3. That would depend greatly on the skill of the psion, but a big construct like a life sized human would take a lot of time and energy (and you could probably do whatever you wanted to accomplish with it nin a lot easier way.)

@Kube again:

Yeah, you've got the right idea about flaring. Psi seems to emit rather than reflect light- which is why it's not always visible. Flaring a construct is programming it to emit enough light to be visible.

@Insectoid3:

DigGil's article is good. For training, I think that walking around your house blindfolded would more be training your visualization skills, not necessarily awareness. Try to put your awareness into an inanimate object, and really try to feel the insides of the object.
