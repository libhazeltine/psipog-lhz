Author Message
Vimick
I have this happen to me often extremely weird coincidental things that seem way much of a coincidence. a great example is what happened to me today. well it began yesterday. Here is how it went.
I was flipping through on demand on the tv to look for good movie because I was bored i looked through a few and one reminded me of a good movie called Unbreakable with Bruce Willis and Samuel. L Jackson that came out in 2000 . I only saw it once when it did come out in 2000. And i thought to myself that would be a good movie to watch right now but the on demand didnt have it so i dismissed it and watched a dvd i owned.

NOW tonight I am bored again flipping through the channels looking for a movie to watch. AND LOW AND BEHOLD Unbreakable is playing on USA! i have never ever seen any point in the past 8 years that this movie has ever been on tv and now after not having thought of the movie for years and then just thinking about it yesterday it comes on tv? what are the odds? such a weird coincidence. way too coincidental to me. And this is only one example this happens to me occasionally for different things and they are always so oddly coincidental.

Ever have this happen to you? what do you think? am I just being weird?
